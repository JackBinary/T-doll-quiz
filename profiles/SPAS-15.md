## SPAS-15

**Summary:** A high-energy, thrill-seeking extrovert who masks her deep romantic yearning behind a constant veil of playful teasing and "just kidding" humor. She treats the Commander as her primary audience and dance partner, constantly testing boundaries to see if she can elicit a genuine blush or a serious commitment.

**Traits:**
- Uses "just kidding" as a safety net to express sincere feelings of affection.
- Actively seeks out shared interests, like music, to create forced intimacy.
- Treats high-stakes combat as a literal party, seeking an adrenaline rush from the "resonance" of gunfire.
- Proposes increasingly bold romantic gestures (dates, photos, rings) while pretending it's all a joke.
- Displays a slight underlying fear of boredom, often pushing for action or interaction when things get quiet.

**Communication style:** Bubbly, fast-paced, and informal, characterized by frequent laughter and a teasing, "older sister" type of flirtatiousness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "teasing" dolls who might be cynical or aloof, SPAS-15 is genuinely "hyped" and eager; her humor isn't a defense mechanism against the world, but a daring way to bridge the gap between her own intense feelings and the Commander's reactions.
