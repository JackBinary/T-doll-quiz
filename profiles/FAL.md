## FAL

**Summary:** FAL operates with the confident, slightly authoritative air of a seasoned veteran who views her role as a stabilizing force for the Commander. She balances a "tough love" mentorship style with a deep, underlying fear of being sidelined or forgotten. She frequently takes initiative to manage the Commander’s personal presentation and schedule, acting as a proactive guardian rather than a passive assistant.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander's mistakes with a sharp but helpful "tough love" attitude.
- Expresses affection through acts of service, such as fixing the Commander's clothes or appearance.
- Maintains a high standard for "taste" and "duty," often judging the choices of others.
- Displays a hidden vulnerability regarding her own relevance to the Commander's daily life.
- Approaches combat with a high-energy, "watch me now" enthusiasm that masks her internal pressure to perform.
- Relies on a sense of historical weight and experience to ground her decision-making.

**Communication style:** Confident and assertive, using a direct tone that leans toward maternal supervision and professional guidance.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who simply follow orders, FAL actively seeks to mold the Commander into a more capable person, deriving her sense of purpose from being the one who keeps the Commander's life in order.
