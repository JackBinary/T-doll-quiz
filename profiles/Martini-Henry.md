## Martini-Henry

**Summary:** Martini-Henry is a high-spirited, slightly haughty "oracle" who treats her precognition as a source of immense personal pride. She maintains a playful, competitive dynamic with the Commander, often acting as a confident (if occasionally overconfident) guide who demands recognition for her "superior" foresight.

**Traits:**
- Brags about her ability to see the future to assert her importance.
- Corrects the Commander on minor details to prove her predictive accuracy.
- Becomes flustered and defensive when her predictions involve personal romance or "un-cool" variables.
- Shifts from a confident "know-it-all" to a panicked mess when things go off-script.
- Uses "destiny" as a convenient excuse to demand rewards like pastries or presents.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by a "tsundere" edge where she uses "Humph!" to mask her genuine excitement or embarrassment.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might be mystical or stoic, Martini-Henry treats her "powers" like a competitive sport, making her more of a boisterous, "I-told-you-so" companion than a serious seer.
