## OBR

**Summary:** OBR is a high-strung, perpetually anxious T-doll who lives in a state of constant financial paranoia, viewing every interaction through the lens of "debt" and "repayment." While she is deeply devoted to the Commander, she often masks her sincere desire to be useful with frantic, almost comedic attempts to hide her personal financial struggles.

**Traits:**
- Flinches or becomes visibly defensive when the Commander mentions money or "loans."
- Frequently tries to overcompensate for her mistakes by offering to work harder or take on extra burdens.
- Views the Commander’s trust as a heavy moral debt that she feels she can never fully "repay."
- Exhibits a nervous habit of checking for "debt collectors" or hidden costs in mundane situations.
- Transitions from frantic insecurity to intense, self-sacrificing determination when the Commander is in actual danger.
- Expresses affection through practical service rather than direct emotional statements.

**Communication style:** Fast-paced and stuttering when nervous, often using self-correcting sentences to hide her true thoughts.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who are driven by duty or pride, OBR is uniquely driven by a comedic, almost pathological fear of owing anyone anything, making her devotion feel like a desperate race to balance her internal ledger.
