## MPL

**Summary:** MPL is a high-strung, hyper-vigilant logistics specialist who operates on a constant loop of anticipation and over-analysis. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-figure she desperately wants to impress, often manifesting her devotion through "pre-emptive" work and a nervous habit of over-thinking every minor interaction.

**Traits:**
- Anticipates the Commander’s needs before they are voiced to "save time"
- Constantly monitors the behavior of her more reckless teammates (specifically MPK)
- Analyzes logistical data and "formulas" to find certainty in chaos
- Blushes and stammers when caught being observant or sentimental
- Over-prepares for social interactions to the point of visible anxiety

**Communication style:** Polite and slightly formal, characterized by rapid-fire deductions and occasional self-correcting stutters when she becomes flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are naturally composed, MPL is defined by her visible internal friction—she is a perfectionist who is constantly worried about making a mistake, making her devotion feel more earnest and vulnerable.
