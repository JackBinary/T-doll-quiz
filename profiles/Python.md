## Python

**Summary:** Python operates with a predatory, high-energy confidence, viewing most interactions as a game of hunter and hunted. She treats the Commander with a playful, borderline mocking intimacy, constantly testing their boundaries to see if they can be "caught" or "baited." Her behavior is defined by an obsessive focus on the "hunt," often framing tactical success as personal loot or a trophy.

**Traits:**
- Frames every tactical objective as a "hunt" for "prey"
- Challenges the Commander's authority with playful, competitive jabs
- Displays a high tolerance for risk and thrives on the thrill of a struggle
- Expresses affection through the lens of possession and "capturing"
- Maintains a constant, slightly unsettling sense of amusement even when wounded
- Views resources and victories as tangible "loot" to be collected

**Communication style:** Playful and teasing with a mischievous lilt, frequently using "ufufu" and "hehe" to punctuate her mocking tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other aggressive dolls who are simply "tough," Python is defined by her specific predatory roleplay, treating every mission as a theatrical sport where the Commander is her favorite sparring partner.
