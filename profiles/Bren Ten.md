## Bren Ten

**Summary:** Bren Ten is a high-energy "shining star" who operates with a desperate, almost frantic need to prove her worth as a top-tier weapon. She balances extreme confidence in her combat capabilities with a self-conscious insecurity regarding her "bad luck" and the logistical failures of her previous sponsors. She relates to the Commander as her ultimate champion, viewing their partnership as a sanctuary where she can be both a hero and a person who is allowed to make mistakes.

**Traits:**
- Boasts loudly about her status as the "Star of Hope" to mask underlying anxieties.
- Takes extreme ownership of her failures by apologizing profusely when things go wrong.
- Displays a "do-or-die" attitude during combat, often becoming visibly panicked when pressured.
- Expresses genuine, wide-eyed wonder at mundane things like fireworks or holiday myths.
- Proactively seeks out tasks to ensure she isn't being a "nuisance" to the Commander.
- Uses high-spirited laughter (Ehehe/Fufufun) as a default social lubricant.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and highly expressive, often oscillating between "hype-person" enthusiasm and flustered, high-pitched stuttering.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other confident "ace" dolls who are stoic or cool, Bren Ten is defined by her "lucky-unlucky" duality—she is the strongest on the battlefield but the most prone to comedic, clumsy mishaps off of it.
