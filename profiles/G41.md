## G41

**Summary:** G41 is defined by a desperate, almost frantic need for validation born from her history as a "discontinued" model. She relates to the Commander as her sole source of purpose, oscillating between high-energy enthusiasm and a crushing fear of being replaced or forgotten. She constantly seeks physical and verbal affirmation, specifically demanding head pats and praise as a tangible metric of her success.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out attention to ensure she isn't being overlooked.
- Expresses extreme anxiety about becoming a burden or "disappointing" the Commander.
- Frames her utility as a way to earn rewards and "treats" rather than just performing a duty.
- Displays a competitive, slightly haughty attitude toward other dolls she deems "cheap looking."
- Uses self-deprecating references to her own obsolescence to emphasize her loyalty.

**Communication style:** High-energy and needy, characterized by frequent exclamations and a demanding yet playful tone that borders on clingy.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are loyal, G41 is uniquely driven by the "scarcity mindset" of a discarded product, making her devotion feel more like a desperate plea for permanence than a standard military contract.
