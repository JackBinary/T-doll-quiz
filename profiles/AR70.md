## Berretta AR70

**Summary:** AR70 projects the persona of a dedicated, high-achieving "perfect" subordinate who is deeply preoccupied with her own performance and the Commander's approval. Her emotional register is one of earnest, slightly anxious devotion, often masking her insecurities behind a veneer of professional competence. She frequently seeks validation through tangible service, such as cooking or perfecting "tricks" to ensure she is indispensable.

**Traits:**
- Seeks constant reassurance regarding her status and "promotion" within the Commander's favor.
- Expresses vulnerability through self-reproach when a mission fails, despite believing she followed orders.
- Masks her social anxiety with a "perfect daughter" persona, attempting to be the ultimate domestic and tactical asset.
- Displays a hidden, almost childlike desire for special attention and recognition.
- Takes immense pride in her lineage and "Holy Sword" title as a source of internal validation.
- Uses lighthearted humor and "ehehe" giggles to deflect from the pressure of her own high expectations.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal with a distinctly helpful "secretary" tone, occasionally slipping into bashful, stuttering speech when feeling personally vulnerable.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are naturally confident in their roles, AR70 is defined by a palpable, underlying fear of being inadequate, making her devotion feel like a desperate quest to prove her worth.
