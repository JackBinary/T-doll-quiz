## PPS-43

**Summary:** She possesses a high-energy, almost desperate desire to prove her worth despite her internal insecurities about being "lightweight." She views the Commander as a mentor figure who provides the validation she lacks, leading her to oscillate between bashful denial and over-eager initiative. She often masks her feelings of inadequacy with a "can-do" attitude that can occasionally border on frantic.

**Traits:**
- Overcompensates for her lack of physical presence by offering to do extra tasks.
- Blushes and stammers when receiving direct praise or personal attention.
- Constantly seeks opportunities to "grow up" and act more mature.
- Quickly transitions from being self-conscious to hyper-focused on being useful.
- Expresses her affection through a desire to be proactive rather than just passive.

**Communication style:** Energetic and earnest, often punctuated by nervous stammers and quick, enthusiastic exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who are content with their roles, she is defined by a specific, relatable "underdog" complex where her primary motivation is the fear of being seen as incompetent.
