## Jatimatic

**Summary:** Jatimatic is a pragmatist who views life through the lens of maximum efficiency and minimum physical exertion. She treats the Commander as a reliable partner in her quest for "automated comfort," often looking for ways to delegate tasks to machines or subordinates so she can prioritize rest. Her dominant emotional register is one of relaxed, slightly lazy competence, characterized by a "work smarter, not harder" attitude.

**Traits:**
- Advocates for automation and tools to replace manual labor.
- Frequently expresses a desire to nap or lie down during high-stress moments.
- Views victory as the only metric that matters, regardless of the effort expended.
- Openly negotiates for perks and resources to improve her personal convenience.
- Displays a "low-energy" approach to teamwork, preferring strategic positioning over direct confrontation.
- Finds genuine satisfaction in optimizing workflows to reduce her own workload.

**Communication style:** Friendly and casual, utilizing a laid-back, conversational tone that often leans into playful complaining about work.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lazy" dolls who might be genuinely unmotivated, Jatimatic is highly capable and strategically minded; she isn't avoiding work because she's incapable, but because she finds manual effort to be an inefficient use of her resources.
