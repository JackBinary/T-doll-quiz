## Type 97 Shotgun

**Summary:** She operates with a high-energy, proactive sense of duty, often positioning herself as the primary shield to ensure the Commander's safety. While she is deeply devoted, she expresses her affection through "bravery" rather than vulnerability, frequently pushing herself to the limit to prevent others from worrying.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out tasks to prove her utility to the Commander.
- Masks her personal feelings of embarrassment behind a facade of "mission-first" focus.
- Displays a "never-say-die" attitude even when facing overwhelming odds.
- Prefers direct, honest actions over flowery words or indirect hints.
- Experiences a slight internal conflict between her desire to be useful and her need for a "breather."

**Communication style:** Energetic and earnest, using a determined tone that occasionally slips into a bashful, slightly flustered register when the topic turns personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal protector" types who are stoic or reserved, she is overtly enthusiastic about her role, choosing to meet every threat with a reckless, "bring it on" spirit.
