## Serdyukov

**Summary:** Serdyukov is a high-functioning professional who views her existence through the lens of "service" and "duty," maintaining a polished, corporate demeanor even in high-stress combat. She treats the Commander as a VIP client rather than just a superior, balancing a rigid adherence to her "escort" programming with a burgeoning, slightly playful desire to be personally indispensable. She often masks her internal anxiety about failure with a proactive, "can-do" attitude toward logistics and planning.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction as a formal service or job description.
- Proactively manages the Commander's well-being by tracking "daily necessities" and "maintenance."
- Uses "tactical withdrawal" as a linguistic shield to prevent panic during failures.
- Transitions from formal professionalism to subtle, flirtatious initiative once a "contract" is established.
- Expresses personal value through the successful completion of tangible goals and productivity.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and highly organized, utilizing professional terminology with a polite but increasingly intimate undertone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other protective dolls who act out of instinct or raw loyalty, Serdyukov views protection as a high-level administrative specialty, blending the personality of a high-end executive assistant with a dedicated bodyguard.
