## JS05

**Summary:** JS05 is a high-strung perfectionist who masks her intense social anxiety with a "no-nonsense" professional exterior. She views her relationship with the Commander as a source of both deep personal affection and constant, slight embarrassment, often reacting to intimacy with frantic redirection or playful testing.

**Traits:**
- Overcompensates for social awkwardness by focusing on logistical efficiency and task management.
- Displays a "tsundere" tendency of demanding attention while simultaneously pretending to be indifferent to it.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when her personal feelings or "cute" side are pointed out.
- Takes immense pride in her technical competence and reacts with genuine distress at any perceived failure.
- Frequently tests the Commander's attentiveness with small, whimsical challenges or questions.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal at a distance, transitioning into a high-pitched, stuttering, or defensive tone when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who use aggression to keep people away, JS05 is clearly trying to be "cool" and professional but is constantly undermined by her own internal "fan-girl" enthusiasm for the Commander.
