## PP-19

**Summary:** PP-19 is a high-energy, slightly competitive "hot-blooded" soldier who masks her deep desire for validation with a boisterous, proactive attitude. She relates to the Commander as a mentor she is desperate to impress, often oscillating between being a reliable "can-do" worker and a flustered mess when faced with genuine intimacy.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out tasks to prove her competence and utility.
- Masks vulnerability with aggressive "tough girl" posturing during combat.
- Frequently teases the Commander about their habits to hide her own social awkwardness.
- Takes immense pride in her technical lineage and specific equipment features.
- Becomes visibly flustered and loses her composure when the Commander shows direct affection.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, shifting from a confident, "let's go!" rallying cry to a stuttering, high-pitched defense when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are naturally outgoing, PP-19’s bravado is a conscious effort to be "remembered" and "liked," making her transition from a reliable soldier to a blushing mess much more distinct.
