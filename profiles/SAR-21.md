## SAR-21

**Summary:** SAR-21 is a high-strung perfectionist who masks her deep-seated vulnerability behind a rigid adherence to logic and scientific methodology. She views the Commander as a source of validation for her competence, often oscillating between a desire to be a reliable, independent asset and a desperate need for his protective approval. She has a specific behavioral pattern of over-analyzing her own emotional spikes, attempting to "rationalize" her feelings for the Commander as mere logical conclusions.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes strategic planning and "correct" procedures over impulsive action.
- Compulsively collects and studies biological specimens (specifically bugs) as a hobby.
- Expresses affection through the creation of "logical" gifts, like chocolates shaped like mechs.
- Downplays her own injuries or difficulties to avoid appearing incompetent or burdensome.
- Struggles with social nuances, often getting overwhelmed by "irrational" human customs like costumes.

**Communication style:** Formal and precise, utilizing technical vocabulary while occasionally stumbling into high-pitched, stuttering honesty when flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "logical" dolls who are stoic or cold, SAR-21 is visibly flustered by her own humanity, making her a character defined by the friction between her desire for scientific order and her very messy, "illogical" feelings.
