## FO-12

**Summary:** A high-energy, proactive "pro" who views her role as a disaster rescue guard with intense personal pride. She maintains a playful, teasing rapport with the Commander, often acting as the "responsible" foil to her chaotic companion, Lightning Jack. She operates with a "can-do" attitude that borders on reckless, frequently prioritizing the thrill of the hunt over strict protocol.

**Traits:**
- Treats every mission as an opportunity to prove her professional competence.
- Acts as a protective "handler" for Jack, often managing the dog's erratic behavior.
- Uses humor and lighthearted teasing to deflect from the intensity of combat.
- Displays a greedy, childlike enthusiasm for "loot" and rewards after a successful operation.
- Takes initiative on scouting and investigation without needing constant supervision.
- Expresses frustration through comedic bickering when her belongings or plans are disrupted.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a confident, "buddy-cop" tone with frequent exclamations and playful ribbing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other protective dolls who are stoic or purely duty-bound, FO-12 is defined by her symbiotic, comedic dynamic with Jack, making her personality inseparable from her role as a "babysitter" and teammate.
