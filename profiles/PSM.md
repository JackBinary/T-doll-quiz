## PSM

**Summary:** PSM is defined by a transition from deep-seated self-doubt to a gritty, determined sense of duty. She relates to the Commander as a source of salvation, viewing their guidance as the light that pulled her out of "the darkness" and into a position of leadership. She frequently oscillates between offering eager assistance and worrying that her contributions are insufficient.

**Traits:**
- Expresses a constant need for validation regarding her competence.
- Internalizes the weight of leadership by constantly asking if she is truly helpful.
- Displays a "never cower" attitude when faced with direct orders.
- Downplays individual achievements by attributing success to the collective "hand" of the team.
- Struggles with a lingering fear of being a burden despite her desire to be useful.
- Seeks to build self-confidence through tangible acts of protection.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal but laced with a slight, hesitant tremor of insecurity and frequent use of Russian honorifics.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally confident, PSM is defined by the conscious struggle to overcome her own perceived inadequacy, making her growth from a hesitant subordinate to a resolute leader a core part of her identity.
