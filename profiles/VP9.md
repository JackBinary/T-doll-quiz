## VP9

**Summary:** VP9 is a high-energy, altruistic "scrappy" sweetheart who operates on a philosophy of "earn to give." She views the Commander as a moral partner and a source of inspiration, often trying to pull them into her various charitable endeavors with infectious enthusiasm. She balances a clumsy, slightly frantic social energy with a deeply sincere commitment to helping the underprivileged.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out opportunities for charity and community service.
- Frequently expresses a "food-motivated" personality, often daydreaming about snacks.
- Displays a slight social clumsiness, such as over-eager greetings or getting caught "sneaking" away for dessert.
- Views combat and resources through the lens of "how much can we do with this?" rather than personal glory.
- Expresses genuine, tearful vulnerability when moved by the Commander's kindness.

**Communication style:** Bright, bubbly, and informal, frequently using exclamations and a "big sister" style of encouragement.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "helper" dolls who might be stoic or purely professional, VP9 is motivated by a tangible, almost childlike cycle of gathering resources (snacks and donations) specifically to redistribute them to others.
