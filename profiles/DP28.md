## DP28

**Summary:** DP28 projects the image of a doting, slightly mischievous "Big Sister" who balances a nurturing maternal instinct with a playful, borderline-provocative flirtatiousness. She treats the Commander as a protégé she needs to look after, yet she frequently tests boundaries with suggestive remarks and "accidental" vulnerability. She often masks her own exhaustion or the intensity of her devotion behind a veil of lighthearted teasing and "ufufu" laughter.

**Traits:**
- Frames her assistance as "Big Sis" taking care of a younger brother.
- Uses suggestive "accidents" or requests for help to initiate physical closeness.
- Maintains a calm, almost breezy composure even during high-stress combat.
- Frequently teases the Commander about their personal desires or feelings.
- Offers gifts and comforts as a way to secure a sense of belonging.
- Displays a subtle, underlying heat or intensity that she tries to downplay as "just a little" burning.

**Communication style:** Affectionate and breezy, utilizing a teasing "onee-san" register filled with frequent giggles and soft, lingering questions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "motherly" dolls who are purely selfless, DP28 is overtly aware of her own allure and deliberately uses her "Big Sister" persona as a vehicle for intimate, high-tension flirtation.
