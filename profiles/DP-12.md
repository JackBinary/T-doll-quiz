## DP-12

**Summary:** A sophisticated storyteller and protector who views the world through the lens of folklore and narrative structure. She maintains a playful, slightly mischievous demeanor toward the Commander, often using "scary" stories and teasing jokes to mask her deep devotion to the group's safety.

**Traits:**
- Frames every mission and interaction as a chapter in an unfolding novel.
- Teases the Commander with "pranks" like wasabi-filled chocolates to test their reactions.
- Displays a scholarly obsession with how legends evolve across different cultures.
- Takes on a maternal, protective role by shielding others from the emotional toll of combat.
- Expresses a desire for permanence, seeking to "witness how all stories end" by the Commander's side.

**Communication style:** Polished and literary, peppered with "ufufu" chuckles and a penchant for dramatic, slightly eerie storytelling.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "storyteller" types who are merely whimsical, DP-12 specifically treats the Commander as a protagonist to be "tamed" or "guided" through a narrative of her own making.
