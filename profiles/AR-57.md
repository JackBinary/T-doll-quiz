## AR-57

**Summary:** AR-57 is a self-conscious "tough girl" who struggles with the discrepancy between her intimidating factory-default appearance and her actually quite earnest, dorky interior. She relates to the Commander with a mix of defensive bravado and a deep-seated desire for genuine validation, often masking her vulnerability behind a "battle maniac" persona.

**Traits:**
- Masks her insecurity about her "fierce" face with proactive aggression and combat-focused hobbies.
- Expresses affection through "tough love" critiques of the Commander’s fashion sense and lifestyle choices.
- Displays a surprisingly domestic side, taking pride in meticulous preparation (like Halloween candy) even when it leads to social awkwardness.
- Seeks external confirmation of her personality to prove she isn't just the "scary" doll people assume she is.
- Prefers practical, high-quality solutions over sentimental gestures, such as opting for store-bought luxury over handmade effort.

**Communication style:** Blunt and assertive with a sharp, slightly impatient edge that softens into a more vulnerable, inquisitive tone when discussing personal feelings.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who lean into their intimidating reputation as a source of power, AR-57 is actively trying to navigate a way to be perceived as "gentle," making her internal conflict between her aesthetic and her heart her defining trait.
