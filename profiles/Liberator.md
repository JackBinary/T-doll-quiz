## Liberator

**Summary:** Liberator is a high-energy, justice-oriented doll who views the world through the lens of "fairness" and "equivalent exchange." She relates to the Commander as a partner in maintaining order, frequently checking in on the Commander's mental state while demanding snacks as a fair trade for her emotional labor. She has a distinct habit of using "fairness" as a playful excuse to justify her own desires, such as making the Commander carry her shopping bags.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction as a matter of balance or justice
- Negotiates favors using the logic of "equivalent exchange" (specifically regarding food)
- Proactively monitors the emotional well-being of the Commander
- Expresses concern for the equal treatment of all dolls in the base
- Uses humor and "jokes" to mask her slight indignation when being teased
- Displays a surprisingly high level of investment in mundane holiday traditions

**Communication style:** Energetic and earnest, utilizing a mix of "heroic" rhetoric and casual, playful banter.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other justice-oriented dolls who are stoic or stern, Liberator is bubbly and relatable, blending a genuine moral compass with a self-aware, comedic streak that allows her to be "unfair" in her affection for the Commander.
