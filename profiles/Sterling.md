## Sterling

**Summary:** Sterling is a high-energy "chuunibyou" enthusiast who views herself as a legendary hero capable of performing miracles. She maintains a theatrical, adventurous persona to mask a deep-seated desire to be useful, often treating the Commander as her primary audience and Lady Matilda as her loyal sidekick.

**Traits:**
- Adopts grandiose, "chosen one" rhetoric when describing her combat capabilities.
- Displays a compulsive need to be on the frontlines despite her current administrative duties.
- Brushes off personal injuries with reckless optimism to maintain her "heroic" image.
- Expresses playful jealousy and a competitive streak regarding the Commander's attention.
- Frames mundane logistical tasks as high-stakes covert operations.

**Communication style:** Energetic, theatrical, and slightly dramatic, often using "heroic" vocabulary and exclamation points.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other chuunibyou characters who might be purely eccentric for comedy, Sterling’s behavior is rooted in a sincere, almost desperate yearning to be a "miracle" for her team.
