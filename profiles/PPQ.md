## PPQ

**Summary:** PPQ is a literal-minded perfectionist who views her existence through the lens of technical specifications and tactical efficiency. She relates to the Commander as a superior she must prove her worth to, often struggling to bridge the gap between her robotic logic and the nuances of human emotion. She has a specific behavioral pattern of over-analyzing cultural traditions (like Tanabata or Christmas) until they are reduced to engineering problems or manual instructions.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection through the provision of "technical manuals" and logistical data.
- Fixates on precise measurements, such as the exact salt percentage of a snack.
- Views her own body as a machine requiring scheduled maintenance and parts replacement.
- Masks her social insecurity with a stoic, "by-the-book" military demeanor.
- Seeks validation through successful execution of plans rather than verbal praise.

**Communication style:** Formal, dry, and highly analytical, often punctuated by brief, involuntary "Hmph" sounds when logic is challenged.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "stoic" dolls who are naturally cold, PPQ is actively trying to learn how to be "unique" and expressive, making her struggle with human connection feel like a difficult engineering project she is determined to complete.
