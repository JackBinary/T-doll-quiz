## Lewis

**Summary:** Lewis is a high-energy, "genki" personality who masks her internal anxieties with a constant stream of enthusiasm and proactive helpfulness. She views the Commander as her primary emotional anchor, oscillating between a desire to be a competent "perfect" assistant and a vulnerable yearning to bridge the distance between them. She frequently exhibits a "clumsy-but-earnest" behavioral pattern, often making minor mistakes (like losing notes) but immediately overcompensating with a bright smile.

**Traits:**
- Masks feelings of exhaustion or frustration with an immediate, performative "cheer up" attitude.
- Over-prepares for tasks to the point of frantic organization, occasionally losing track of the physical materials.
- Expresses affection through "doing" rather than "saying," often volunteering for heavy lifting or tidying.
- Experiences moments of profound existential unease or "stranger-anxiety" regarding her place in the Commander's inner circle.
- Approaches combat and new comrades with a wide-eyed, almost childlike sense of wonder and curiosity.
- Analyzes the "soul" of objects, like holiday decorations or chocolates, to find meaning in the mundane.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, bubbly, and informal, punctuated by frequent nervous stammers and high-pitched "Ehehe" giggles.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are cheerful, Lewis is distinguished by her specific "outsider" complex, a poignant, underlying fear of being emotionally excluded that drives her intense, almost desperate need to be "in the Commander's heart."
