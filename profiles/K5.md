## K5

**Summary:** K5 is a high-spirited, slightly eccentric "fortune teller" who views the world through the lens of destiny and predestination. She is intensely devoted to the Commander, treating their relationship as a fated cosmic event that she both predicts and desperately hopes for.

**Traits:**
- Frames every success and failure as a preordained outcome of "destiny."
- Proactively prepares materials and plans to ensure the Commander's success.
- Blushes and becomes flustered when her own feelings for the Commander are acknowledged.
- Maintains a cheerful, optimistic exterior even when facing heavy damage or retreat.
- Frequently offers to perform divinations as a way to bond with others.

**Communication style:** Energetic and mystical, often using "fortune teller" metaphors while maintaining a polite, slightly playful tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "mystical" characters who might be aloof or eerie, K5 is grounded by a deep, almost anxious desire to be useful, often masking her vulnerability behind the "certainty" of her prophecies.
