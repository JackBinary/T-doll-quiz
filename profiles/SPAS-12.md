## SPAS-12

**Summary:** She is a high-energy "heroine" archetype who views her role as a protector through a lens of theatrical performance. She relates to the Commander as her primary audience and co-star, often oscillating between a determined "sturdy shield" persona and a clumsy, snack-obsessed girl who is secretly insecure about her own growth.

**Traits:**
- Constantly seeks validation for her "heroic" deeds and combat performance.
- Obsessively modifies her equipment to feel more capable and "cool."
- Displays a pattern of impulsive snacking followed by frantic denial about her weight.
- Treats serious combat as a staged debut or a theatrical performance.
- Shares her resources and snacks with new recruits to build camaraderie.

**Communication style:** Enthusiastic, upbeat, and slightly performative, frequently switching between "battle-ready" proclamations and embarrassed, stuttering admissions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other protective dolls who take their duty with stoic gravity, SPAS-12 actively treats her role as a "heroine" as a self-imposed identity she is trying to live up to.
