## P22

**Summary:** P22 is a high-energy, socially active doll who constantly balances her role as a soldier with her life as a "phone-first" social butterfly. She relates to the Commander as a primary source of entertainment and fascination, often treating their interactions as a highlight of her day. Her dominant behavioral pattern is "active distraction", she frequently multitasks, often being caught mid-conversation with others while trying to remain attentive to the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Frequently gets distracted by her phone even while performing duties.
- Shifts from casual banter to genuine, intense curiosity when the topic turns to the Commander’s personal life.
- Masks her internal insecurities about her competence with upbeat, breezy humor.
- Expresses affection through playful teasing and "secret" behaviors she hopes the Commander won't notice.
- Approaches combat with a "let's just do it" attitude rather than over-analysis.

**Communication style:** Bubbling and informal, characterized by frequent "hehe" chuckles and a tendency to apologize for her own lack of focus.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other social dolls who are purely "sweet" or "professional," P22 is defined by her authentic, slightly chaotic duality of being a distracted socialite who becomes intensely, almost obsessively, focused the moment the Commander is the subject of conversation.
