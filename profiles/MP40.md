## MP40

**Summary:** MP40 is a diligent, slightly high-strung "straight-A" student type who approaches her duties with a sense of earnest, almost anxious responsibility. She views the Commander as a mentor and role model, often seeking his validation through her performance in both combat and personal hobbies like music. She frequently overcompensates for her nerves by trying to be as useful and reliable as possible.

**Traits:**
- Expresses anxiety through stuttering or sudden startled reactions when surprised.
- Seeks constant reassurance regarding whether she is following "procedure" correctly.
- Channels her internal feelings into structured activities like music or massage therapy.
- Displays a "work harder" mentality, often pushing herself to the point of exhaustion to meet expectations.
- Projects a fiercely loyal, almost protective stance against the "lies and betrayal" of the outside world.

**Communication style:** Polite, earnest, and slightly formal, often punctuated by small hesitations or "e-ehs" when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest soldier" types who are naturally confident, MP40 is defined by her visible effort to overcome her own bashfulness and nerves to be the perfect subordinate.
