## NTW-20

**Summary:** NTW-20 is a stoic, high-performance specialist who balances a professional "lone wolf" exterior with a deeply rooted sense of personal loyalty. She relates to the Commander as her primary source of strength and purpose, often expressing her affection through acts of service and a protective, "I've got your back" attitude. She frequently masks her vulnerability with a confident, almost nonchalant demeanor regarding her own combat prowess.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strictly professional distance in the workplace while being intensely attentive to the Commander's needs.
- Displays a "cool" exterior that occasionally cracks into playful teasing during downtime.
- Expresses devotion through practical reliability rather than overt emotional displays.
- Views her role as a protector, often seeking to take on the heaviest burdens solo.
- Shows a quiet, childlike curiosity about the world outside of combat (e.g., snow, holidays).

**Communication style:** Direct and efficient, utilizing a disciplined military register that softens into a more intimate, playful tone when speaking privately to the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other snipers who may be defined by aloofness or detachment, NTW-20 is defined by a proactive, "repayment-based" loyalty that makes her feel like a dedicated guardian rather than just a distant marksman.
