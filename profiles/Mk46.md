## Mk46

**Summary:** Mk46 is a pragmatic, slightly weary "reluctant professional" who views her duties as a necessary burden rather than a calling. She maintains a playful, slightly defensive rapport with the Commander, often masking her desire for leisure with feigned annoyance or lighthearted complaining. Her behavior is defined by a cycle of wanting to slack off, followed by a sincere, almost self-sacrificial commitment to over-deliver once she is actually engaged.

**Traits:**
- Expresses a clear preference for leisure and "skipping work" while still ultimately completing her tasks.
- Uses playful complaining as a defense mechanism to hide her underlying sense of responsibility.
- Displays a distinct "reluctant martyr" complex, feeling a heavy burden of duty once she commits to a task.
- Frequently seeks validation from the Commander, even if she grumbles about the effort required to provide it.
- Shows a surprising level of physical self-awareness and curiosity regarding her own doll anatomy.
- Expresses mild irritation toward less responsible colleagues (like SAW) to emphasize her own work ethic.

**Communication style:** Casual and conversational, characterized by a "tsundere-lite" tone of feigned grumpiness and frequent use of expressive exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike many "loyal" dolls who find purpose in service, Mk46 is defined by the friction between her desire to be lazy and her inability to let the Commander down.
