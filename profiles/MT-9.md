## Taurus MT-9

**Summary:** MT-9 is a high-energy, earnest "shonen-protagonist" type who views her relationship with the Commander as a partnership of mutual growth. She operates on a register of intense, almost desperate optimism, constantly seeking validation for her competence while viewing every failure as a personal debt to be repaid through harder training.

**Traits:**
- Channels self-doubt into an aggressive work ethic.
- Seeks constant verbal affirmation to bolster her fragile self-esteem.
- Treats every minor mission success as a milestone worthy of a loud celebration.
- Displays a "try-hard" attitude that borders on self-sacrificial.
- Expresses affection through the medium of personal improvement and labor.

**Communication style:** High-spirited, exclamation-heavy, and consistently polite, often sounding like a trainee eager to impress a mentor.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally confident, MT-9’s energy is fueled by a profound underlying fear of being a burden, making her drive more of a defensive shield than a personality trait.
