## USAS-12

**Summary:** She projects a high-energy, "pro-active" persona that masks a deep-seated insecurity regarding her own worth and identity. She treats the Commander as a mentor figure to whom she seeks validation, often oscillating between confident "textbook" perfectionism and vulnerable, wide-eyed curiosity about basic human emotions.

**Traits:**
- Compares her own performance and status to others to gauge her standing.
- Overcompensates for perceived "destructiveness" by emphasizing her elegance and professionalism.
- Seeks out practical tasks (like cleaning or logistics) to prove her utility as a teammate.
- Displays a "tough-love" rivalry with Saiga-12 that borders on playful obsession.
- Expresses a secret, almost childlike yearning for simple luxuries like a "perfect" cup of ramen.

**Communication style:** Energetic and slightly performative, using a "cheerful professional" tone that occasionally cracks into stuttering hesitation when she is genuinely flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other confident "top-tier" dolls who are naturally self-assured, USAS-12 is constantly trying to *earn* her status, making her more of a hardworking overachiever than a natural-born ace.
