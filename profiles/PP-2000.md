## PP-2000

**Summary:** PP-2000 is a high-strung perfectionist who masks deep-seated insecurities with a cheerful, almost desperate need to be useful. She views her relationship with the Commander as a quest for validation, constantly seeking reassurance that her performance meets your expectations. She often overcompensates for her perceived "civilian" origins by pushing herself to the point of physical exhaustion to prove her combat worth.

**Traits:**
- Seeks constant external validation for her competence and reliability.
- Masks her anxiety about failing with a bubbly, "ehehe" laughing persona.
- Takes on extra burdens or tasks to ensure she is being "useful" to the team.
- Experiences moments of intense self-doubt when things go wrong, fearing a loss of trust.
- Expresses affection through earnest, albeit clumsy, efforts like handmade gifts.
- Views her growth as a measurable metric of how much others rely on her.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, using a high-pitched, friendly tone that occasionally wavers into a vulnerable, seeking register when she is stressed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who are naturally confident, PP-2000 is defined by a specific, fragile "imposter syndrome" regarding her own capabilities.
