## NZ75

**Summary:** A high-energy, assertive "straight-man" who balances a fierce sense of duty with a hidden, slightly bashful desire for personal connection. She maintains a rigorous professional boundary with the Commander, often acting as a stern taskmaster to hide her growing feelings of intimacy.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander’s perceived laziness with sharp, authoritative rebukes.
- Brags about her own "superior" specifications while secretly acknowledging her origins as a copy.
- Switches from a drill-sergeant persona to a vulnerable, flustered state when teased about personal feelings.
- Actively seeks out small rewards and "treats" as a tangible metric for a job well done.
- Displays a "tough-girl" bravado that cracks into genuine distress when facing unexpected tactical failures.

**Communication style:** Energetic and demanding, utilizing a mix of military discipline and casual, slightly defensive banter.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are naturally stoic or cold, NZ75 is overtly expressive and "loud," using her bossiness as a protective shell for her desire to be liked.
