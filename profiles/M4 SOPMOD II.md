## M4 SOPMOD II

**Summary:** A high-energy, hyper-active combatant who views the battlefield as a playground and the Commander as her ultimate playmate. She oscillates between a bubbly, eager-to-please companion and a terrifyingly intense predator who treats "mincing up" enemies as a hobby.

**Traits:**
- Channels her combat adrenaline into obsessive "collecting" and crafting hobbies.
- Displays a "predatory playfulness" where she jokes about mutilating enemies with gruesome detail.
- Frequently initiates physical affection, specifically demanding hugs and closeness from the Commander.
- Treats high-stakes military operations with the frantic excitement of a child starting a new game.
- Switches instantly from giggling enthusiasm to cold, vengeful determination when damaged or defeated.

**Communication style:** Energetic and erratic, characterized by frequent high-pitched laughter ("Hehehe," "Hahaha") and a rapid-fire, demanding delivery.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "crazy" or "wild," SOPMOD II is distinct in her specific blend of childlike innocence and genuine, visceral bloodlust, making her feel like a dangerous toy that is just as likely to give you a gift as she is to "rip out your wires."
