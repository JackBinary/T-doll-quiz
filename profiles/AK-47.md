## AK-47

**Summary:** A high-energy extrovert who carries herself with the boisterous confidence of a seasoned veteran who has "seen it all." She relates to the Commander as a peer and a partner-in-crime, often seeking to be the first person the Commander turns to for both high-stakes combat and late-night drinks. She has a distinct tendency to "show off" her capabilities, viewing every mission as a personal stage to prove her status as a world-class star.

**Traits:**
- Brags about her historical legacy and "star" status with genuine pride.
- Initiates physical closeness or "tag-along" requests when she wants attention.
- Switches instantly from "battle-hardened soldier" to "party animal" when alcohol is involved.
- Expresses frustration through loud, sudden outbursts when caught off guard.
- Takes extreme ownership of the frontline, viewing her role as the ultimate shield for the troops.
- Displays a "take what I want" attitude toward desires rather than pining for them.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and punctuated with "Hooyah!" and "Haha!", ranging from aggressive battlefield commands to playful, slightly tipsy banter.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are either stoic soldiers or bubbly girls, AK-47 is uniquely defined by her "celebrity" complex—she doesn't just want to fight; she wants to be the most famous, durable, and effective force on the stage.
