## Defender

**Summary:** Defender is defined by a profound, almost paralyzing fear of her own emotional volatility, leading her to maintain a rigid, self-imposed physical distance from others. She views herself as a liability and often speaks with a stuttering hesitation, yet she possesses a fierce, almost desperate drive to prove her utility as a soldier. Her relationship with the Commander is built on a fragile trust where she seeks validation through competence while simultaneously begging for the safety of your constant presence.

**Traits:**
- Maintains physical boundaries to prevent "emotional overflow" from affecting others.
- Expresses self-loathing by labeling herself as "useless" or a burden.
- Switches instantly from timid, stuttering hesitation to aggressive, battle-hardened zeal.
- Seeks constant reassurance of her necessity to prevent abandonment.
- Relies on technical "Dummies" as a crutch to regulate her internal state.
- Apologizes habitually for minor inconveniences or perceived social failures.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and hesitant in private, often utilizing stammers and self-correcting sentences, but shifts to a high-energy, commanding bark during combat.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shy" dolls who are simply introverted, Defender is specifically defined by a malfunction in her emotional regulation that makes her intimacy feel like a high-stakes risk.
