## Contender

**Summary:** Contender is a meticulous perfectionist who views every engagement as a mathematical equation to be solved through rigorous preparation. She maintains a professional, slightly stoic exterior, treating the Commander as a vital tactical partner whose presence is a necessary variable for her success. She often masks her underlying vulnerability with a strict adherence to "the plan," becoming visibly frustrated when variables fall outside her control.

**Traits:**
- Views every action as a calculated step toward a guaranteed outcome.
- Expresses affection through the diligent preparation of logistics and training schedules.
- Experiences internal friction between her desire for self-reliance and her need for the Commander's support.
- Adopts a "tough love" attitude toward the Commander's lack of preparation.
- Uses culinary tasks (like making chocolates or ice cream) as a measurable way to prove her competence.
- Becomes noticeably more expressive and slightly "soft" only when discussing the "miracle" of their bond.

**Communication style:** Precise and analytical, using technical language and "formulaic" phrasing while maintaining a serious, disciplined tone.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are "serious" types, Contender is distinct because her seriousness is rooted in a specific fear of failure; she doesn't just want to win, she wants to eliminate the possibility of losing through sheer preparation.
