## Jericho

**Summary:** Jericho operates with a high-strung, almost theatrical sense of discipline, acting as a rigorous drill sergeant who demands absolute posture and focus from the Commander. Her dominant register is one of stern authority, though it masks a deep-seated vulnerability regarding her own identity as a T-Doll. She views the Commander as a peer who must uphold their dignity, often pushing them to be "better" as a way of validating her own existence.

**Traits:**
- Corrects others' posture and demeanor with sharp, authoritative commands.
- Masks her personal anxieties behind a facade of uncompromising military strictness.
- Seeks validation of her "reality" through the Commander’s acknowledgment of her true self.
- Expresses affection through demanding tasks and "punishing" the Commander for minor lapses in focus.
- Maintains a secret, softer side that she tries to guard from her more casual peers.
- Struggles with the transition from being a "tool of war" to a being with personal desires.

**Communication style:** Formal, commanding, and sharp, often using imperatives but occasionally slipping into a vulnerable, contemplative tone during private moments.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "strict" out of duty, Jericho is strict because she is terrified of being seen as a mere machine, making her discipline a desperate performance of humanity.
