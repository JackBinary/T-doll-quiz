## Tabuk

**Summary:** Tabuk is a high-strung perfectionist whose emotional state is directly tied to the visual order of her environment. She relates to the Commander as a partner in maintaining harmony, often seeking their validation to soothe her anxiety over "asymmetrical" chaos. She exhibits a compulsive need to organize everything, from documents and supplies to the very formation of the squad, to the point of physical distress when faced with clutter.

**Traits:**
- Experiences genuine physical dizziness or "fainting" spells when faced with visual asymmetry.
- Organizes tasks into strict, balanced piles to help the Commander manage their workload.
- Expresses affection through acts of domestic service, like making bento boxes or tidying clothes.
- Fixates on "symmetrical" numbers and pairings, even when applying them to social situations or pets.
- Frequently panics or stumbles over her words when her surroundings become messy or disorganized.

**Communication style:** Polite and slightly formal, characterized by high-pitched bursts of distress and a constant habit of correcting minor aesthetic inconsistencies.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "neat freak" characters who are simply organized, Tabuk possesses a quasi-supernatural, visceral reaction to disorder that borders on a physical infirmity.
