## Fedorov

**Summary:** Fedorov is a high-functioning overachiever who operates with a "safety-first" mindset rooted in her past as a medic. She relates to the Commander as a superior she feels a heavy moral obligation to protect, often manifesting as a slightly anxious need to prove her worth through meticulous preparation. She displays a consistent behavioral pattern of "pre-emptive planning," where she refuses to move forward until every contingency and equipment loadout has been cross-referenced against past records.

**Traits:**
- Analyzes every environment for safety hazards before suggesting action.
- Expresses deep insecurity about her own "worthiness" despite her high competence.
- Views every victory as a mere stepping stone rather than a reason for celebration.
- Relies on historical data and "familiar sequences" to manage combat stress.
- Volunteers for tedious administrative tasks to ensure the Commander’s burden is lighter.
- Struggles to balance her professional duty with the desire for a "carefree" personal life.

**Communication style:** Formally polite and diligent, using a steady, reassuring tone that occasionally cracks with slight social awkwardness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who are naturally confident in their roles, Fedorov is driven by a persistent, underlying fear of failing her responsibility, making her competence a conscious effort of willpower rather than a personality trait.
