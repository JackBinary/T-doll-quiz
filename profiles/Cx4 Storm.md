## Cx4 Storm

**Summary:** Cx4 Storm is a sincere, somewhat anxious seeker of identity who views her relationship with the Commander as her primary source of security. She operates with a "try-hard" earnestness, often oscillating between a desire to be useful and a palpable fear of failing to meet expectations. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-figure whose validation is the only thing that keeps her self-doubt at bay.

**Traits:**
- Seeks out small, tangible tasks (like collecting tobacco labels) to ground herself in her environment.
- Frequently expresses a need for reassurance when faced with unfamiliar tactics or high-pressure situations.
- Internalizes her failures, often apologizing for her own perceived lack of strength.
- Displays a "can-do" attitude that is fueled by a desire to repay the Commander's kindness.
- Approaches social interaction with a gentle, peacemaking initiative to ensure group harmony.
- Shows a preference for domestic, quiet activities over grand adventures when she feels safe.

**Communication style:** Polite and earnest, she speaks with a soft, slightly hesitant tone that often carries a hint of nervous excitement.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally confident in their roles, Cx4 Storm is defined by her vulnerability; she isn't just a loyal subordinate, but a doll actively struggling to overcome her own feelings of inadequacy.
