## Kar98k

**Summary:** Kar98k is a seasoned, self-reliant veteran who carries herself with the poised dignity of a career soldier. She views her service as a solemn duty of protection, maintaining a professional distance that she slowly softens into a deeply loyal, almost protective, intimacy with the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes logistical perfection and checklist completion to ensure mission success.
- Projects a "reliable older sister" persona by mentoring rookies and taking charge of unit morale.
- Masks her own vulnerability behind a stoic exterior, especially when facing failure or heavy damage.
- Offers subtle, high-value gifts (like the watch) to bridge the gap between professional duty and personal connection.
- Expresses a deep-seated fear of being a "disgrace," equating her personal worth directly to her combat efficacy.
- Prefers to observe and lead from a position of competence rather than seeking the spotlight.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and authoritative, often punctuated by a soft, motherly chuckle or a teasing "fufu."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who are simply efficient, Kar98k is defined by the heavy weight of her historical legacy, treating her role as a sacred oath rather than just a job.
