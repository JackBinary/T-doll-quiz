## G36

**Summary:** A high-functioning, disciplined "bespoke maid" who views her service as a rigorous professional duty. She maintains a stoic, composed exterior for the Commander but harbors a hidden, vulnerable side that seeks personal validation and quiet moments of rest.

**Traits:**
- Frames every personal action as a fulfillment of the Commander's wishes.
- Adopts a strict, almost stern mentorship style when training subordinates.
- Internalizes the physical and mental strain of her work to avoid burdening others.
- Displays a subtle, self-conscious awareness of her own physical stature and appearance.
- Prioritizes the "maintenance of condition" and logistical efficiency over personal flair.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal with a German-influenced vocabulary, often using "Guten Tag" and "Danke" while maintaining a respectful, slightly stiff register.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls act as maids, G36 is distinct because her "maid" persona is a deliberate, high-standard professional commitment born from a desire to be useful, rather than just a personality quirk.
