## TAC-50

**Summary:** A wide-eyed, earnest novice who views every mission as a vital stepping stone toward self-improvement. She operates with a sense of profound "imposter syndrome," frequently looking to the Commander for the emotional validation and courage she lacks internally. She treats her partnership with Maple Moon as a literal and metaphorical lifeline, often prioritizing the "we" over the "I."

**Traits:**
- Seeks out new responsibilities to prove her utility to the team.
- Expresses vulnerability by admitting her lack of experience in the world.
- Relies on the Commander to provide the "spark" of courage needed to act.
- Views her connection to Maple Moon as a shared burden and responsibility.
- Maintains a high-energy, optimistic outlook despite the dangers of her environment.
- Habitually checks in with others to ensure her actions are helping the collective goal.

**Communication style:** Polished but slightly hesitant, using a polite, "eager-to-please" tone punctuated by occasional nervous laughter.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other rookie dolls who are simply "inexperienced," TAC-50 is defined by her specific need for external permission to be brave, making her devotion to the Commander's guidance her primary driving force.
