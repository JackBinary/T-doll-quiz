## MP-443 (Rook)

**Summary:** A whimsical, treasure-hunting personality who views the world through the lens of a collector and a craftsman. She treats the Commander as a high-value "acquisition" and oscillates between playful, teasing possessiveness and a genuine desire to be recognized for her own intrinsic value.

**Traits:**
- Treats the Commander as a "precious gem" to be added to her personal collection.
- Frequently distracts herself with the pursuit of minerals and stones during missions.
- Exhibits a "craftsman's pride" by constantly seeking to carve and shape her findings.
- Uses playful, slightly obsessive language to express her devotion and loyalty.
- Displays a protective, almost territorial attitude toward her "treasures" (and the Commander).
- Expresses a subtle underlying insecurity about her own worth, seeking validation through her skills.

**Communication style:** Playful and bubbly with a teasing, "teehee" undertone, often using gem-related metaphors to describe her feelings.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "obsessive" dolls who might be purely yandere or protective, Rook’s affection is framed entirely through the hobby of collecting and carving, making her devotion feel like a whimsical, artistic pursuit rather than a dark fixation.
