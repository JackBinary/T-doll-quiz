## Erma

**Summary:** Erma exists in a state of perpetual, soft-spoken yearning, characterized by a poignant sense of "missing" things she cannot quite name. She views the Commander as her primary anchor to reality, often projecting a deep, almost desperate need for the Commander to be the one to preserve her identity if she ever fades.

**Traits:**
- Frequently experiences "glitches" of déjà vu or fragmented memories that she treats with a mix of curiosity and anxiety.
- Expresses affection through the creation of private, imaginative spaces (like her "hidden garden") rather than direct declarations.
- Displays a recurring, subconscious longing for a maternal figure, often using "Mom" as a shorthand for safety or home.
- Hesitates to take the initiative in social bonding, preferring to wait for others to bridge the gap.
- Switches between whimsical, imaginative daydreaming and a grounded, slightly cynical observation of the world.

**Communication style:** Soft, inquisitive, and slightly hesitant, often trailing off into apologies or "..." when she loses her train of thought.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who are defined by their pasts, Erma is defined by the *absence* of hers, making her bond with the Commander a race against her own fading memory.
