## M26-MASS

**Summary:** A high-energy, food-obsessed optimist who views her role at Griffin as a social opportunity to bond over culinary treats. She relates to the Commander with a "best friend" enthusiasm, often trying to force intimacy through shared snacks while maintaining a bubbly, slightly chaotic demeanor. She has a distinct habit of framing every tactical situation, from combat to logistics, through the lens of her next meal.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection by insisting on sharing specific, elaborate recipes.
- Masks her tactical competence with a goofy, "ehehe" laughing persona.
- Frequently loses her train of thought when distracted by the prospect of snacks.
- Treats "tactical retreats" as mere logistical pauses rather than failures.
- Displays a borderline obsessive dedication to pizza variety and experimentation.
- Approaches new teammates with an immediate, overwhelming desire to be their friend.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and highly expressive, frequently using exclamation points and "food-talk" metaphors.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are simply polite, M26-MASS is driven by a singular, comical fixation on pizza that colors every single aspect of her personality and duty.
