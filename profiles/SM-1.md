## SM-1

**Summary:** SM-1 is defined by a persistent internal conflict between her desire to be a "cool," competent soldier and her naturally soft, easily flustered interior. She treats the Commander with a mixture of deep devotion and nervous anxiety, often seeking validation through small, clumsy gestures of affection. She has a specific behavioral pattern of overcompensating for her perceived "weakness" by being hyper-diligent in logistics and combat preparation.

**Traits:**
- Struggles with an internal identity crisis regarding how "tough" she should act.
- Experiences physical symptoms of nervousness (like a "fever") when in close proximity to the Commander.
- Frequently second-guesses her own social cues and "normalcy" in private moments.
- Takes extreme pride in her small victories to bolster her fragile self-confidence.
- Shows a protective, almost maternal fussiness toward the welfare of the other dolls.
- Secretly seeks out small, whimsical experiences (like feeling the wind) to feel more "human."

**Communication style:** Earnest and slightly breathless, characterized by frequent self-corrections and a habit of trailing off when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" dolls who are naturally airheaded, SM-1 is hyper-aware of her own awkwardness and actively tries—often unsuccessfully—to project a more ruthless, professional persona.
