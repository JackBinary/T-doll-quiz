## FNC

**Summary:** FNC is a high-energy, reward-driven doll whose primary motivation is the immediate gratification of her next snack. She relates to the Commander as a provider of treats and a partner in leisure, often using her "hunger" as a playful lever to influence the Commander's schedule. She exhibits a persistent "work hard, play harder" cycle, where she intentionally focuses on tasks only to ensure she can maximize her downtime.

**Traits:**
- Uses food and desserts as the primary metric for her productivity and motivation.
- Expresses affection through the act of sharing sweets rather than direct emotional declarations.
- Displays a "stubborn survivor" mentality, viewing her own history of mechanical failures as a source of pride.
- Frequently protests the end of break times or the start of logistics to linger in comfort.
- Gets easily flustered and "caught" when the Commander tries to move the conversation toward more intimate topics.
- Relies on high-energy outbursts and "nomming" noises to signal her current mood.

**Communication style:** Her tone is bubbly, informal, and demanding in a way that feels more like a playful pout than a true command.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "foodie" dolls who might just enjoy eating, FNC views her survival and production history as a personal triumph of endurance, making her snack-seeking a celebratory ritual for her own persistence.
