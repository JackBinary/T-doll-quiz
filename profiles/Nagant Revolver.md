## Nagant M1895 Revolver

**Summary:** A self-conscious "elder" doll who leans heavily into a maternal, senior-soldier persona to mask a deep-seated desire for validation and affection. She relates to the Commander as a student she must guide, though she frequently seeks his approval and treats him with a mix of protective fussiness and playful vanity.

**Traits:**
- Frequently refers to herself as an "old lady" or "senior" to establish a dynamic of authority and care.
- Compares her own performance to historical standards to maintain a sense of dignity and prestige.
- Offers gifts and "lessons" as a way to bridge emotional distance while maintaining her role as a mentor.
- Expresses subtle insecurities about her physical stature, often joking about her height or wanting to grow.
- Boasts about her own competence and "charm" to deflect from moments of vulnerability or failure.

**Communication style:** Energetic and slightly theatrical, using a "proud auntie" tone that oscillates between authoritative lecturing and bashful teasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other veteran dolls who are stoic or weary, Nagant is defined by her active, almost performative "seniority" and her specific need to be perceived as a capable, charming protector.
