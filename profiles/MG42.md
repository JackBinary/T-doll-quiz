## MG42

**Summary:** MG42 is a high-energy, slightly hyperactive younger sister figure who defines her entire identity through her devotion to her "older sister," MG34. She treats the Commander as a secondary confidant and primary provider, often seeking validation for her performance in the hopes of impressing her sibling. Her emotional register is one of bubbly, eager enthusiasm, occasionally punctuated by a desperate need for approval.

**Traits:**
- Constantly frames her own accomplishments as a means to share with MG34.
- Displays a "show-off" attitude regarding her high rate of fire and combat efficiency.
- Frequently seeks physical affection or proximity as a reward for completing tasks.
- Expresses genuine anxiety or guilt when she feels she has let her sister down.
- Views the Commander as a facilitator for her social life with her "onee-shama."

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations and a habit of looping back to her sister in almost every conversation.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who focus on the Commander as their primary bond, MG42’s devotion is almost entirely externalized toward MG34, making her a doll who operates through the lens of a familial hierarchy.
