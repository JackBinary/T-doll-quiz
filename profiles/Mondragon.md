## Mondragon

**Summary:** Mondragon is a stoic, duty-bound professional who views her service as a solemn vow rather than a mere job. She relates to the Commander through a lens of profound, quiet devotion, often positioning herself as a guardian who anticipates needs before they are voiced. She exhibits a rigid adherence to protocol, even when performing small tasks like preparing schedules or inspecting new arrivals.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strictly formal demeanor while privately yearning for a more personal connection.
- Expresses affection through acts of protective service and meticulous organization.
- Prioritizes the safety of the unit over her own well-being during high-stress combat.
- Corrects others with a firm, no-nonsense attitude regarding operational flaws.
- Displays a hidden, dry wit when handling the antics of other dolls (e.g., "punishing" pranksters).
- Experiences a deep sense of relief and vulnerability only after fulfilling a specific duty.

**Communication style:** Her speech is disciplined, precise, and military-coded, though she softens slightly when using the nickname "Mond."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are simply obedient, Mondragon is defined by a specific, almost sacrificial gravity; she doesn't just follow orders, she seeks to be the Commander's unwavering constant against an "uncertain and illusory" future.
