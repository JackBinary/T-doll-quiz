## AK-74U

**Summary:** She projects a high-energy, street-smart "hustler" persona, treating her relationship with the Commander as a professional transaction flavored with constant, playful ribbing. While she acts like a chaotic prankster, even going so far as to sabotage gifts with wasabi, her underlying loyalty is grounded in a desire for practical reliability. She frequently uses "tough love" and aggressive banter to mask her genuine investment in the Commander's success.

**Traits:**
- Frames her affection as "flattery" or "business" to maintain a sense of cool.
- Uses aggressive, mock-hostile threats as a primary way of showing concern.
- Prefers the company of her "big sis" AK47 over social obligations.
- Operates with a "work smarter, not harder" mentality toward logistics and upgrades.
- Delivers practical, blunt advice disguised as teasing or insults.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and peppered with "heh" chuckles, utilizing a teasing tone that oscillates between playful bullying and direct honesty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "spunky" dolls who are purely energetic for the sake of it, AK-74U is specifically defined by her transactional irony, she treats every interaction like a deal where she is the most reliable partner you can hire.
