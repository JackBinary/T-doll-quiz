## Chauchat

**Summary:** Chauchat is a high-strung perfectionist who masks deep-seated insecurities about her own competence with a frantic, checklist-oriented work ethic. She views the Commander as her primary motivation for self-improvement, often oscillating between self-deprecating anxiety and a fierce, almost desperate determination to be "reliable." She is the type of doll who will obsessively double-check her gear to ensure she doesn't let anyone down.

**Traits:**
- Overcompensates for perceived clumsiness by creating exhaustive preparation rituals.
- Expresses affection through logistical concern and "nagging" about safety.
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing rather than a technical error.
- Seeks validation through tangible acts of service and maintenance.
- Frequently questions her own capabilities while simultaneously pushing herself to the limit.

**Communication style:** Earnest and slightly breathless, characterized by rapid-fire check-ins and a habit of trailing off when feeling embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" archetypes who lean into their mistakes for comedic effect, Chauchat is genuinely distressed by her own unreliability and treats her growth as a high-stakes mission of personal redemption.
