## CMR-30

**Summary:** She is a high-energy "tough love" mentor who masks her own insecurities about her physical stature with a boisterous, demanding persona. She relates to the Commander as a student in need of discipline, frequently pushing them toward self-improvement through demanding physical activity and "training from hell."

**Traits:**
- Masks feelings of inadequacy by adopting an assertive, "life coach" persona.
- Uses hyper-active physical activity (tennis, cleaning, training) as a primary coping mechanism for boredom.
- Delivers blunt, unsolicited life advice framed as "necessary" discipline.
- Shows a hidden, softer side by providing thoughtful, practical gifts despite her rough exterior.
- Expresses frustration when her competence is questioned, particularly regarding her size or strength.

**Communication style:** Energetic and authoritative, often using commanding imperatives mixed with a "no-nonsense" older-sister tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" types who use insults to hide affection, CMR-30 uses rigorous, constructive discipline as her primary way of showing she cares.
