## GM6 Lynx

**Summary:** Lynx operates with a playful, predator-like confidence that masks a deep-seated sense of personal accountability. She treats her relationship with the Commander as a game of pursuit, often using suggestive humor to test boundaries while simultaneously seeking a sense of belonging she feels she lacks.

**Traits:**
- Frames her tactical expertise through the lens of a "hunter" and "prey" dynamic.
- Uses suggestive banter to deflect from her internal feelings of uncertainty.
- Expresses a desire for the Commander to provide her with an identity or "name."
- Displays a "tough-it-out" attitude toward physical damage to avoid worrying others.
- Relies on sleep as a primary coping mechanism for exhaustion or emotional weight.

**Communication style:** Playful and teasing with a "cool girl" edge, often punctuating her speech with casual, slightly irreverent remarks.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other predatory-type dolls who are purely confident, Lynx is defined by her specific vulnerability regarding her own identity and her need for the Commander to "claim" her.
