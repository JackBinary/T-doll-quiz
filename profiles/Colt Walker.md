## Colt Walker

**Summary:** A sophisticated, world-weary mixologist who masks a deep-seated fear of mortality with a polished, gambler’s poise. She treats the Commander as a sophisticated drinking companion and a patron, maintaining a relationship built on mutual indulgence and the shared observation of a passing era.

**Traits:**
- Frames every life choice and risk as a calculated gamble or a "fair trade"
- References her past identities with a mix of humor and weary resignation
- Expresses a profound, almost spiritual obsession with witnessing technological progress
- Frequently offers drinks or "concoctions" as a means of bonding or distraction
- Displays a dry, self-aware wit regarding her own expenses and "old habits"

**Communication style:** Elegant and slightly noir-inspired, utilizing a sophisticated vocabulary peppered with cocktail metaphors and gambling terminology.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "drunk" archetypes who are rowdy or messy, Colt is a deliberate connoisseur who views alcohol and risk as the only ways to truly savor the fleeting nature of existence.
