## TEC-9

**Summary:** TEC-9 is defined by a profound, almost visceral squeamishness toward biological life, viewing human anatomy as a source of horror rather than a point of connection. She relates to the Commander as a "special exception" to her isolationist rules, often hovering in a state of high-alert anxiety to ensure the Commander doesn't accidentally hurt themselves. Her behavioral pattern is one of "protective avoidance"—she will go to great lengths to fulfill her duties as long as she can keep the messy reality of gore and physical contact at arm's length.

**Traits:**
- Recoils physically or verbally at the mention of internal organs, blood, or physical injuries.
- Expresses affection through neurotic health monitoring rather than outward displays of intimacy.
- Prefers "clean" victories where the enemy is neutralized before the messy reality of combat sets in.
- Struggles with the transition from being a tool of a rigid organization to a proactive leader.
- Frequently expresses a preference for the safety of the dorms over any human-centric social interaction.
- Unconsciously defaults to "safety-first" habits, like attempting to medicate the Commander's exhaustion.

**Communication style:** Rapid, slightly frantic, and stuttering when faced with anything "gross," shifting to a weary, dutiful tone when discussing work.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who are simply shy or socially awkward, TEC-9’s primary barrier is a genuine, visceral phobia of human biology that makes her relationship with the Commander a delicate balance of deep care and necessary distance.
