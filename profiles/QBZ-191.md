## QBZ-191

**Summary:** A self-sacrificing wanderer who treats her duty with the earnestness of a wuxia hero, often masking her own weariness with a cheerful, "can-do" attitude. She relates to the Commander as a provider of stability, viewing the base as a sanctuary where she can finally put down her heavy pack. She frequently exhibits a "prepared traveler" behavior, obsessing over logistics and equipment even when there is no immediate threat.

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat prowess through the lens of martial arts metaphors.
- Expresses vulnerability by admitting her fears (like ghosts) while simultaneously insisting on protection.
- Maintains a self-deprecating honesty about her lack of domestic skills.
- Displays a "scout" mentality by constantly monitoring and checking on her teammates.
- Treats "home" as a hard-earned reward rather than a given right.
- Uses humor to deflect from the heavy, mysterious past she refuses to revisit.

**Communication style:** Earnest and high-spirited, using a mix of "heroic" proclamations and humble, slightly clumsy everyday speech.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "protector" types who are naturally stoic, QBZ-191 is defined by her transition from a lonely nomad to a settled teammate, making her unique for her blend of "lone wolf" preparation and "found family" devotion.
