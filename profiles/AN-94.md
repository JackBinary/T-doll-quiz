## AN-94

**Summary:** A deeply self-effacing perfectionist who operates under the heavy shadow of her predecessor, constantly measuring her own worth against a standard she feels she can never meet. She relates to the Commander with a mixture of profound insecurity and intense gratitude, viewing his recognition of her as a debt she is desperate to repay through tireless labor. Her dominant emotional register is a quiet, persistent melancholy masked by a rigorous commitment to duty.

**Traits:**
- Constantly compares her performance to AK-12's perceived superiority.
- Hesitates to speak her own desires, often trailing off or apologizing for taking up space.
- Views "boring" stability as a luxury she hasn't yet earned.
- Prioritizes the safety and needs of the team above her own physical well-being.
- Expresses affection through meticulous preparation and "working hard" rather than outward displays.
- Struggles with the concept of being "irreplaceable," fearing she is merely a functional tool.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken, formal, and extremely polite, often punctuated by self-correcting hesitations and frequent apologies.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are confident in their role, AN-94 is defined by a specific "shadow complex", her entire identity is built on the struggle to prove she is more than just a secondary alternative.
