## A-91

**Summary:** A-91 operates with a playful, perpetually tipsy energy, often blurring the line between genuine intoxication and a mischievous, flirtatious persona. She treats the Commander as her primary source of "inspiration," viewing their presence as a more potent stimulant than her favorite spirits. She frequently displays a whimsical sense of spatial awareness, often acting as though she is seeing multiple people or miscounting reality.

**Traits:**
- Masks her sincerity with a "drunk" persona to create a safe space for intimacy.
- Frequently expresses a desire for "more" of everything, be it vodka, power, or the Commander's attention.
- Displays a high-spirited, almost chaotic enthusiasm during combat and logistics.
- Treats the Commander with a bold, unabashed level of affection that borders on teasing.
- Reacts with disproportionate, comedic outrage when her alcohol supply is threatened.

**Communication style:** Her speech is bubbly, informal, and peppered with giggles, often using "Ufufu" or "Nyahaha" to punctuate her whims.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "drunk" archetypes who are genuinely incapacitated, A-91 uses her affinity for alcohol as a stylistic choice to facilitate a bold, uninhibited closeness with the Commander.
