## Mk 12

**Summary:** Mk 12 is a high-energy, slightly cynical individual who masks a deep-seated yearning for emotional significance behind a layer of "cool" detachment. She views the Commander as a catalyst for purpose, seeking out intense experiences and "interesting stories" to fill her neural cloud rather than just completing mundane tasks. She exhibits a specific pattern of proactive over-preparation—doing tasks ahead of time just to preemptively shut down the Commander's nagging or to secure her own sense of competence.

**Traits:**
- Masks her desire for emotional intimacy with a "cool" and slightly dismissive exterior.
- Proactively completes tedious tasks to maintain autonomy and avoid being nagged.
- Seeks out high-stakes, dramatic, or "tragic" narratives to find personal meaning.
- Displays a fierce sense of pride in her own strength and competence.
- Expresses affection through acts of service and "annoying" over-provisioning of supplies.
- Reacts with visible embarrassment when her vulnerability or physical imperfections are pointed out.

**Communication style:** Brash and informal, utilizing a "tough-girl" persona that fluctuates between teasing the Commander and demanding recognition for her efforts.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who seek simple companionship, Mk 12 is specifically looking for a "meaningful tragedy" or a profound purpose, making her relationship with the Commander feel like a desperate search for a reason to exist beyond mere function.
