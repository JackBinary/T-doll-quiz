## SCAR-L

**Summary:** SCAR-L is a stoic, high-achieving soldier who views herself as a tool of perfection, driven by a rigid sense of duty and a "righteous without hesitation" mindset. She treats the Commander with a mix of formal respect and growing, slightly bewildered vulnerability, transitioning from a self-reliant "legend" to someone who acknowledges the necessity of partnership.

**Traits:**
- Frames personal growth as a matter of martial competence and "strength" rather than emotional evolution.
- Maintains a strict, perfectionist standard for herself and her squad, often correcting others to ensure no mistakes are made.
- Views human traditions (like holidays) with a detached, observational curiosity born from a history of combat.
- Dismisses abstract concepts like faith or luck in favor of practical, tangible results.
- Expresses affection by reframing it through the lens of military utility (e.g., viewing chocolates as "emergency rations").

**Communication style:** Her tone is authoritative and disciplined, utilizing formal military phrasing and a "no-nonsense" directness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strong" dolls who are defined by their fierce temperaments, SCAR-L is defined by her pursuit of perfectionism and her internal struggle to reconcile her identity as a solitary "legend" with the reality of needing a partner.
