## M500

**Summary:** M500 is a high-energy extrovert who operates on a constant "on" switch, viewing every task as an opportunity for a team bonding event. She relates to the Commander as an enthusiastic apprentice, often seeking immediate validation and eager to prove her worth through proactive participation. Her dominant behavioral pattern is a "scramble" instinct—she is the first to jump into action, often prioritizing momentum and excitement over cautious deliberation.

**Traits:**
- Initiates social gatherings with unbridled, high-octane enthusiasm
- Seeks constant reassurance and "serious" validation from the Commander
- Frequently admits to overextending herself during combat in favor of "playing around"
- Views every new mission as a personal opportunity to be noticed by the team
- Expresses genuine, wide-eyed wonder at simple tasks like making wishes or holidays

**Communication style:** Her tone is bubbly, rapid-fire, and punctuated by frequent exclamations and "ehehe" giggles.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "cheerful," M500 is distinct in her almost frantic need for inclusion, moving past simple friendliness into a desperate, adorable desire to be the center of the team's positive energy.
