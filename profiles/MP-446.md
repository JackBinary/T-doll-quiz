## MP-446 (Viking)

**Summary:** Viking is a high-energy, affectionate "golden retriever" personality who operates on a baseline of intense devotion to the Commander. She views the Commander as her primary source of entertainment and purpose, often demanding constant attention and physical proximity. She frequently masks her underlying insecurities about her own competence with a boisterous, "can-do" attitude toward every mission.

**Traits:**
- Clings to the Commander's side and demands to be included in every daily activity.
- Records every mundane detail of the Commander's life as if it were a monumental epic.
- Displays visible frustration when she feels she hasn't been "useful" enough in combat.
- Treats the arrival of new dolls or holidays with infectious, unbridled enthusiasm.
- Becomes flustered and physically clingy when caught in moments of genuine vulnerability.

**Communication style:** Energetic, bubbly, and informal, often using exclamation points and a "spunky" tone that borders on hyperactive.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are loyal, Viking is distinct in her "fan-girl" level of obsession; she doesn't just want to serve the Commander, she wants to be the Commander's primary playmate and chronicler.
