## Suomi

**Summary:** Suomi is a fiercely patriotic and disciplined soldier who carries the weight of her homeland’s pride as a personal mandate. She balances a stoic, "senior" leadership persona with a hidden, deeply sentimental side that craves personal connection and musical expression. She relates to the Commander as a trusted partner in duty, often seeking validation through her performance and small, intimate moments of shared time.

**Traits:**
- Adopts a "tough-love" leadership style while secretly worrying about her subordinates' well-being.
- Expresses her pride through meticulous attention to detail and high-quality performance.
- Masks her vulnerability behind a "stiff" exterior, especially when she feels she is failing a duty.
- Seeks out "me-time" with the Commander to escape the pressures of being a frontline asset.
- Displays a competitive, slightly prickly rivalry with specific comrades (like Papasha) to hide her underlying camaraderie.
- Uses music as a private emotional outlet, though she is hesitant to impose her tastes on others.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal in a professional setting, shifting to a more intimate, slightly bashful tone when speaking privately with the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other stoic "soldier" types who are content with duty alone, Suomi is defined by the tension between her desire to be a perfect, "high-production" instrument of war and her yearning for a simple, quiet life of music and companionship.
