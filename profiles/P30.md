## P30

**Summary:** P30 is an intensely observant, high-energy doll who views every shared moment with the Commander as a permanent data point to be cherished. Her emotional register is one of playful, almost possessive devotion, often masking a deep-seated fear of being forgotten or replaced. She behaves like a "memory keeper," constantly referencing past events to solidify her bond with the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction as a "memory" to be archived and protected.
- Displays a competitive, slightly aggressive streak when teased or challenged.
- Expresses possessiveness by making it clear she doesn't want to be "given up" to others.
- Proactively seeks out new experiences and "treats" to expand her internal database.
- Uses a "payback" dynamic as a way to engage in playful, lighthearted friction.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a mix of bubbly enthusiasm and sharp, teasing retorts.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be loyal, P30 is uniquely obsessed with the *permanence* of memory, making her devotion feel like a deliberate, curated collection of "forever" moments rather than just a general sense of duty.
