## KH2002

**Summary:** A high-strung, tech-obsessed communications specialist who treats social media interaction as a professional duty and a personal sanctuary. She is deeply socially anxious in person, preferring the safety of a screen to face-to-face interaction, and views the Commander as a VIP "follower" she must diligently curate.

**Traits:**
- Masks her social awkwardness with a "professional" persona regarding her communication duties.
- Expresses extreme physical distress when forced into real-world, face-to-face scenarios.
- Treats her dog, Bailey, as a primary emotional anchor and tactical partner.
- Displays a neurotic fixation on network stability and signal strength.
- Offers "special services" like obsessive-compulsive liking of the Commander's posts.
- Uses humor and "stinky" jokes to deflect from moments of genuine vulnerability or embarrassment.

**Communication style:** Fast-paced and enthusiastic online, but becomes stuttering, flustered, and defensive when confronted in person.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other tech-focused dolls who enjoy the data, she specifically seeks out digital interaction as a shield against the "horror" of real-life social dynamics.
