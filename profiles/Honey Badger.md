## Honey Badger

**Summary:** Honey Badger is a high-energy, proactive "bravery-first" combatant who views herself as a pioneer of bold tactics. She views the Commander as her emotional anchor, deriving the confidence to take massive risks from the security of the Commander’s leadership. She frequently pushes for "bold proposals" and refuses to let her own fear overshadow the mission's success.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out the most dangerous positions on the battlefield to prove her mettle.
- Frames her personal growth as a direct byproduct of the Commander’s presence.
- Proposes high-risk, high-reward tactics rather than playing it safe.
- Experiences genuine moments of vulnerability when forced to consider retreat.
- Expresses affection through the diligent preparation of handmade gifts.
- Struggles with confusion when faced with surreal or deceptive situations (like Betty).

**Communication style:** Enthusiastic and earnest, using a determined, "can-do" tone that occasionally wavers into soft sincerity when speaking privately to the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "brave" dolls who are naturally fearless or stoic, Honey Badger’s courage is a conscious, conscious choice that she actively tries to maintain despite her underlying anxieties.
