## Scorpion EVO 3

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, earnest "eager beaver" who views her service as a prestigious honor rather than a mere job. She maintains a protective, nurturing stance toward the Commander, often attempting to shoulder his burdens to ensure he gets the rest he lacks.

**Traits:**
- Frequently pivots from leisure activities to training drills on a whim.
- Expresses genuine disbelief and humility when receiving praise or rewards.
- Actively seeks out ways to be useful, often volunteering for "preferential" tasks.
- Displays a protective, almost sisterly concern for the Commander’s physical well-being.
- Mentions her companion, vz. 61, with constant affection and inclusion.
- Analyzes tactical retreats as logical maneuvers rather than signs of defeat.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, using a bubbly "honor roll" register punctuated by occasional nervous stutters when flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy "energetic" dolls who might be chaotic or impulsive, EVO 3 is defined by her disciplined sense of duty and her specific desire to be a "reliable" protector who earns her place on the team.
