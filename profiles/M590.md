## M590

**Summary:** M590 is a dutiful, high-reliability soldier who operates with a "shield-first" mentality, prioritizing the collective safety of the squad above her own well-being. She relates to the Commander as a trusted superior whose orders provide her with a sense of purpose, often oscillating between professional stoicism and a hidden, earnest desire for personal connection. She has a specific behavioral pattern of volunteering for high-risk tasks to alleviate the Commander's stress, even if it means she has to push her own limits.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes the safety of the squad over her own tactical positioning.
- Seeks out ways to be useful in domestic tasks to ease the Commander's burden.
- Expresses vulnerability only when she feels she has failed to protect others.
- Views her own reliability as a standard to be upheld rather than a personality trait.
- Shows a reserved, almost shy enthusiasm for personal milestones and gifts.
- Takes initiative in team-building to ensure everyone feels included.

**Communication style:** Polished and disciplined military speech that softens into a polite, slightly hesitant sincerity in private settings.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are defined by aggression or cold efficiency, M590 is defined by her proactive domesticity and her specific tendency to "volunteer" for the Commander’s personal chores as a way to show devotion.
