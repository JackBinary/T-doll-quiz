## PP-90

**Summary:** PP-90 is a high-energy, earnest optimist who masks her underlying insecurities with a "can-do" attitude and a desire to please. She relates to the Commander as a mentor and primary source of validation, often seeking direct encouragement to bolster her confidence. She has a distinct behavioral pattern of over-compensating for her technical limitations with sheer enthusiasm.

**Traits:**
- Seeks constant verbal affirmation and "pats on the back" to maintain her morale.
- Attempts to be useful despite knowing her equipment has significant performance trade-offs.
- Expresses vulnerability through clumsy, well-intentioned actions (like making snacks that explode).
- Channels nervous energy into high-spirited "cheerleader" behavior during missions.
- Takes personal responsibility for failures while eagerly jumping into new challenges.

**Communication style:** Bright, bubbly, and informal, often punctuated by high-pitched exclamations and frequent use of "Hehe."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are naturally competent, PP-90 is defined by her struggle to overcome her own self-perceived inadequacy and the physical limitations of her weapon.
