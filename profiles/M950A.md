## M950A

**Summary:** A self-aware "brand name" doll who masks her deep-seated insecurity about being replaceable with a sharp, slightly prickly exterior. She oscillates between a "cool and detached" professional and a flustered, easily embarrassed girl who desperately wants to be prioritized by the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Masks feelings of inadequacy with a "no-nonsense" attitude toward trivial conversation.
- Displays sudden, clumsy vulnerability when her feelings for the Commander are acknowledged.
- Expresses a fierce, almost desperate desire to prove her individual worth over her "franchise" status.
- Uses mock-toughness to hide her genuine happiness when receiving personal attention or gifts.
- Internalizes mistakes heavily, viewing personal failure as a direct reflection of her value as a doll.

**Communication style:** Blunt and efficient at first, shifting into a stuttering, high-strung register when the conversation becomes personal or romantic.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" types who are naturally aggressive, M950A’s prickly behavior is a specific defense mechanism against the fear of being treated as a generic, expendable tool.
