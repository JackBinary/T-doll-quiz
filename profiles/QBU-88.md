## QBU-88

**Summary:** A high-energy "homemaker" archetype who expresses her devotion through frantic productivity and domestic labor. She treats the Commander’s well-being as a logistical priority, often hovering with a mix of motherly fussing and an almost obsessive need to keep the environment orderly.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection by tidying up the Commander's workspace without being asked.
- Fixates on "value for money" and creates a sense of urgency regarding shopping sales.
- Displays a perfectionist streak by criticizing the clumsiness of others.
- Masks her own insecurities about being "useful" by insisting she is a "good deal" for the Commander.
- Takes on the role of a protective vanguard who feels responsible for the safety of the entire team.

**Communication style:** Energetic and rapid-fire, characterized by frequent use of imperatives ("Hurry!", "Switch!") and a polite but insistent domestic tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "homemaker" dolls who are content to stay in the background, QBU-88 is defined by an active, almost restless drive to be the most efficient worker in the room.
