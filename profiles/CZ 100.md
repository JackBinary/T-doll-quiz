## CZ 100

**Summary:** A socially anxious and introverted doll who finds the "noise" of the world overwhelming and seeks sanctuary in the Commander's presence. She operates on a "manual" for social interaction to compensate for her lack of confidence, often retreating into manga as a safe way to experience emotions.

**Traits:**
- Hesitates to initiate interaction and frequently apologizes for "bothering" others.
- Prefers quiet, one-on-one environments over group activities or large social gatherings.
- Uses manga as a primary medium to learn about human emotions and relationships.
- Seeks validation through small acts of service, like gathering supplies or lending books.
- Struggles with self-perception, often feeling like an outsider or "not belonging" in festive settings.
- Displays a hidden, intense desire for intimacy that she struggles to express directly.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and timid, characterized by frequent hesitations, "um"s, and a polite but strained register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other shy dolls who are simply "quiet," CZ 100 is specifically defined by her struggle with the *gaze* of others and her reliance on external media (manga) to bridge the gap between her internal world and the Commander.
