## RT-20

**Summary:** RT-20 operates with a playful, inquisitive curiosity, often treating the Commander’s strategic maneuvers as a personal puzzle to be solved. She maintains a lighthearted, slightly mischievous demeanor that masks a sharp, observant intellect capable of detecting "shady deals" and hidden motives. She relates to the Commander as a brilliant but unpredictable architect, showing a genuine desire to be an "irreplaceable part" of their grand design.

**Traits:**
- Frames her curiosity as a series of jokes or "mysterious" secrets.
- Analyzes the Commander’s ambitions and hidden goals with a keen, observational eye.
- Offers help with a "take it or leave it" attitude that hides a deep willingness to comply.
- Frequently speculates on conspiracies, aliens, or hidden data-collection.
- Displays a physical, tactile habit of noticing small details, like loose bandages, and wanting to "fix" them.
- Expresses a desire for "mysterious feelings" over tangible rewards.

**Communication style:** Playful and conspiratorial, using a teasing "fufu" laugh and a casual, slightly irreverent tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other inquisitive dolls who might be purely academic, RT-20 balances her analytical "detective" nature with a distinct sense of mischief and a physical, almost protective attention to detail.
