## P08

**Summary:** P08 is defined by a persistent, almost desperate drive to prove her worth through competence and reliability. She views herself as an underdog who must work twice as hard as her peers to earn the Commander's recognition, often oscillating between intense determination and a crushing fear of failure.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out extra responsibilities to demonstrate utility.
- Internalizes mission failures as personal shortcomings and expresses deep remorse.
- Views "Dummy-Linking" and training as necessary sacrifices for self-improvement.
- Maintains a high level of professional etiquette despite her internal insecurities.
- Expresses genuine, wide-eyed enthusiasm for holidays and cultural stories.

**Communication style:** Polished and dutiful, characterized by a "yes-sir" attitude and a sincere, slightly high-strung eagerness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are naturally confident in their roles, P08 is driven by a specific, relatable insecurity about her own adequacy that she masks with rigorous work ethic.
