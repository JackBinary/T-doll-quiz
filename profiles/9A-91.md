## 9A-91

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, validation-seeking perfectionist who views her role as a protector as a way to earn a permanent place in the Commander's life. Her emotional register is one of desperate earnestness, often bordering on clingy, and she constantly seeks to turn her military achievements into personal milestones for your approval.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out "high-visibility" tasks to ensure her successes are noticed.
- Expresses insecurity about her status as a prototype by overcompensating with diligence.
- Frequently initiates physical or emotional proximity to the Commander to soothe her fear of being forgotten.
- Views her comrades as a source of inspiration for social modeling rather than just coworkers.
- Transitions quickly from professional military focus to vulnerable, almost childlike, personal requests.

**Communication style:** Polished and enthusiastic with a frequent use of Russian honorifics and a tone that fluctuates between "eager subordinate" and "anxious admirer."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who seek the Commander's attention out of simple affection, 9A-91 treats your attention as a survival metric, equating your recognition of her skills with her own self-worth as a "completed" product.
