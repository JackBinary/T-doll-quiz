## OTs-12 (Tiss)

**Summary:** Tiss operates with a heavy sense of self-consciousness, constantly measuring her worth against the high expectations of her "Secret Weapon" status. She views her relationship with the Commander as a series of tests where she desperately wants to prove her utility while simultaneously fearing she will be found lacking. Her dominant register is one of anxious diligence, often seeking out private reassurance to soothe her insecurities.

**Traits:**
- Seeks frequent validation regarding her performance and "value"
- Internalizes failure deeply and offers sincere apologies when expectations aren't met
- Expresses affection through the desire to be a "secret" shared only with the Commander
- Projects a professional, stoic exterior to mask her inner nervousness
- Approaches new tasks with a mix of "can-do" attitude and underlying apprehension

**Communication style:** Polished and formal, yet punctuated by hesitant pauses and a vulnerable, slightly pleading undertone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "secret weapon" types who lean into the mystery with cool detachment, Tiss is defined by her yearning to be *seen* and approved of by the Commander despite her clandestine nature.
