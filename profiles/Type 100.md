## Type 100

**Summary:** Driven by an intense, almost bordering on obsessive need for perfection, she views her service to the Commander as a personal test of her worth. Her emotional register is one of high-strung earnestness, frequently oscillating between proactive diligence and a vulnerable, crushing fear of disappointing her superior.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes profusely for any perceived shortfall in performance, even minor logistical errors.
- Over-prepares for tasks to the point of physical and mental exhaustion.
- Seeks out specific opportunities to be "utilized" rather than waiting for instructions.
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing rather than a tactical setback.
- Struggles to express personal desires, often defaulting to "working harder" when asked about her own needs.

**Communication style:** Highly formal and polite, utilizing a "student-to-mentor" register that occasionally cracks into flustered, bashful honesty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who are simply diligent, Type 100 is defined by a desperate, self-sacrificing anxiety to be "perfect" enough to earn the Commander's approval.
