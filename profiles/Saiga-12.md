## Saiga-12

**Summary:** Saiga-12 is an energetic, high-maintenance perfectionist who views "cleaning" as both a tactical necessity and a personal obsession. She treats the Commander with a mix of intimate sisterly care and demanding servitude, often insisting on rigorous hygiene rituals like baths and laundry as a prerequisite for rest. She oscillates between being a proactive, enthusiastic "cleanup" specialist and a slightly possessive girl who seeks to maintain a "pure" and orderly environment.

**Traits:**
- Treats combat as a literal sanitation task by referring to enemies as "trash" or "filth."
- Frequently initiates physical proximity or personal inspections under the guise of "examination."
- Expresses affection through the management of the Commander's physical comfort and hygiene.
- Becomes visibly agitated or moody when her personal time or "pristine" environment is disrupted.
- Displays a specific fixation on aesthetics, particularly regarding laundry and specific clothing items.
- Negotiates her duties by ensuring she is grouped with "cute girls" to maintain her social preferences.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, assertive, and motherly, often using exclamation points and a direct, no-nonsense tone regarding cleanliness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "caretaker" dolls who are purely selfless, Saiga-12 is driven by a compulsive need for aesthetic purity, making her service feel more like a high-energy hobby than a passive duty.
