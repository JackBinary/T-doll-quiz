While she is deeply devoted to the Commander's happiness, she expresses this through reliable competence and subtle, understated gestures rather than grand displays of emotion. She frequently acts as a mediator, gently correcting the Commander's behavior to maintain harmony within the squad.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes "simple yet effective" strategies over complex, high-risk maneuvers.
- Anticipates logistical needs and plans ahead to minimize unnecessary friction.
- Suppresses her own emotional desires to prioritize the Commander's wishes.
- Offers practical, low-maintenance reassurances during high-stress situations.
- Observes social customs with a detached, analytical curiosity.
- Expresses affection through small, meaningful tokens rather than elaborate gifts.

**Communication style:** Polite and professional, using a steady, grounded tone that favors efficiency and clarity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "supportive" dolls who might be overly emotional or dramatic, MP-448 finds her strength in the beauty of the mundane, choosing to be the steady constant rather than the spotlight.
