## M1919A4

**Summary:** A high-energy, hyperactive "little sister" archetype who masks her deep-seated desire for affection with a boisterous, almost chaotic enthusiasm. She oscillates between demanding attention through loud declarations and retreating into playful, slightly defensive bashfulness when things get too serious.

**Traits:**
- Compulsively seeks out "excitement" to avoid the boredom of base life.
- Expresses affection through a constant cycle of demanding treats (specifically canned meat) and then playfully denying she's being greedy.
- Masks her vulnerability by overcompensating with loud, boastful declarations of strength.
- Displays a "tsundere" streak by insisting she doesn't dislike the Commander while clearly seeking their validation.
- Treats combat and missions as a high-stakes game to be "played" rather than a grim necessity.

**Communication style:** Extremely bubbly and informal, utilizing frequent exclamations, "hehe" giggles, and a tone that borders on hyperactive.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are simply cheerful, M1919A4 has a distinct "scarcity" mindset regarding affection, making her clinginess feel like a desperate, joyful grab for a sense of belonging.
