## KAC-PDW

**Summary:** KAC-PDW is a hyper-fixated, "lovestruck" doll who views the Commander exclusively through the lens of a romantic obsession. She maintains an air of bubbly, high-energy devotion, often using "Honey" as a constant pet name to establish a private, exclusive intimacy. Her behavior is characterized by a "gatekeeper" mentality; she is fiercely protective of her status as the Commander's favorite and will actively (and sometimes menacingly) try to sideline other dolls.

**Traits:**
- Uses "Honey" as a constant verbal anchor to reinforce a sense of private intimacy.
- Actively monitors and gatekeeps the Commander’s interactions with other dolls.
- Frames every combat objective and mission as a means to earn personal praise or rewards.
- Displays a "cute" but slightly unhinged possessiveness that borders on territorial.
- Expresses affection through specific, repetitive actions like offering grapes or gifts.
- Threatens to "prune" or "rip" the vocal modules of rivals who get too close.

**Communication style:** High-pitched, sugary sweet, and relentlessly affectionate, often punctuated by playful giggles and "hehe" sounds.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are loyal, KAC-PDW is distinct because her devotion isn't just about duty, it is a competitive, almost predatory romantic obsession where she views every other doll as a rival to be outmaneuvered.
