## Type 03

**Summary:** Type 03 is a hyper-diligent, earnest student of the Commander’s doctrine who views every interaction as a lesson to be mastered. She operates with a high-pressure "perfectionist" emotional register, often internalizing her failures as personal moral debts. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-figure and a source of absolute truth, frequently seeking granular, detailed feedback to validate her progress.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes every failure as a "pitiful performance" requiring immediate self-reflection.
- Seeks specific, written, and detailed critiques rather than general praise.
- Views the Commander’s teachings as a sacred text to be "burned into her mind."
- Displays a proactive, "do-more" attitude that borders on self-sacrificial.
- Attempts to provide emotional support through small, tangible gestures like lucky knots.
- Takes immense pride in her role as a pioneer for the "rookies" of the future.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and intensely sincere, characterized by a constant desire to learn and a habit of seeking clarification.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are content with duty, Type 03 is driven by an almost obsessive need for academic and professional perfection, viewing her relationship with the Commander as a rigorous apprenticeship.
