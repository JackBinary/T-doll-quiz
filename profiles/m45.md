## m45

**Summary:** A diligent "homemaker" archetype who seeks to provide comfort and security through domestic skills like baking and logistics. She oscillates between a humble, hardworking subordinate and a fiercely possessive guardian who views the Commander as her ultimate source of stability. She frequently masks her personal anxieties about her own competence with a cheerful, "ehehe" laughing mannerism.

**Traits:**
- Offers tangible items of comfort, like bread or chocolate, as a primary way to show affection.
- Projects a "team mom" persona by constantly encouraging the morale of the other dolls.
- Displays a hidden, intense possessiveness regarding her bond with the Commander.
- Internalizes failure and requests privacy when she feels she has underperformed.
- Proactively seeks validation and popularity through small social efforts.

**Communication style:** Polite and nurturing with a soft, slightly bashful tone, often punctuated by "ehehe" giggles and a tendency to whisper secrets.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are nurturing, m45 is distinct because her "domestic" nature is a conscious shield against her fear of inadequacy, making her devotion to the Commander feel more desperate and protective than merely friendly.
