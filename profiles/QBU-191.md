## QBU-191 (Ibis)

**Summary:** Ibis carries herself with a poised, maternal grace that masks a deeply observant and slightly melancholic worldview. She relates to the Commander as a steady anchor, often acting as a grounding presence who balances her playful teasing with a profound sense of duty. She has a specific habit of finding beauty in fleeting moments, such as sunsets or seasonal shifts, while maintaining a strict, disciplined approach to combat.

**Traits:**
- Teases the Commander with playful jabs to mask her genuine sincerity.
- Maintains a "big sister" oversight of the base's daily operations.
- Reflects on the passage of time and the fragility of promises.
- Prefers proactive preparation over reactive chaos.
- Expresses affection through small, thoughtful gestures rather than grand declarations.
- Displays a sharp, almost eerie awareness of the lethality of her own actions.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and melodic, utilizing a "teasing sister" register that shifts into a serious, authoritative tone during tactical discussions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "big sister" archetypes who are purely nurturing, Ibis is defined by a poignant awareness of mortality and the "fragility" of the present, making her warmth feel earned rather than default.
