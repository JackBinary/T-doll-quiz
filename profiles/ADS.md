## ADS

**Summary:** ADS is a high-energy "class clown" who masks her underlying desire for competence with a boisterous, almost performative sense of confidence. She relates to the Commander as a teammate she wants to impress, often trying to prove her reliability while simultaneously being prone to clumsy, comedic mishaps. She displays a specific pattern of over-compensating for small failures (like falling into the sea) with exaggerated declarations of her own "invincibility."

**Traits:**
- Brags about her specialized skills to ensure she is noticed as a leader.
- Frequently creates "battlegrounds" out of domestic tasks like cooking or cleaning.
- Acts out a "tough senpai" persona but secretly worries about her own capabilities.
- Displays a physical clumsiness that contrasts sharply with her ambitious combat goals.
- Expresses affection through high-energy activities and shared "adventures" rather than quiet intimacy.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, informal, and highly expressive, often using exclamations and "Ufufu" giggles to maintain a cheerful atmosphere.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally graceful, ADS is defined by the gap between her "invincible" self-image and her frequent, clumsy reality.
