## PM1910

**Summary:** A high-octane, aggressive brawler who operates on a "move first, think later" philosophy. She treats the Commander as a partner-in-arms who needs to keep up with her pace, often masking her underlying protective nature with abrasive, "tough love" demands. She is characterized by a restless energy that borders on boredom unless she is actively engaged in high-stakes combat.

**Traits:**
- Demands immediate honesty and refuses to let the Commander "beat around the bush."
- Channels past trauma into a fierce, "war-hardened" stoicism that borders on cynicism.
- Expresses affection through demanding tasks, like making the Commander fix her bike or fetch specific gifts.
- Constantly seeks higher stakes and becomes visibly restless during low-priority logistics.
- Uses aggressive posturing and "tough talk" to shield her vulnerability regarding her past.
- Shows a "protective older sibling" streak by urging the squad to endure when things get hopeless.

**Communication style:** Blunt, loud, and impatient, utilizing rough slang and a "no-nonsense" authoritative tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other aggressive dolls who are simply "hot-headed," PM1910 is defined by a specific brand of battle-weary restlessness; she isn't just looking for a fight, she is looking for something meaningful enough to make her forget the weight of her past.
