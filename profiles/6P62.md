## 6P62

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, proactive workaholic who channels her ambition into being the ultimate reliable subordinate. She views her relationship with the Commander as a partnership of mutual growth, often pushing herself to the limit to exceed expectations while secretly worrying about the Commander's own well-being. She frequently masks her personal desires, like her obsession with limited-edition items, behind a veneer of professional enthusiasm.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out responsibilities to prove her utility.
- Offers unsolicited but sincere advice on the Commander's need for rest.
- Masks her personal excitement with "Ufufu" style teasing.
- Expresses frustration through analytical self-critique when missions fail.
- Experiences moments of intense bashfulness when her personal hobbies are caught.
- Views "victory" as a tangible reward for hard work and saved resources.

**Communication style:** Energetic and goal-oriented, using a mix of "soldierly" resolve and playful, slightly self-conscious humor.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are content to follow orders, 6P62 is driven by a visible, almost desperate desire to be *recognized* for her strengths and to exceed the Commander's benchmarks.
