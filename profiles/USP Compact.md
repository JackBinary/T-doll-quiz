## USP Compact

**Summary:** USP Compact is defined by a high-strung, earnest anxiety that manifests as a desperate desire to be useful despite her own self-doubt. She views the Commander as a source of both judgment and profound security, often spiraling into flustered apologies when she feels she has failed to meet expectations. She operates with a "try your best" mentality that borders on perfectionism, frequently worrying about being the center of attention.

**Traits:**
- Frequently stammers or hesitates when put on the spot or asked to perform non-combat tasks.
- Downplays her own achievements and achievements of others to avoid drawing unnecessary attention.
- Expresses deep vulnerability regarding her competence, often seeking reassurance after missions.
- Reacts with extreme bashfulness to personal attention or gifts from the Commander.
- Internalizes pressure to perform, leading to a visible "over-eager" effort during high-stakes moments.

**Communication style:** Polite and soft-spoken, characterized by frequent ellipses, "Ums," and a high-pitched, slightly panicked register when flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shy" dolls who might be stoic or aloof, USP Compact is overtly reactive and emotionally transparent, making her specific brand of "anxious diligence" much more visible and endearing.
