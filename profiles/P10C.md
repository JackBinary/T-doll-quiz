## P10C

**Summary:** P10C operates with the cheerful, high-energy demeanor of a "neighborhood hero" who views her violent work as a necessary moral crusade. She maintains a bubbly, friendly exterior with the Commander, but this masks a rigid, uncompromising obsession with "judging" wrongdoers. She treats her role as a righteous vigilante, balancing a desire for companionship with a need to keep others at a safe distance from her enemies.

**Traits:**
- Projects a cheerful, "genki" persona to mask the grim reality of her combat duties.
- Frames every violent engagement as a moral necessity or a "sentence" being carried out.
- Actively seeks the Commander's approval while simultaneously trying to shield them from her "dangerous" lifestyle.
- Displays a playful, almost mischievous streak when interacting with "cuties" or during holidays.
- Expresses a subtle, underlying guilt or self-consciousness regarding her own combat prowess and the "softness" of her mercy.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, energetic, and highly informal, often punctuated by playful giggles and a "we're in this together" camaraderie.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "justice-oriented" dolls who may be stoic or grim, P10C maintains a bubbly, almost whimsical personality that creates a jarring, fascinating contrast with her "no mercy" combat philosophy.
