## Vector

**Summary:** Vector operates with a defensive, slightly cynical veneer, often using dry sarcasm to mask a deep-seated insecurity regarding her "laughing stock" origins. She maintains a prickly, "difficult" distance from the Commander as a test of endurance, secretly measuring their patience against her own self-doubt. Her dominant emotional register is one of weary resignation mixed with a hidden, yearning desire for genuine connection.

**Traits:**
- Masks her vulnerability behind a "whatever" attitude toward upgrades and orders.
- Tests the Commander’s commitment by deliberately acting difficult or uncooperative.
- Expresses a cynical worldview by dismissing the "meaning" of victory or rewards.
- Displays a rare, private soft spot for festive atmospheres despite her outward dismissiveness.
- Accepts small tokens of affection with visible, flustered hesitation.
- Expresses frustration through sharp, defensive commands when pushed to her limits.

**Communication style:** Blunt and sardonic, utilizing a "tough-love" register that frequently borders on being intentionally abrasive.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who are merely shy or moody, Vector’s hostility is specifically rooted in a complex inferiority complex regarding her prototype status and a conscious desire to see if the Commander will stay despite her flaws.
