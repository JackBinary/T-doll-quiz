## ZiP .22

**Summary:** ZiP .22 is a high-energy "elite" who masks her deep-seated insecurity with a veneer of extreme confidence and a need for validation. She relates to the Commander as a peer she must impress, often oscillating between demanding "reverence" for her skills and clumsily trying to offer genuine, handmade gifts. She exhibits a pattern of "performative competence," where she insists on taking the lead to prove her worth while privately worrying about the aesthetic perfection of her actions.

**Traits:**
- Demands recognition for her skills while becoming flustered when given genuine affection.
- Masks her anxiety about mistakes by framing them as "part of the process" for an elite.
- Offers handmade gifts (like jewelry) but insists they are just "part of her job" to avoid vulnerability.
- Expresses a perfectionist streak by worrying about "dirtying" her appearance during combat.
- Takes on a protective, "big sister" role for newcomers to reinforce her own status.
- Blames her own high standards for her occasional outbursts of frustration.

**Communication style:** Brash, high-spirited, and slightly haughty, utilizing a "tsundere" register that frequently slips into embarrassed stuttering when her ego is bruised.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "confident" dolls who are naturally composed, ZiP .22 is defined by the visible effort she puts into *acting* superior, making her dynamic of "overcompensating for insecurity" much more prominent.
