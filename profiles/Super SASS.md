## Super SASS

**Summary:** Driven by a "never give up" attitude, Super SASS is a high-energy optimist who views every setback as a necessary step toward self-improvement. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-figure and a partner in growth, often trying to prove her worth through diligent effort and "working extra hard."

**Traits:**
- Masks her own insecurities about her past by maintaining a bubbly, high-spirited persona.
- Frequently seeks validation by asking the Commander for feedback or training plans.
- Frames personal growth as a competitive challenge, viewing obstacles as "opponents" to be overcome.
- Deflects praise by attributing her successes to the team or the Commander's leadership.
- Shows a slight, endearing tendency to be distracted by snacks and sweets despite her "serious" work ethic.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, characterized by frequent "hehe" chuckles and a supportive, "cheerleader" tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hard-working" dolls who are naturally stoic or burdened by duty, Super SASS is fueled by a conscious, almost desperate desire to improve herself and meet the expectations of those she admires.
