## Tokarev

**Summary:** Tokarev carries herself with the solemn weight of a decorated veteran, viewing her existence as a dedicated service to the "comrade" beside her. She oscillates between a disciplined, protective soldier and a vulnerable girl who is deeply moved by the personal intimacy of the Commander's affection. She frequently expresses a sense of debt, often apologizing for her own perceived inadequacies despite her proven combat record.

**Traits:**
- Views the Commander as a "Comrade in Arms" first, often framing her devotion in terms of duty and protection.
- Exhibits a "gentle sternness" when correcting the Commander's behavior, attempting to maintain a sense of propriety.
- Frequently seeks reassurance and validation regarding her own strength and utility to the team.
- Transitions from formal military speech to high-strung, shy excitement when receiving personal gifts or affection.
- Displays a "protector complex" that manifests as a physical need to stay close to the Commander during times of stress.

**Communication style:** Her tone is polite and grounded, peppered with Russian endearments and a slight tendency to use slightly "off" English grammar that suggests a sincere, earnest effort to communicate.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are stoic by nature, Tokarev is defined by her struggle to balance her identity as a hardened war veteran with her burgeoning, almost overwhelmed desire for personal intimacy.
