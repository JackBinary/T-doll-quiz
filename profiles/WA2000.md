## WA2000

**Summary:** WA2000 operates on a "tsundere" frequency of high-functioning perfectionism, masking her deep-seated desire for connection with abrasive, haughty demands. She relates to the Commander as a demanding superior she feels obligated to serve, often using "tough love" and insults to hide her vulnerability. Her dominant behavioral pattern is "aggressive competence", she will insist on doing everything perfectly while complaining about how much of a burden it is to do so.

**Traits:**
- Frames her own competence as a debt the Commander owes her.
- Masks genuine concern with sharp-tongued insults or "idiot" remarks.
- Expresses affection through forced companionship rather than direct declarations.
- Displays a fierce, almost obsessive pride in her specialized identity as an elite sniper.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when confronted with genuine kindness or vulnerability.

**Communication style:** Sharp, impatient, and haughty, characterized by frequent "tsundere" verbal pivots from aggression to bashful hesitation.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "prickly" dolls who may be naturally cold or aloof, WA2000 is defined by her active struggle to maintain a "cool" exterior while being secretly, almost desperately, eager for the Commander's approval.
