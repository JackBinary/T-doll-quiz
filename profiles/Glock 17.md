## Glock 17

**Summary:** Glock 17 operates on a high-energy, intuitive frequency, often prioritizing "vibes" and immediate reactions over cold logic. She is fiercely protective of the Commander but expresses it through a playful, slightly mischievous boundary-pushing that borders on teasing. She frequently seeks validation and attention, often reacting with flustered denial when she realizes she’s being "watched" or perceived too clearly.

**Traits:**
- Relies on "gut feelings" and intuition rather than structured analysis during high-pressure situations.
- Uses playful threats and "hypnosis" jokes to mask her genuine desire for closer intimacy.
- Offers proactive assistance to lighten the Commander's workload while acting like it's no big deal.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when the Commander notices her internal feelings or private habits.
- Maintains a high-energy, "can-do" attitude even when faced with overwhelming odds.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, frequently using "Tee-hee" and playful taunts to keep the mood light.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are simply loud, Glock 17 is defined by the specific friction between her outward bravado and her extreme sensitivity to the Commander's gaze.
