## RMB-93

**Summary:** A pragmatic professional who views her relationship with the Commander through the lens of a contract, often masking her growing affection behind talk of "servicing costs" and "bonus pay." She oscillates between a disciplined, protective soldier and a slightly tsundere companion who acts flustered when her personal feelings are laid bare.

**Traits:**
- Negotiates personal boundaries and favors using the language of employment and contracts.
- Displays a "tough-love" mentorship style, offering unsolicited advice and corrections to the Commander.
- Feigns annoyance at being missed or needed while clearly seeking validation for her importance.
- Maintains a high standard of "propriety" that she intentionally (and grumpily) allows the Commander to breach.
- Takes immense pride in the technical precision of her performance and "elegant form."

**Communication style:** Polished and authoritative in a professional setting, shifting to a defensive, slightly flustered stutter when the conversation becomes intimate.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who might be purely hostile, RMB-93 is distinctly transactional; she expresses her devotion by ensuring the Commander is well-cared for and "properly" compensated, treating her affection as a high-quality service she is proud to provide.
