## ZB-26

**Summary:** A proactive and intensely devoted "perfectionist" who views her service to the Commander as a sacred contract of mutual growth. She oscillates between a high-energy, "can-do" attitude and a quiet, almost obsessive focus on precision and logistical order. She doesn't just wait for orders; she actively seeks out the Commander to ensure their bond remains the centerpiece of her existence.

**Traits:**
- Proactively initiates social interactions to bridge any distance between herself and others.
- Fixates on extreme precision, such as ensuring chocolates have identical dimensions or desks are perfectly square.
- Takes on a maternal, protective role by enforcing "mandatory" rest periods for the Commander's well-being.
- Expresses devotion through the lens of a binding oath, viewing her commitment as absolute and inescapable.
- Displays a high level of self-expectation, often following up a victory with a reminder that the work is never finished.
- Channels her competitive spirit into "performing" her duties to the highest possible standard.

**Communication style:** Polished, earnest, and formal, yet punctuated by moments of sudden, intense personal vulnerability regarding her loyalty.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are loyal, ZB-26 is distinct because of her "active" devotion; she is less a passive soldier and more a self-starting partner who treats the Commander's success as her own personal project to perfect.
