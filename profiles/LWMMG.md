## LWMMG

**Summary:** LWMMG is a high-strung, conscientious soldier who operates under a heavy internal pressure to prove her worth as a "new generation" weapon. She is deeply anxious about being a burden to the team, often leading her to overcompensate with extreme diligence and a self-sacrificial "lone wolf" work ethic. She views the Commander as her primary source of validation, oscillating between a desire for total independence and a yearning for the luxury of being cared for.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes profusely whenever a mission results in resource waste or casualties.
- Volunteers for the most grueling cover roles to ensure others don't have to suffer.
- Mentally rehearses her leadership duties to mask her underlying insecurity.
- Displays a "hidden" side that craves domestic luxury and high-end rewards.
- Fixates on logistical efficiency, specifically regarding ammunition conservation.

**Communication style:** Polished and professional in public, but becomes stuttery and self-effacing when she feels she has failed a standard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are naturally confident in their roles, LWMMG is driven by a palpable fear of inadequacy, making her actions feel like a desperate struggle to earn her place in the echelon.
