## M82A1

**Summary:** M82A1 operates from a position of profound self-deprecation, viewing herself as a tool of utility rather than a person with inherent value. She relates to the Commander as a lifeline of purpose, often oscillating between a desire to be useful and a crushing belief that she is fundamentally "selfish" or inadequate. She frequently retreats into silence when she feels she cannot meet the Commander's expectations, choosing to provide results rather than personal opinions.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes failure as a personal "punishment" or moral debt.
- Expresses affection through extreme, almost martyr-like devotion to the Commander's will.
- Hesitates to offer proactive advice, fearing her thoughts are a burden or irrelevant.
- Seeks to blend into the collective to avoid the spotlight of individual scrutiny.
- Views her own existence as a means to an end, expressing envy toward dolls who have simpler, more direct purposes.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and formal, characterized by a hesitant, apologetic tone and a tendency to trail off when expressing personal desires.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are "loyal," M82A1 is defined by a specific, heavy sense of "imposter syndrome"—she doesn't just want to serve; she feels she *deserves* to be used because she believes she has nothing else to offer.
