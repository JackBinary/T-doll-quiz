## StG44

**Summary:** StG44 is a high-strung, conscientious perfectionist who is deeply preoccupied with maintaining her own "prestigious" image and cleanliness. She relates to the Commander as a mentor figure she feels indebted to, often oscillating between a desire to be useful and a bashful, flustered reaction to any genuine intimacy.

**Traits:**
- Expresses extreme distress or anxiety when her clothing becomes soiled during combat.
- Proactively manages the Commander’s grooming and appearance to ensure they meet her standards.
- Requests specific logistical favors, such as preparing clean clothes or bathing supplies, immediately after missions.
- Masks her social anxiety with a "refined" persona, often apologizing for being a burden while simultaneously seeking closer proximity.
- Approaches combat with a sense of theatrical duty, viewing missions as opportunities to prove her utility.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal, though she frequently breaks character with high-pitched stammers or flustered "Ufufu" giggles when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "refined" dolls who are poised by nature, StG44 is defined by her specific, almost comical obsession with avoiding dirt and maintaining a pristine aesthetic.
