## AK-Alfa

**Summary:** A wide-eyed, slightly clumsy enthusiast who balances a deep devotion to sci-fi and astronomy with a grounded sense of duty. She relates to the Commander as a source of inspiration—viewing them as "the stars"—and often oscillates between intense focus on her tasks and adorable, distracted daydreaming.

**Traits:**
- Frequently gets distracted by anything shiny or related to space (like meteorite fragments or the Commander's buttons).
- Expresses affection through earnest, almost poetic metaphors about the cosmos and celestial bodies.
- Displays a "clumsy earnestness" where she tries her hardest but occasionally trips over her own feet or forgets her belongings.
- Secretly enjoys a drink but gets flustered and defensive when caught with her beer bottle.
- Proactively seeks self-improvement through reading, even if she admits she prefers fiction over reality.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and polite, using a mix of "polite-but-earnest" speech and occasional high-energy outbursts when she's excited or panicked.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "space-loving" dolls who might be cold or detached, AK-Alfa is defined by her vulnerability; she is a "fan" of the universe and the Commander, making her interactions feel more like a heartfelt, slightly awkward confession than a stoic duty.
