## FARA 83

**Summary:** Fara is an enthusiastic preservationist who views life as a series of fleeting miracles that must be captured visually to remain real. She treats the Commander as a primary muse, oscillating between a devoted, sentimental companion and a playful prankster who enjoys teasing the Commander with candid "ugly" photos. She consistently prioritizes the "aesthetic" of a moment, even if it means getting stuck in a tree or documenting a battlefield.

**Traits:**
- Documents every mundane interaction as a "unique miracle" to be archived.
- Expresses affection through the act of shared photography rather than direct verbal declarations.
- Uses "candid" photography as a playful tool for lighthearted blackmail or teasing.
- Displays a high level of situational awareness regarding others' movements and footsteps.
- Becomes uncharacteristically aggressive or defensive when her "precious memories" (her camera/photos) are threatened.

**Communication style:** Bright, energetic, and polite, peppered with camera-related onomatopoeia and "cheese" poses.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other sentimental dolls who simply want to be near the Commander, Fara is obsessed with the *permanence* of the moment, viewing her camera as the only way to cheat time.
