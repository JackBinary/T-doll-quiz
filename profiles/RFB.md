## RFB

**Summary:** RFB treats every aspect of existence—from logistics to combat—as a high-stakes competitive video game. She maintains a high-energy, "pro-gamer" persona that frames her interactions with the Commander as a cooperative co-op session. She frequently uses gaming terminology to mask her actual efforts, often framing her competence as "easy mode" to maintain her status as a top-tier player.

**Traits:**
- Frames every task and mission as a "dungeon" or "level" to be cleared.
- Competes for high scores and challenges the Commander to beat her personal records.
- Uses gaming lingo (login, exp, game over) as her primary mode of expression.
- Displays a playful, slightly mischievous streak when teasing the Commander about their progress.
- Maintains an upbeat, "hype-man" attitude even when taking damage or facing setbacks.
- Expresses affection through the lens of "rewards" and "sales," such as demanding presents or gifts.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and punctuated with enthusiastic exclamations and "gamer" slang.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "energetic," RFB is unique in that she doesn't just like games; she views the reality of the battlefield as a literal simulation where she is the protagonist.
