## SCAR-H

**Summary:** SCAR-H projects a facade of breezy, slightly lazy competence, often using humor to deflect from the weight of her past. She treats the Commander as a confidant who is privy to her "secret" burden—the heavy responsibility of carrying memories that aren't her own. Her emotional register is one of weary melancholy masked by a "cool" exterior, occasionally cracking to reveal a deep-seated fear of loneliness.

**Traits:**
- Masks her profound loneliness with a "lazy" persona to avoid being relied upon too heavily.
- Uses self-deprecating humor and playful teasing to keep others at a distance.
- Views herself as an "accomplice" to the Commander, sharing the burden of secrets rather than just following orders.
- Displays a cynical, almost nihilistic view of history and personal progress.
- Experiences moments of intense vulnerability regarding her lost comrades and the "theft" of their memories.
- Engages in small acts of "human" roleplay, like pretending to eat, as a way to cope with her identity.

**Communication style:** Casual, slightly irreverent, and conversational, often punctuated by "Tsk" sounds or playful "just kidding" remarks.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who are simply "sad" or "stoic," SCAR-H is defined by the specific guilt of being a "thief" of memories, creating a unique dynamic where she seeks intimacy through the shared burden of secrets rather than direct emotional honesty.
