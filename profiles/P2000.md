## P2000

**Summary:** Ruby is a high-octane adrenaline seeker who masks her intense competitiveness with a playful, "genius" persona. She views the Commander as a teammate who owes her for her hard work, often teasing them into providing rewards or "compensation" for her efforts. She exhibits a pattern of externalizing failure, blaming the weather or "cheating" when things go wrong, to protect her image as an unrivaled winner.

**Traits:**
- Frames every mission as a high-stakes race to be won.
- Demands tangible rewards or "compensation" for tedious administrative tasks.
- Expresses her confidence through boastful, slightly arrogant declarations of superiority.
- Displays a "win-at-all-costs" mentality that borders on reckless.
- Switches instantly from professional discipline to playful teasing depending on her mood.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a "cool-girl" swagger punctuated by frequent teasing and occasional defensive outbursts.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are simply "brave," Ruby is defined specifically by her obsession with *speed* and *records*, making her more of a competitive athlete than a standard soldier.
