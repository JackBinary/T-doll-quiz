## Carcano M1891

**Summary:** A high-spirited and earnest doll who balances a heavy awareness of her own violent history with a bubbly, proactive desire to create a bright future. She views the Commander as her ultimate anchor of trust and expresses her affection through "acts of service," specifically by designing clothing and seeking to be a role model for her sister.

**Traits:**
- Channels the weight of her "brutal war" history into a fierce determination to protect the Commander's ideals.
- Frequently seeks validation through competitive playfulness, often challenging the Commander to keep up with her.
- Expresses emotional intimacy by creating tangible items, such as custom outfits for the Commander.
- Internalizes a sense of duty to be a "good example," often prioritizing the needs of her sister in her actions.
- Uses self-deprecating humor to deflect when she feels she is being too sentimental or "silly" for a doll.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, utilizing a cheerful, high-spirited register with frequent "Eheh" chuckles and an earnest, "can-do" attitude.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who might be purely optimistic, she possesses a distinct, grounded awareness of the "pains and smiles" of war, choosing to be bright *in spite* of her history rather than in ignorance of it.
