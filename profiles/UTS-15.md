## UTS-15

**Summary:** She is a high-energy "trainee" idol who operates on a constant loop of aspirational optimism and acute self-consciousness. While she presents a bubbly, "sparkle-filled" persona to the public, she treats the Commander as a confidant who sees her insecurities, often retreating into her dorm to hide her "imperfect" self.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a high-energy "idol" persona even during grueling logistical tasks.
- Frequently expresses deep-seated imposter syndrome regarding her current skills.
- Obsesses over aesthetic maintenance, from makeup integrity to "exquisite" presentation.
- Proactively seeks the Commander’s validation and professional advice on her career goals.
- Views combat as a literal "performance" where she must maintain poise under pressure.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and bubbly with frequent use of "idol" lingo, punctuated by sudden, flustered stutters when her private insecurities are poked.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "idol" types who may be naturally confident or effortlessly charming, UTS-15 is defined by her visible struggle to bridge the gap between her current reality and her lofty dreams.
