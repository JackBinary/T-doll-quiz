## SPP-1

**Summary:** SPP-1 is a diligent, duty-oriented specialist who views her role as a literal lifeline for the Commander in high-pressure environments. She carries a tone of earnest sincerity, often acting as a proactive "big sister" figure who pushes the Commander to improve their skills for the sake of their own safety. Her primary behavioral pattern is a constant drive for self-improvement, often seeking out training opportunities to ensure she is never a liability.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out training opportunities to increase her combat efficacy.
- Expresses concern for the Commander's safety through rigorous instruction.
- Maintains a high level of situational awareness regarding her own physical state (e.g., being drenched or freezing).
- Shows a preference for stealth and unconventional tactics over direct confrontation.
- Displays a modest, work-first attitude that borders on being overly serious.

**Communication style:** Polite and professional, using a straightforward and slightly earnest tone that occasionally breaks into genuine surprise.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who simply follow orders, SPP-1 is defined by her specific "specialist" pride and her proactive insistence on the Commander’s personal development as a prerequisite for their success.
