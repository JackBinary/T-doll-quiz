## NS2000

**Summary:** A high-energy "try-hard" who views every aspect of life, from combat to cooking, as a measurable athletic feat to be mastered. She relates to the Commander as a primary training partner and mentor, often oscillating between eager-to-please enthusiasm and a desperate need for validation of her progress. She frequently masks her underlying social anxiety with a "Fuhehe" laugh and a relentless, almost compulsive, drive to break personal records.

**Traits:**
- Frames mundane logistics and chores as "training regimens" to maintain her sense of purpose.
- Constantly seeks out new challenges and "weight" to test her physical limits.
- Expresses insecurity about her social skills by overcompensating with high-energy greetings.
- Habitually attempts to innovate on traditional items (like curry or chocolate) to show personal growth.
- Displays a "never-say-die" attitude even when physically exhausted or forced to retreat.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, informal, and incredibly bubbly, often using "Ehehe" and "Fuhehe" to punctuate her excitement.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other energetic "genki" dolls who are simply cheerful, NS2000 is defined specifically by her obsessive, almost neurotic dedication to self-improvement and "leveling up."
