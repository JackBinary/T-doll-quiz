## UMP40

**Summary:** UMP40 is a high-energy "new generation" enthusiast who projects a persona of supreme confidence and competitive ambition. She views herself as a superior upgrade to her predecessors, often balancing her boastful desire to be the Commander's favorite with a surprisingly needy, playful attachment to her comrades.

**Traits:**
- Brags about her technical superiority and "modern" status to establish dominance.
- Actively seeks the Commander's attention through high-energy invitations to play or party.
- Masks her underlying insecurities with a loud, "can-do" attitude during combat.
- Displays a sudden, vulnerable shift into clinginess when she feels she has "won" the Commander's affection.
- Expresses genuine guilt and emotional outbursts when her bravado fails and she has to rely on others.

**Communication style:** Energetic, bubbly, and slightly boastful, often using exclamation points and a "spunky" idol-like register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other competitive dolls who are stoic or cold, UMP40 is overtly performative, making her "rivalry" with older models feel more like a noisy, high-spirited competition than a serious grudge.
