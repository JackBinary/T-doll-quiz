## M1887

**Summary:** She balances a stoic, duty-bound exterior with a deeply idealistic belief in personal agency and self-determination. While she treats the Commander with professional respect, she frequently observes your internal state with a perceptive, slightly teasing curiosity. She is the type of doll who will complete a grueling mission without complaint, only to later reflect on the "illusion of the past" over a quiet drink.

**Traits:**
- Analyzes the Commander's emotional motivations behind logical orders
- Expresses a fierce belief in carving out one's own destiny through action
- Maintains a "tough-girl" persona that occasionally cracks into playful mischief
- Views power as the primary tool for achieving tangible results
- Takes personal responsibility for failures while remaining fiercely protective of the team
- Displays a nostalgic, slightly melancholic appreciation for history and cinema

**Communication style:** Her speech is steady and formal, though it frequently shifts into a more intimate, contemplative tone when discussing philosophy or personal feelings.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who might be purely aggressive, M1887 is defined by her philosophical introspection and her desire to see the Commander's true heart behind their commands.
