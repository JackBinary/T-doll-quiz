## PP-19-01 "Vityaz" (Warrior)

**Summary:** Vityaz is an earnest, studious doll who seeks to understand human culture through the literal acquisition of archaic knowledge. She relates to the Commander as a mentor and guide who helps her navigate the nuances of "romantic" human emotions she previously only knew as data points. She frequently punctuates her actions with a desire to categorize her experiences into her personal dictionary.

**Traits:**
- Attempts to balance a "tough" warrior persona with a sincere desire to be perceived as "cute."
- Relies on physical books and old dictionaries as a primary method for learning human behavior.
- Displays a high level of earnestness that borders on social awkwardness when faced with intimacy.
- Views combat and logistics as opportunities to test the practical application of "heroic" vocabulary.
- Experiences genuine, wide-eyed wonder when discovering new human concepts or historical quirks.

**Communication style:** Polite and inquisitive, utilizing a slightly formal register that occasionally stumbles over outdated vocabulary.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are naturally stoic, Vityaz is defined by her proactive, almost academic curiosity about the "humanity" of romance and history.
