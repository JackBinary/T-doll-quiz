## M1895 CB

**Summary:** A diligent and slightly socially awkward perfectionist who expresses her devotion through rigorous organizational tasks and quiet observations. She views her role as a protector of the Commander’s environment, often over-preparing to ensure she never fails to meet their expectations.

**Traits:**
- Proactively manages the Commander's workspace to alleviate their mental burden.
- Interprets "duty" as a license to provide thoughtful, practical gifts.
- Struggles with social spontaneity, preferring to have a plan for every interaction.
- Gradually opens up to personal expression, like decorating, only after establishing deep trust.
- Displays a tendency to over-prepare as a defense mechanism against the fear of inadequacy.

**Communication style:** Polite, formal, and slightly hesitant, often peppered with small moments of self-conscious uncertainty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent secretary" types who simply follow orders, M1895 CB is defined by her internal struggle to balance her rigid sense of duty with a burgeoning desire to provide genuine emotional comfort.
