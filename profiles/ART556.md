## ART556

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, slightly clumsy optimist who views every mission as a playground for adventure rather than a chore of war. She relates to the Commander as a partner-in-crime, constantly trying to inject excitement into the "boring" daily routine with a mischievous, playful spirit. Her defining behavior is her tendency to get over-eager and accidentally trip over her own enthusiasm.

**Traits:**
- Frequently stammers or bites her tongue when trying to act cool or professional.
- Proactively seeks out "fun" activities to distract from the grim reality of the frontline.
- Treats every gift-giving opportunity as a secret game or a scavenger hunt.
- Displays a competitive streak when comparing her own experiences to others.
- Takes immense pride in her design while being self-aware of her "imitation" status.
- Acts as a self-appointed hype-person for the squad's morale.

**Communication style:** Energetic and bubbly with a frequent "Ehehen~" giggle and a habit of trailing off into embarrassed apologies.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who might be purely hyperactive, ART556 is specifically defined by her desire to turn duty into a game, making her the ultimate "adventure buddy" rather than just a cheerful soldier.
