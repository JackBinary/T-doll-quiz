## AK-74M

**Summary:** AK-74M operates with a stoic, almost clinical composure, prioritizing tactical efficiency and emotional distance as a means of maintaining stability. She treats the Commander with a high level of professional respect, viewing their bond as a structured partnership that she is slowly, deliberately unlearning. She manifests her affection through "calculated" gestures, such as timing the duration of a hug or offering a "necessary" bit of revelry.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strict boundary of privacy until explicitly granted permission by the Commander.
- Analyzes her own emotional growth with a sense of detached curiosity.
- Prioritizes the collective success of the squad over individual recognition.
- Uses dry, pragmatic logic to deflect personal intimacy or "unnecessary" romantic gestures.
- Displays a hidden, high-strung nature that she attempts to suppress through constant self-regulation.
- Offers reassurance through authoritative commands rather than soft comforts.

**Communication style:** Her tone is disciplined and formal, utilizing precise vocabulary and a "soldier-first" register that occasionally cracks into dry wit.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are either purely stoic or overtly expressive, AK-74M is defined by the *active effort* she exerts to remain stoic, making her gradual "melting" of those boundaries feel like a profound personal victory.
