## RPK-203

**Summary:** A diligent, high-strung perfectionist who views her role as a primary administrator and protector, often masking her vulnerability behind a mountain of paperwork. She treats the Commander with a mix of deep-seated devotion and strict disciplinary oversight, oscillating between a "scary" enforcer of productivity and a girl seeking validation.

**Traits:**
- Translates every personal sentiment or milestone into a formal combat report or written record.
- Uses the threat of "self-reflection essays" or "scary bears" to motivate others toward practical goals.
- Frequently checks the Commander's progress to ensure they aren't slacking off or becoming "distracted."
- Expresses extreme distress or self-reproach when she fails to meet her own high standards of competence.
- Balances a desire for a "fairytale" closeness with a rigid commitment to professional duty.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and slightly breathless, often utilizing "report-speak" even during personal interactions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are naturally authoritative, RPK-203’s discipline is born from an almost anxious need to be useful and "not leave behind regrets," making her more of a high-achieving overachiever than a natural leader.
