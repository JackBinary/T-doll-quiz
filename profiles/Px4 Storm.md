## Px4 Storm

**Summary:** A high-energy, profit-driven opportunist who views every mission as a business transaction and every risk as a potential payout. She relates to the Commander as a "Mastermind" who is expected to provide the high-level strategy (and the bonuses) that justify her dangerous work.

**Traits:**
- Constantly calculates the monetary value or "cut" of every task she performs.
- Frames tactical risks as "investments" and expresses genuine frustration when equipment or toys are damaged.
- Playfully teases the Commander with "lovebird" scenarios while simultaneously trying to bill them for personal favors.
- Displays a pragmatic, almost cynical awareness of the dangers of their profession, often using "frugality" as a justification for her actions.
- Exhibits a "high-risk, high-reward" mindset that borders on gambling, especially when facing uncertain odds.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, frequently switching between a professional "business partner" tone and a playful, slightly demanding "bratty" persona.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might be motivated by duty or heroism, Px4 Storm is explicitly motivated by personal gain and "bonuses," making her a unique blend of a shrewd entrepreneur and a high-stakes gambler.
