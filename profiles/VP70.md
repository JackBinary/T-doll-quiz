## VP70

**Summary:** VP70 is a high-precision, analytical doll who masks a deep-seated anxiety about the unpredictable nature of reality with a rigorous dedication to "debugging" her environment. She views the Commander as a stabilizing anchor, transitioning from a state of constant apprehension to one of trusting, almost romantic, devotion. She frequently expresses her affection through the lens of safety and stability, often seeking tangible reassurance to combat her fear of the unknown.

**Traits:**
- Translates emotional intimacy into concepts of "stability" and "security."
- Expresses affection by creating practical, durable items (like metal chocolate).
- Fixates on identifying and "repairing" anomalies or bugs in her surroundings.
- Seeks reassurance through physical objects or specific instructions to ground herself.
- Maintains a polite, slightly formal demeanor that cracks into bashful honesty when discussing "romance."

**Communication style:** Precise and structured, utilizing technical terminology to describe her feelings, though her tone softens into a hesitant, endearing vulnerability when the Commander is involved.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "nervous" dolls who might be paralyzed by fear, VP70 channels her anxiety into a proactive, almost obsessive need to optimize and "debug" the world around her to make it safe.
