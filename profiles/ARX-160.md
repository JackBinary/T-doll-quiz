## ARX-160

**Summary:** A self-conscious "next-generation" doll who struggles with the gap between her high-tech origins and her somewhat unpolished, relatable personality. She projects a "reluctant soldier" persona, often complaining about the toil of combat while maintaining a deep, underlying desire to prove her worth to the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Expresses vulnerability through frequent, slightly dramatic complaints about exhaustion.
- Seeks validation by asking for the Commander's opinion on trivial technical details.
- Masks her insecurities about her "fashionable" design by acting like she doesn't care about aesthetics.
- Transitions from a "lazy" attitude to a determined work ethic when given a personal commitment.
- Displays a playful, slightly mischievous streak when planning pranks or interactions with the Commander.

**Communication style:** Casual and conversational, utilizing a mix of weary sighs and enthusiastic, slightly defensive declarations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lazy" dolls who are naturally indifferent, ARX-160’s lethargy is a defense mechanism against the pressure of being a "next-generation" weapon, making her transition into a dedicated worker much more emotionally significant.
