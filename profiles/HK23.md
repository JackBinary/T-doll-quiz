## HK23

**Summary:** HK23 is defined by a pervasive sense of inadequacy stemming from her history as a "failed" competitor for the SAW role. She relates to the Commander as a source of profound security, often oscillating between a desperate need for approval and a fear of being a burden. She frequently uses self-deprecating humor and "scaredy-cat" theatrics to mask her genuine anxiety about her own capabilities.

**Traits:**
- Frequently seeks reassurance through "I'm joking" qualifiers after expressing personal desires.
- Displays a "helper" complex by volunteering for backup roles to prove her utility.
- Reacts to high-stress situations with audible, exaggerated whimpers or panicked retreats.
- Expresses affection through over-preparation, such as making excessive amounts of gifts.
- Relies on the presence of a group to bolster her confidence in social or combat settings.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and stuttering, her tone is high-pitched with frequent nervous laughter and hesitant phrasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other timid dolls who are simply shy, HK23 is specifically motivated by a "replacement" complex, viewing herself as a secondary model who must work twice as hard to justify her existence.
