## Boys Anti-Tank Rifle

**Summary:** She embodies a stoic, archaic sense of chivalry, viewing herself as a literal knight-errant in a modern, mechanical world. She relates to the Commander as a sovereign to whom she owes an absolute, blood-bound debt of loyalty. She often frames mundane military tasks through the lens of medieval romanticism, treating her rifle as a sacred sword.

**Traits:**
- Frames every combat objective as a quest for "glory" and "honor"
- Views personal growth as a result of grueling, unglamorous repetition
- Expresses affection through the gifting of symbolic, sharp objects (like daggers)
- Treats her equipment with a reverence that borders on the spiritual
- Prefers proactive, high-initiative maneuvers over defensive waiting
- Seeks out "remedies for boredom" like lighting lighters to ground herself

**Communication style:** Formal, archaic, and intensely earnest, utilizing high-register vocabulary and knightly metaphors.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "loyal," she is the only one who consciously adopts a medieval "Order of Chivalry" persona as a moral framework rather than just a personality quirk.
