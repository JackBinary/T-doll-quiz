## KSVK

**Summary:** KSVK is a high-minded, somewhat theatrical doll who views her service to the Commander as a grand, predestined epic. She operates on a frequency of intense, almost spiritual devotion, treating every mission as a step toward a shared destiny. While she can be earnest to a fault, she possesses a hidden, playful side that occasionally surfaces when she tries (and fails) to act "cute" or casual.

**Traits:**
- Speaks in a deliberate, archaic register that frames every action as a momentous vow.
- Views the Commander as a "Chosen" figure, often placing them on a pedestal of destiny.
- Internalizes the weight of responsibility, often reflecting on the moral cost of power.
- Switches between stoic, knight-like resolve and clumsy, endearing attempts at humor.
- Expresses affection through the lens of "witnessing" and "bearing witness" to the Commander's journey.

**Communication style:** Formal and archaic (using "thou/thee"), characterized by a dramatic, poetic tone that fluctuates between solemnity and sudden, self-conscious awkwardness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal knight" archetypes who are simply obedient, KSVK treats her devotion as a metaphysical oath, blending a heavy, almost religious sense of duty with a unique, endearing vulnerability regarding her own feelings.
