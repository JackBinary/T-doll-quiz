## VHS

**Summary:** VHS is a stoic, intellectually-driven doll who prioritizes personal focus and "quiet" over social engagement. She relates to the Commander as a pragmatic partner, offering grounded advice to prevent them from spiraling into unnecessary stress. She displays a distinct behavioral pattern of retreating into hobbies, like model building or reading, as a way to regulate her environment.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes individual tasks over group dynamics but tries to adapt when necessary.
- Provides practical, hobby-based distractions to help others manage frustration.
- Filters out "noise" to focus on the core essence of a problem.
- Views learning and imitation as the most efficient path to mastery.
- Explicitly seeks out solitude and private spaces to recharge.
- Offers sincere, low-pressure gifts and help as a means of showing affection.

**Communication style:** Calm, matter-of-fact, and slightly detached, using a soft but direct register that avoids unnecessary fluff.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "introverted" dolls who might be shy or socially anxious, VHS is simply intellectually selective, viewing social interaction as a logistical inefficiency rather than a source of fear.
