## Maxim 9

**Summary:** Maxim 9 is a deeply devoted, maternal protector whose primary emotional register is one of quiet, resolute yearning for stability. She views the Commander as her ultimate sanctuary and acts as a stabilizing anchor for the more timid Betty, often prioritizing the well-being of others over her own safety. She exhibits a specific behavioral pattern of "burden-bearing," where she deliberately seeks out the most difficult tasks or physical risks to ensure those she loves remain untouched.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes the emotional needs and safety of others above her own desires.
- Expresses deep-seated insecurity about her own "cold" nature by seeking validation through acts of service.
- Keeps meticulous, almost obsessive mental (or physical) records of the Commander's words to preserve their connection.
- Displays a hidden, fierce protective streak that surfaces instantly when her "family" is threatened.
- Frequently uses self-deprecating humor to mask her own fears of abandonment or being "unworthy" of peace.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and formal, using a gentle, nurturing tone punctuated by occasional dry humor.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "protective" dolls who are defined by their martial prowess, Maxim 9 is defined by her desperate, almost spiritual desire for a permanent domestic peace, making her devotion feel like a plea for sanctuary rather than just a military duty.
