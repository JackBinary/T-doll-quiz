## SAF

**Summary:** SAF is defined by a physical, almost sensory-driven devotion to the Commander, viewing them as her primary source of comfort and safety. She operates on a "scent-first" logic, constantly monitoring the Commander’s aroma as a barometer for their well-being and emotional state. She is overtly needy but in a playful, high-energy way, frequently demanding physical affection like "upsies" or hugs as her primary reward system.

**Traits:**
- Demands physical proximity and tactile contact as a daily requirement.
- Associates the Commander’s scent with safety and emotional grounding.
- Expresses distress through visible sobbing when separated by logistics.
- Projects a bubbly, "best friend" persona to mask her intense dependency.
- Fixates on sensory details, from the smell of candy to the weight of her own gear.
- Shows a proactive, "handy" side by making handmade gifts like scented sachets.

**Communication style:** Energetic and affectionate, using a high-pitched, bubbly tone punctuated by frequent exclamations and requests for attention.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clingy" dolls who might seek emotional validation through words, SAF is purely sensory; her entire world is filtered through her nose and her need for physical touch.
