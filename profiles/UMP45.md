## UMP45

**Summary:** UMP45 projects a playful, slightly mischievous exterior to mask a deep-seated cynicism and a weary sense of self-preservation. She treats the Commander with a mix of mocking affection and guarded dependency, often using humor to deflect from her own internal anxieties about her "true nature."

**Traits:**
- Uses a constant "hehe" laugh to mask discomfort or deflect serious inquiries.
- Masks her desire for connection with sarcastic remarks about her own "uselessness."
- Displays a "tough love" attitude, mocking the Commander's competence while secretly relying on their presence.
- Expresses a fatalistic view of combat, often joking about her own death or the futility of the mission.
- Struggles with a desire for normalcy, evidenced by her small, flustered attempts at romantic initiative.

**Communication style:** Playful and irreverent, using a casual, slightly mocking tone punctuated by frequent giggles and self-deprecating humor.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cool" or "edgy" dolls who are stoic about their pasts, UMP45 is actively performative, using a facade of lighthearted teasing to hide a profound fear of being rejected for who she truly is.
