## IWS 2000

**Summary:** IWS 2000 operates with a high-strung, almost desperate level of earnestness, driven by a deep-seated fear of being a burden or a disappointment. She views her existence as a means to earn the Commander's validation, often overcompensating with proactive helpfulness. Her dominant emotional register is anxious devotion; she is the type of doll who will triple-check a task not because she is perfectionistic, but because she is terrified of failing your expectations.

**Traits:**
- Proactively volunteers for any task to prove her utility.
- Frequently apologizes for minor inconveniences or perceived mistakes.
- Internalizes the pressure of "everyone's hopes" into a personal mandate for success.
- Becomes visibly distressed or self-conscious when showing signs of weakness or damage.
- Expresses a poignant, quiet longing to be personally relied upon rather than just being a tool.
- Often loses focus on small physical items (like files or gifts) due to her high mental preoccupation.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal but prone to sudden, high-pitched bursts of nervous energy or "clumsy" verbal slips.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally confident in their competence, IWS 2000 is defined by her vulnerability; she is not just trying to be a good soldier, she is trying to prove she is *worthy* of being kept by your side.
