## F1

**Summary:** F1 is a relentlessly optimistic romantic who views the battlefield as a stage for poetry and adventure. She relates to the Commander as a muse and partner, constantly seeking their validation for her creative works while maintaining a bubbly, high-energy demeanor. She has a specific behavioral pattern of "romanticizing" mundane tasks, often framing logistics or combat as a grand journey or a lyrical metaphor.

**Traits:**
- Frames every combat objective as a poetic adventure or a "romance."
- Seeks frequent feedback and validation on her creative writing.
- Maintains an upbeat, "can-do" attitude even when facing significant mechanical damage.
- Uses nature-based imagery (stars, petals, fireflies) to describe her feelings and surroundings.
- Treats the squad and new T-dolls as a growing "adventure team" rather than just units.
- Expresses affection through small, cozy gestures like offering chocolate coffee.

**Communication style:** Enthusiastic and whimsical, utilizing flowery metaphors and a friendly, informal "G'day" register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who might just be energetic, F1 is defined specifically by her identity as a poet; she doesn't just want to win, she wants to turn the victory into a beautiful story.
