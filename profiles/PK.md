## PK

**Summary:** PK operates with a stoic, "lone wolf" exterior that masks a deeply protective sense of duty toward the Commander. She maintains a prickly, professional distance as a defense mechanism, yet she actively seeks out shared secrets and private moments of companionship. Her dominant emotional register is one of guarded sincerity; she expresses affection through acts of competence rather than words.

**Traits:**
- Establishes strict physical boundaries while simultaneously demanding the Commander's presence.
- Treats shared experiences as "secrets" to be guarded against the rest of the world.
- Expresses frustration through blunt, short interjections like "What?" or "Hmph."
- Analyzes her own failures with a pragmatic, growth-oriented mindset.
- Displays a cynical view of holidays, preferring personal milestones of strength over public celebrations.
- Projects an aura of "overpowered" confidence that borders on arrogance when facing weaker foes.

**Communication style:** Her speech is clipped, direct, and borderline abrasive, utilizing a "tough-soldier" register that softens only in private, conspiratorial moments.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cool" or "stoic" dolls who are content with mere professional distance, PK actively seeks to build a private, exclusive bond with the Commander that exists entirely outside of their official duties.
