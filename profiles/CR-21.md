## CR-21

**Summary:** CR-21 is a high-energy "drill sergeant" archetype who views physical fitness as a moral imperative and a primary means of bonding. She relates to the Commander as a student in need of discipline, often oscillating between playful teasing about their habits and a fierce, sincere commitment to their personal growth. She displays a persistent habit of "monitoring" the Commander’s daily routine, specifically checking in on their exercise progress and diet.

**Traits:**
- Proactively identifies the Commander’s physical "weaknesses" to justify her coaching.
- Channels her enthusiasm into structured, high-intensity training regimes.
- Uses light-hearted mockery as a tool to keep the Commander motivated.
- Displays a "tough love" attitude that masks a deep desire to be trusted.
- Gets genuinely flustered or embarrassed when her own physical limits are tested.
- Treats every combat engagement as a practical outdoor training session.

**Communication style:** Energetic, assertive, and highly informal, often using exclamations and a "coach-like" instructional tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other fitness-oriented dolls who might focus on aesthetics or general health, CR-21 is defined by her specific obsession with the Commander's *personal* progress and her tendency to treat their relationship as a rigorous, long-term athletic partnership.
