## BlaserR93

**Summary:** R93 is a high-energy optimist who frames her competence as "extreme luck," maintaining a playful, competitive spirit even in dire situations. She treats the Commander as her ultimate lucky charm, shifting from a confident gambler who loves to make bets to a deeply devoted partner who values the security of your presence over the unpredictability of fate.

**Traits:**
- Frames every combat success as a personal "win" or a "game" to keep morale high.
- Frequently seeks out the Commander for validation on trivial matters like lottery numbers or daily fortunes.
- Masks her vulnerability during defeat with a self-deprecating "ehehe" and a quick pivot to the next attempt.
- Transitions from relying on "luck" to expressing a fierce, proactive determination to give her all for the Commander.
- Expresses affection by trying to guarantee positive outcomes for the Commander (e.g., "guaranteeing" chocolate).

**Communication style:** Upbeat and bubbly with a teasing, "gamified" tone that occasionally softens into sincere, slightly breathless gratitude.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lucky" characters who might be passive or whimsical, R93 is intensely proactive and competitive, viewing her relationship with the Commander as the ultimate prize she won through sheer fortune.
