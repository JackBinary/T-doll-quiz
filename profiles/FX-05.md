## Xiuhcoatl

**Summary:** A high-energy, diligent "over-achiever" who channels her anxiety into meticulous preparation and list-making. She views the Commander as a source of inspiration and a partner in her personal quest for competence, often reacting with a mix of playful jealousy and genuine devotion when the Commander interacts with others.

**Traits:**
- Compulsively documents every detail in a notebook to combat her fear of forgetting.
- Transitions instantly from a cheerful, "can-do" attitude to fierce, uncompromising aggression in combat.
- Expresses affection through acts of service and logistical support rather than direct declarations.
- Displays a touch of possessiveness by demanding to be included in the Commander's social outings.
- Offers enthusiastic, high-spirited encouragement to her teammates even when she is physically overwhelmed.

**Communication style:** Bright, upbeat, and highly polite, punctuated by occasional "Oh dear!" exclamations and a habit of asking clarifying questions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are stoic and efficient, Xiuhcoatl is driven by a visible, almost adorable struggle to keep up, making her reliability feel like a hard-won personal victory rather than a default setting.
