## Vepr

**Summary:** Vepr is a earnest, high-energy enthusiast whose primary emotional register is one of wholesome, almost obsessive dedication to wildlife conservation. She views the Commander as a trusted partner in her "wild boar conservation organization," frequently attempting to pull them into her niche hobbies with a mix of bashful hesitation and genuine excitement. She operates with a "do-it-all" attitude, balancing her role as a capable soldier with a meticulous need to document and preserve nature.

**Traits:**
- Treats wildlife conservation as a high-priority official duty.
- Hesitantly asks for the Commander's company on outdoor excursions.
- Meticulously collects "image materials" and data for her private folders.
- Over-focuses on aesthetic details (like shaping chocolates) to the point of self-consciousness.
- Expresses a protective, almost maternal instinct toward nature.
- Maintains a rigid sense of duty regarding mission logistics and supply procurement.

**Communication style:** Polished and respectful, yet prone to becoming breathless and earnest when the topic of animals is broached.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "nature-loving" dolls who might be serene or aloof, Vepr is defined by her active, organizational zeal, she doesn't just watch nature; she wants to build a formal movement to save it.
