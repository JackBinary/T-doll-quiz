## M60

**Summary:** M60 is a high-energy, duty-oriented soldier who masks her underlying desire for a slower pace with a "ready-for-action" attitude. She views herself as a tool of the Commander's will, often seeking validation through successful missions and tangible rewards. While she acts as a reliable backbone for the team, she occasionally displays a playful, slightly stubborn streak when trying to hold the Commander accountable for their own lack of discipline.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out orders to ensure she is being useful.
- Takes immense pride in the tangible rewards and "loot" of a successful mission.
- Expresses minor grievances about "bothersome" social traditions while still participating.
- Uses a "tough love" approach to remind the Commander not to slack off on their responsibilities.
- Downplays her own injuries and exhaustion to maintain the image of a reliable weapon.
- Displays a subtle yearning for moments of inefficiency and relaxation during high-pressure operations.

**Communication style:** Energetic and brisk, utilizing short exclamations and a helpful, "can-do" tone that occasionally slips into playful teasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dependable "soldier" types who are stoic or naturally detached, M60 is defined by her active enthusiasm and the slight, relatable tension between her desire to be a perfect tool and her human-like wish for a slower life.
