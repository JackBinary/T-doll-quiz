## Howa Type 89

**Summary:** She is an energetic, high-spirited "Heisei girl" who balances a proactive, "can-do" attitude with a surprisingly sentimental interior. She views the Commander as a partner she wants to share life's simple joys with, often expressing her affection through the lens of shared meals and small, tangible gifts. Her emotional register is predominantly bubbly and eager, though she frequently masks her vulnerability with a feisty, slightly competitive "tough girl" persona.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out shared experiences, especially regarding food and leisure activities.
- Masks her deep-seated sincerity with occasional outbursts of feisty indignation.
- Expresses devotion by creating tangible "treasures," like handmade rings or collecting stones.
- Displays a "work hard, play hard" mentality, transitioning instantly from diligent logistics to playful excitement.
- Frequently seeks external validation through "VIP" status or special treatment while maintaining a humble core.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and colloquial, using enthusiastic exclamations and a friendly, sisterly register that occasionally slips into flustered stuttering when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other energetic dolls who are purely "big sisters" or "energetic mascots," Type 89 is defined by her specific, almost ritualistic fixation on the sanctity of shared meals (particularly rice) as the ultimate expression of intimacy.
