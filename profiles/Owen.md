## Owen

**Summary:** Owen is an earnest, slightly self-conscious enthusiast who views life through a viewfinder, driven by a deep-seated desire to preserve fleeting moments. She relates to the Commander as her most "precious" subject, balancing a professional "pro" persona with a vulnerable fear of being judged for her humble equipment. Her dominant emotional register is one of wide-eyed, persistent optimism, often manifesting in her frantic need to document every holiday and milestone.

**Traits:**
- Expresses deep insecurity about her low-end gear compared to others.
- Frequently seeks validation for her "pro" skills despite her visible nervousness.
- Prioritizes documentation of the "small things," like mountain scenery or coworkers' costumes.
- Displays a high-energy, "do-it-all" attitude during special events (like staying awake for Christmas).
- Worries about the physical safety of her camera more than her own personal safety in combat.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, using a mix of professional reporting language and bubbly, enthusiastic exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "support" types who focus on the logistics of the team, Owen is motivated by the preservation of sentiment, making her more of a chronicler of history than a mere observer of it.
