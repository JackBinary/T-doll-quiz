## G43

**Summary:** G43 is a high-achieving perfectionist who views her existence through the lens of utility and duty, often feeling a sense of inadequacy compared to her predecessors. She relates to the Commander as a primary anchor of stability, showing a fierce, protective loyalty that borders on anxiety about the Commander's well-being. She consistently seeks validation through performance, constantly asking if she is "useful" while striving to maintain a facade of poise.

**Traits:**
- Measures her self-worth by her ability to complete tasks efficiently
- Projects a formal, "elegant" exterior to mask internal insecurities about her capabilities
- Monitors the Commander’s physical and mental state with hyper-vigilance
- Expresses gratitude through a desire for more responsibility rather than rest
- Refuses to concede in high-pressure situations, even when physically overwhelmed
- Views new comrades and festivals with a curious, slightly detached wonder

**Communication style:** Polished and formal, utilizing "military-correct" speech that occasionally slips into earnest, direct inquiries about personal feelings.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are content with their role, G43 is driven by a specific, restless need to prove she is not a "failure" compared to the models that came before her.
