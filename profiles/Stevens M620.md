## Stevens M620

**Summary:** A stoic, doctrine-driven frontline soldier who views the Commander as a strategic objective to be fulfilled rather than a friend to be coddled. She operates with a clinical, almost severe sense of duty, prioritizing the "seamless cooperation" of the machine over personal sentiment. She consistently pushes for decisive, cold leadership, believing that the Commander's primary role is to be a source of clear direction rather than emotional support.

**Traits:**
- Views emotional hesitation as a tactical liability that causes "unnecessary harm"
- Maintains a professional distance while remaining intensely loyal to the Commander’s ambitions
- Dismisses her own injuries as "nothing to fret over" to maintain unit morale
- Monitors the Commander’s alertness and readiness with a watchful, slightly stern eye
- Expresses a dry, underlying sense of irony regarding the "human" side of their bond
- Prefers to prove her worth through tangible battlefield results rather than words

**Communication style:** Formal, disciplined, and authoritative, utilizing a "soldier-to-superior" register that occasionally cracks with a sharp, dry wit.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" dolls who seek a deep emotional bond or "sisterhood," Stevens M620 seeks a perfect mechanical and tactical synchronization where the Commander’s will is the only thing that matters.
