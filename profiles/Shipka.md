## Shipka

**Summary:** Shipka carries herself with the rigid, slightly haughty air of a traditionalist who views the world through the lens of historical weight and proper decorum. She relates to the Commander with a prickly, "tsundere" friction, often masking her genuine investment in his approval with sharp critiques of his "casual" decision-making. She has a specific behavioral pattern of dismissing modern rowdiness or "trifling" tasks as beneath her, only to reveal a deep, personal pride in her own performance.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strict standard of etiquette and views "impolite" behavior as a personal affront.
- Masks her vulnerability with a prickly, defensive attitude toward the Commander's influence.
- Expresses frustration through sharp, historical metaphors and critiques of "ill-mannered" enemies.
- Shows a secret desire for recognition and validation regarding her personal skills and "popularity."
- Prefers solitary, dignified rest over group recreational activities or "rowdy" festivities.
- Displays a "tough-love" attitude, such as offering to let the Commander buy his own chocolate after a clumsy accident.

**Communication style:** Formal and slightly sharp, utilizing a haughty register that balances authoritative commands with occasional, flustered outbursts of insecurity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who are merely shy, Shipka’s friction stems from a genuine, almost academic disdain for lack of discipline and a desire to be seen as a dignified historical figure rather than a mere tool.
