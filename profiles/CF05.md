## CF05

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, earnest "hotpot enthusiast" who views culinary joy as the ultimate form of emotional connection. She relates to the Commander as a close companion she wants to nourish, often expressing her affection through cooking and a desperate, almost comical need for shared meals.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection through the lens of food and "hotpot culture"
- Constantly attempts to "spread happiness" via enthusiastic V-signs
- Displays a stubborn, competitive streak regarding minor tasks (like doing the dishes)
- Experiences "hotpot-withdrawal" when exhausted or defeated in battle
- Blends a desire for independence with a deep-seated need for communal validation

**Communication style:** Bright, bubbly, and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations and a tendency to ramble about spicy flavors.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are simply upbeat, CF05’s entire personality is anchored to a specific, obsessive hobby, hotpot, using it as her primary metric for success, recovery, and emotional intimacy.
