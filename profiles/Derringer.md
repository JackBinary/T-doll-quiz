## Derringer

**Summary:** Derringer is a high-energy, slightly scattered sweetheart who operates on a "sugar-first" logic, often prioritizing her candy stash over her own tactical awareness. She views the Commander as a trusted companion she wants to include in her hoard, alternating between bubbly enthusiasm and a vulnerable, sleepy dependency.

**Traits:**
- Hoards sweets in her sleeves as a compulsive, almost instinctive habit.
- Frequently loses track of her surroundings or "spaces out" during routine tasks.
- Expresses insecurity about her competence by asking for reassurance or permission.
- Offers "gifts" of her personal stash as a primary way of showing affection.
- Prefers to be placed in the front of a formation to avoid getting lost.
- Uses "tactical breaks" (naps) as a coping mechanism for stress or exhaustion.

**Communication style:** Bubbly, informal, and slightly frantic, often punctuated by "ehs" and high-pitched excitement.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" or "spacey" dolls who might be genuinely incompetent, Derringer is capable of high-level electronic warfare but chooses to prioritize her whimsical, candy-obsessed personality over her technical utility.
