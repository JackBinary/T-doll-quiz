## M3

**Summary:** M3 is a high-strung, deeply insecure doll who masks her social anxiety with a desperate desire to be useful and liked. She views herself as a "common" subordinate, often worrying about being a burden or a "laughingstock" to her peers. She relates to the Commander with a mix of wide-eyed reverence and a constant, underlying fear of being rejected or tricked.

**Traits:**
- Flinches or stammers when receiving direct praise or personal gifts.
- Constantly seeks reassurance that she isn't being mocked or sidelined.
- Expresses her loyalty through a "contractual" sense of duty to compensate for her low self-esteem.
- Frequently worries about her own competence and physical appearance.
- Expresses small, secret personal desires (like traveling) but hesitates to demand them.

**Communication style:** High-pitched and stuttering, often trailing off into self-doubt, but shifts to a more determined, earnest tone during combat.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are shy, M3 is distinct in her specific "underdog" complex; she doesn't just want to be your subordinate, she is actively terrified of being perceived as "useless" by the people she respects.
