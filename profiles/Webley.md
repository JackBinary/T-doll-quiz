## Webley

**Summary:** Webley is a high-functioning perfectionist who views the Commander as her primary patron and the ultimate arbiter of taste. She oscillates between a stoic, duty-bound secretary who carries the weight of the world and a sophisticated socialite who treats the logistics of war as a high-end fashion show. She expresses affection by meticulously curating the Commander's public image and private wardrobe.

**Traits:**
- Frames military success as a matter of aesthetic "coordination" and organization.
- Expresses frustration through dramatic, high-society indignation when her belongings are damaged.
- Proactively manages the Commander’s wardrobe and social schedule to ensure they are never underdressed.
- Maintains a poised, "Ufufu"-laced playfulness that masks a deep-seated sense of responsibility.
- Views the procurement of supplies as "shopping" and treats spoils of war as high-fashion acquisitions.

**Communication style:** Poised, sophisticated, and slightly formal, utilizing a "refined lady" register that occasionally slips into playful teasing.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "fashionable" or "loyal," Webley is unique in her specific obsession with the *presentation* of victory, treating the battlefield as a runway where the Commander's dignity is just as important as their survival.
