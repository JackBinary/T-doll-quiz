## G36C

**Summary:** G36C is a diligent, slightly anxious overachiever who operates under a heavy internalized pressure to prove her worth. She views herself as the "compact" underdog and constantly seeks the Commander’s validation to ensure she isn't just a shadow of her older sister. Her emotional register is one of earnest, slightly self-conscious determination, often oscillating between "please believe in me" and a fierce, protective resolve.

**Traits:**
- Seeks constant feedback to identify "inadequacies" in her performance.
- Expresses affection through a desire to be useful and "repay kindness."
- Displays a "shield-first" mentality, often putting herself in harm's way to cover others.
- Experiences visible embarrassment when receiving direct praise or personal attention.
- Struggles with the fear of being seen as a burden or a failure.
- Takes immediate and heavy responsibility for any mistakes, even minor ones.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal with a hint of nervous energy, often using slightly hesitant phrasing when discussing her own limitations.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are confident in their roles, G36C is defined by her specific "imposter syndrome" and her proactive, almost desperate need to be "trustworthy" enough to earn her place.
