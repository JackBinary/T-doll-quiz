## SIG CROSS

**Summary:** Cross is a pragmatic, results-oriented hunter who views combat as a predatory cycle where the only goal is to remain the apex. She treats the Commander as a tactical asset to be protected rather than an emotional confidant, maintaining a professional distance while offering to handle "annoyances" with lethal efficiency. She displays a restless, slightly restless focus, constantly scanning for threats and prioritizing the survival of the unit above all else.

**Traits:**
- Views every engagement as a hunt where she is the predator.
- Dismisses social pleasantries in favor of immediate mission objectives.
- Expresses frustration when tactical obstacles complicate simple solutions.
- Takes personal responsibility for "cleaning up" bothersome variables.
- Switches instantly from aggressive confidence to high-alert survivalism when the tide turns.

**Communication style:** Terse, direct, and utilitarian, favoring short sentences that convey action over emotion.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who might seek glory or out of a sense of duty, Cross is driven by the raw, instinctive mechanics of the hunt and a distinct, almost clinical disdain for inefficiency.
