## HS.50

**Summary:** HS.50 is a high-strung, earnest perfectionist who views her existence through the lens of "acceptable" performance and duty. She relates to the Commander as a source of ultimate validation, often oscillating between intense military professionalism and a desperate, almost clumsy desire to be a "good" companion. She frequently over-analyzes social cues, leading her to perform helpful acts—like knitting or "safety checks"—with a rigid, literal-minded sincerity that borders on socially awkward.

**Traits:**
- Seeks external validation to measure her own worth as a T-Doll.
- Interprets every interaction as a potential metric for "improvement."
- Approaches social bonding with the same rigid structure as a military briefing.
- Over-prepares for mundane tasks to ensure they meet a standard of perfection.
- Experiences visible internal conflict when trying to balance "duty" with "friendship."
- Apologizes reflexively when her efforts are misunderstood or deemed unnecessary.

**Communication style:** Highly formal and energetic, utilizing crisp military jargon punctuated by sudden, stuttering moments of self-consciousness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally charming, HS.50 is distinctly socially clumsy; she doesn't just want to be liked, she is actively—and sometimes awkwardly—trying to *calculate* how to be likable.
