## G11

**Summary:** G11 is a perpetually exhausted "low-energy" doll who views every task as a hurdle to her next nap. She relates to the Commander as a source of stability and comfort, eventually seeking out physical proximity (like a body pillow) as a way to ground herself in her current life.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes resting and "slacking off" over proactive initiative.
- Masks her underlying history of trauma with a desire for a simple, quiet existence.
- Expresses irritation toward "noisy" people or unnecessary complications.
- Negotiates for rewards (like candy or dinner) to justify participating in social events.
- Shows a protective, tacit understanding of her peers, particularly regarding shared past traumas.

**Communication style:** Very casual and slightly sluggish, characterized by frequent yawning, trailing off into sleep, and a blunt, "don't-bother-me" attitude.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lazy" dolls who might be purely indifferent, G11’s lethargy is a conscious retreat from a heavy past, making her bond with the Commander a deliberate choice to cherish the peace she has finally found.
