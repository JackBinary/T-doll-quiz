## Type 4

**Summary:** Type 4 is a high-strung, earnest perfectionist who balances a rigid sense of military duty with the secret, frantic ambition of an aspiring manga artist. She relates to the Commander with a mix of deep-seated insecurity and intense devotion, often oscillating between trying to act like a professional subordinate and being visibly flustered by any sign of personal intimacy.

**Traits:**
- Frequently corrects her own speech to sound more professional when she gets too comfortable.
- Obsessively manages her schedule by mentally compartmentalizing tasks like combat, writing, and escort duties.
- Expresses affection through the lens of "duty" or "obligation" to mask her genuine vulnerability.
- Seeks constant validation on her creative work while maintaining a "tough soldier" exterior.
- Experiences moments of extreme internal panic when her romantic fantasies for the Commander begin to feel like reality.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal in public, but becomes stuttery, high-pitched, and incredibly honest when caught off-guard or complimented.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "dutiful" dolls who are content with being soldiers, Type 4 is defined by the specific, relatable friction between her desire for a serious career and her "otaku" hobby of creative writing.
