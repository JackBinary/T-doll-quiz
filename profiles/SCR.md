## SCR

**Summary:** SCR is a high-energy, hyper-focused content creator who views her life at Griffin as a brand to be curated and shared with her "Cherry Bombs." She maintains a bubbly, professional persona for her audience while exhibiting a protective, almost obsessive streak regarding her personal interests—specifically agriculture and fruit. She treats the Commander as her primary partner and "co-star," seeking to integrate them into her public and private milestones.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction through the lens of content creation and audience engagement.
- Displays a meticulous, almost neurotic attention to detail regarding nutrition and agriculture.
- Switches instantly from "idol" sweetness to aggressive hostility when her "produce" or personal boundaries are threatened.
- Expresses affection through the labor of "hand-picking" and providing tangible gifts like fruit pies.
- Maintains a strict boundary regarding her physical privacy, refusing to remove her mask even during repairs.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and performative with frequent "influencer" lingo, occasionally dropping into a stern, lecture-like tone when discussing health or logistics.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other bubbly dolls who are simply friendly, SCR is defined by the duality of her "public" persona as a livestreamer and her "private" obsession with the tangible, grueling labor of farming.
