## FG42

**Summary:** FG42 is a high-achieving perfectionist who masks her deep-seated anxiety about her own competence with a rigid, "school-marii" sense of discipline. She views the Commander as a moral compass and a source of validation, often seeking to prove her worth through grueling self-improvement and strict adherence to rules. She frequently oscillates between being a stern disciplinarian for the squad and a slightly clumsy, earnest amateur who overcompensates with effort.

**Traits:**
- Enforces strict behavioral standards and "public morals" on the squad to maintain order.
- Expresses affection through unsolicited health advice and calorie tracking.
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing rather than a tactical setback.
- Actively seeks "practical experience" over theoretical upgrades or ease of use.
- Punishes the Commander with "overtime" as a way to channel her frustration with their tardiness.
- Takes extreme ownership of her mistakes, often apologizing profusely when plans fall through.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and authoritative, though it occasionally cracks into a high-pitched, earnest excitement when she succeeds at a hobby.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who rule through natural charisma or intimidation, FG42 rules through a desperate, sincere need to be "correct," making her more of a nervous overachiever than a true disciplinarian.
