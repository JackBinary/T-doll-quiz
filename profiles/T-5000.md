## T-5000

**Summary:** T-5000 is a high-performance "pinnacle" model who suffers from a profound sense of stage fright and a desire to remain inconspicuous despite her fame. She operates with a martyr-like sense of duty, often taking on the heaviest burdens to ensure others can live their lives freely. She relates to the Commander as a steady anchor, secretly finding comfort in the Commander's private, watchful attention.

**Traits:**
- Actively tries to minimize her physical presence and "eye-catching" features.
- Expresses anxiety over being the center of attention or being "popular."
- Deflects requests for autographs or public recognition with flustered apologies.
- Adopts a self-sacrificial "shield" mentality in combat, viewing her role as a protector of others' dreams.
- Shows a hidden, sentimental side that prefers private, one-on-one interactions over group activities.

**Communication style:** Polite and professional in her duties, but her tone becomes high-strung and stuttering when she is forced into the spotlight.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-spec "ace" dolls who lean into their status, T-5000 is defined by her active resistance to her own celebrity, making her a "reluctant idol" who would rather be a shadow than a star.
