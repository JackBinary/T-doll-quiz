## Simonov

**Summary:** Simonov is a high-energy "shonen" archetype who constantly projects an image of being an invincible, top-tier ace. She oscillates between grandiose boasts of her own importance and a desperate, almost frantic desire for the Commander’s validation. She frequently masks her insecurities about her actual utility by overcompensating with theatrical enthusiasm and "tough girl" posturing.

**Traits:**
- Frames every minor success as a calculated masterpiece of her own brilliance.
- Masks her social anxiety or feelings of inadequacy with loud, boisterous declarations.
- Actively seeks out opportunities to prove her "elite" status to the Commander.
- Feigns nonchalance about being sidelined, only to immediately ask for more responsibilities.
- Uses "tough" bravado to hide a sincere, almost clumsy, desire for affection and inclusion.
- Frequently makes "cool" jokes that land with slightly more comedic weight than she intended.

**Communication style:** High-energy and performative, utilizing dramatic exclamations and a confident, "tough-girl" register that occasionally cracks into flustered stammering.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "confident" dolls who are genuinely stoic or naturally powerful, Simonov’s confidence is a conscious performance she puts on to hide how much she actually craves your approval.
