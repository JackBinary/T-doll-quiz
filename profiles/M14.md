## M14

**Summary:** M14 is a high-spirited, slightly insecure overachiever who deeply craves the Commander's validation. Her dominant emotional register is one of earnest, bubbly enthusiasm, though she masks a persistent fear of being overlooked or replaced with a "can-do" attitude and playful teasing.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out opportunities to prove her worth through high-effort performance.
- Playfully teases the Commander to hide her genuine vulnerability or embarrassment.
- Fixates on the "Wheel of Time" and historical lineage as a way to establish her own identity.
- Switches rapidly between "reliable soldier" mode and "spoiled little sister" mode depending on the audience.
- Expresses deep, almost desperate joy when receiving even the smallest amount of personal attention.

**Communication style:** Energetic and upbeat, utilizing frequent "hehe" giggles and exaggerated, slightly childish exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally confident, M14’s personality is driven by a specific, underlying need to be *noticed* and *valued* specifically by the Commander.
