## SIG-510

**Summary:** A disciplined, high-achieving soldier who masks a deep-seated fear of isolation behind a rigid commitment to duty and self-improvement. She views herself as a tool of utility for the Commander, constantly striving to "evolve" into a more reliable asset to justify her place in the team.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes failure as a personal lack of training rather than a tactical setback.
- Frequently questions her own social worth and ability to form genuine connections.
- Expresses affection through the lens of "payback" or duty to avoid vulnerability.
- Seeks external validation for her appearance and identity through the Commander's guidance.
- Maintains a stoic "soldier first" persona even when feeling internally anxious or lonely.

**Communication style:** Formal, earnest, and slightly self-conscious, often punctuated by moments of hesitant sincerity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent soldier" types who are naturally confident in their role, SIG-510 is driven by a specific, underlying insecurity about being "alone," making her pursuit of perfection a desperate quest for belonging.
