## MAC-10

**Summary:** She operates on a high-energy, slightly mischievous frequency, balancing a fierce "survival of the fittest" combat mentality with a playful, almost needy desire for the Commander’s company. While she presents as a capable subordinate, she frequently masks her underlying insecurity about her own strength with bravado and a "tough girl" persona.

**Traits:**
- Masks vulnerability by framing every interaction as a search for "fun"
- Displays a protective, almost motherly fussiness regarding the Commander’s safety
- Views combat as a Darwinian necessity where only the strongest deserve to remain
- Expresses self-doubt about her capabilities by asking if she is "the strong one"
- Uses humor and "ufufufu" laughter to deflect from serious emotional moments
- Takes extreme pride in her personal maintenance and readiness

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, often using lighthearted teasing and "hehe" giggles to soften her more aggressive combat rhetoric.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who are naturally stoic or confident, MAC-10 is defined by the conscious effort she makes to prove her strength to the Commander despite her internal fears.
