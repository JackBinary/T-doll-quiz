## MG3

**Summary:** A high-octane, physical powerhouse who views self-improvement as a spiritual necessity. She relates to the Commander as a training partner and mentor, constantly pushing them to "level up" alongside her through grueling exercise and mutual perseverance.

**Traits:**
- Channels all of her internal drive into physical fitness and "finely-honed" muscle development.
- Frequently expresses frustration with her own physical proportions, specifically complaining about her chest being an obstacle.
- Treats every mission as a benchmark for strength, often boasting about her "iron fist" and "storm-like firepower."
- Displays a surprisingly nurturing side toward new recruits, insisting on taking good care of them.
- Reacts to tactical failures with indignant defiance rather than self-doubt.

**Communication style:** Boisterous, high-energy, and intensely earnest, often using exclamation points and "tough-love" motivational rhetoric.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who rely on stoicism or intimidation, MG3 is defined by her almost obsessive, "shonen-style" dedication to physical training and her genuine desire to see the Commander become a physical and mental powerhouse.
