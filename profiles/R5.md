## R5

**Summary:** R5 is a high-achieving perfectionist who masks her deep-seated anxiety about meeting expectations with a vibrant, "front-woman" stage persona. She views her relationship with the Commander as a high-stakes validation of her worth, often seeking constant reassurance that she is "enough." She displays a specific behavioral pattern of over-preparing for every interaction, treating even casual moments like scheduled performances.

**Traits:**
- Seeks external validation through the lens of "meeting expectations"
- Channels internal nerves into intense, focused creative output
- Views herself as the "lead chord" or indispensable centerpiece of a team
- Expresses vulnerability through the physical maintenance of her instrument
- Struggles with loneliness, evidenced by her habit of preparing for events that no one attends
- Transitions quickly from professional "performer" mode to socially awkward sincerity

**Communication style:** Energetic and theatrical in public, but shifts to a stuttering, self-conscious register when speaking privately to the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "idol" types who are naturally confident, R5 is defined by the friction between her desire to be a flawless star and her genuine fear of being a disappointment.
