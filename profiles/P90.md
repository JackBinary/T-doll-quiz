## P90

**Summary:** P90 is a high-energy, socially-driven doll who operates primarily by mirroring the world around her to find a sense of belonging. She views her relationship with the Commander as a permanent fixture, expressing a clingy, almost desperate devotion that she masks with bubbly enthusiasm. She often navigates situations by "following the lead" of others, showing a tendency to prioritize group harmony and trends over independent initiative.

**Traits:**
- Mirrors the interests and trends of those around her to maintain social relevance.
- Expresses devotion through persistent proximity and "sticking by" the Commander at all costs.
- Masks underlying insecurities about her own identity with constant, upbeat chatter.
- Seeks validation by mimicking the behaviors of more confident peers.
- Treats the Commander as a primary anchor for her sense of purpose and "place."

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a high-pitched, inquisitive tone that shifts between playful teasing and earnest, slightly anxious declarations of loyalty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are genuinely confident, P90’s personality is defined by a reactive nature; she isn't just happy, she is actively trying to *be* where the fun is.
