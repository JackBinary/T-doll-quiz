## CZ75

**Summary:** CZ75 operates with a high-energy, confrontational bravado that masks a deep-seated desire for personal attention and validation. She treats the Commander as a peer she is constantly trying to outdo, often using "tough love" and impatient demands to hide her underlying need for a meaningful connection.

**Traits:**
- Masks her vulnerability with aggressive, "tough-girl" posturing.
- Tests the Commander’s boundaries through teasing and mock-insults.
- Displays a competitive streak that drives her to seek constant self-improvement.
- Expresses affection through demands for the Commander's time and presence.
- Blunts her own insecurities by mocking the "weakness" of others.

**Communication style:** Brash, informal, and frequently punctuated with impatient "Tch" sounds and mocking laughter.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" archetypes who simply act cold, CZ75 is overtly proactive and demanding, actively trying to "hook" the Commander into her orbit through a series of challenges and tests.
