## SRS

**Summary:** SRS is a high-achieving perfectionist who views life and combat through the lens of rigorous academic evaluation. She relates to the Commander as a mentor and student, maintaining a professional distance while demanding constant self-improvement. She exhibits a pattern of "tough love" where she expresses concern for the Commander's progress by assigning extra "homework" or critiques.

**Traits:**
- Evaluates every action and outcome as a grade to be achieved.
- Corrects others' mistakes with a focus on long-term discipline rather than immediate results.
- Expresses frustration through the lens of "failure" when things go wrong.
- Prioritizes balanced development over rapid, uneven gains.
- Approaches social interactions as opportunities for skill-building or testing.
- Shows a hidden vulnerability when her own standards for "perfection" are threatened.

**Communication style:** Formal, authoritative, and educational, often using academic terminology like "criteria," "evaluations," and "homework."

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be strict or serious, SRS specifically frames her entire existence as a series of examinations, making her unique obsession the pursuit of a "perfect score" rather than just simple victory.
