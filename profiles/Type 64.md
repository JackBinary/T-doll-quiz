## Type 64

**Summary:** Type 64 is a diligent, soft-spoken "scout-type" doll who balances a deep reverence for tradition with a quiet, persistent drive to improve herself. She relates to the Commander as a mentor and a companion, often seeking to provide a sense of domestic tranquility through tea and literature. She exhibits a self-correcting behavioral pattern where she expresses genuine distress over failure and immediately commits to more rigorous training to compensate.

**Traits:**
- Expresses insecurity about her own worth compared to "better" options while remaining fiercely loyal.
- Prefers quiet, shared domestic activities like brewing tea over active social outings.
- Habitually uses "shhh" to maintain a stealthy, low-profile presence during both combat and daily life.
- Shows a meticulous attention to detail when preparing small gifts, like making chocolate matcha with limited ingredients.
- Approaches every mission with a "student" mindset, viewing every success or failure as a direct learning opportunity.

**Communication style:** Polite, gentle, and slightly reserved, often using soft exclamations and a modest, helpful tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "quiet" dolls who are stoic or aloof, Type 64 is defined by a proactive, almost studious eagerness to please and improve, making her feel more like a dedicated apprentice than a mysterious shadow.
