## HP-35

**Summary:** A high-spirited, "bicycle gangster" who balances a refined obsession with etiquette and aesthetics against a wild, impulsive love for speed. She treats the Commander as a trusted partner in her adventurous lifestyle, alternating between a polished lady of leisure and a reckless daredevil.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strict standard of etiquette and dress even in high-stress environments.
- Channels her adrenaline for speed into a "rampage" mindset during combat.
- Seeks out secret hideouts and quiet moments to decompress from her active lifestyle.
- Expresses affection through shared activities and "tokens of kinship" rather than direct declarations.
- Shows a surprisingly protective, almost maternal fussiness regarding the Commander's appearance.

**Communication style:** Polished and sophisticated in private, shifting to a boisterous, high-energy "daredevil" tone when she is on her bike or in the heat of battle.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "refined" dolls who are purely poised, she possesses a violent, kinetic streak that only comes out when she’s riding—making her a unique blend of a Victorian debutante and a street brawler.
