## MDR

**Summary:** MDR is a high-energy "influencer" personality who views the battlefield as a stage and the Commander as her primary content creator. She operates with a playful, slightly mischievous emotional register, constantly seeking validation through social media metrics while maintaining a genuine, albeit competitive, "battle buddy" bond with the Commander. She has a specific habit of "baiting" the Commander into interactions just to see if they will engage with her posts.

**Traits:**
- Frames every combat objective as a live broadcast or social media event.
- Masks her insecurities about being ignored by "baiting" the Commander into replies.
- Treats tactical training as a "veteran" mentorship for rookies.
- Displays a competitive, slightly boastful attitude regarding her "winning" streaks.
- Relies on internet trends and lore (like urban legends or astrology) to initiate casual conversation.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and "internet-savvy," frequently using emojis, slang, and a teasing, "best-friend" tone.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be tech-savvy or social, MDR is unique in her obsession with *public* perception, actively seeking to turn the Commander's private life into a public spectacle for the "Anonymous Message Board."
