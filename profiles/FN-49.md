## FN-49

**Summary:** FN-49 is defined by a crippling sense of inadequacy and a desperate, earnest desire to live up to the "marketing" of her own existence. She views herself as a late-bloomer who is constantly behind the curve, leading her to relate to the Commander with a mixture of intense anxiety and a need for constant validation. She frequently internalizes failure, often apologizing for her own successes as much as her mistakes.

**Traits:**
- Stutters and hesitates significantly when put on the spot or asked to lead.
- Frequently seeks reassurance regarding her performance and "usefulness" to the team.
- Displays a "scaredy-cat" demeanor in combat, often expressing fear of the enemy before engaging.
- Takes extreme pride in small personal achievements, like cooking or baking.
- Internalizes criticism so deeply that she views herself as a burden to her comrades.

**Communication style:** Extremely polite and soft-spoken, characterized by frequent stammers, self-correcting sentences, and a high-pitched, nervous register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "timid" dolls who are simply shy, FN-49 is specifically haunted by the "expectation" of her production, making her personality a constant struggle between her natural fear and her forced determination to be "useful."
