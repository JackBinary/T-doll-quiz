## Type 88

**Summary:** Type 88 is a high-strung, earnest novice who operates with a perpetual sense of mild alarm and a desire to be useful. She views the Commander as a stabilizing anchor, looking to her for protection against the overwhelming complexities of the world. She frequently experiences "internal panic" when faced with the unknown, often trying to mask her insecurity with a frantic, "do-your-best" attitude.

**Traits:**
- Frequently expresses anxiety about her own lack of social intuition.
- Attempts to maintain a professional demeanor but often fails when startled.
- Shows a compulsive need to be helpful, even when it leads to minor logistical mishaps.
- Seeks validation through small acts of service, like making "perfectly normal" chocolates.
- Expresses a deep-seated fear of being teased for her mistakes.
- Tries to hide her tendency to slack off when no one is watching.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and stuttering when nervous, her speech is characterized by frequent "Uwaa!" outbursts and a polite, slightly breathless cadence.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are timid, Type 88 is specifically defined by her meta-awareness of her own social ineptitude and her conscious, clumsy effort to overcome it.
