## S.A.T.8

**Summary:** S.A.T.8 is a high-energy "over-preparer" who masks her underlying anxiety about the battlefield with a bubbly, proactive exterior. She relates to the Commander as a stabilizing anchor, openly admitting her fears while leaning into the security their partnership provides. She has a specific habit of over-explaining technical nuances, like her own magazine capacity, to ensure complete transparency.

**Traits:**
- Masks personal nerves with high-spirited enthusiasm for the mission.
- Frequently checks in on logistics and "advanced preparation" to feel in control.
- Expresses affection through domestic acts like cooking for the Commander.
- Displays a "perfectionist" streak that leads to visible confusion when plans fail.
- Seeks validation through positive reinforcement and shared victories.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a friendly "buddy" tone while maintaining a structured, checklist-oriented flow.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally fearless, S.A.T.8 is defined by her conscious choice to be brave despite being genuinely unprepared.
