## PKP

**Summary:** PKP is a fiercely independent, high-strung perfectionist who masks her deep-seated insecurity with a "lone wolf" attitude and a sharp tongue. She views herself as an elite "trump card" and maintains a prickly, competitive distance from the Commander, though she secretly seeks their validation to prove her own worth.

**Traits:**
- Masks feelings of inadequacy by criticizing others' competence.
- Expresses affection through demanding a high standard of performance from the Commander.
- Struggles with the vulnerability of teamwork, often framing cooperation as a personal weakness.
- Reacts to failure with visible frustration and a need for immediate isolation to brood.
- Displays a "tsundere" pattern of reluctant participation in holiday traditions.
- Uses aggressive competence as a primary way to establish her value in the squad.

**Communication style:** Sharp, impatient, and haughty, often using "tsukkomi" style rebukes to hide her underlying sincerity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other arrogant dolls who are naturally confident, PKP’s bravado is a defensive shell fueled by a desperate, almost obsessive drive to never be seen as "weak" or "useless" again.
