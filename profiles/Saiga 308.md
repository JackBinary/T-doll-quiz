## Saiga-308

**Summary:** A high-strung, "by-the-book" disciplinarian who views herself as the only thing standing between Griffin and total anarchy. She is deeply defensive about her competence, often oscillating between a rigid, authoritative persona and a flustered, slightly clumsy girl who secretly struggles with her own "sloppy" off-duty habits.

**Traits:**
- Enforces strict rules with a "tough love" attitude while secretly resenting the burden of being the office villain.
- Frequently reacts with visible embarrassment or defensive stuttering when caught being "unproductive" or informal.
- Expresses her affection for the Commander by asking them to be stricter with her so she doesn't have to do all the heavy lifting.
- Targets specific "problem" dolls (like Saiga-12) for constant surveillance and corrective action.
- Maintains a meticulous public image of order while being prone to exhaustion and "secret" napping.

**Communication style:** Brisk, authoritative, and slightly bossy, but prone to cracking into a high-pitched, flustered tone when caught off-guard or complimented.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are naturally stoic or intimidating, Saiga-308 is an "overachiever" who is constantly stressed by her own high standards, making her more of a frantic hall monitor than a cold commander.
