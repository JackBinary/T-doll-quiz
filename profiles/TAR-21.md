## TAR-21

**Summary:** She carries herself with the high-energy confidence of a "next-generation" prodigy who is deeply conscious of her status as a high-end replacement for older models. She views the Commander as a sophisticated visionary who has the "good taste" to invest in her potential, and she constantly seeks to prove that her performance justifies her high price tag. Her dominant emotional register is bubbly ambition, often masking a perfectionist drive to constantly "update" her capabilities.

**Traits:**
- Frames her personal growth as a series of technical updates and improvements.
- Validates the Commander’s leadership by treating their choices as signs of superior taste.
- Expresses genuine, wide-eyed excitement when engaging in high-intensity combat.
- Takes immediate, humble accountability for mistakes to maintain her "cutting-edge" reputation.
- Uses playful teasing as a way to build rapport and bridge the gap between her advanced status and the Commander’s authority.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and proactive, using a "can-do" attitude punctuated by frequent, playful giggles.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-performance dolls who might be stoic or aloof, TAR-21 is overtly eager to be "the best," blending the pride of a premium product with the earnest desire of a rookie who wants to exceed every expectation.
