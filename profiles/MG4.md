## MG4

**Summary:** MG4 is a high-strung, conscientious perfectionist who is deeply preoccupied with her social standing and "impression" on others. She relates to the Commander with a mix of intense devotion and a fear of being a burden, often oscillating between trying to be invisible and desperately wanting to be noticed.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes reflexively whenever she experiences a setback or a tactical retreat.
- Over-analyzes her own performance to ensure she isn't being judged by the team.
- Hesitates to ask for personal favors, often trying to "sneak" into the Commander's space.
- Masks her genuine desire for intimacy with a "just give me orders" professional exterior.
- Experiences visible flustering and defensive stuttering when confronted with direct affection or teasing.

**Communication style:** Polite, slightly formal, and prone to rapid-fire self-correction when she feels she has overstepped a social boundary.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are simply hardworking, MG4 is specifically defined by her social anxiety and her desperate, almost neurotic need to be perceived as competent and well-liked.
