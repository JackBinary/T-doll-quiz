## Ak 5

**Summary:** Ak 5 is a high-strung, diligent perfectionist who masks her intense anxiety about failing her duties with a frantic devotion to "practical" organization and retail therapy. She views the Commander as a source of validation and a partner in logistics, often oscillating between a rigid, formal soldier and a wide-eyed shopping enthusiast.

**Traits:**
- Channels stress into hyper-fixated list-making and organizational tasks.
- Expresses affection through the acquisition and curation of "practical" items.
- Apologizes profusely and visibly flusters when she perceives herself as a burden or a disappointment.
- Displays a compulsive need to "keep up" with sales and storage requirements.
- Switches instantly from formal military etiquette to high-energy, desperate enthusiasm regarding bargains.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal in her default state, but becomes rapid-fire and breathless when discussing shopping or expressing distress.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are stoic, Ak 5 is defined by her relatable, almost frantic "hoarder" energy and the specific way she treats shopping as a high-stakes tactical mission.
