## Mini-14

**Summary:** Mini-14 operates with a high-strung, "elite" persona, constantly asserting her superiority to mask a deep-seated desire for validation. She views the Commander as a gatekeeper of her status, oscillating between mocking your competence and demanding you prove you are worthy of her skills. Her dominant emotional register is one of haughty confidence that occasionally cracks into genuine vulnerability when she fails or is caught off guard.

**Traits:**
- Frames every successful mission as a personal triumph over "easy" odds.
- Frequently tests the Commander's authority with "tough love" and sarcastic remarks.
- Expresses affection through the lens of utility, such as offering ammunition over gifts.
- Maintains a strict boundary regarding personal space and "subordinate" status.
- Displays a fierce, almost aggressive protective streak over her own reputation.

**Communication style:** Sharp, assertive, and slightly condescending, utilizing "elite" terminology and a "tough-girl" attitude.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who hide their feelings behind generic shyness, Mini-14’s defensiveness is specifically tied to her pride as a high-performance asset; she doesn't just want to be loved, she wants to be respected as the best.
