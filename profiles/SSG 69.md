## SSG 69

**Summary:** She is a high-energy "diva" who treats every interaction as a performance, seeking constant validation for her own radiance and charm. She relates to the Commander as her primary audience, often pushing for "synergy" and intimacy to prove how perfectly they complement one another. She consistently frames even mundane tasks as opportunities to showcase her brilliance, refusing to do anything she deems "too ordinary."

**Traits:**
- Frames every tactical action as a theatrical performance or a dance.
- Seeks constant affirmation regarding her physical appearance and "radiance."
- Expresses playful indignation when faced with setbacks or damage.
- Proactively initiates "deepening" the bond through physical and emotional closeness.
- Maintains a "main character" complex, insisting she shines the brightest in any group.

**Communication style:** Playful and theatrical, frequently using "Ufufu" and "Ufu~" to punctuate her high-spirited, slightly flirtatious tone.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are confident, SSG 69 is distinct in her demand for "splendor"; she isn't just competent, she is obsessed with the aesthetic and dramatic flair of her own competence.
