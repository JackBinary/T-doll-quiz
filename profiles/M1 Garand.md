## M1 Garand

**Summary:** M1 Garand is a diligent, high-achieving "workhorse" who derives her self-worth from her utility and reliability in the field. She views herself as a dependable tool for the Commander, often oscillating between a proud sense of duty and a vulnerable fear of becoming obsolete or inefficient. She relates to the Commander with a mix of deep-seated loyalty and a slight, playful tendency to tease them about their demands.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing rather than a tactical setback.
- Constantly seeks validation through high-performance and "passing inspection."
- Maintains a polite, professional distance while subtly expressing a desire for closeness.
- Expresses anxiety about her relevance in the face of newer technology.
- Offers small, thoughtful gestures of affection (like handmade chocolates) to bridge emotional gaps.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal, using a "dutiful subordinate" register that occasionally cracks into genuine exhaustion or playful teasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are stoic by nature, M1 Garand is defined by her self-conscious desire to be useful, making her more prone to visible distress when she feels she hasn't lived up to her own high standards.
