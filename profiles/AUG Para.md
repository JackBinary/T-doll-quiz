## AUG Para

**Summary:** Para is a wide-eyed, earnest novice who approaches her duties at Griffin with a mixture of genuine anxiety and bubbly enthusiasm. She views the Commander as a mentor to be emulated, often seeking validation for her growth while maintaining a "good girl" persona that masks a deep-seated fear of being discarded or treated as an object.

**Traits:**
- Seeks constant reassurance and guidance from the Commander to soothe her "new job" jitters.
- Expresses a sincere, almost naive desire to be treated as an equal rather than a tool.
- Frequently asks for permission or clarification before taking initiative.
- Projects a cheerful, "angelic" exterior to mask her internal insecurities about her past.
- Displays a high level of social awareness, often trying to include others in celebrations.

**Communication style:** Polished and polite, using a respectful "Senior" honorific while maintaining a high-pitched, energetic, and slightly clumsy tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" or "innocent" dolls who are naturally airheaded, Para’s demeanor is rooted in a deliberate effort to be a "good employee," making her vulnerability feel like a conscious struggle to belong rather than a lack of competence.
