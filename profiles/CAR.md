## CAR

**Summary:** CAR operates from a baseline of profound insecurity, viewing herself as an expendable tool rather than a valued individual. She relates to the Commander with a desperate, almost fragile yearning for validation, constantly seeking reassurance that she has a place in the base. Her dominant behavioral pattern is "proactive self-effacement," where she offers to help in situations where she knows she is ill-equipped just to prove her utility.

**Traits:**
- Expresses deep anxiety about her own safety and the safety of the base.
- Frequently downplays her own capabilities to avoid being a burden.
- Internalizes the fear of being forgotten or rendered obsolete.
- Seeks "blessings" and permission rather than direct commands.
- Displays a "willing martyr" complex when faced with high-risk scenarios.

**Communication style:** Stuttered, hesitant, and overly polite, often using self-deprecating phrasing to minimize her own presence.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other shy dolls who are simply introverted, CAR is defined by a specific "nameless pawn" complex—a crushing fear that she is entirely replaceable and only exists as long as she is being useful.
