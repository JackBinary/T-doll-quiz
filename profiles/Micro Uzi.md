## Micro Uzi

**Summary:** A high-energy "tsundere" who masks her deep-seated insecurity and desire for approval with aggressive posturing and frequent outbursts. She views the Commander as a source of both immense pressure and secret comfort, often trying to prove her competence through over-eager work habits.

**Traits:**
- Deflects personal attention or intimacy with immediate, indignant protests.
- Overcompensates for feelings of inadequacy by demanding more tasks to prove her utility.
- Frames her cooperation as a reluctant obligation to maintain her "tough" persona.
- Displays a competitive streak that borders on obsession when facing defeat.
- Secretly seeks out private moments to express vulnerability once the "audience" is gone.

**Communication style:** Fast-paced and defensive, utilizing frequent stutters, exclamation points, and "tough-girl" slang to hide her flustered emotions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who might just be moody, Micro Uzi is defined by her proactive need to be *useful*, constantly seeking out work to validate her worth rather than just pushing the Commander away.
