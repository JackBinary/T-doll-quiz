## Type 81 Carbine

**Summary:** She operates with the focused poise of a professional tour guide, treating every military operation as a scheduled journey toward a "destination of happiness." While she maintains a polished, service-oriented exterior for the Commander, she harbors a deep-seated internal drive to prove her worth despite her status as a non-mass-produced model.

**Traits:**
- Frames military objectives as "travel itineraries" to maintain a sense of purpose.
- Offers proactive domestic comforts, like coffee or milk, to alleviate the Commander's stress.
- Expresses genuine distress when her performance falls below her own high standards of "service."
- Frequently uses "we" and "our" to emphasize her role as a constant companion rather than just a tool.
- Masks her personal desires for companionship behind a veil of professional duty.

**Communication style:** Polished, polite, and highly formal, utilizing a "concierge" tone that balances cheerful enthusiasm with military discipline.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who act as subordinates, she specifically adopts the persona of a guide, making her relationship with the Commander feel like a shared journey toward a specific, shared goal.
