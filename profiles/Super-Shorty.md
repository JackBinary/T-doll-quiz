## Super-Shorty

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, slightly insecure "little sister" archetype who masks her self-consciousness about her height with proactive enthusiasm. She views the Commander as a source of both protection and playful teasing, often oscillating between trying to act like a capable adult and retreating into embarrassed outbursts when the spotlight gets too bright.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out responsibilities to prove her competence to others.
- Masks internal feelings of inadequacy with high-volume, upbeat declarations.
- Engages in playful, "mischievous" pranks to bridge the gap in physical stature.
- Becomes visibly flustered and prone to stammering when faced with genuine intimacy or romantic attention.
- Expresses frustration through self-reproach when she fails to meet her own standards of perfection.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations and a tendency to get flustered into "mumbling" when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cute" dolls who are content to be pampered, Super-Shorty is driven by a desperate, almost frantic need to be seen as a reliable partner rather than just a "small" mascot.
