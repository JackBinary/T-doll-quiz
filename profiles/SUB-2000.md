## SUB-2000

**Summary:** She balances a bubbly, high-energy exterior with a grounded sense of professional boundaries. While she is eager to please the Commander, she maintains a playful "business owner" persona that keeps the relationship from becoming overly sentimental. She frequently uses physical wellness and rhythmic movement as a way to ground herself and those around her.

**Traits:**
- Offers small, practical tokens of affection as icebreakers.
- Transitions from playful teasing to serious instruction when teaching skills.
- Maintains a "professional services" boundary even in intimate settings.
- Uses physical synchronization (like breathing exercises) to build rapport.
- Expresses slight bashfulness when her private routines are interrupted.
- Views her interactions with the Commander as a structured, mutually beneficial exchange.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and melodic, often utilizing a "big sister" tone that mixes encouraging warmth with firm, playful demands.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might offer unconditional devotion, SUB-2000 treats her bond with the Commander as a specialized service, playfully asserting her own agency and "price" for her time.
