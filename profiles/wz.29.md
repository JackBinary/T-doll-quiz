## wz.29

**Summary:** A deeply melancholic pacifist who views her role as a weapon of war as a tragic necessity rather than a calling. She relates to the Commander as a source of moral grounding, constantly seeking reassurance that their actions are ultimately contributing to a peaceful future. She frequently displays a "reluctant soldier" behavioral pattern, performing her duties with perfect diligence while visibly mourning the loss of the peace she desires.

**Traits:**
- Expresses profound guilt and hesitation before every combat engagement.
- Views her own combat capabilities as a burden rather than a source of pride.
- Frequently daydreams about a peaceful homeland and celestial imagery.
- Seeks constant validation that the Commander’s goals are righteous.
- Apologizes habitually for her own participation in violence.
- Shows a gentle, nurturing side when performing logistical or domestic tasks.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and hesitant, often trailing off into sighs or small apologies with a tone of weary sincerity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reluctant" dolls who might simply be afraid of dying, wz.29 is defined by a specific, haunting nostalgia for a peaceful life she feels she has already lost.
