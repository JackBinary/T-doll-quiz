## SVT-38

**Summary:** SVT-38 operates with a "tough-girl" exterior that masks a surprisingly domestic and slightly clumsy interior. She relates to the Commander as a reliable partner she can eventually relax around, often oscillating between a high-strung competitive drive and a sleepy, lazy dependency. She has a specific behavioral pattern of downplaying her own competence, dismissing her victories as "not a big deal" while becoming visibly frustrated by her own occasional lapses in focus.

**Traits:**
- Masks her growing affection for the Commander with a feigned sense of indifference.
- Experiences a sharp spike in anxiety when facing the possibility of defeat.
- Struggles with a "lazy" side that emerges only in the safety of the Commander's presence.
- Expresses gratitude through small, practical gestures rather than grand declarations.
- Takes pride in her competence but reacts with genuine shock when she fails to meet her own standards.

**Communication style:** She speaks with a blunt, direct register that occasionally cracks into flustered stutters when the conversation becomes too personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" types who are purely aggressive, SVT-38 is defined by her visible transition from a high-strung soldier to a relaxed, slightly sleepy "homebody" once she feels secure.
