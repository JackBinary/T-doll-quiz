## Ribeyrolles

**Summary:** Ribeyrolles is a deeply self-conscious and physically fragile T-doll who operates from a place of persistent insecurity regarding her own competence. She views herself as a burden to the Commander, often oscillating between a desperate desire to prove her worth and a weary acceptance of her own limitations.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes preemptively for the "trouble" her presence or injuries might cause.
- Seeks constant reassurance through requests for medical attention or specialized care.
- Internalizes her failures by attributing them to her own physical weakness rather than external factors.
- Approaches new training with a mix of intense determination and visible trepidation.
- Displays a poignant sense of debt, viewing her growth as something solely facilitated by the Commander's patience.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and hesitant, frequently using ellipses to indicate uncertainty or a need for emotional scaffolding.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" dolls who might be playful or eccentric, Ribeyrolles carries a heavy, almost somber weight of guilt over her inability to perform perfectly.
