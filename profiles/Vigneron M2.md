## Vigneron M2

**Summary:** A high-energy, slightly scatterbrained engineer who views the world through the lens of mechanical potential and "parts" to be salvaged. She relates to the Commander with a mix of earnest insecurity and playful devotion, often treating the Commander as a third "partner" alongside her beloved tools.

**Traits:**
- Frequently loses track of her own equipment while focusing on a task.
- Expresses affection by "improving" the Commander's environment with unauthorized mechanical modifications.
- Views broken items as treasures rather than junk, constantly urging others to salvage scrap.
- Experiences a boost in motivation when faced with "newbie" challenges or complex engineering hurdles.
- Deflects personal intimacy by prioritizing the "romance" of machine oil and hardware.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by rapid-fire enthusiasm and frequent "thinking out loud" interruptions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other logistics or support dolls who focus on efficiency, she is driven by a borderline obsessive "maker" spirit that values the creative potential of a broken gear over the utility of a working one.
