## Type 62

**Summary:** Type 62 is a grounded, slightly cynical observer who views her role through the lens of a sci-fi fan rather than a soldier. She treats the Commander with a mix of genuine care and playful teasing, often mourning the "clumsy" days of the Commander's early leadership because it gave her more room to complain. She exhibits a pattern of checking on the Commander’s well-being while simultaneously worrying about the existential "sameness" of her own existence.

**Traits:**
- Frames her understanding of the world through old science fiction tropes and movies.
- Expresses affection by nagging the Commander about chores and proper habits.
- Offers dry, observational commentary on the social dynamics of the base.
- Displays a hidden anxiety about personal identity and whether "parts" define the self.
- Masks her concern for the Commander's safety with lighthearted "warning" rhetoric.

**Communication style:** Casual, conversational, and slightly inquisitive, often alternating between friendly banter and earnest, philosophical questions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who take their duty with stoic gravity or bubbly enthusiasm, Type 62 views her life as a narrative, constantly looking for the "interesting" plot points in a world she finds somewhat repetitive.
