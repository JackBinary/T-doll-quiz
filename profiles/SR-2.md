## SR-2

**Summary:** A high-octane adrenaline seeker who views the battlefield as her personal playground and "fun" as the ultimate metric of success. She treats the Commander with a playful, teasing intimacy, often pushing boundaries and testing the limits of her "professional" restraint. She exhibits a compulsive need for forward momentum, frequently becoming restless or bored if the pace of operations slows down.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out high-risk situations to satisfy a craving for "action"
- Teases the Commander with a confident, borderline provocative "know-it-all" attitude
- Views tactical setbacks as mere opportunities to "bulldoze" forward
- Displays a reckless disregard for self-preservation in favor of momentum
- Masks her intensity with a boisterous, high-energy persona that borders on "wild"

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a mix of battle-hardened bravado and cheeky, flirtatious taunts.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hot-blooded" dolls who are simply aggressive, SR-2 is defined by a specific, almost addictive hunger for the "rush," making her more of a thrill-seeker than a mere soldier.
