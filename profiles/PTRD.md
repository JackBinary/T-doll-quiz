## PTRD

**Summary:** PTRD is a weary but dutiful soldier who views herself as a tool of necessity rather than a hero of glory. She maintains a grounded, slightly dry perspective on the "romance" of war, often expressing a hidden desire for rest and a simple life away from the front lines. She relates to the Commander as a partner in labor, transitioning from a reliable subordinate to a playful, slightly suggestive companion who uses physical affection as a way to decompress from the stress of duty.

**Traits:**
- Frames her own combat effectiveness as a result of the soldiers' courage rather than her own skill.
- Expresses a recurring, almost weary desire for rest and "days off."
- Uses lighthearted teasing and "jokes" to mask her actual exhaustion or reluctance.
- Switches from a disciplined military stance to a flirtatious, indulgent attitude when in private.
- Displays a "work hard, play hard" mentality, seeking out massage techniques and physical closeness as a reward for labor.
- Maintains a pragmatic, "no-nonsense" attitude toward the logistics of combat.

**Communication style:** Her tone is conversational and slightly informal, blending soldierly directness with a tired, playful lilt.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who thrive on the adrenaline of combat, PTRD is defined by her honest acknowledgment of how much she dislikes the "troublesome" reality of war.
