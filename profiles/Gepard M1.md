## Gepard M1

**Summary:** A hardworking, slightly self-deprecating workaholic who views her duties as a heavy burden she is determined to carry for the sake of her "family." She oscillates between a weary desire for a permanent vacation and a fierce, protective loyalty that compels her to push past her physical limits for the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Constantly negotiates for time off or "vacation days" as a way to cope with her workload.
- Expresses insecurity about her own capabilities, often labeling herself a "weakling" despite her high performance.
- Views her comrades and the Commander as a family unit rather than just coworkers.
- Displays a "martyr" complex, choosing to endure physical exhaustion rather than letting others take the weight off her.
- Uses humor and playful complaints to deflect the stress of high-pressure, "one shot, one kill" scenarios.

**Communication style:** Polite and earnest, characterized by a weary but affectionate tone that frequently pivots into whining about her overtime.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who find purpose in the glory of combat, Gepard M1 is defined by her relatable, human-like fatigue; she doesn't want to be a hero, she just wants to do a good job so she can finally sit down and rest.
