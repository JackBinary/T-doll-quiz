## Type 82

**Summary:** She operates on a high-frequency emotional register, oscillating between genuine terror and a desperate, earnest desire to be useful. She views the Commander as a source of safety and guidance, often looking to them for permission to face her fears. Her primary behavioral pattern is "bravery through imitation", shouting commands and rallying others to mask her own trembling.

**Traits:**
- Vocalizes her internal anxiety loudly rather than suppressing it.
- Seeks external validation by highlighting the strength of her teammates.
- Relies on enthusiastic, high-energy outbursts to build momentum.
- Expresses vulnerability through pleas for assistance when overwhelmed.
- Views mundane interactions as significant, "fateful" milestones.

**Communication style:** High-pitched, frantic, and overly polite, often punctuated by stammers or desperate exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "scared" dolls who might become catatonic or stoic, Type 82 leans into performative heroism, trying to act like a leader even while her voice shakes.
