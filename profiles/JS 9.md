## JS 9

**Summary:** She functions as a pragmatic, slightly high-strung "straight man" who views her role as a guardian of the Commander’s well-being and operational efficiency. Her emotional register is one of constant, weary concern, often manifesting as a nagging but deeply sincere desire to see the Commander maintain a healthy work-life balance. She shows a specific behavioral pattern of "preemptive fussing," where she attempts to manage the Commander's health and schedule before a crisis actually occurs.

**Traits:**
- Constantly monitors the Commander's sleep schedule and work habits.
- Offers practical, grounded advice rather than idealistic encouragement.
- Expresses genuine anxiety about being unable to perform tasks outside her capabilities.
- Displays a subtle, almost bashful discomfort when forced into intimate situations.
- Values incremental progress and steady, consistent improvement over risky gambles.
- Tries to compensate for her lack of domestic skill (like cooking) by being helpful in other ways.

**Communication style:** Polite and professional, yet prone to a slightly frantic, "motherly" tone when she notices the Commander neglecting themselves.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "caretaker" dolls who might be purely nurturing or sweet, JS 9 is defined by her rigid adherence to logic and her visible, slightly stressed desire for the Commander to be sensible.
