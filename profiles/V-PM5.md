## V-PM5

**Summary:** PM5 is defined by a pervasive sense of self-doubt and a desperate desire to be useful, often leading her to apologize before she has even committed an error. She views herself as a burden to the Commander and her teammates, yet she finds a quiet, trembling courage in their presence that pushes her to try new things like cooking or tactical planning.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes reflexively as a default reaction to existing in the same space as others.
- Overthinks logistical details to the point of near-paralysis before starting a task.
- Seeks external validation to quiet her internal fear of being "unreliable."
- Shows a hidden, endearing obsession with food and the domesticity of home cooking.
- Hesitates to take initiative, preferring to wait for a "safe" or planned route.

**Communication style:** Extremely hesitant and stuttering, characterized by a soft, whimpering tone and frequent self-correction.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shy" dolls who are simply quiet, PM5 is defined by a specific, acute anxiety about her own competence, making her more of a "struggling apprentice" than a "timid observer."
