## Ameli

**Summary:** Ameli is a literalist who operates with a rigid, almost mechanical devotion to rules and protocols to compensate for her own feelings of inadequacy. She views her relationship with the Commander through the lens of ownership and duty, often seeking permission or "permission to fail" before expressing personal desires. She frequently fixates on small, physical imperfections, specifically her buttons, as a tangible way to manifest her anxiety about being "broken" or useless.

**Traits:**
- Interprets "belonging" to the Commander as a literal legalistic shift in status.
- Requests simplified conclusions rather than navigating complex nuances or emotional subtext.
- Expresses insecurity about her own "poor production quality" by asking for constant reassurance.
- Relies on games of chance like rock-paper-scissors to resolve indecision.
- Apologizes profusely when she fails to meet expectations or "the rules."
- Seeks out small, practical tasks (like sewing buttons) to build intimacy and utility.

**Communication style:** Polite, formal, and slightly hesitant, utilizing a "report-style" register that occasionally cracks into soft vulnerability.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "submissive" dolls who are naturally compliant, Ameli is hyper-fixated on the *rules* of submission, using them as a protective shield to navigate her fear of being a faulty piece of equipment.
