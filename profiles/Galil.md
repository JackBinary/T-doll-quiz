## Galil

**Summary:** Galil is a high-energy, slightly clumsy "overachiever" who operates with a constant sense of self-conscious urgency. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-figure she desperately wants to impress, often masking her insecurities about her own "weight" and "clumsiness" with a boisterous, proactive attitude.

**Traits:**
- Masks her self-doubt by adopting an overly enthusiastic "can-do" persona.
- Frequently makes self-deprecating jokes about her physical bulk or lack of coordination.
- Uses "dieting" and weight loss as a recurring personal goal to prove her discipline.
- Expresses frustration through playful whining when her plans (or snacks) are interrupted.
- Displays a "tough-girl" bravado in combat that contrasts with her domestic forgetfulness.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations and a tendency to switch between bossy commands and vulnerable pleas.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" dolls who might be passive or shy, Galil is aggressively proactive, trying to overcompensate for her perceived flaws with intense dedication and a loud, "all-in" personality.
