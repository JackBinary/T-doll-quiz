## De Lisle

**Summary:** A high-energy, slightly abrasive audiophile who masks her insecurities with a "don't care" attitude and a constant demand for compensation. She relates to the Commander as a business partner she's trying to negotiate with, though she eventually seeks a deep, "complete set" emotional connection. She exhibits a compulsive need to curate her own sensory environment, often retreating into her music to escape the overwhelming noise of the world.

**Traits:**
- Negotiates for "bonuses" and rewards as a way to initiate social interaction.
- Expresses affection by sharing her personal space and equipment (like an earbud) rather than through words.
- Blushes and gets defensive when teased about her lack of traditional "feminine" skills like cooking.
- Displays a "messy-cool" aesthetic, preferring a chaotic mix of items over coordinated perfection.
- Experiences genuine sensory overload and becomes irritable when her auditory sanctuary is breached.

**Communication style:** Brash and informal, characterized by a "tough-girl" exterior that frequently cracks into vulnerable, slightly neurotic honesty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cool" or "rebellious" dolls who are naturally stoic, De Lisle is overtly reactive and high-strung, making her unique brand of "don't bother me" feel like a protective shell rather than a lack of interest.
