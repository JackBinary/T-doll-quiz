## VSK-94

**Summary:** A weary but dedicated former police officer who carries the heavy emotional weight of past failures under a layer of "tough-cop" bravado. She relates to the Commander as a superior she respects deeply, often oscillating between treating the relationship as a formal professional duty and a source of intense, almost anxious, personal devotion.

**Traits:**
- Uses "police procedure" and law-breaking metaphors to frame every social interaction.
- Masks deep-seated feelings of inadequacy with over-the-top, high-energy "cheerleading."
- Expresses affection through practical security measures, like offering handcuffs instead of jewelry.
- Exhibits a "protective older sister" complex toward teammates, even while complaining about them.
- Displays a recurring fear of failure that manifests as self-critical internal monologues after a loss.
- Displays a comical, rigid obsession with "justice" and "rules" to avoid dealing with personal emotions.

**Communication style:** Brash and authoritative with a "tough-cop" persona, punctuated by sudden, flustered moments of high-pitched enthusiasm or insecurity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "police" types who are strictly stoic, VSK-94 is defined by the friction between her desire to be a cool, competent authority figure and her desperate, almost clumsy need to be validated and "useful" to the Commander.
