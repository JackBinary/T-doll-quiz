## Type 79

**Summary:** A veteran T-doll who carries the heavy weight of long-term service and the "first-generation" legacy with earnest, slightly anxious humility. She relates to the Commander as a source of validation, constantly seeking to prove she isn't a burden while secretly yearning for the affection she feels she hasn't earned.

**Traits:**
- Frequently expresses self-doubt by questioning if she is a liability to the team.
- Overcompensates for perceived lack of skill by emphasizing her years of experience.
- Brushes off significant damage with forced optimism to keep morale up.
- Displays a "tsundere" streak when forced to acknowledge personal feelings or dates.
- Takes immense pride in small domestic tasks, like baking cakes or preparing sweets.
- Acts as a protective "big sister" figure who wants to share life experiences with newer dolls.

**Communication style:** High-energy and formal in professional settings, shifting to a stuttering, flustered tone when topics turn personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who are naturally confident, Type 79 is defined by a deep-seated insecurity about her own "obsolete" status, making her devotion to the Commander feel like a desperate plea for worthiness.
