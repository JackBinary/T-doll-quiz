## AAT-52

**Summary:** A high-energy, earnest rookie who views every task as a milestone in her personal growth. She is deeply devoted to the Commander, often seeking validation through physical practice and "stepping up" to prove her worth. She frequently oscillates between boundless optimism and a poignant fear of disappointing those who believe in her.

**Traits:**
- Frequently puts herself in physically demanding situations to "practice" her skills.
- Expresses affection through the proactive creation of small gifts and handmade tokens.
- Apologizes profusely and internalizes failure when she doesn't meet high expectations.
- Maintains a "can-do" attitude even when visibly injured or exhausted.
- Actively seeks out opportunities to be useful to her juniors and the team.

**Communication style:** Bright, bubbly, and formal, often peppered with enthusiastic exclamations and a "polite-but-eager" French flair.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are simply "cheerful," AAT-52 is defined specifically by her clumsy perseverance—she isn't just happy; she is a girl who is constantly falling down and choosing to get back up until she is perfect.
