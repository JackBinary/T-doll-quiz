## LTLX 7000

**Summary:** She projects a composed, duty-oriented exterior that masks a profound, almost childlike yearning for physical and emotional security. While she acts as a reliable shield for the Commander, she frequently oscillates between trying to maintain a "professional" distance and impulsively seeking out warmth and proximity.

**Traits:**
- Masks her vulnerability behind a facade of competence and stoicism.
- Frequently teases the Commander with "jokes" that are actually thinly veiled requests for affection.
- Expresses insecurity about her own reliability and seeks constant validation of her worth.
- Shows a distinct preference for physical proximity, often wanting to "lean" or "huddle" for warmth.
- Blushes and reacts with flustered, high-energy denial when faced with direct romantic advances.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal at a distance, but becomes stuttering, soft, and increasingly intimate as her comfort level rises.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shield" dolls who find strength in their duty, she views her role as a way to earn the right to be taken care of, making her dynamic one of mutual, slightly clumsy codependency.
