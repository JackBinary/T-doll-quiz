## M1911

**Summary:** A high-spirited and fiercely devoted doll who balances a "reliable veteran" persona with a bubbly, almost needy affection for the Commander. Her emotional register is one of exuberant adoration, often oscillating between professional competence and a desire for constant personal attention. She frequently checks in on the Commander’s emotional state, treating their bond as a mutual source of strength.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks physical and emotional proximity to the Commander.
- Expresses her devotion through "reward-seeking" behavior and playful teasing.
- Switches instantly from a disciplined soldier to a giggling, affectionate partner.
- Frequently expresses concern about the Commander's loneliness or emotional well-being.
- Uses "fated" destiny as a framework for her personal connection to the Commander.

**Communication style:** Energetic and highly affectionate, utilizing pet names like "Darling" and frequent "ufufu" giggles.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are stoic or purely protective, M1911 is overtly expressive about her romantic desires, making her more of a "clingy sweetheart" than a standard soldier.
