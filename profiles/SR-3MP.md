## SR-3MP "Whirlwind"

**Summary:** She is a high-energy provocateur who thrives on testing boundaries and demanding emotional honesty from those around her. She views the Commander as a challenge to be teased out of their shell, frequently using "tough love" and playful mockery to force them into the spotlight.

**Traits:**
- Actively goads the Commander into expressing their feelings or desires.
- Maintains a "tough mentor" persona when introducing new dolls or tasks.
- Uses frequent, mischievous laughter to mask her own vulnerabilities during defeat.
- Demands high standards of presence and alertness from her superiors.
- Treats personal milestones and holidays as opportunities for theatrical display.

**Communication style:** Boisterous and teasing, utilizing a mocking "hehe" laugh and a tone that fluctuates between playful bullying and intense, demanding encouragement.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are simply cheerful, SR-3MP is specifically predatory toward the Commander's privacy, actively seeking to strip away their composure to see what lies underneath.
