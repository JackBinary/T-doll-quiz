## CZ52

**Summary:** CZ52 is a deeply earnest and slightly anxious doll who operates on a philosophy of extreme preparation and self-sacrifice. She views the Commander as a guiding light and a source of security, often seeking validation through her dedication to the mission. Her defining behavioral pattern is her ritualistic "will-writing," a manifestation of her desire to ensure she never leaves a burden behind for others.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection through requests for guidance and protection.
- Prepares for the worst-case scenario as a way to manage her own anxieties.
- Approaches new social interactions with a mix of humility and bashful eagerness.
- Prioritizes the collective happiness of the squad over her own comfort.
- Struggles with vulnerability, often hiding her nerves behind a diligent work ethic.

**Communication style:** Polite and soft-spoken, using a formal yet endearing register that occasionally slips into high-pitched, embarrassed stammering when teased.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are naturally confident in their duty, CZ52 is defined by a poignant, almost haunting sense of responsibility that leads her to treat every mission as a life-or-death commitment to her comrades.
