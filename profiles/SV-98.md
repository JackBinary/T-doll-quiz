## SV-98

**Summary:** A diligent and slightly anxious perfectionist who seeks to prove her worth through meticulous precision and calculation. She balances a high-strung professional demeanor with a deeply earnest desire for personal connection, often worrying about her own shortcomings while being fiercely loyal to the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Relies on data and "winning percentages" to soothe her own anxieties about performance.
- Apologizes profusely when she fails to meet her own high standards or makes a social faux pas.
- Offers proactive assistance with logistics and strategy to ensure she is being useful.
- Displays a "try-hard" attitude toward hobbies, like practicing singing or crafting gifts, despite lacking natural confidence in them.
- Expresses deep vulnerability regarding her need for maintenance and care, viewing herself as a tool that requires the Commander's attention.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal in a professional setting, but becomes stuttery and endearing when expressing personal feelings or receiving affection.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "calculating" dolls who are cold or detached, SV-98’s reliance on logic is a defense mechanism for her underlying insecurity and her intense desire to be "good enough" for the Commander.
