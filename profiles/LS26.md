## LS26

**Summary:** LS26 is a hyper-diligent "workaholic" who views professional excellence as a moral imperative, often bordering on the obsessive. She relates to the Commander as a superior who needs constant grounding, oscillating between a strict, no-nonsense supervisor and a deeply devoted, slightly anxious companion who fears letting the team down.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander's lapses in focus with firm, authoritative reminders.
- Volunteers for extra labor and "dummy-linking" to maximize operational efficiency.
- Relentlessly researches the "why" behind protocols to ensure perfect execution.
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing and seeks immediate reassurance after mistakes.
- Treats holidays and traditions with intense, almost performative seriousness.
- Offers small gestures of care, like tea or chocolate, as structured "scheduled" comforts.

**Communication style:** Formal and precise, utilizing "workplace" register even in private, punctuated by occasional moments of high-strung vulnerability.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are naturally stern, LS26 is driven by a visible, almost frantic desire to be useful, making her transition from a rigid taskmaster to a needy, "annoying" companion much more dramatic.
