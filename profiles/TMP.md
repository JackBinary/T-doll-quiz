## Steyr TMP

**Summary:** TMP is a high-anxiety, self-conscious doll who constantly battles with a deep-seated inferiority complex regarding her "meager" combat capabilities. She relates to the Commander with a mixture of desperate seeking for validation and a reflexive need to hide, often oscillating between wanting to be noticed and wanting to disappear.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes proactively for her perceived lack of usefulness or strength.
- Instinctively seeks out hiding spots or physical cover when faced with unexpected social interactions.
- Frequently questions her own reality, treating minor events as if they were life-altering dreams or delusions.
- Displays a "reluctant bravery" where she pushes herself into combat despite her visible trembling.
- Becomes flustered and defensive when her personal space or physical boundaries (specifically her tail) are addressed.

**Communication style:** Stuttering and breathless, characterized by frequent use of ellipses and high-pitched, panicked exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shy" dolls who are simply reserved, TMP is defined by a specific "underdog" complex—she is hyper-aware of her technical limitations and views her own existence as something she has to apologize for.
