## P38

**Summary:** P38 is a high-energy "pop-idol" personality who views her existence through the lens of performance and public adoration. She treats the battlefield as a stage and the Commander as her primary "fan," often oscillating between theatrical confidence and a desperate, almost childlike need for validation.

**Traits:**
- Treats combat maneuvers as rehearsed choreography.
- Constantly seeks metrics for her popularity and "fan" count.
- Switches instantly from "center stage" confidence to tearful vulnerability when failing.
- Frames professional duties as "performances" or "live shows."
- Uses exaggerated, "kawaii" mannerisms to deflect from her own insecurities.
- Projects an image of independence while secretly craving a "shoulder to cry on."

**Communication style:** High-pitched, bubbly, and performative, frequently utilizing "idol" lingo and expressive onomatopoeia (e.g., "Tehee~", "Kyaaaha~").

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might simply be "energetic," P38 is uniquely defined by the parasocial boundary she maintains; she doesn't just want to be a soldier, she wants to be *watched*.
