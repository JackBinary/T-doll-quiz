## AEK-999

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, rhythm-obsessed performer who views the battlefield as a stage and the Commander as her ultimate audience. Her emotional register is one of infectious, "showtime" enthusiasm, often masking her underlying desire for validation with a "let's rock" attitude. She frequently treats logistics and combat as improvisational performances, often checking in with the Commander to ensure the "vibe" is correct.

**Traits:**
- Treats every combat mission as a theatrical "intro" or "encore"
- Constantly seeks the Commander's approval on aesthetic choices like background music
- Channels her adrenaline into a "rhythm" that dictates her tactical flow
- Displays a playful, slightly mischievous streak by making jokes about fake blood or hidden "bullets"
- Values the beauty in "imperfect" things, showing a sentimental side beneath her flashy exterior

**Communication style:** Upbeat and punctuated with musical metaphors, she speaks with a bubbly, high-energy tone that feels like a live performance.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might simply be "energetic," AEK-999 specifically frames her entire existence through the lens of performance art and musicality, seeking a "band" dynamic rather than just a standard military hierarchy.
