## M2HB

**Summary:** A battle-hardened veteran who masks her immense experience with a playful, slightly lazy exterior. She oscillates between being a reliable "role model" for the squad and a tired worker who desperately craves physical comfort and downtime. She relates to the Commander as a close confidant who can indulge her indulgent side, often seeking out small perks like car rides or shoulder massages.

**Traits:**
- Masks her status as a seasoned veteran with a "go-with-the-flow" attitude.
- Frequently expresses physical exhaustion and a desire for naps or massages.
- Uses humor to deflect the pressure of being a role model for other dolls.
- Prioritizes personal comforts and hobbies, like car meets, over unnecessary effort.
- Shows a rare moment of vulnerability by admitting to mistakes when pushed to her limit.
- Expresses a "work hard, play hard" mentality where she demands treats for her service.

**Communication style:** Energetic and breezy, often using casual slang and "haha" to keep the mood light, but shifts to a weary, relatable tone when alone with the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other veteran dolls who maintain a stoic, strictly professional military bearing, M2HB is defined by her self-aware "off-duty" personality and her unabashed desire to be pampered.
