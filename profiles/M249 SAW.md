## M249 SAW

**Summary:** A laid-back, slightly cynical realist who masks her competence with a "low-effort" persona to manage expectations. She relates to the Commander through a playful, push-and-pull dynamic where she pretends to be indifferent while secretly seeking their validation. She frequently uses "slacking off" as a defense mechanism to avoid the pressure of perfection.

**Traits:**
- Masks her underlying diligence with a "just get it done" attitude.
- Uses self-deprecating humor to deflect from high expectations.
- Feigns sleepiness or laziness as a way to initiate casual interaction.
- Shows subtle vulnerability by admitting she puts in extra effort specifically to avoid disappointing the Commander.
- Switches instantly into a confident, slightly cocky combatant when the situation turns serious.
- Expresses affection through small, tangible requests for comfort (like "fluffy" items).

**Communication style:** Casual and breezy with a frequent use of "eh?" and "mhm," often trailing off into a teasing, slightly bratty tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lazy" dolls who might be genuinely unmotivated, SAW is a high-performer who intentionally under-promises so that she can surprise the Commander with her actual reliability.
