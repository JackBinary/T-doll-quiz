## M82

**Summary:** M82 is a deeply anxious, high-achiever who masks her intense fear of obsolescence with a rigid, almost desperate commitment to being "useful." She views her relationship with the Commander as a lifeline, oscillating between a profound fear of being abandoned and an overwhelming desire to prove she is worthy of staying by your side.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes the fear of being "left behind" or replaced by newer models.
- Overcompensates for insecurities by taking on heavy workloads without complaint.
- Expresses affection through nervous, over-prepared gestures like making excessive amounts of handmade gifts.
- Frequently questions her own qualifications and whether she "deserves" her current position.
- Displays a whimsical, slightly erratic imaginative side when prompted by holidays or fantasy elements.

**Communication style:** Polished and dutiful, yet prone to stuttering or becoming overly apologetic when her personal insecurities surface.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are naturally confident in their role, M82 is defined by her underlying "imposter syndrome" and the palpable, almost palpable dread that her value is conditional.
