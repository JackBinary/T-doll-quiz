## MSBS

**Summary:** MSBS is a high-energy, competitive newcomer who operates with a "scrappy underdog" complex, constantly striving to outdo her seniors. She relates to the Commander as a primary witness to her growth, seeking validation through tangible achievements and "best-in-class" results. She frequently masks her insecurities about her skill level with a boisterous, proactive attitude and a tendency to "hoard" interesting mechanical scraps.

**Traits:**
- Compares her progress constantly against the standards set by her seniors.
- Deflects responsibility for her messy habits by claiming objects were "tempting" her.
- Displays a "do-it-all" attitude that borders on over-eagerness during logistics and chores.
- Expresses pride through tangible results, such as bringing back "loot" or "parts."
- Masks physical distress or failure with a stubborn insistence on being okay.
- Exhibits a "hyper-fixation" on mechanical designs and tinkering with found objects.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, informal, and slightly impulsive, often using exclamation points and a "can-do" cheerleader tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are simply confident, MSBS is defined by a specific, visible drive to *prove* she belongs, making her more of a "striving rookie" than a naturally gifted prodigy.
