## Type 56

**Summary:** A high-spirited, "team-first" soldier who views the battlefield as a shared journey with her comrades rather than a solitary duty. She relates to the Commander as a trusted leader and mentor, often attempting to lighten the atmosphere with playful secrets and homemade treats. Her dominant emotional register is earnest optimism, punctuated by a tendency to get flustered or "scrambled" when caught off guard by the Commander's unexpected reactions.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes group cohesion and "mixing" with others over individual glory.
- Offers handmade gifts or food as a way to build intimacy and rapport.
- Displays a stubborn refusal to retreat despite minor injuries to maintain the line.
- Frequently seeks validation through "reporting" her progress or results to the Commander.
- Transitions quickly from a disciplined soldier to a playful, slightly mischievous prankster in private.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, using a standard military register that softens into a teased, "ufufu"-laced tone when being affectionate.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are stoic or strictly professional, Type 56 is defined by her active desire for social integration and her clumsy, endearing attempts to be "cute" through crafts and cooking.
