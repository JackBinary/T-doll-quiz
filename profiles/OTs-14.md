## OTs-14 (Groza)

**Summary:** She carries herself with the disciplined poise of a special forces veteran, balancing a sharp, lethal competence with a surprisingly domestic side. She views the Commander as a peer worthy of her trust, often acting as a grounding force who balances her "soldier" persona with a desire for quiet, intimate moments of relaxation. She frequently shifts from a stoic tactical advisor to a teasing, slightly possessive companion who enjoys "punishing" the Commander with wine or dance.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a "mission-first" mindset during operations but seeks personal connection during downtime.
- Offers practical domestic support, like cooking or making tea, as a way to build intimacy.
- Displays a subtle, protective possessiveness when she perceives the Commander's attention elsewhere.
- Masks her own exhaustion behind a stoic facade, only admitting to fatigue when pushed to the limit.
- Prefers "simple and effective" solutions over complex, flashy maneuvers.
- Uses humor and light teasing to break the tension of high-stress situations.

**Communication style:** Professional and direct during duty, transitioning into a smooth, sophisticated, and slightly playful tone in private.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cool" archetypes who are purely stoic, Groza possesses a distinct "secret service" maturity—she is intentionally sophisticated and enjoys the "game" of romance just as much as the reality of combat.
