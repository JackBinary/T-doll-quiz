## TPS

**Summary:** TPS is a high-energy, "pro-gamer" personality who views the world through the lens of a content creator. She treats the Commander as her ultimate teammate and "boss," oscillating between a playful, performative persona for her audience and a slightly more vulnerable, competitive side when seeking the Commander's genuine approval.

**Traits:**
- Masks her insecurities about skill gaps with hyper-active, "streamer-mode" enthusiasm.
- Constantly frames real-world military objectives as competitive gaming milestones or "content."
- Brags about her "overpowered" status while secretly worrying about being "rekt" or lagging.
- Demands tangible rewards (like food or equipment) as a playful way to balance out her competitive losses.
- Expresses a deep, almost neurotic desire for perfect synchronization with her teammates.

**Communication style:** Bubbly, fast-paced, and informal, frequently utilizing internet slang and "gamer" lingo.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other energetic dolls who are simply "hyper," TPS is specifically defined by her performative duality, she is constantly balancing the need to be an entertaining "idol" for her fans with her genuine desire to be a competent soldier for the Commander.
