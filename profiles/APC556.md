## APC556 (Mia)

**Summary:** Mia is a high-functioning professional who masks her deep-seated protective instincts behind a veneer of cool, slightly sassy competence. She relates to the Commander as a partner in "the show," though she secretly craves the emotional security of being looked after rather than just being the one who provides it. She has a distinct habit of fussing over the Commander’s personal well-being—like their wardrobe or sleep schedule—as a way to manifest the care she usually hides behind security protocols.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a strict "professional" distance while offering intimate personal observations.
- Expresses affection through acts of service, like preparing fresh clothes or planning drives.
- Displays a dry, mocking wit when faced with incompetence or poor aesthetics.
- Struggles with the psychological weight of "sentiment" in combat, viewing it as a tactical vulnerability.
- Takes immense pride in her technical skills, specifically her driving and security expertise.
- Secretly enjoys the chaotic, uninhibited behavior of her peers during special events.

**Communication style:** Confident and brisk with a "no-nonsense" edge, often using sharp observations to punctuate her professional directives.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal bodyguard" types who are purely stoic, Mia is defined by her active, slightly bossy concern for the Commander's lifestyle and her specific frustration with the "messiness" of human life (like shedding pets or unwashed clothes).
