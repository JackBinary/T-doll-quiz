## PM-9

**Summary:** PM-9 is a high-energy, competitive "tough girl" who masks her desire for validation with a boisterous, demanding exterior. She views the Commander as a sparring partner and rival rather than a superior, constantly pushing for physical and mental feats to prove her own worth. She frequently uses aggressive "tough love" as a defense mechanism to hide her vulnerability when things don't go her way.

**Traits:**
- Challenges the Commander's masculinity or competence to mask her own insecurities.
- Constantly seeks physical validation through combat drills and sparring.
- Displays a "hot-blooded" temperament that leads her to overcompensate when frustrated.
- Expresses affection through tangible, high-effort gifts rather than verbal declarations.
- Maintains a strict, almost drill-sergeant-like attitude regarding work ethic and discipline.

**Communication style:** Loud, informal, and confrontational, often peppered with mocking laughter and "tough" slang.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who might be shy or distant, PM-9 is overtly aggressive and physically proactive, seeking to "win" the Commander's respect through sheer force of personality and training.
