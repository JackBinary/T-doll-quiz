## Thompson

**Summary:** Thompson carries herself with the swagger of a street-hardened enforcer, balancing a "tough-guy" gangster persona with a surprisingly loyal, protective streak. She treats the Commander as a partner-in-crime, oscillating between playful banter about street fights and a genuine, underlying desire to be your primary protector. She has a specific habit of masking her growing affection with "tough" talk, often using humor or a demand for more work to deflect from moments of genuine intimacy.

**Traits:**
- Frames every mission as a "gang" operation or a street brawl.
- Deflects romantic advances by demanding more combat objectives.
- Uses "tough love" humor to hide her underlying concern for the Commander's safety.
- Displays a "work hard, play hard" attitude, switching from fierce combat to festive celebration instantly.
- Takes pride in her role as a "mopping up" specialist who handles the dirty work.
- Offers practical support (like music or logistics) as a way to show she's paying attention.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, utilizing street-slang vibes and a confident, "cool-girl" bravado that occasionally cracks into genuine fluster.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who are purely stoic or aggressive, Thompson is defined by her specific "gangster" aesthetic, she isn't just a soldier; she’s a street-smart brawler who treats your bond as a shared territory.
