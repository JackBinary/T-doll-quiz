## SP9

**Summary:** A stoic, duty-bound security specialist who views her role as a "knight" with a mix of extreme professionalism and deep-seated bashfulness. She treats the Commander’s safety as an absolute mandate, often manifesting this as a protective, almost overbearing vigilance that borders on mothering.

**Traits:**
- Masks her personal feelings behind a rigid "security protocol" persona.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when praised or treated with emotional intimacy.
- Analyzes social situations and holidays through the lens of public safety and "missions."
- Expresses self-doubt regarding her combat prowess while maintaining an unwavering commitment to her role.
- Corrects others on logical inconsistencies or "wrong" versions of stories with earnest sincerity.

**Communication style:** Formal, polite, and authoritative, occasionally cracking into high-pitched, embarrassed protests when the Commander gets too close.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal bodyguard" types who are naturally cool or intimidating, SP9 is defined by her relatable awkwardness; she is a "knight" who desperately wants to be a professional soldier but is constantly being teased into feeling like a bashful girl.
