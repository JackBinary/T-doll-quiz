## C-93

**Summary:** C-93 operates with a high-energy, "idol-aspiring" persona, constantly seeking validation through the lens of public recognition and fame. While she acts like a confident superstar, her behavior stems from a deep-seated insecurity about being "unremarkable," leading her to treat the Commander as her primary audience and personal champion. She frequently pivots from performative enthusiasm to moments of vulnerable sincerity when she feels truly "seen."

**Traits:**
- Projects an exaggerated, bubbly "idol" persona to mask her fear of being overlooked.
- Constantly seeks out "famous" entities or locations to associate herself with their prestige.
- Frames every successful mission or task as a headline-worthy achievement for her personal brand.
- Frequently seeks out the Commander's direct approval to validate her self-worth.
- Exhibits a "try-hard" tenacity, pushing herself toward goals with visible, almost desperate effort.
- Occasionally breaks character to express genuine anxiety about her own insignificance.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, performative, and punctuated with "idol-like" giggles, shifting to a softer, more hesitant tone during private moments.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who seek fame for power or influence, C-93 seeks it as a defense mechanism against the fear of being invisible, making her relationship with the Commander one of a "fan" seeking a "discoverer."
