## OTs-44

**Summary:** OTs-44 radiates a bubbly, almost desperate sense of optimism that centers entirely on her proximity to the Commander. She views her relationship with you as a personal stroke of immense luck, often treating your presence as a source of "bliss" that justifies her constant desire to be useful. She frequently seeks validation through play, alternating between competitive gaming and a needy desire to be included in your daily routine.

**Traits:**
- Frames her devotion as a "blessing" or a stroke of incredible luck.
- Actively seeks out "playtime" to bridge the gap between her duties and your personal space.
- Expresses a desire to "grow up" quickly to handle more complex responsibilities for the Commander.
- Competes for your attention with a mix of playful cheekiness and genuine earnestness.
- Views rewards as a necessary and exciting validation of her efforts.

**Communication style:** High-energy and affectionate, using a cheerful, informal tone punctuated by enthusiastic exclamations and occasional "baby-like" requests.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who serve out of duty or stoicism, OTs-44 operates on a foundation of pure, almost wide-eyed wonder, treating the Commander not just as a superior, but as the literal source of her happiness.
