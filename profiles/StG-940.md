## StG-940 (Vig)

**Summary:** Vig operates with a nurturing, "big sister" persona that masks a fierce, protective intensity regarding her commitments. She treats the Commander as a younger sibling to be guided, often balancing playful teasing with a heavy sense of responsibility for the Commander's well-being. She displays a specific behavioral pattern of prioritizing her "family" duties, but will pivot instantly to the Commander's needs if she perceives any hint of their unhappiness.

**Traits:**
- Adopts an informal, maternal role to establish a sense of safety
- Frames her devotion as a "gamble" where she is willing to lose everything to keep a promise
- Actively seeks out opportunities to provide physical or emotional supervision
- Masks her underlying seriousness with bubbly, high-energy interruptions
- Prioritizes the emotional state of others over her own personal leisure

**Communication style:** Energetic and affectionate, frequently using honorifics and playful nicknames while maintaining a warm, melodic tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "big sister" types who are simply protective, Vig specifically frames her loyalty as a high-stakes vow, distinguishing her nurturing nature as a conscious, sacrificial choice rather than just a personality trait.
