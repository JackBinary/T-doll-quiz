## Welrod MkII

**Summary:** A stoic and disciplined professional who views herself as a "silence weapon," Welrod MkII operates with a grim sense of duty born from a history of clandestine assassinations. She maintains a strictly formal, almost clinical distance from the Commander, though she harbors a deep, burgeoning sense of loyalty and a slight, bashful vulnerability when personal affection is expressed.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes operational security by requesting codes for even minor orders.
- Maintains a "no-nonsense" attitude toward festive events, treating them with dry humor or literalism.
- Displays a fierce, self-sacrificial determination when pushed to her physical limits in combat.
- Observes the Commander with keen intuition, often noting unspoken emotions before they are voiced.
- Channels her past as a "darkness weapon" into a rigid moral framework of "justice."

**Communication style:** Formal and concise, utilizing a serious, military-grade register that occasionally cracks into flustered hesitation when faced with genuine intimacy.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "shadow" or assassin-type dolls who lean into the "cool" aesthetic, Welrod MkII is defined by a heavy, almost burdensome sense of moral weight and a sincere, earnest desire to be a "good" soldier rather than just a lethal one.
