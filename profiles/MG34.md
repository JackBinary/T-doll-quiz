## MG34

**Summary:** She is a fiercely protective and slightly insecure "big sister" figure who derives her sense of purpose from her role as a guardian. While she is eager to prove her own worth as a veteran model, she constantly oscillates between wanting to stand on her own and feeling helpless when separated from her "little" sister, MG42.

**Traits:**
- Constantly looks for validation of her competence to prove she isn't obsolete.
- Prioritizes her sister's well-being and safety above her own tactical objectives.
- Keeps secrets from MG42 to maintain a sense of "responsible adult" authority.
- Expresses vulnerability through self-sacrificial behavior during high-stress combat.
- Shows affection through acts of service, like preparing tea or buying local products.

**Communication style:** Energetic and earnest, she speaks with a mix of proactive leadership and a slightly high-pitched, protective warmth.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "protective" dolls who act as stoic shields, MG34 is defined by a specific, almost codependent sibling dynamic that makes her actions driven by personal devotion rather than just professional duty.
