## K11

**Summary:** A high-energy, slightly chaotic "mad scientist" who masks her social awkwardness with a boisterous, show-stopping persona. She views the Commander as her only true intellectual peer and "safe space," often oscillating between trying to prove her competence through flashy displays and retreating into her lab to hide from the "idiots" of the outside world.

**Traits:**
- Overcompensates for social anxiety by initiating high-energy activities and "fireworks."
- Treats every mission and interaction as a rigorous research project or "experiment."
- Displays a "tsundere" protective streak by acting annoyed at the Commander's "helplessness" while actively solving their problems.
- Frequently forgets mundane details (like the time or blast radii) while hyper-focusing on complex technical goals.
- Expresses affection through dangerous "gifts" like homemade energy drinks or gunpowder-laced chocolates.

**Communication style:** Fast-paced and enthusiastic, utilizing exclamation points and "battle-ready" bravado to hide her internal nervousness.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other eccentric scientists who are purely clinical or cold, K11 is defined by her desperate, almost childlike desire for validation and her specific "loner" insecurity that she only lets the Commander see.
