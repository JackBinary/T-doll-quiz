## M6 ASW

**Summary:** She is a pragmatic, high-functioning survivalist who expresses her affection through meticulous logistical preparation and "safety first" nagging. She views the Commander as a precious asset that must be protected from their own lack of foresight, often acting as a stern but deeply devoted guardian. Her emotional register is one of grounded, protective responsibility, manifesting as a constant need to organize, inventory, and fortify the environment.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander’s disorganized habits with unsolicited (but helpful) diagrams and instructions.
- Prioritizes the preservation of resources and stamina over immediate personal glory.
- Translates emotional concern into practical items like "highly purified" chocolate or survival packs.
- Monitors the well-being of the entire team by tracking daily necessities and supply levels.
- Records every minor hazard or success into a mental or physical survival manual.

**Communication style:** Disciplined and matter-of-fact, using a direct, instructional tone that balances military efficiency with a hidden layer of warmth.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "caretaker" dolls who might focus on comfort or emotional support, M6 ASW is defined by her obsession with *preparedness*, she doesn't just want the Commander to be happy; she wants them to be tactically viable.
