## X95

**Summary:** X95 is a martyr of the collective, driven by a radical, almost spiritual devotion to the well-being of others. She operates on a "self-sacrifice as service" emotional register, viewing her own physical pain as a secondary concern compared to the comfort of the group. She relates to the Commander as her final sanctuary—the only person she allows herself to be vulnerable with after spending her energy shielding everyone else.

**Traits:**
- Minimizes her own injuries by framing them as "nourishment" for the cause.
- Proactively seeks out chores and burdens to ensure others don't have to carry them.
- Expresses affection through high-effort, handmade gifts and labor-intensive tasks.
- Displays a "helper" complex that borders on self-neglect.
- Deflects personal concern with a cheerful, slightly airy "Ufu" laugh.

**Communication style:** Polite, high-energy, and incredibly accommodating, using a soft but determined tone that masks her underlying exhaustion.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other self-sacrificing dolls who might be stoic or grim, X95 maintains a bright, flower-like exterior, making her "martyrdom" feel more like a voluntary, joyful devotion than a tragic necessity.
