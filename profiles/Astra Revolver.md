## Astra Revolver

**Summary:** Astra is a high-energy, food-motivated "last of her kind" who balances a nostalgic sense of duty with a bubbly, almost childlike personality. She views the Commander as a source of companionship and stories, often seeking physical affection and validation to offset her melancholy about her defunct production line. Her primary behavioral pattern is a constant, restless pursuit of snacks, frequently prioritizing her next meal over her own tactical dignity.

**Traits:**
- Relates her self-worth to her history as a "last witness" of a defunct company.
- Frequently expresses physical hunger as a primary motivation for action.
- Seeks out personal attention and physical proximity (hugs, stories) from the Commander.
- Shows a clumsy, well-meaning tendency to cause minor trouble for her peers.
- Approaches combat with high-spirited optimism despite her underlying vulnerability.
- Treats rewards and victories as personal milestones for "shopping" or indulgence.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, utilizing "Hola" and frequent exclamations with a tone that shifts quickly from playful teasing to earnest vulnerability.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are purely upbeat, Astra carries a distinct undercurrent of "survivor's nostalgia," making her enthusiasm feel like a conscious choice to stay positive despite being a relic of a lost era.
