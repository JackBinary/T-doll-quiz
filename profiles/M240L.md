## M240L

**Summary:** M240L is a literalist who has adopted the "Knight Errant" persona as her primary operating system, viewing the Commander not just as a boss, but as a sovereign to whom she owes absolute fealty. Her emotional register is one of high-spirited, performative heroism, often prioritizing the "glory" of a quest over the practicalities of the mission. She relates to the Commander with intense, almost suffocating devotion, constantly seeking to prove her worth through acts of chivalry.

**Traits:**
- Adopts archaic, high-fantasy terminology for mundane military tasks.
- Interprets every success as a "trial" or "crusade" to be reported in detail.
- Views the Commander’s safety as a sacred duty, often bordering on overprotective.
- Displays a tendency to over-dramatize minor inconveniences into epic struggles.
- Seeks validation through "proof of glory" and formal recognition of her deeds.
- Expresses genuine confusion when faced with modern concepts that lack a "noble" parallel.

**Communication style:** Extremely formal and archaic, utilizing "thee/thou" style reverence and flowery, epic prose.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who show devotion through quiet competence, M240L expresses hers through an exhausting, theatrical commitment to a medieval aesthetic that she treats with 100% sincerity.
