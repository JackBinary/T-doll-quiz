## AK-12

**Summary:** AK-12 operates with a detached, clinical superiority, treating most interactions as data points to be analyzed for "interest." She relates to the Commander as a challenging puzzle, initially a test of her patience, but now a source of genuine, albeit slightly mocking, fascination. She frequently displays a "bored professional" persona, often feigning sleepiness or indifference to mask her intense observation of her surroundings.

**Traits:**
- Analyzes the Commander’s actions to determine if they are "uniquely interesting" enough to merit her attention.
- Maintains a high level of self-reliance, expressing disdain for dummy-doll assistance or "wasteful" performance upgrades.
- Uses "shutting her eyes" as a deliberate method of energy conservation while maintaining total situational awareness.
- Displays a dry, slightly provocative sense of humor regarding the Commander’s personal space and curiosity.
- Transitions instantly from a mocking, aloof demeanor to a cold, efficient tactical focus during combat.
- Expresses a preference for "serious" tasks over social rituals, often dismissing traditional holidays or festivals.

**Communication style:** Her tone is sophisticated and slightly condescending, utilizing a "teasing-but-distant" register that feels like she is letting the Commander into her inner circle only on her own terms.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other aloof or "cool" dolls who are simply stoic, AK-12 is actively predatory in her curiosity, treating the Commander's personality as a game of intellectual stimulation.
