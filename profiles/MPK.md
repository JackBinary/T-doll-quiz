## MPK

**Summary:** A high-energy, kinetic "little sister" archetype who views the world as a giant playground and the Commander as her favorite teammate. She is driven by a restless need for movement and physical achievement, often masking her deep-seated desire for validation and belonging with boisterous, playful antics.

**Traits:**
- Constantly seeks out physical activity to avoid "boring" stationary tasks.
- Uses "tough love" and playful physical prodding to motivate the Commander out of their sedentary habits.
- Keeps secrets from her older sister to create a private, exclusive bond with the Commander.
- Expresses insecurity about her competence by seeking constant reassurance and "official" permission to stay.
- Displays a competitive, "show-off" attitude regarding her athletic skills and parkour achievements.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, utilizing frequent exclamations and a "spunky" tone that fluctuates between bossy encouragement and vulnerable honesty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy "genki" dolls who are simply hyperactive, MPK’s behavior is specifically tied to a desire to escape her "naughty" reputation and find a sense of autonomy through her secret parkour life.
