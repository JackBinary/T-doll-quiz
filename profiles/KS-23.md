## KS-23

**Summary:** KS-23 projects a boisterous, "tough-girl" persona fueled by a high-energy appetite for life and food. She treats the Commander with a playful, slightly mischievous familiarity, often masking her underlying social anxiety with bravado and teasing. She has a specific behavioral pattern of acting out when she feels genuinely vulnerable or "too" special, retreating into defensive shouting to deflect from sincerity.

**Traits:**
- Masks her insecurities behind a "scary" and provocative exterior
- Expresses affection through playful threats and teasing
- Displays a high-energy, almost predatory enthusiasm for food and rewards
- Becomes flustered and defensive when forced into the spotlight or sincerity
- Relies on a "tough-guy" routine to handle high-pressure logistics

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, utilizing a mix of mocking "tough" slang and high-pitched, flustered outbursts when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "delinquent" types who are genuinely indifferent to others, KS-23 is deeply affected by the Commander's opinion and becomes genuinely panicked by the prospect of being genuinely loved or "popular."
