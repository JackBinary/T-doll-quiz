## M16A1

**Summary:** A rough-around-the-edges veteran who masks her deep-seated insecurities with a boisterous, "cool older sister" persona. She views her relationship with the Commander as a partnership of mutual reliability, often seeking to prove her worth through high-risk competence while secretly craving emotional intimacy. She has a compulsive need to be the "reliable one," frequently taking on the heaviest burdens to ensure the team's success.

**Traits:**
- Projects a "tough-girl" exterior to overcompensate for past operational failures.
- Frequently initiates social plans involving alcohol to bridge the gap of awkward intimacy.
- Takes extreme pride in her role as a protective older sibling to the other dolls.
- Displays a "work hard, play hard" mentality, demanding immediate celebration after a mission.
- Expresses vulnerability through reluctant honesty when caught in a moment of sincerity.

**Communication style:** Casual, high-energy, and peppered with slang, shifting into a slightly hesitant, softer tone when the conversation becomes genuinely personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who are stoic or aloof, M16A1 is defined by her active, almost desperate desire to be liked and respected by her "family."
