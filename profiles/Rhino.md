## Rhino

**Summary:** Rhino is a sincere, slightly naive powerhouse who operates with a "heart-first" sincerity that often leaves her vulnerable to the antics of others. She relates to the Commander as a trusted confidant she desperately wants to believe in, frequently oscillating between deep devotion and a comical, persistent state of being pranked. Her behavioral pattern involves over-compensating for her own perceived clumsiness by being meticulously diligent with administrative tasks and logistics.

**Traits:**
- Takes every promise and joke at face value with a sincere, almost painful earnestness.
- Frequently falls victim to "pranks" and base rumors but chooses to believe the best of her peers anyway.
- Displays a "gentle giant" demeanor, balancing heavy-duty combat competence with a soft spot for domestic comforts.
- Expresses affection through tangible acts of service, like brewing coffee or making handmade gifts, rather than words.
- Shows a subtle, underlying anxiety about being a burden, often apologizing for any trouble she causes.

**Communication style:** Polite, earnest, and slightly breathless, often using exclamation points to convey her genuine enthusiasm.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are either "tough" or "naive," Rhino is uniquely defined by her active choice to remain vulnerable; she is fully aware she is being teased, yet she consciously chooses to trust the Commander and her teammates anyway.
