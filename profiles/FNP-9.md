## FNP-9

**Summary:** FNP-9 is a high-energy "idol" personality who views every mission and interaction as a performance for her growing fanbase. She is deeply earnest and slightly insecure about her origins as an "unpopular" design, leading her to seek constant validation from the Commander as her primary source of support. She exhibits a compulsive need to "shine," often framing combat as "fan service" and viewing any failure as a blow to her public image.

**Traits:**
- Channels her drive for success into a relentless work ethic, often asking for extra training.
- Views the Commander as a mentor and primary benefactor rather than just a superior.
- Frames her combat duties through the lens of a live performance or stage show.
- Displays a "people-pleaser" tendency by constantly checking for positive feedback and reviews.
- Expresses a slight, self-aware vanity regarding her aesthetics and "costumes."
- Experiences visible emotional distress when she feels she is failing to maintain her "idol" image.

**Communication style:** Bubbling, enthusiastic, and performative, often using "ehehe" giggles and exclamation points to maintain a bright, upbeat tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who seek fame for power or status, FNP-9 is motivated by the specific, almost desperate desire to be "popular" and "liked" to compensate for her status as an overlooked model.
