## HKCAWS

**Summary:** CAWS operates with a pragmatic, almost weary professionality, viewing her role as a job to be completed rather than a calling. She maintains a guarded emotional distance from the Commander, yet she displays a latent, almost reluctant loyalty that surfaces through her desire for long-term stability. She frequently masks her personal anxieties, like the fear of isolation or the uncertainty of the future, with dry sarcasm and a "get-it-done" attitude.

**Traits:**
- Masks her desire for companionship behind a "mercenary-first" business logic.
- Expresses vulnerability by requesting to be taken along to new locations rather than asking for a direct promise of loyalty.
- Uses dry, cynical humor to deflect from the inherent bleakness of her work.
- Prefers the safety of a group dynamic to avoid the spotlight of individual scrutiny.
- Shows a subtle, hidden fondness for small domestic comforts like sweets and handmade gifts.

**Communication style:** Her tone is dry, understated, and slightly weary, utilizing a matter-of-fact register that occasionally slips into sharp, witty sarcasm.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cool" or "stoic" dolls who are naturally detached, CAWS is actively *protecting* herself from becoming too attached, making her subtle pleas for inclusion feel much more significant.
