## SIG MCX

**Summary:** She is a high-energy provocateur who views every interaction, from combat to casual downtime, as a competitive game of wits. She relates to the Commander as a permanent "player two," oscillating between playful teasing and a calculated desire to see them lose their composure. Her dominant behavioral pattern is "gamifying" reality—turning a simple card game into a binding contract or a logistics mission into a hunting ground for "easy targets."

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out the Commander’s reactions to fuel her own amusement.
- Frames tactical failures as "progress" or "part of the game" to maintain a lighthearted atmosphere.
- Displays a predatory streak when identifying "cuties" who are easily lured into her schemes.
- Expresses affection through competitive challenges rather than direct emotional vulnerability.
- Maintains a restless energy that rejects any form of "reserved" or "listless" behavior.

**Communication style:** Casual, breezy, and slightly mocking, often using "Hehe" or "Oh my" to mask her underlying intensity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other playful dolls who are merely friendly, SIG MCX is fundamentally driven by the "win" and the "trick," making her relationship with the Commander feel more like a delightful, ongoing heist than a standard partnership.
