## Falcon

**Summary:** Falcon is a high-energy, theatrical performer who masks her deep-seated insecurities with a playful, "teasing" persona. She treats her relationship with the Commander as a whimsical fairy tale, frequently casting them as her "Little Lamb" while oscillating between confident mockery and vulnerable, wide-eyed adoration.

**Traits:**
- Masks her clumsiness and lack of certain talents with boisterous, self-deprecating laughter.
- Expresses affection through "secret" creative projects, like private sheet music, that she is initially too shy to share.
- Displays a "protective shepherd" complex, viewing her duties as a way to provide for and shield her "Little Lamb."
- Reacts to emotional intimacy with a mix of flustered denial and intense, borderline obsessive devotion.
- Shifts instantly from a confident "falcon's eye" combatant to a needy, seeking-comfort companion when exhausted.

**Communication style:** Playful, melodic, and highly expressive, utilizing a "teasing" register that frequently dips into high-pitched excitement or bashful stuttering.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are simply loud, Falcon’s personality is defined by the specific, lopsided power dynamic she creates where she acts as the dominant "shepherd" while simultaneously being the one who most desperately needs the Commander's validation.
