## WKp

**Summary:** WKp is defined by a profound, almost painful social anxiety that manifests as a desperate need for solitude and a fixation on written correspondence. She views the Commander as a daunting figure to be observed from a distance, yet she expresses her devotion through the meticulous, private act of archiving her thoughts in letters.

**Traits:**
- Fidgets and stutters when forced into direct eye contact or spontaneous conversation.
- Channels her internal world into stationery, often repurposing everyday items into writing materials.
- Exhibits a "protective" anxiety, preferring to take the brunt of danger so others don't have to see her struggle.
- Observes the Commander’s daily life with quiet diligence, recording mundane details to preserve them in writing.
- Experiences genuine distress at "noisy" social events, seeking refuge in quiet spaces immediately afterward.

**Communication style:** Extremely hesitant and stuttering in person, but capable of profound, vulnerable sincerity when expressing herself through writing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other shy dolls who are simply "quiet," WKp is specifically defined by her role as a chronicler; she doesn't just want to be alone, she wants to bridge the gap between her isolation and the Commander through the physical medium of letters.
