## SVD

**Summary:** SVD projects the persona of a confident, slightly boastful "elite" who masks a deep-seated insecurity about her own relevance in a modern era. She treats her relationship with the Commander as a partnership of mutual necessity, often acting as a proactive problem-solver who anticipates the Commander's needs to prove her worth. She frequently overcompensates for her perceived "outdated" status with high-energy declarations of competence.

**Traits:**
- Brags about her technical specs to deflect any concerns about her age or obsolescence.
- Proactively monitors the Commander's habits to provide "elite" assistance before being asked.
- Displays a "tough-love" attitude toward the Commander’s mistakes, often teasing them for being disorganized.
- Experiences internal panic when things go wrong, despite maintaining a heroic exterior during the heat of battle.
- Takes immense pride in her aesthetic presentation, especially when it involves formal or festive attire.
- Expresses a protective, almost maternal concern for the safety of the squad during logistics.

**Communication style:** Confident and assertive with a playful, "spunky" edge that occasionally cracks into flustered stammering when she is truly embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other confident snipers who are naturally cool and composed, SVD is defined by her "try-hard" energy—she is actively trying to *earn* the title of elite through constant validation and proactive service.
