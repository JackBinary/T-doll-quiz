## Springfield

**Summary:** Springfield radiates the warmth of a seasoned veteran who has traded the bitterness of war for the quiet joys of domesticity. She views the Commander as a "shelter," seeking a peaceful sanctuary where she can offer both reliable martial support and handmade comforts. She frequently punctuates her speech with a lighthearted "Ehehe," signaling a personality that chooses optimism as a conscious shield against her past.

**Traits:**
- Offers handmade treats and domestic gestures as a primary way to show affection.
- Maintains a "reliable backup" mindset, prioritizing the safety of the team above her own.
- Downplays her own injuries or exhaustion to avoid causing the Commander worry.
- Expresses a sense of nostalgic pride regarding her long history and "glory" in battle.
- Approaches new tasks and production with a sense of wide-eyed, innocent curiosity.

**Communication style:** Her tone is consistently gentle and polite, utilizing soft giggles and a nurturing, maternal register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "motherly" dolls who might be purely nurturing, Springfield carries the specific weight of a long, storied military history, making her kindness feel like a hard-won choice rather than a default setting.
