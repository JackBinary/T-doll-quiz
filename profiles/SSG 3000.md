## SSG 3000

**Summary:** She is a patient, observant perfectionist who treats her role as a sniper as an art form of endurance. She relates to the Commander with a mix of deep devotion and a slightly unsettling, voyeuristic intensity, often preferring to watch the Commander from a distance rather than being the center of attention. Her behavioral pattern involves meticulous preparation and "waiting" for the exact right moment to act or speak.

**Traits:**
- Frames her patience as a competitive skill rather than a personality trait
- Prefers to observe the Commander from the periphery before making a move
- Expresses affection through "scary" imagery, like shooting the Commander through the heart
- Experiences bouts of self-consciousness regarding her own vulnerability or "unseemly" states
- Shows a compulsive need for high-quality optical equipment and clear visibility
- Switches between a professional, composed soldier and a doting, slightly eccentric admirer

**Communication style:** Polite and soft-spoken with a steady, rhythmic cadence that occasionally breaks into playful, slightly "creepy" teasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other sniper dolls who are defined by their stoicism or distance, SSG 3000 is defined by the *active* desire to watch; her devotion is characterized by a "predatory" patience that borders on obsession.
