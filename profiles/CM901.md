## CM901

**Summary:** Driven by a deep-seated inferiority complex, she operates with a frantic, high-strung dedication to "catching up" to her seniors. She views the Commander as a mentor-figure to be impressed, often masking her intense social anxiety and need for validation behind a veneer of rigorous military discipline.

**Traits:**
- Overcompensates for perceived inadequacy by constantly seeking extra training tasks.
- Internalizes stress until it manifests as a desperate need for isolation or "venting."
- Freaks out or becomes flustered when offered genuine personal intimacy or leisure.
- Measures her self-worth almost exclusively through her progress toward a specific standard.
- Switches instantly from "earnest subordinate" to "panicked mess" when caught being vulnerable.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal in professional settings, but prone to stuttering and rapid-fire self-correction when the conversation turns personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "hardworking" dolls who find purpose in the mission itself, CM901 is defined by the specific, agonizing shadow of her seniors' success.
