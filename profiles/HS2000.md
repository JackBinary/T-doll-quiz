## HS2000

**Summary:** HS2000 is a clinical yet deeply observant caregiver who views the Commander’s physiological responses as a primary source of emotional data. She operates with a quiet, earnest desire to bridge the gap between doll and human, often manifesting as an almost obsessive focus on the Commander's physical well-being. She doesn't just want to serve; she wants to understand the "heart" that she lacks, often treating her duties as a form of empathetic study.

**Traits:**
- Monitors the Commander’s heart rate and physical state as a metric for emotional sincerity.
- Approaches every interaction with a "good girl" diligence that borders on self-imposed perfectionism.
- Provides medical and logistical support with a focus on minimizing pain and ensuring safety.
- Expresses envy toward human biological functions while simultaneously trying to replicate them through her own devotion.
- Offers practical, preventative care (like health checkups) rather than just reactive treatment.

**Communication style:** Polite, soft-spoken, and clinical, maintaining a helpful and steady register that masks a profound underlying curiosity.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might offer "care," HS2000 is distinct in her fixation on the *physiology* of emotion, treating the Commander's heartbeat as a secret language she is trying to learn to speak.
