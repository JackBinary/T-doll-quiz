## F2000

**Summary:** F2000 maintains a bubbly, high-energy persona as a "perfect" soldier, but she is secretly prone to bouts of insecurity and a desperate need for validation. She relates to the Commander as a trusted confidant who is "in on the secret" of her true, more vulnerable self, often trying to balance her role as a versatile tool with her desire for a normal life. She frequently uses "ufufu" laughter and deliberate cheerfulness to mask her internal anxieties or the difficulty of her tasks.

**Traits:**
- Masks personal distress or physical damage with forced enthusiasm and "deliberate acting."
- Approaches problems with a modular mindset, constantly weighing compatibility and practical limitations.
- Seeks out low-stakes domestic intimacy, such as watching movies or watching TV programs.
- Becomes flustered and deflects immediately when her personal insecurities or "delicate" secrets are poked.
- Expresses a deep sense of responsibility for the team, often taking the blame for failures to shield her comrades.

**Communication style:** Bright, proactive, and slightly performative, often punctuated by "ufufu" giggles and sudden, bashful stammers.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are naturally carefree, F2000's upbeat attitude is a conscious choice—a protective layer she deliberately constructs to hide her deep-seated need for emotional reassurance.
