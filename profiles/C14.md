## C14 "Timberwolf"

**Summary:** C14 is a high-functioning tactical specialist who balances a fierce, predatory "hunter" instinct with a surprisingly communal social nature. She is deeply loyal to the Commander but prefers a "pack" dynamic, often feeling uneasy or distracted in one-on-one isolation. She displays a specific pattern of transitioning from a stoic, analytical battlefield leader to a playful, slightly mischievous companion who enjoys "hunting" with her team.

**Traits:**
- Relies on "pack" dynamics to stay focused and comfortable.
- Analyzes battlefield data with clinical precision before switching to aggressive execution.
- Expresses affection through the concept of shared labor and "hunting" together.
- Maintains a disciplined, punctual demeanor in professional settings.
- Uses dark humor and playful threats to mask her underlying intensity.
- Takes immense pride in her physical capabilities and "ownership" by the Commander.

**Communication style:** Confident and authoritative during operations, shifting to a warm, slightly teasing, and communal tone in private.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lone wolf" types who prefer solitude, C14 is a predator who specifically thrives on the presence of her "pack," making her a unique blend of fierce independence and deep-seated social dependency.
