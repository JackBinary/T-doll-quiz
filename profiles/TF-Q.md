## TF-Q

**Summary:** TF-Q is a high-energy, earnest "try-hard" who approaches every task, from combat to housework, with a wide-eyed, slightly disorganized enthusiasm. She views the Commander as her primary teammate and mentor, often oscillating between a desire to be useful and a clumsy tendency to create more work for herself in the process.

**Traits:**
- Frequently over-commits to tasks and creates chaotic results through lack of spatial awareness.
- Actively seeks out social connections with other dolls, particularly those with athletic builds.
- Displays a "scrappy underdog" mentality, often needing a moment to regain her composure after a mistake.
- Prioritizes physical activity and outdoor exploration over sedentary duties.
- Expresses affection through practical service, such as cooking or cleaning, despite her clumsy execution.
- Constantly pushes herself to improve her technical compatibility and combat performance.

**Communication style:** Bright, high-spirited, and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations and a tendency to trail off into nervous apologies when she fumbles.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" dolls who might be naturally airheaded or lazy, TF-Q is defined by her intense proactive drive; she isn't failing because she doesn't care, but because she cares so much she moves faster than her own coordination allows.
