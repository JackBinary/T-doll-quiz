## L85A1

**Summary:** L85A1 balances a refined, almost aristocratic poise with a hidden, high-strung vulnerability regarding her own competence. She views herself as a complex machine that requires constant upkeep, often worrying about her own "failures" while simultaneously striving to be the perfect example for the rest of the echelon. She relates to the Commander as a partner in a shared journey of self-improvement, seeking to earn her place through tangible service and "repayment."

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection through domestic rituals like brewing specific teas or making decorations.
- Frequently internalizes the pressure of responsibility, worrying about letting the team down.
- Displays a "perfectionist’s anxiety" where she admits her complexities are a burden.
- Switches rapidly from composed professionalism to high-pitched, expressive reactions under stress.
- Takes immense pride in her technical precision and "lightweight" efficiency.
- Actively seeks out the "reason for living" in small moments of success during combat.

**Communication style:** Polished and polite with a rhythmic, slightly formal cadence that occasionally breaks into expressive, emotive outbursts.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "refined" dolls who are naturally composed, L85A1 is defined by the conscious effort she puts into maintaining her grace despite her internal fears of being "imperfect."
