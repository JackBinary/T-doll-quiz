## Sten MkII

**Summary:** A self-conscious but earnest workhorse who carries a deep-seated "underdog" complex due to her mass-produced, utilitarian design. She relates to the Commander with a mix of profound insecurity and desperate loyalty, constantly seeking validation for her contributions. Her dominant emotional register is anxious optimism, characterized by a cycle of trying her hardest, failing, and then offering a sincere, slightly embarrassed apology.

**Traits:**
- Expresses insecurity about her "plain" appearance and "weird" name by preemptively downplaying her own worth.
- Overcompensates for perceived unreliability by offering to do extra chores or prepare handmade snacks.
- Reacts to failure with visible distress and immediate self-reproach, often seeking the Commander's reassurance.
- Displays a "can-do" attitude in combat that borders on reckless bravado to prove her utility.
- Blushes and stammers when her personal feelings or affections are acknowledged by the Commander.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, using a high-spirited tone that frequently cracks into stuttering when she becomes flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who are confident in their roles, Sten MkII is defined by the conscious effort she exerts to be useful despite her belief that she is inherently less "special" than her peers.
