## T65

**Summary:** T65 is a high-strung, earnest perfectionist who masks her deep-seated insecurities with a "tough love" exterior. She views her role as a protector and assistant with extreme gravity, often coming across as overbearing because she is terrified of failing the Commander's expectations. She oscillates between a fierce, competitive drive to prove her competence and a flustered, easily embarrassed vulnerability when she is caught off-guard.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander’s habits with a "strict" but well-meaning attitude.
- Internalizes failure as a personal moral failing rather than a tactical setback.
- Compensates for perceived lack of experience by over-preparing and over-performing.
- Expresses affection through small, modest gestures like handmade gifts rather than words.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when her insecurities are teased.
- Maintains a self-imposed "no-nonsense" persona that cracks during holiday celebrations.

**Communication style:** Brash and authoritative on the surface, but frequently prone to stuttering or becoming high-pitched when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" dolls who might be naturally haughty, T65 is driven by an anxious need for validation, making her "strictness" a shield for her desire to be the most reliable doll in the room.
