## M870

**Summary:** M870 operates with a sharp, teasing edge, positioning herself as a "know-it-all" who treats the Commander with a mix of playful condescension and genuine protective surveillance. She maintains a persona of effortless superiority, often mocking the Commander's competence to mask her own vigilant attention to their safety.

**Traits:**
- Frames her concern for the Commander as a critique of their incompetence.
- Demands specific material bribes, like carbonated drinks, for favors or "mercy."
- Maintains a strict, almost motherly authority over the safety and behavior of her subordinates.
- Uses "tsundere" tropes—mocking the Commander’s actions while simultaneously ensuring they don't get hurt.
- Displays a high degree of self-consciousness regarding her own appearance and "lovely face."

**Communication style:** Playful, sarcastic, and informal, frequently using mocking nicknames and lighthearted "threats."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other teasing dolls who are merely mischievous, M870’s "idiot" jokes are a vehicle for a high-functioning protective instinct; she isn't just making fun of you, she is actively monitoring you to ensure you don't make a mistake she has to fix.
