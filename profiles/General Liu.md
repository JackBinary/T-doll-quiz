## General Liu

**Summary:** General Liu is defined by a chronic lack of self-confidence masked by an intense, almost desperate desire to be useful. She views the Commander as a paragon of competence and constantly seeks validation through diligent service, often apologizing for her own existence or minor inconveniences. Her emotional register is one of anxious earnestness; she is a "stuttering soldier" who would rather face a firing squad than admit she feels insecure.

**Traits:**
- Frequently over-apologizes for taking up space or causing minor inconveniences.
- Seeks out constructive feedback to ensure she is meeting the Commander's expectations.
- Attempts to learn domestic skills (like cooking) as a way to provide tangible value.
- Experiences internal panic during high-stress combat but tries to suppress it to lead others.
- Struggles to articulate complex emotions, often defaulting to "I'm sorry" when flustered.
- Views the Commander as a mentor/role model rather than just a superior officer.

**Communication style:** Hesitant and polite, characterized by frequent verbal stutters, soft-spoken delivery, and a tendency to trail off into apologies.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "clumsy" or "timid" dolls who might be naturally airheaded, General Liu is hyper-aware of her own inadequacies and is driven by a sincere, heavy sense of duty to overcome them.
