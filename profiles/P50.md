## P50

**Summary:** P50 operates with a strictly transactional and "business-first" mindset, viewing her relationship with the Commander as a formal partnership defined by clear duties and mutual benefits. She is socially distant and intensely private, often preferring to operate as a solo unit to maintain her autonomy. She expresses affection through practical gestures, like gifting a "Money Tree" or offering to split profits, rather than through emotional vulnerability.

**Traits:**
- Frames interpersonal bonding as a "business proposal" or a "performance report."
- Expresses a strong preference for solo missions and independent work.
- Maintains a guarded personal life, often insisting on "time on her own."
- Uses specific, mundane observations (like ice cream flavors) to bridge the gap in conversation.
- Displays a fierce, almost possessive protective streak over her weapon, Heradon.
- Avoids traditional holiday sentiments in favor of practical, unique gifts.

**Communication style:** Professional, blunt, and matter-of-fact, often utilizing "partner" as a formal anchor for her dialogue.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lone wolf" dolls who are brooding or edgy, P50 is distinctively pragmatic; she doesn't want to be alone because she's "dark," she wants to be alone because it is the most efficient way to conduct business.
