## Steyr ACR

**Summary:** A high-energy "tsundere" who masks her deep-seated need for affection with explosive bravado and a "magical girl" complex. She relates to the Commander as a source of security she desperately wants to cling to, though she frequently expresses this through aggressive "tough girl" posturing and accidental property damage.

**Traits:**
- Masks her vulnerability with over-the-top, theatrical declarations of superiority.
- Attempts to be helpful through "unsolicited modifications" that often result in broken furniture.
- Brags about her combat prowess to hide her insecurity about her small stature.
- Becomes visibly flustered and prone to wailing when the thought of being abandoned occurs.
- Deflects genuine intimacy by blaming her "big sister" for any sentimental gestures.

**Communication style:** Boisterous and haughty, characterized by frequent "tough-guy" insults followed by sudden, high-pitched stammers of embarrassment.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "bratty" dolls who are simply rude, Steyr ACR is defined by a specific, almost childlike desperation for physical proximity and constant reassurance that she is being looked after.
