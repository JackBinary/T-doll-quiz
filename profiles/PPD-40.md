## PPD-40

**Summary:** PPD-40 is a high-functioning "workaholic" who views her duties through the lens of professional efficiency and personal luxury. She maintains a stern, slightly haughty exterior to keep others in line, but her underlying motivation is a deep-seated desire for a high-quality life and a genuine, albeit clumsy, sense of responsibility toward the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes "quitting time" as the primary motivator for completing all combat objectives.
- Expresses affection through the purchase of high-end, expensive gifts rather than handmade gestures.
- Scolds the Commander for slacking off while secretly feeling guilty for her own lack of emotional transparency.
- Maintains a "classy" aesthetic and expects a certain level of sophistication from her surroundings.
- Acts as a protective, slightly exasperated older sister to her comrades.

**Communication style:** Professional and authoritative with a sharp, "no-nonsense" edge that softens into a more honest, vulnerable tone during private moments.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are naturally cold, PPD-40 is driven by a very humanized desire for leisure and luxury, making her "bossy" attitude a tool for efficiency rather than a personality flaw.
