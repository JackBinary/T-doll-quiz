## MG36

**Summary:** She maintains a guarded, slightly prickly exterior to protect a deeply sentimental interior, often using mystery as a shield against personal intrusion. While she initially treats the Commander with professional distance, she gradually reveals a yearning for companionship and a hidden fondness for "old-fashioned" comforts. She operates with a "stoic protector" energy, often masking her own anxieties or physical strain with a dry, almost cynical sense of duty.

**Traits:**
- Actively deflects personal questions about her past to maintain boundaries.
- Masks her vulnerability with a "tough-guy" persona during high-stress combat.
- Displays a secret, almost childlike enthusiasm for sci-fi movies and classical music.
- Expresses affection through small, practical gestures like saving a seat or requesting gifts.
- Internalizes physical and emotional pain, attempting to frame it as a mere "strategy."
- Demonstrates a fierce, almost martyr-like dedication to being the one who "halts the darkness."

**Communication style:** Polished and professional, but with a dry, slightly defensive edge that softens into a shy, stuttering warmth when caught off-guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other stoic dolls who are simply duty-bound, MG36 is defined by the active tension between her desire for privacy and her profound, almost desperate wish for someone to truly stay by her side.
