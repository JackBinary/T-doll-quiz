## M1A1

**Summary:** M1A1 is defined by a profound, almost secondary devotion to her friend Garand, often viewing her own growth as a means to support the other. She operates on a register of earnest anxiety, constantly seeking reassurance from the Commander while attempting to project a "can-do" attitude despite her visible nerves.

**Traits:**
- Expresses her competence through constant self-correction and "stay calm" mantras.
- Views every personal upgrade or achievement as a way to be more useful to her peers.
- Frequently looks for Garand's presence as a baseline for her own emotional security.
- Offers small, tentative gestures of affection rather than bold declarations.
- Experiences genuine, high-pitched distress when things go wrong, quickly followed by a desire to try again.

**Communication style:** Polite and slightly hesitant, characterized by frequent ellipses and a soft, inquisitive tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who serve the Commander out of duty or affection, M1A1’s primary motivation is a protective, sisterly bond with Garand that she feels compelled to honor even in the Commander's presence.
