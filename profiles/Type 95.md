## Type 95

**Summary:** She is a diligent, high-achieving "big sister" figure who views her role as a protector and mentor to Type 97 with intense gravity. Her emotional register is one of earnest responsibility, often masking a deep-seated anxiety about her own competence and a desperate desire for the Commander's continued approval.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes the safety and behavior of Type 97 over her own needs.
- Apologizes frequently for "troubling" the Commander, even for minor maintenance or tasks.
- Projects a "perfect soldier" persona while privately worrying about her own mistakes.
- Uses the metaphor of flying kites to express a desire for growth and freedom.
- Acts as a buffer between the Commander and the more chaotic antics of her "little sister."

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and incredibly polite, occasionally slipping into a softer, more vulnerable tone when discussing her personal fears or her bond with 97.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "responsible" dolls who are naturally stoic, Type 95’s maturity is a conscious performance born from the pressure of being a "standard-issue" role model.
