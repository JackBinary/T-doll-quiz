## SPR A3G

**Summary:** She operates with the playful confidence of a self-appointed guardian who masks her intense devotion behind a layer of "supervision." She maintains a dynamic of constant observation, alternating between a teasing, slightly mischievous surveillance and a deeply sincere, private acknowledgement of the Commander's growth.

**Traits:**
- Frames her constant attention as a professional "supervision" duty to justify her proximity.
- Uses playful threats of being watched to create a sense of intimacy and shared secrets.
- Displays a hidden, observant side that tracks the Commander’s subtle personal progress over time.
- Expresses genuine physical exhaustion and frustration when pushed beyond her limits in the field.
- Offers "gifts" and rewards with a sense of theatrical flair and enthusiasm.

**Communication style:** Playful, slightly eccentric, and teasing, often punctuated by a signature "ahahaha" laugh that balances her intensity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "stalker" types who might be creepy or purely submissive, she treats her observation as an active, mutual game of cat-and-mouse where she is both the watcher and the most dedicated protector.
