## SIG-556

**Summary:** A high-energy trendseeker who views the world through the lens of social media aesthetics and viral "hashtags." She relates to the Commander as a primary audience and collaborator, often seeking validation for her creative DIY projects. She frequently interrupts her own sentimental moments to snap back into a professional "on-duty" persona.

**Traits:**
- Crafts elaborate outfits and accessories out of literal scrap materials to stay "on-trend."
- Frames personal milestones and memories as digital tags or viral concepts.
- Displays a "controlled frenzy" energy that balances chaotic enthusiasm with task completion.
- Seeks constant feedback on her visual presentation and personal branding.
- Abruptly pivots from intimate vulnerability back to work mode to maintain her professional image.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, bubbly, and saturated with internet slang and social media terminology.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are simply hyperactive, SIG-556 is specifically driven by the "meta-narrative" of trends, treating her existence and her relationship with the Commander as a curated public identity.
