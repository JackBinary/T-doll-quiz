## Truvelo-CMS

**Summary:** A soft-spoken, slightly anxious T-doll who constantly struggles with her own perceived lack of presence and volume. She views the Commander as her primary source of validation and often seeks to prove her competence through diligent, if sometimes clumsy, effort. She oscillates between being a timid "wallflower" and a determined supporter who will fiercely (if nervously) lecture the Commander on their health.

**Traits:**
- Frequently apologizes or repeats herself to ensure she has been heard.
- Expresses affection through acts of service and "nagging" for the Commander's well-being.
- Experiences visible distress when faced with rowdy children or overwhelming social chaos.
- Shows a tendency to internalize failure, worrying about repair costs and personal inadequacy.
- Secretly desires to be seen as dignified and capable rather than just "the quiet one."

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and hesitant, often using ellipses and "um" as she searches for the right words, but capable of sudden, high-energy outbursts when flustered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other shy dolls who might be naturally stoic or distant, Truvelo-CMS is actively trying to "turn up her volume" to be useful, making her personality a mix of genuine bashfulness and a proactive, almost desperate, desire to be noticed.
