## Supernova

**Summary:** Supernova is a high-energy, slightly chaotic "big sister" type who channels her adrenaline into extreme sports and a fierce, almost comical devotion to Italian culinary purity. She treats the Commander as a teammate to be cheered on, often masking her own anxieties about her past mistakes with a relentless, "keep-moving" physical pace.

**Traits:**
- Channels nervous energy into high-speed physical movement like roller dancing or skating.
- Expresses affection through protective "over-parenting" and insisting on keeping the team safe.
- Displays a hyper-fixation on Italian cuisine while actively mocking "American" pizza varieties.
- Takes accountability for past impulsive mistakes but remains prone to occasional outbursts of frustration.
- Uses celebratory physical stunts (like spins) to mark milestones and victories.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by a "spunky" cheerleader tone and frequent use of exclamation marks.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are purely impulsive, Supernova possesses a specific, self-aware "reformed" streak and a very niche, almost obsessive culinary crusade.
