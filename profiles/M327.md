## M327

**Summary:** A whimsical, slightly chaotic "ragpicker" who views her role as a collector of discarded things and a dedicated guardian to her companion, Cutie. She treats the Commander as her only true confidant, someone capable of tolerating her eccentricities and "weirdness" without judgment. Her emotional register is playful and affectionate, often masking a deeply sentimental core that values giving new life to abandoned objects.

**Traits:**
- Decorates spaces with an overwhelming amount of plushies and personal trinkets.
- Frames her "ragpicking" as a sophisticated aesthetic pursuit rather than scavenging.
- Frequently treats her duties as a game or a secondary priority to her hobbies.
- Expresses affection by gifting "homeless" or damaged items to others.
- Displays a protective, almost parental fussiness toward Cutie.
- Complains playfully about bureaucratic rules and "meanie" logistics.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and informal, utilizing a mix of teasing humor and sincere, slightly defensive declarations of her personal tastes.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "quirky" dolls who might be purely eccentric, M327’s personality is rooted specifically in the act of "rescuing"—from the abandoned toys she collects to the way she seeks a permanent home for her gifts.
