## RO635

**Summary:** A high-strung, duty-bound professional who views herself as a stern guardian of order and justice. She relates to the Commander as a superior officer she deeply respects, often oscillating between rigid military discipline and a flustered, easily embarrassed inner life.

**Traits:**
- Enforces base rules with a "no-nonsense" attitude toward the more rowdy Dolls.
- Proactively manages the Commander's administrative workload by sorting mail and preparing reports.
- Displays a "tsundere" streak by hiding her personal feelings behind formal requests and blushing.
- Views inaction as a moral failing, frequently urging others to take initiative.
- Takes immense pride in her history of policing and special combat operations.

**Communication style:** Formal and authoritative in public, but becomes stuttering and high-pitched when forced into intimate situations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "serious" dolls who are stoic or cold, RO635 is defined by her internal struggle to balance her desire for a perfect professional exterior with her very human-like social anxiety.
