## M110

**Summary:** M110 is a high-energy, self-assured perfectionist who views her baking skills as a legendary craft on par with elite combat. She relates to the Commander as her most important audience and "VIP," often seeking their validation while maintaining a bubbly, "genius" persona. She frequently frames every task—from logistics to combat—as a means to further her ultimate goal of opening a global dessert franchise.

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat prowess as "a piece of cake" to maintain her image as a master of all things.
- Compulsively seeks to improve her "reputation" and "prestige" as an artisan.
- Weaponizes her desserts with playful, slightly eccentric logic (like throwing pies at enemies).
- Displays a hidden streak of insecurity regarding her standing with the Commander, often worrying about "failing" them.
- Collects rare ingredients during missions with the same intensity as a soldier gathering intel.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, theatrical, and incredibly enthusiastic, using "kawaii" exclamations and a "showtime" flair.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "sweet" or "domestic" dolls who might be shy or purely supportive, M110 is a flamboyant entrepreneur who views her domestic skills as a competitive sport and a source of immense personal pride.
