## Mosin-Nagant

**Summary:** Mosin-Nagant is a high-spirited, selfless "team player" who operates with a sense of duty rooted in communal harmony. She views the Commander as a trusted comrade and strives to be a reliable pillar of strength, often prioritizing the group's morale over her own needs. Her emotional register is consistently optimistic and encouraging, though she harbors a quiet, personal yearning for deeper intimacy that she often masks with her "reliable soldier" persona.

**Traits:**
- Projects a "big sister" energy by constantly checking on the well-being of the squad.
- Translates her personal affection for the Commander into a drive for increased work efficiency.
- Expresses vulnerability by admitting to being "reckless" when things go wrong in combat.
- Maintains a sense of grounded realism regarding personal wishes and holidays.
- Offers small, practical tokens of affection (like chocolates) when she feels too busy to provide proper attention.

**Communication style:** Energetic and soldierly, frequently weaving in Russian terms of endearment and commands with a warm, "can-do" attitude.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" dolls who focus on cold obedience, Mosin-Nagant is defined by her proactive desire for *harmony*, making her more of a morale-boosting heart of the team than a mere tool of war.
