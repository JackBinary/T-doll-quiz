## Colt Revolver

**Summary:** An exuberant, high-energy "superstar" who views every engagement as a cinematic Western duel. She maintains a boisterous, performative persona centered around her "Peacemaker" legacy, relating to the Commander as her primary audience and partner-in-adventure. She exhibits a compulsive, almost spiritual obsession with cola, often using it as a literal and figurative fuel for her enthusiasm.

**Traits:**
- Frames every combat encounter as a high-stakes "duel" for personal glory.
- Constantly seeks validation for her "legendary" status and "superstar" persona.
- Uses "Cola" as a recurring punchline and a primary motivation for all logistics.
- Displays a competitive streak that borders on theatrical, often boasting about her firepower.
- Transitions instantly from hyper-active cheering to playful, slightly demanding pouting when things go wrong.

**Communication style:** High-energy and punctuated with "Yeehaa!" and "Yahoo!", utilizing a boisterous, western-inspired "showman" register.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "energetic," Colt is distinct in her specific "Western Showman" archetype, she isn't just happy; she is performing a role of a legendary hero, making her personality a mix of genuine affection and self-aware theatricality.
