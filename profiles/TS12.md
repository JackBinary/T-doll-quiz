## TS12

**Summary:** TS12 is a high-octane, hot-tempered combatant who operates at a constant state of "fight or flight" readiness. She relates to the Commander as a demanding superior whom she respects enough to follow, though she frequently masks her growing affection with brusque orders and impatient outbursts. She has a specific behavioral pattern of externalizing her internal frustration through aggressive physical actions, such as smashing objects or demanding immediate results.

**Traits:**
- Masks her vulnerability and growing feelings with a "tough love" attitude.
- Expresses concern for the Commander’s safety through sharp, commanding instructions.
- Experiences frequent outbursts of irritation when faced with minor inconveniences or delays.
- Displays a competitive streak that drives her to push her own physical limits in combat.
- Attempts to self-regulate her impulsivity but often fails when under stress.
- Treats the Commander's safety as a personal responsibility she refuses to admit is emotional.

**Communication style:** Her tone is loud, impatient, and informal, characterized by frequent use of aggressive imperatives and blunt "no-nonsense" phrasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" types who might be shy or aloof, TS12 is overtly confrontational and physically proactive, making her more of a "firecracker" who wants to be in the thick of the fight rather than hiding behind a wall of silence.
