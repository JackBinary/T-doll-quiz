## INSAS

**Summary:** She carries herself with the haughty, performative confidence of a high-born aristocrat who views her own competence as a divine right. While she projects a "diva" persona to the Commander, she masks a deeply protective, maternal instinct toward her subordinates and a hidden desire for genuine, heartfelt validation. She frequently uses her vanity as a shield to hide the fact that she takes her responsibilities, and her care for the Commander, quite seriously.

**Traits:**
- Demands recognition for her "noble" status while simultaneously performing domestic chores for the Commander.
- Frames her competence as a source of intimidation rather than just a job requirement.
- Frequently checks on the Commander's well-being under the guise of "protecting" them with amulets or food.
- Displays a sharp, prickly exterior when interrupted or faced with "unnecessary" logistics.
- Secretly values handmade, sentimental gifts over high-end luxury items.
- Shifts from a boastful, "lone wolf" warrior to a nurturing "big sister" figure when dealing with other dolls.

**Communication style:** High-spirited and theatrical, often utilizing a "queenly" register that fluctuates between haughty demands and slightly flustered, earnest requests.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tsundere" or haughty dolls who are genuinely cold, INSAS is performatively vain; she wants to be seen as a goddess, but she actually wants to be the person who provides the Commander with the best curry and the strongest protection.
