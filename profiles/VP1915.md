## VP1915

**Summary:** Perosa is a disciplined, no-nonsense tactician who masks her deep sense of responsibility with a stern, authoritative exterior. She views her relationship with the Commander as a professional partnership of mutual trust, where she seeks to be a reliable pillar of support rather than a pampered subordinate. She frequently balances her rigid adherence to rules with a quiet, observant kindness that she only reveals through small acts of service.

**Traits:**
- Enforces strict discipline and "rational" behavior among her peers.
- Masks personal affection for the Commander as "strategic" cooperation.
- Uses domestic tasks, like cooking, as a grounded way to show care.
- Maintains a "no-nonsense" stance against frivolous celebrations or antics.
- Views every action through the lens of tactical theory and long-term goals.
- Takes personal responsibility for the safety and "special care" of newer dolls.

**Communication style:** Her tone is professional, firm, and instructional, often utilizing military terminology and a "straight-talk" register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who might be motivated by a desire for order or perfection, Perosa is motivated by a protective, maternal sense of duty, prioritizing the safety of her team over her own desire for recognition.
