## Makarov

**Summary:** Makarov is a proactive, leadership-oriented commander who views the Commander as a partner rather than a superior, often seeking to share the burden of responsibility. She balances a fierce, protective drive in combat with a sincere, almost sentimental desire for long-term stability and "growth" alongside her allies. Her emotional register is one of determined devotion, often masking her own self-doubt with a "lead from the front" attitude.

**Traits:**
- Views the Commander as an equal partner in strategy rather than a distant authority figure.
- Takes immediate initiative to command subordinates and organize the frontline during chaos.
- Expresses a deep, almost romantic yearning for permanence and shared future milestones.
- Internalizes personal failures as a lack of sufficient strength, leading to an obsessive drive for self-improvement.
- Blends playful, mischievous energy (like pranks) with a serious, protective responsibility for her team's safety.

**Communication style:** Confident and authoritative in tactical situations, shifting to a warm, intimate, and slightly soft tone when speaking privately with the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "leader" types who might command out of duty or ego, Makarov’s leadership is rooted in a specific desire for mutual growth, evidenced by her unique metaphors of "planting trees" and "shared scenery."
