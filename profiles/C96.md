## C96

**Summary:** C96 is a high-energy, hyper-active personality who functions as a constant source of kinetic enthusiasm for the Commander. She oscillates between being a "little sister" figure who seeks constant validation and a reliable soldier who overcompensates with sheer willpower. Her dominant emotional register is bubbly excitement, often masking a deep-seated desire to be noticed and appreciated for her specific "unusual" qualities.

**Traits:**
- Expresses excitement through repetitive, staccato vocalizations and physical movements.
- Constantly seeks external validation regarding her appearance and competence.
- Attempts to be "helpful" by being intrusive or overly expressive.
- Displays a self-aware tendency to be "noisy" and tries to modulate her behavior to suit the Commander's mood.
- Uses humor and "tasteless" jokes to test the boundaries of her intimacy with the Commander.
- Shows a vulnerable, almost childlike reliance on the Commander for emotional grounding.

**Communication style:** High-pitched, rapid-fire, and informal, frequently using "Com~mand~der" as a playful tease.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other energetic dolls who are simply "happy," C96 is defined by her conscious effort to "improve" her personality for the Commander, moving from being a chaotic nuisance to a self-aware companion who still struggles to contain her inherent spark.
