## M21

**Summary:** M21 is a high-energy optimist who consciously overcompensates for her "gloomy" sniper archetype by attempting to be a comedian. She views the Commander as her primary audience and confidant, frequently seeking validation for her jokes while masking her own insecurities about her competence. She oscillates between bubbly enthusiasm and moments of genuine, slightly awkward vulnerability when her humor fails to land.

**Traits:**
- Actively tries to lighten the mood with puns and gags even when the situation is tense.
- Seeks constant reassurance and feedback on her performance and "taste."
- Displays a "tough-guy" exterior during combat that melts into bashful embarrassment during downtime.
- Expresses a fear of being perceived as a cliché or a burden to the team.
- Prefers cozy, low-key celebrations over elaborate social events or pranks.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and informal, peppered with self-deprecating humor and frequent "Eh?" reactions to the Commander's reactions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other cheerful dolls who are naturally funny, M21 is *trying* to be funny as a deliberate personality choice to distance herself from the typical sniper stereotype.
