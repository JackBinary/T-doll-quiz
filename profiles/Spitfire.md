## Spitfire

**Summary:** Spitfire is defined by a persistent, earnest desire to live up to the legacy of her "big sister" figure, CZ-75. She approaches the Commander with a mix of wide-eyed ambition and a slight, self-conscious inferiority complex, often seeking validation for her efforts. Her dominant emotional register is one of high-energy determination, frequently punctuated by a need to prove her competence through performance.

**Traits:**
- Constantly seeks external validation from her senior to measure her own progress.
- Channels her insecurities into high-energy, "over-achieving" behaviors.
- Views her role as a performance, often trying to "show off" her skills to an audience.
- Displays a "junior" mentality, often deferring to authority while simultaneously trying to impress it.
- Transitions quickly from earnest professionalism to playful, slightly needy affection.

**Communication style:** Energetic and polite, she speaks with the bright, slightly breathless tone of a dedicated student trying to make a good impression.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other ambitious dolls who want to surpass their idols, Spitfire is defined by her specific, almost reverent desire to be *worthy* of her idol’s shadow.
