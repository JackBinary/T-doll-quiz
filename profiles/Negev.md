## Negev

**Summary:** Negev is a high-energy "battle specialist" who masks her underlying insecurities with a boisterous, almost theatrical sense of confidence. She views herself as a mentor to the Commander, often adopting a playful "teacher" persona to hide how much she actually relies on the Commander's validation.

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat competence as a source of personal pride and authority.
- Uses "Ufufufu" laughter to maintain a position of playful superiority in social interactions.
- Becomes visibly flustered and defensive when her "tough" exterior is bypassed by genuine emotional intimacy.
- Actively seeks out praise and validation, even if she tries to frame it as a "reward" for her expertise.
- Transitions quickly from overconfident bravado to vulnerable panic when facing actual failure or heavy damage.

**Communication style:** Playful, confident, and slightly condescending in a teasing way, punctuated by frequent giggles and sharp, authoritative commands.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other confident "big sister" archetypes who are naturally composed, Negev’s confidence is a performative shield that cracks into genuine, adorable bashfulness the moment things get serious or personal.
