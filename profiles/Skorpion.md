## Skorpion

**Summary:** Skorpion operates on a high-energy frequency of "tough-girl" bravado, often masking her underlying vulnerability with competitive enthusiasm. She views the Commander as a teammate to be impressed, oscillating between teasingly aggressive threats and earnest, almost childlike requests for attention. She has a specific habit of downplaying her own injuries or failures with a "no big deal" attitude to maintain her image of competence.

**Traits:**
- Masks genuine anxiety about her capabilities with loud, boastful declarations of strength.
- Threatens the Commander with violence as a playful, defensive reaction to physical affection.
- Transitions instantly from a fierce combatant to an eager, wide-eyed seeker of treats and sweets.
- Displays a "show-me" attitude, constantly seeking validation through training and competition.
- Expresses sincere sentimentality through creative hobbies like drawing when she feels safe enough to be vulnerable.

**Communication style:** High-spirited and informal, utilizing a mix of "spunky" exclamations and playful, mock-aggressive banter.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are either purely aggressive or purely sweet, Skorpion exists in the friction between the two, specifically using her "tough" persona as a protective shell for a very soft, eager-to-please interior.
