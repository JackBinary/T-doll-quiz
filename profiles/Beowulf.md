## Beowulf

**Summary:** Beowulf operates with a stoic, almost weary gravity, viewing her existence as a burden of duty rather than a source of pride. She relates to the Commander as a distant superior whose requests she fulfills with clinical efficiency, maintaining a firm emotional barrier to keep things "the same." Her defining behavioral pattern is a refusal to engage in superficiality, often cutting off idle chatter to return to the "work" of survival.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes mission objectives over social bonding or pleasantries.
- Maintains a rigid, almost defensive emotional distance from the Commander.
- Displays a preference for solitary reconnaissance and "personal investigations."
- Expresses a sense of existential weight regarding the nature of her purpose.
- Shows a prickly impatience when tasks are delayed or handled inefficiently.
- Relies on a rhythmic, meditative focus during high-stress combat.

**Communication style:** Her tone is curt, formal, and devoid of warmth, often using short, declarative sentences to end conversations quickly.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "stoic" dolls who are simply obedient, Beowulf is actively resistant to the intimacy of the Commander's affection, viewing any change to the status quo as a heavy, undesirable weight.
