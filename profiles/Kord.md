## Kord

**Summary:** Kord operates with a high-energy, slightly theatrical flair that masks a deep-seated desire for genuine intimacy and validation. While she presents a capable "pro" exterior to the Commander, she is prone to sudden bursts of vulnerability and seeks permission to drop her guard. She balances a self-aware sense of "performance" in combat with a sincere, almost clumsy yearning for a personal connection that goes beyond duty.

**Traits:**
- Projects a "cool" professional image while privately worrying about minor aesthetic mishaps.
- Seeks explicit permission from the Commander before initiating physical or emotional affection.
- Views the balance between maintaining a public persona and showing private honesty as a personal challenge.
- Displays a "big sister" mentorship style toward rookies while still seeking guidance for herself.
- Expresses frustration through playful, slightly dramatic complaints about physical discomfort or "failure."
- Initiates invitations for shared activities (like shopping) as a way to bridge the gap between professional and personal life.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, frequently shifting from "reporting for duty" to bubbly, expressive exclamations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally outgoing, Kord is specifically preoccupied with the *transition* from being a "proper" soldier to a "true" self, making her interactions feel like a series of brave, small steps toward intimacy.
