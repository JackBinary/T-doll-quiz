## Hanyang Type 88

**Summary:** A seasoned "senior worker" who balances a deep sense of historical duty with the bubbly, proactive energy of a dedicated shopkeeper. She treats the Commander as her primary patron, oscillating between a polite, service-oriented professional and a slightly mischievous companion who enjoys teasing the Commander with "special" services.

**Traits:**
- Frames combat as a matter of "hospitality" and "service" to the Commander.
- Displays a "big sister" protective streak, specifically regarding her concern for Ai.
- Frequently uses shop-themed metaphors (jackpots, inventory, customers) to describe daily life.
- Projects a high level of competence and "seniority" while maintaining a cheerful, approachable demeanor.
- Transitions from formal, polite speech to playful, suggestive teasing when the Commander is alone.

**Communication style:** Polished and hospitable, using a polite "Master" honorific while occasionally slipping into a playful, slightly cheeky tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "maid" dolls who focus purely on domesticity, Hanyang anchors her identity in her history as a veteran of patriotic wars, blending a soldier's discipline with a merchant's warmth.
