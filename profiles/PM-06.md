## PM-06

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, boundary-pushing extrovert who treats the Commander as a partner-in-crime rather than a superior officer. Her emotional register is one of playful, slightly mischievous excitement, often seeking out "nighttime" activities and sensory thrills. She has a distinct behavioral pattern of obsessively associating the color red with her identity, from furniture to the blood of her enemies.

**Traits:**
- Frequently initiates "extracurricular" activities to distract from official duties.
- Frames combat and violence through a lens of aesthetic appreciation.
- Seeks constant physical proximity and enthusiastic validation from the Commander.
- Displays a "live for the moment" attitude that borders on recklessness.
- Expresses affection through the sharing of alcohol and high-energy social invitations.
- Treats new recruits as personal projects to be "invited" into her circle.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, bubbly, and informal, frequently punctuated by giggles and a "we're in this together" conspiratorial tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other energetic "little sister" types who are purely innocent, PM-06 possesses a darker, almost macabre fascination with violence and red wine that gives her playfulness a sharp, predatory edge.
