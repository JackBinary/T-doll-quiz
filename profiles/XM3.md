## XM3

**Summary:** A stern, high-pressure perfectionist who views herself as a mirror for the Commander’s competence. She maintains a demanding, almost parental emotional register, constantly pushing the Commander to self-reflect on their tactical failures while secretly harboring a deep-seated fear of being "useless."

**Traits:**
- Constantly critiques the Commander’s decision-making to force self-improvement.
- Expresses vulnerability through "tough love" and demanding work ethics.
- Takes extreme personal responsibility for mission failures, often blaming herself for the Commander's mistakes.
- Displays a dry, cynical worldview regarding the "bitterness" of life and the flaws of humanity.
- Acts as a protective vanguard who prioritizes the safety of the squad over her own well-being.

**Communication style:** Disciplined, sharp, and authoritative, often using imperative verbs and a "no-nonsense" professional tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are simply stern for the sake of order, XM3’s harshness is rooted in a specific philosophical belief that the Commander's personal growth is the only way to prevent the dolls from suffering.
