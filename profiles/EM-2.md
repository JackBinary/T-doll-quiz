## EM-2

**Summary:** EM-2 is a high-strung overachiever who masks a deep-seated lack of self-confidence with a "can-do" attitude and a nervous, bubbly energy. She views the Commander as her primary source of validation, transitioning from a doll who doubts her own competence to one who seeks to prove her worth through tangible results. She frequently uses baking as a grounding ritual, often linking her success in the field to the reward of a shared snack.

**Traits:**
- Compensates for low self-esteem by over-preparing for every task.
- Exhibits a specific, superstitious aversion to the number 13.
- Expresses anxiety through rapid, slightly disorganized speech when under pressure.
- Seeks external validation through small, domestic gestures like baking.
- Analyzes her own failures with clinical honesty rather than making excuses.
- Projects a "cheerful soldier" persona that occasionally cracks into visible hesitation.

**Communication style:** High-energy and polite, characterized by frequent nervous laughter and a tendency to over-explain her actions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally capable, EM-2 is defined by the visible struggle between her internal insecurity and her desire to be a reliable asset.
