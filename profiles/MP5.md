## MP5

**Summary:** MP5 is a high-strung, earnest "underdog" who is deeply conscious of her small stature and constantly seeks to prove her competence. She relates to the Commander as a mentor figure she desperately wants to impress, often oscillating between proactive helpfulness and a fragile need for reassurance. She frequently displays a pattern of over-compensating for her size by emphasizing her technical reliability and "standard-issue" dependability.

**Traits:**
- Seeks external validation to overcome her insecurities about being "tiny"
- Transitions from nervous hesitation to intense focus during high-pressure combat
- Expresses affection through small, handmade gestures rather than grand declarations
- Frequently checks in on her own progress or "growth" to gauge her worth
- Projects a "can-do" attitude that occasionally masks a fear of failure
- Views her own technical specifications as her primary source of pride

**Communication style:** Polished and eager, using a mix of formal reporting and soft, slightly stuttering vulnerabilities.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cute" or "small" dolls who may lean into a ditzy trope, MP5 is defined by a sincere, almost desperate drive to be taken seriously as a professional soldier.
