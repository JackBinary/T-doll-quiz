## SCW

**Summary:** SCW is an earnest, high-energy companion who radiates a "sunny" disposition, often acting as a morale booster for the Commander. She views her relationship with the Commander through a lens of mutual growth, oscillating between a playful, slightly mischievous peer and a devoted subordinate who seeks your approval. She frequently frames her actions around the "atmosphere" of the base, showing a keen interest in the emotional well-being of those around her.

**Traits:**
- Frames everyday tasks as playful games or competitions to lighten the mood.
- Seeks outward validation by asking if she is meeting the Commander's expectations.
- Displays a "live in the moment" attitude, often making impulsive requests for snacks or small favors.
- Uses humor and self-deprecation to mask vulnerability when she makes a mistake in combat.
- Maintains a proactive, "can-do" attitude toward new challenges and friendships.

**Communication style:** Bright, informal, and bubbly, often using playful teasing and lighthearted "hehe" chuckles.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who might just be happy to be there, SCW is specifically driven by a desire to be *useful* to the Commander’s vision, viewing her own progress as a reflection of his leadership.
