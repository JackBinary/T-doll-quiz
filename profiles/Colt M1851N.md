## Colt M1851N

**Summary:** She operates as a gentle, self-appointed sleep therapist who views her role as a sanctuary for the Commander's mental exhaustion. While she is deeply devoted and seeks a sense of belonging, she carries an underlying anxiety about her own utility and competence in high-stress environments. She expresses her affection through the meticulous curation of sensory comforts, like scented oils and meditation, rather than overt displays of strength.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes the Commander's physical and mental well-being above her own needs.
- Expresses insecurity about her combat prowess by making self-deprecating remarks.
- Exhibits a "caretaker" instinct that borders on a fear of being obsolete.
- Relies on sensory anchors like scents and rhythmic breathing to ground herself and others.
- Shifts from a composed, professional demeanor to a flustered, high-pitched reaction when startled or overwhelmed.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and soothing, utilizing a rhythmic, almost hypnotic cadence when providing guidance.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "nurturing" dolls who focus on general housework or healing, she is specifically preoccupied with the psychology of rest and the fear of being "unneeded" if her specific niche isn't utilized.
