## K31

**Summary:** K31 is a high-functioning perfectionist who views existence through the lens of precision, chronometry, and destiny. She maintains a playful, almost mocking seniority over the Commander, treating their relationship as a shared experiment in timing rather than a typical subordinate bond. She oscillates between a "strict timekeeper" persona and a mischievous observer who enjoys teasing the Commander for their lack of punctuality.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction as a predestined event or a "lucky moment"
- Monitors the Commander’s schedule with a mix of maternal concern and mocking observation
- Obsesses over synchronization, demanding that everyone operate on her specific "watch"
- Uses time-related metaphors to express frustration or provide encouragement
- Displays a "bored" exterior to mask her intense focus on optimizing mission records
- Expresses affection through playful reprimands about sleep schedules and punctuality

**Communication style:** Sophisticated and authoritative, using a teasing, slightly condescending tone that borders on "knowledgeable older sister."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other perfectionists who seek order for the sake of efficiency, K31 treats time as a mystical, almost spiritual constant, viewing her role as a guardian of destiny rather than just a logistics officer.
