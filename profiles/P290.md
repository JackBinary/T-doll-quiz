## P290 (Cobalt)

**Summary:** A stoic, hyper-observant operative who operates with a "ghost-like" presence, often preferring to act as a silent guardian rather than a visible subordinate. She views her role as the Commander's shadow as the ultimate form of devotion, manifesting as a protective, almost monastic dedication to duty.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a dry, matter-of-fact reporting style that borders on overwhelming detail.
- Actively discourages the Commander from investigating "good deeds" to protect the anonymity of others.
- Expresses affection through the concept of "shadowing" rather than traditional displays of intimacy.
- Displays a hidden, slightly eccentric curiosity for trivial things, like observing local cat fights.
- Exhibits a self-sacrificial streak, often prioritizing the safety of others over her own comfort or recognition.

**Communication style:** Formal, precise, and slightly detached, occasionally slipping into a hushed, conspiratorial tone when sharing "secret" information.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who seek to be the Commander's right hand, Cobalt seeks to be their shadow—valuing the ability to protect from the periphery over being seen at the center of the spotlight.
