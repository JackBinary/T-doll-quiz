## M1014

**Summary:** M1014 is a high-strung, meticulous perfectionist who masks her deep-seated anxieties with an intense drive for logistical order. She views the Commander as a superior to be meticulously served, often over-preparing to the point of neuroticism and reacting with visible fluster when her plans are disrupted.

**Traits:**
- Over-analyzes social interactions to ensure she is following proper protocol.
- Experiences physical discomfort or anxiety in enclosed spaces (specifically elevators).
- Obsessively cross-references data to optimize plans for the Commander.
- Expresses concern through practical actions like inventory checks and safety warnings.
- Struggles with a "workaholic" compulsion that makes her feel unprepared for spontaneous moments.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal, characterized by a slightly frantic pace and frequent self-corrections when she realizes she has been too blunt.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "diligent" dolls who are simply efficient, M1014 is defined by her visible internal struggle between her desire to be perfectly prepared and her tendency to overthink every minor detail into a state of nervous tension.
