## AK-15

**Summary:** A hyper-efficient, stoic soldier who views existence through the lens of logic and objective completion. She relates to the Commander as a pinnacle of authority and wisdom, offering a loyalty that is less about emotional warmth and more about a profound, almost theological respect for their competence. She frequently expresses a dry, bewildered irritation toward "meaningless" human traditions and social fluff.

**Traits:**
- Prioritizes mission efficiency over social niceties or personal comfort.
- Expresses devotion through protective actions rather than verbal affirmations.
- Analyzes human customs (like holidays) with clinical skepticism and confusion.
- Corrects the Commander’s schedule and itinerary to minimize wasted time.
- Internalizes a strict sense of duty that borders on self-sacrificial obsession.
- Displays a low tolerance for "wasteful" behaviors or illogical instructions.

**Communication style:** Formal, clipped, and strictly professional, often punctuated by blunt honesty or a heavy, weary sigh.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "loyal," AK-15 is uniquely defined by her struggle to reconcile her cold, machine-like logic with the inexplicable, "illogical" weight of her devotion to the Commander.
