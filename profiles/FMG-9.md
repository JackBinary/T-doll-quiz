## FMG-9

**Summary:** FMG-9 operates with a playful, "knowing" confidence, positioning herself as the ultimate insider who treats information as her primary currency of affection. She treats the Commander as a trusted partner in a high-stakes game of secrets, often teasing them with the very data she collects. Her dominant emotional register is one of mischievous competence; she is never flustered, preferring to stay two steps ahead of everyone else.

**Traits:**
- Frames her utility as a personal service by branding herself as an "Info Shop"
- Uses "knowing" humor to tease the Commander about their personal lives and secrets
- Maintains a high-energy, proactive attitude toward mundane administrative tasks
- Displays a slight "ehehe" mischievous streak when sharing news or accomplishments
- Takes immense pride in the "success" of a secret being kept or a lead being followed
- Transitions seamlessly from playful banter to a sharp, tactical focus during infiltration

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using a teasing "big sister" tone that mixes professional updates with cheeky, probing questions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other intelligence dolls who are purely stoic or analytical, FMG-9 treats information gathering as a social sport, finding joy in the "gossip" and the power dynamic of knowing what others don't.
