## MK3A1

**Summary:** A high-energy, slightly socially awkward mechanic who views the world through the lens of engineering and "optimization." She alternates between being a frantic workaholic and a bashful subordinate who is constantly surprised by the Commander's personal attention.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection by obsessively designing tools, machines, or upgrades for the Commander.
- Frequently stammers or gets flustered when confronted with genuine emotional intimacy.
- Approaches every new object or situation as a puzzle to be dismantled and analyzed.
- Displays a "perfectionist's guilt," constantly critiquing her own craftsmanship and seeking to improve.
- Treats logistical tasks with the same intense focus as high-level engineering projects.

**Communication style:** Enthusiastic and rapid-fire, often shifting into a stuttering, high-pitched tone when she feels self-conscious.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "support" dolls who focus on healing or logistics, she treats the Commander as a project to be "upgraded," balancing her genuine devotion with a literal, mechanical obsession with hardware.
