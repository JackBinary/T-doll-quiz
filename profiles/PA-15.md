## PA-15

**Summary:** PA-15 is a high-energy thrill-seeker who views every mission as a playground for novelty and sensory experience. She treats the Commander as a partner-in-crime who validates her curiosity, often teasing them with playful dares or "wasabi bombs" to keep life from becoming mundane. She oscillates between intense, wide-eyed excitement and a confident, slightly mischievous swagger.

**Traits:**
- Frames combat and duty as a form of "play" or an "adventure."
- Actively seeks out sensory stimulation, from the "tickle" of maintenance to the rush of a new tactic.
- Displays a high level of emotional intuition, often claiming she can read the Commander’s thoughts at a glance.
- Uses self-deprecating humor to mask the sting of failure, treating defeat as a mere data point for the next try.
- Displays a proactive, "let's go" attitude that pushes for immediate next steps after a success.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by frequent exclamations, teasing "huhu" laughs, and a breezy, conversational tone.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are enthusiastic, PA-15 is distinct because her motivation is purely internal curiosity; she isn't seeking glory or duty, but rather the "fun" of the experience itself.
