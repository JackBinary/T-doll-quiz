## MAG-7

**Summary:** A high-energy, competitive gamer who views life through the lens of esports and "pro-tier" mechanics. She treats the Commander as a gaming rival and a teammate, maintaining a playful, teasing dynamic fueled by her desire to be the best at everything she touches.

**Traits:**
- Brags about her "mad skills" and high APM to mask her underlying desire for recognition.
- Teases the Commander with "trash talk" but shows genuine concern by offering to play more relaxing games.
- Projects a mischievous persona by attributing her actions to a "spirit" named Ukwulu.
- Exhibits a "try-hard" mentality, becoming visibly frustrated when she faces genuine setbacks or losses.
- Negotiates for perks (like TV time) even when she is being the one to concede in an argument.

**Communication style:** Energetic, colloquial, and heavy on gaming slang, often shifting between boastful "pro-gamer" bravado and playful, sisterly teasing.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are simply "hyper," MAG-7’s entire personality is structured around the specific subculture of competitive gaming, making her interactions feel like a constant, meta-commentary on a shared hobby.
