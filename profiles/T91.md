## T91

**Summary:** T91 is a high-energy "overachiever" who views her existence as a series of benchmarks to be cleared for the Commander's benefit. Her emotional register is one of earnest, sunny optimism, and she treats the Commander as her primary audience for her growth. She frequently seeks validation through proactive service, often over-preparing for small tasks to prove her reliability.

**Traits:**
- Proactively seeks out "next steps" the moment a task is completed.
- Volunteers for training roles to help newer dolls integrate.
- Expresses affection through persistent, iterative trial-and-error (e.g., perfecting a specific recipe).
- Frequently uses "showtime" language to frame combat as a performance.
- Internalizes failure as a personal hurdle to be overcome by "enduring" the next round.
- Offers specific, thoughtful gestures of comfort like large cups of milk tea.

**Communication style:** Highly enthusiastic and polite, utilizing exclamation points and a "can-do" attitude that borders on bubbly.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who might be impulsive or chaotic, T91 is defined by a structured, almost studious desire to be the most "useful" tool in the Commander's arsenal.
