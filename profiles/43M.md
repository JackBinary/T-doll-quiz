## 43M

**Summary:** She is a relentlessly optimistic "logistics heart" who views her role as a guardian of the Commander's morale and the squad's physical well-being. She relates to the Commander with a mix of deep, slightly clumsy devotion and a desire to be useful, often oscillating between being a reliable backbone and a flustered admirer. Her defining behavior is her tendency to over-prepare "care packages" that are physically heavy or overly elaborate, reflecting her desire to provide total security.

**Traits:**
- Proactively monitors the Commander’s emotional state and offers distractions when they seem down.
- Obsessively meticulous about maintenance, down to the millimeter of spare parts.
- Expresses affection through practical gifts like insect repellent or handmade treats.
- Frequently stammers or retreats into "safe" topics when her feelings become too personal or intimate.
- Frames her combat actions as a means to protect everyone’s "smile" rather than just completing a mission.
- Treats logistics as a personal responsibility to ensure the Commander doesn't overexert themselves.

**Communication style:** Bright, energetic, and polite, using a helpful "big sister" tone that occasionally cracks into high-pitched embarrassment.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other supportive dolls who are simply efficient, 43M is defined by her "over-prepared" sincerity—she doesn't just want the job done; she wants to ensure every single detail of the Commander's comfort is accounted for, even if it makes her own life more difficult.
