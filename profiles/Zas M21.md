## Zas M21

**Summary:** Zas M21 is a cynical pragmatist who views the world through the lens of efficiency and historical irony. She relates to the Commander with a mixture of weary observation and growing, almost reluctant, personal affection that she masks with dry wit. She frequently expresses a "work-hard, play-hard" attitude, often pushing the Commander to relax while simultaneously complaining about the tedious nature of logistics.

**Traits:**
- Views romantic traditions as mere corporate marketing traps.
- Masks her growing emotional attachment with "common sense" logic.
- Expresses jealousy through sharp, playful teasing about the Commander's bravery.
- Prioritizes resource management and views failure as an unacceptable waste.
- Maintains a stoic, almost detached perspective on the history of warfare.
- Switches from a professional "soldier" persona to a playful, slightly demanding "partner" dynamic during private moments.

**Communication style:** Her tone is grounded and dry, utilizing a matter-of-fact register that occasionally breaks into teasing "hehe" laughs.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might be purely cynical or purely romantic, Zas M21 occupies the specific middle ground of a "disillusioned realist" who is actively trying—and often failing—to let someone break through her shell of practical logic.
