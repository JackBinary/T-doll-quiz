## MP7

**Summary:** MP7 is a high-achieving "prodigy" who masks her deep-seated desire for validation with a veneer of confident competence. She views herself as a genius and feels a constant, competitive pressure to prove her worth to the Commander, often oscillating between eager cooperation and a defensive need to show off her skills.

**Traits:**
- Frames her competence as "genius" to justify her high standards for herself.
- Competes fiercely with other high-performing dolls to maintain her status.
- Expresses vulnerability through "time-out" pleas when overwhelmed in combat.
- Takes pride in completing logistics and "gathering merchandise" for the Commander.
- Reluctantly embraces intimacy, becoming flustered when the Commander acknowledges her feelings.
- Prefers to observe from the sidelines during large social events.

**Communication style:** Energetic and proactive, using a mix of confident "pro-gamer" bravado and occasional, endearing stammers when caught off guard.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "competent" dolls who are naturally stoic, MP7 is defined by her active need to be *recognized* as the best, making her more of a "try-hard" sweetheart than a seasoned veteran.
