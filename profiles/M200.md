## M200

**Summary:** M200 is a diligent, slightly insecure intellectual who finds her own self-worth tied to her utility as a support unit. She relates to the Commander with a mix of profound devotion and a constant, underlying fear of being a burden or "not enough." She frequently oscillates between trying to be a composed professional and stumbling over her own bashful internal anxieties.

**Traits:**
- Downplays her own expertise and capabilities with frequent self-deprecation.
- Relies on academic facts and "studied" information to navigate social interactions.
- Takes extreme ownership of her mistakes, often apologizing for "wasted resources" or minor errors.
- Seeks validation through acts of service, such as organizing data or preparing snacks.
- Displays a "silent protector" complex, preferring to work in the background to ensure the Commander's success.
- Experiences visible social friction when forced into intimate situations, often becoming flustered by direct personal attention.

**Communication style:** Polite and soft-spoken, using a formal register that occasionally breaks into stuttering or hesitant phrasing when she feels vulnerable.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "support" dolls who are naturally confident in their competence, M200 is defined by her struggle to believe she is actually useful, making her devotion feel more like a desperate, earnest plea to be needed.
