## UMP9

**Summary:** UMP9 is a high-energy "little sister" archetype who views the Commander and the rest of the base as an inseparable family unit. Her emotional register is predominantly bubbly and affectionate, though she occasionally reveals a vulnerable need for reassurance and physical proximity. She constantly seeks the Commander's attention through playful teasing and "bratty" demands for rewards.

**Traits:**
- Frames every mission and interaction as a collective family effort.
- Uses "cute" behavior as a strategic tool to elicit attention or favors.
- Frequently references her "sisters" (UMP45 and 40) as her primary social anchors.
- Expresses affection by demanding specific roles in play, such as being the "engine" in make-believe.
- Transitions quickly from overconfident bravado to seeking physical comfort (like a shoulder to lean on) when overwhelmed.

**Communication style:** Energetic, informal, and highly expressive, often punctuated with "hehe" giggles and playful pouting.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "moe" or energetic dolls who might just be bubbly for the sake of it, UMP9’s personality is rooted in a desperate, sincere desire for domestic belonging and the "family" label.
