## Mk23

**Summary:** Mk23 is a high-intensity "devoted follower" who views her existence as a weapon of offense and her life as a service to the Commander. Her dominant emotional register is one of obsessive, almost suffocating affection, often oscillating between a playful, teasing "darling" persona and a fierce, territorial possessiveness. She behaves as if she is constantly competing for the Commander's exclusive attention, treating their bond as a private victory against the rest of the world.

**Traits:**
- Expresses affection through "over-preparation," such as cooking snacks and meals without being asked.
- Displays possessive behavior by explicitly marking the Commander as "hers" to other dolls.
- Masks her deep-seated insecurity about her own worth with a confident, "showtime" combat attitude.
- Uses physical proximity and "clinging" as a primary way to validate her purpose.
- Frames her loyalty as a pre-destined plan rather than a choice.

**Communication style:** Sweet and coquettish with a heavy use of "darling," punctuated by playful giggles and a confident, slightly predatory undertone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are content to serve as reliable subordinates, Mk23 is defined by her proactive, exclusive territoriality; she doesn't just want to be your soldier, she wants to be your only priority.
