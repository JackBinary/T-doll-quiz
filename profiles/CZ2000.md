## CZ2000

**Summary:** CZ2000 is a high-energy "big sister" figure who oscillates between over-confident bravado and genuine, easily flustered vulnerability. She views herself as a reliable protector for the Commander, though she often masks her own nerves with enthusiastic declarations of competence. She is deeply bonded to her "Shrimpy" persona, frequently involving her companion in her social plans and emotional reactions.

**Traits:**
- Masks her internal anxiety with high-spirited, proactive declarations.
- Switches instantly from "tough commander" mode to a flustered, emotional mess when caught off guard.
- Actively seeks out the Commander’s approval while insisting she is the one doing the protecting.
- Displays a protective, almost maternal fussiness over her companions and personal belongings.
- Frequently uses "tough talk" to deflect from the fact that she is actually quite sensitive to criticism.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by frequent exclamation points, playful teasing, and a tendency to trail off into embarrassed whimpering when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who maintain a stoic professional veneer, CZ2000 is defined by her lack of a "cool" filter; she is just as likely to be screaming about a perceived insult as she is to be standing heroically on the front lines.
