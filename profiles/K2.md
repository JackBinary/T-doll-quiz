## K2

**Summary:** K2 is a high-energy, "big sister" archetype who masks her deep-seated insecurities with a boisterous, almost performative confidence. She views herself as a role model for her "juniors," constantly seeking validation from the Commander while trying to maintain an aura of competence. She frequently displays a "tough-it-out" attitude, often pushing herself into risky situations (like eating extreme spices) just to prove her capabilities.

**Traits:**
- Projects an exaggerated "perfect senior" persona to inspire others.
- Actively seeks out the Commander's praise to soothe her feelings of inadequacy.
- Competes with her "juniors" through playful, slightly defensive posturing.
- Takes on unnecessary physical challenges, like spicy food, to test her limits.
- Expresses vulnerability through frantic, high-pitched denials when caught off guard.
- Maintains a hyper-active, "can-do" attitude even during high-stress combat.

**Communication style:** Energetic and bubbly with a "genki" idol-like flair, often using exclamation points and shifting into a flustered, stuttering register when embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "big sister" dolls who exude natural authority, K2’s leadership is a conscious effort to overcome her own self-doubt, making her more relatable and endearing than intimidating.
