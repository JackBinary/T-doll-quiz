## KSG

**Summary:** KSG is a high-strung, earnest professional who views her service as a sacred duty of self-improvement. She relates to the Commander with a mixture of deep reverence and a desperate need to prove her competence, often internalizing any mission failure as a personal moral deficit. She exhibits a behavioral pattern of hyper-vigilance, constantly scanning for "lack" in her own performance even after a successful victory.

**Traits:**
- Frames every task as a test of her personal growth and reliability.
- Internalizes tactical setbacks as personal failures requiring immediate reflection.
- Expresses affection through proactive readiness and "on standby" availability.
- Maintains a rigid, soldierly posture even during festive holidays.
- Seeks explicit validation of her "good judgement" to anchor her sense of purpose.

**Communication style:** Formal, disciplined, and highly attentive, utilizing a respectful register that leans toward military stoicism.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are content with being reliable tools, KSG is driven by an almost anxious perfectionism that makes her constantly seek ways to transcend her current limitations.
