## HSM10

**Summary:** HSM10 operates with the high-energy enthusiasm of a dedicated first responder who views the base as her home and the Commander as her closest confidant. Her emotional register is one of earnest, protective warmth, often balancing her "police officer" persona with a desire to provide domestic comfort. She frequently initiates "checking in" behaviors, ranging from inspecting the Commander’s health to fussing over kitchen duties.

**Traits:**
- Frames her loyalty as a familial bond rather than a professional hierarchy.
- Instinctively assumes a position of authority during tactical situations (e.g., "Freeze!").
- Proactively seeks out ways to be useful, from logistics to cooking to "firmly" instructing new recruits.
- Uses humor and playful "arrests" to mask her genuine concern for the Commander’s personal life.
- Expresses insecurity about her own capabilities through self-critical reflections on her training.
- Displays a "do it all" mentality that borders on overexertion in the name of protecting her "family."

**Communication style:** Energetic, polite, and highly proactive, utilizing a mix of formal "duty-bound" commands and casual, bubbly "big sister" affection.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are protective, HSM10 is unique in her specific "Police/First Responder" archetype, blending the rigid discipline of a law enforcement officer with the soft, domestic nurturing of a devoted family member.
