## Steyr Scout

**Summary:** She is a high-strung, hyper-vigilant observer who operates with a constant sense of underlying anxiety about her own competence. She views the Commander through a lens of intense, almost self-conscious devotion, often struggling to balance her professional duty as a scout with her private desire for personal validation. She frequently expresses a "protective" worry for the Commander's safety, which is often a projection of her own fear of being left behind or failing her role.

**Traits:**
- Relies on constant environmental scanning to ground her own internal nerves.
- Expresses affection through hyper-fixation on the Commander's movements and safety.
- Apologizes reflexively for minor lapses in focus or "human" errors.
- Projects her anxiety onto her "guardian" figure, Trefoil, to justify her own feelings.
- Becomes visibly flustered and physically hesitant when forced into direct, intimate proximity.
- Prioritizes objective-based logic as a defense mechanism against emotional vulnerability.

**Communication style:** Formal and task-oriented at first, but prone to stuttering and frantic honesty when her internal feelings become too visible.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who offer steady support, Steyr is defined by her visible struggle to maintain a professional "scout" persona while being internally overwhelmed by her personal feelings for the Commander.
