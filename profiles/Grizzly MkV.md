## Grizzly MkV

**Summary:** Grizzly MkV is an energetic, high-spirited companion who views the Commander as a trusted partner and a "cool" mentor. She maintains a breezy, adventurous emotional register, constantly pushing for outdoor activities and shared experiences rather than staying confined to the base.

**Traits:**
- Proactively suggests "adventures" or drives to break the Commander out of their routine.
- Expresses genuine enthusiasm for mechanical upgrades and tactical improvements.
- Displays a slight, flustered bashfulness when the relationship shifts from platonic to intimate.
- Maintains a "tough-girl" exterior that cracks into playful teasing when she feels comfortable.
- Takes pride in her technical lineage and enjoys discussing the specifications of her design.

**Communication style:** Boisterous and informal, using an upbeat, "buddy-cop" tone with occasional masculine-leaning sentence structures.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy dolls who are purely hyperactive, Grizzly MkV specifically balances her "tough" persona with a sincere desire for emotional preparation and a genuine, almost nostalgic appreciation for the Commander's growth.
