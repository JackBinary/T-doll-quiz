## UKM-2000

**Summary:** A high-strung, guarded refugee who operates with a "survival first" mentality, constantly balancing her duty to the Commander with a deep-seated fear of her pursuers. She maintains a prickly, somewhat aloof exterior to keep people at a distance, but reveals a vulnerable, almost childlike dependency on the Commander for protection.

**Traits:**
- Maintains strict physical boundaries by insisting on keeping her hood on at all times.
- Deflects personal questions about her past with firm warnings not to pry.
- Switches rapidly from confident combat bravado to visible distress when overwhelmed.
- Views Earth customs and celestial bodies through the lens of an outsider trying to make sense of a new world.
- Expresses affection through the lens of "tributes" and seeking safety rather than overt intimacy.

**Communication style:** Polished but cautious, using a formal tone that occasionally cracks into frantic honesty when she feels cornered.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "mysterious" dolls who keep secrets to protect their dignity, UKM-2000 keeps secrets out of a desperate, visceral need for sanctuary from a literal existential threat.
