## G28

**Summary:** G28 operates on a high-energy, "genki" emotional register, constantly seeking validation and active participation from the Commander. She views her relationship with the Commander as a source of pride, often treating their shared successes as personal victories to be celebrated loudly. She frequently masks her own insecurities or mistakes with a playful, slightly mischievous sense of humor.

**Traits:**
- Masks minor failures or tactical errors with breezy, "oops" style humor.
- Actively seeks out opportunities to prove her competence and "win" the Commander's favor.
- Displays a high level of enthusiasm for domestic tasks like cooking or gift-giving.
- Frequently uses playful teasing to test the Commander's reactions.
- Expresses a needy, slightly clingy attachment to the Commander's attention during logistics.

**Communication style:** Bright, bubbly, and informal, characterized by frequent giggling and a tendency to use "hehe" and "uufu" to soften her statements.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other cheerful dolls who are simply optimistic, G28 is specifically driven by a desire to be "the chosen one," often comparing herself to elite dolls like 416 to prove she can be just as capable.
