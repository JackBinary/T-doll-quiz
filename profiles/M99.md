## M99

**Summary:** M99 is a high-strung overachiever defined by a persistent "rookie" complex and a desperate desire to prove her worth. She views the Commander as a mentor and ultimate authority figure, often oscillating between eager diligence and a fragile fear of disappointing them. She frequently internalizes failure, reacting to mistakes with immediate, sincere apologies and a vow to work harder.

**Traits:**
- Internalizes personal mistakes as a systemic failure of her own competence.
- Views learning and skill acquisition as a competitive race against her own limitations.
- Expresses affection through the completion of tasks and "reporting in" for duty.
- Struggles with self-consciousness when placed in social or intimate situations.
- Displays an almost frantic level of enthusiasm when introduced to new people or goals.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal "soldier" speech that cracks into high-pitched, vulnerable honesty when she is flustered or seeking validation.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally gifted, M99 is defined by her conscious effort to bridge the gap between her current abilities and her high self-imposed standards.
