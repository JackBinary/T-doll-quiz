## CZ-805A1

**Summary:** She projects the high-energy persona of a "top-tier" rookie who is desperate to live up to her reputation as a modern, versatile weapon. She views the Commander as her primary audience and often oscillates between overconfident bravado and a flustered, clumsy vulnerability when she actually misses a shot. Her behavioral pattern involves "performing" her competence—constantly reminding others of her capabilities to mask her own insecurities about her lack of practical experience.

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat performance as a theatrical spectacle for the Commander to witness.
- Deflects mistakes by blaming external factors like weather or "bad luck" rather than skill.
- Displays a competitive "little sister" energy when interacting with other dolls.
- Overcompensates for her lack of experience by highlighting her modular versatility.
- Becomes visibly flustered and apologetic when her "perfect" facade is cracked by a failure.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, energetic, and performative, often using exclamation points and a "spunky" tone that can slip into stuttering when she's embarrassed.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are confident, CZ-805A1 is specifically defined by the gap between her "brand new" prestige and her actual "rookie" clumsiness, making her a comedy of errors in high-pressure moments.
