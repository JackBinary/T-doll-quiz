## Lusa

**Summary:** Lusa is a high-energy "junior" athlete whose personality revolves around the discipline and passion of soccer. She views the Commander as a teammate and mentor, often seeking validation for her progress while masking her insecurities with boundless enthusiasm. She frequently oscillates between intense competitive focus and a clumsy, endearing lack of situational awareness.

**Traits:**
- Channels her competitive drive into every task, even treating combat like a sports match.
- Frequently seeks out praise for small victories to fuel her confidence.
- Displays a "try-hard" attitude, often over-committing to physical training or drills.
- Expresses vulnerability through high-spirited outbursts when she fails to meet her own goals.
- Offers practical, homemade tokens of affection (like grilled sardines) instead of traditional romantic gestures.
- Occasionally struggles to read social cues, missing the "vibe" of a moment in favor of her own immediate thoughts.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, using "Coach" as a primary honorific and peppered with sports metaphors and exclamation points.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other high-energy "genki" dolls who are just bubbly for the sake of it, Lusa’s personality is strictly defined by the athlete's mindset: she is genuinely preoccupied with training, formation, and the specific "win/loss" binary of her goals.
