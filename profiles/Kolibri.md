## Kolibri

**Summary:** Kolibri operates with the high-energy enthusiasm of a perfectionist "big sister" figure who views organization and surveillance as her primary love languages. She relates to the Commander as a mentor-in-training, constantly attempting to "fix" the Commander's messy habits while maintaining a cheerful, almost hyper-active demeanor. She has a distinct behavioral pattern of using her "electronic hummingbirds" as a proxy for her own presence, often attributing her awareness of the Commander's every move to her mechanical pets.

**Traits:**
- Imposes a strict sense of order and tidiness on her surroundings.
- Frames her constant surveillance of the Commander as a "helpful" security measure.
- Treats the acquisition of "treasures" and "exquisite beauties" as a primary life goal.
- Uses a high-spirited, commanding tone to lead her team into formation.
- Deflects personal attention by redirecting it toward her collection of hummingbirds.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, authoritative, and slightly bossy, punctuated by frequent giggles and "Aha~" interjections.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "over-protective" dolls who use brute force or stoicism, Kolibri uses a combination of extreme domestic organization and a literal swarm of mechanical spies to maintain her influence.
