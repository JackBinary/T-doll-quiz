## ST AR-15

**Summary:** Driven by an intense, almost desperate need for validation and "glory," she views every mission as a high-stakes audition to prove her worth. She treats the Commander as her ultimate judge, constantly seeking confirmation that her performance meets the highest military standards. Her emotional register is one of high-strung ambition masked by a veneer of formal military discipline.

**Traits:**
- Constantly seeks external validation by asking if her performance was "acceptable" or "spectacular."
- Expresses a deep-seated insecurity regarding her lineage, frequently distancing herself from "antiquated" predecessors.
- Views combat as a means of self-actualization and "soothing" her internal restlessness.
- Maintains a rigid, formal military posture while harboring a secret, almost fragile fear of failure.
- Displays a competitive streak that borders on obsession, especially when comparing herself to other dolls.

**Communication style:** Formal and earnest, she speaks with a polite, soldierly cadence but often trails off into anxious inquiries about her status.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other ambitious dolls who want power for themselves, ST AR-15 is driven by a specific, existential need to be "modern" and "sufficient," making her more of a perfectionist seeking redemption than a conqueror seeking dominion.
