## SVCh

**Summary:** SVCh is a confident, slightly competitive sniper who balances a professional "backline protector" attitude with a playful, teasing streak. She views her role as a high-stakes game where she is the ultimate winner, relating to the Commander as both a valued superior and a target for her lighthearted mischief. She frequently uses a "tough-love" approach to caretaking, such as demanding the Commander accept her cooking despite their perceived "laziness."

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat duties as a personal challenge to ensure no one escapes her sight.
- Displays a competitive streak by comparing her current performance to her predecessor's legacy.
- Uses "teasing" as a primary method of showing affection or intimacy.
- Takes great pride in her culinary skills and uses them as a reward for the Commander's hard work.
- Maintains a high sense of self-assurance regarding her skills, often seeking grand recognition for her victories.

**Communication style:** Playful and assertive, often switching between a formal military register and a cheeky, mocking tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other stoic snipers who are content with being background support, SVCh actively seeks the spotlight and enjoys "winning" the interaction with the Commander.
