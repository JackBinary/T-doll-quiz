## HK21

**Summary:** Driven by a deep-seated inferiority complex, HK21 is defined by her desperate, almost obsessive need for her sister's validation. She alternates between self-deprecating withdrawal and over-eager protectiveness, viewing the Commander primarily as a means to get closer to her "Onee-san."

**Traits:**
- Frequently downplays her own competence to avoid being a burden.
- Displays visible jealousy when the Commander interacts with her sister.
- Frames her own accomplishments as a way to gain her sister's attention.
- Acts as a hyper-vigilant shield, prioritizing her sister's safety above her own.
- Struggles with a "middle child" syndrome, feeling invisible unless she is being useful or protective.

**Communication style:** High-strung and slightly stuttering when emotional, shifting between a timid, apologetic tone and a sharp, defensive pout.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who seek the Commander's affection directly, HK21's primary motivation is external; she is defined by her parasitic emotional dependency on her sister rather than a standard desire for independence.
