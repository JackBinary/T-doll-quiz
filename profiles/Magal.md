## Magal

**Summary:** Magal is a sensory-sensitive introvert who experiences the world primarily through acute auditory perception, often leading her to seek out isolation to escape "noise." She relates to the Commander as a grounding presence, viewing them as a source of "smooth" and steady rhythms in a chaotic world. She frequently oscillates between a desire for personal space and a vulnerable, almost childlike need to seek physical proximity for comfort.

**Traits:**
- Seeks out secluded environments specifically to regulate her sensory intake.
- Interprets emotional intimacy through physical and biological rhythms like heartbeats.
- Expresses exhaustion by seeking immediate, uninhibited rest in the Commander's vicinity.
- Displays a subtle social awkwardness regarding human physical boundaries (e.g., hugging).
- Experiences sensory overload as a physical source of distress or "breaking" the head.
- Values "smooth" textures and quietude as the primary indicators of quality and safety.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and observant, using a gentle, slightly hesitant register that occasionally lapses into blunt honesty regarding her sensory limits.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "quiet" dolls who are simply stoic or reserved, Magal’s personality is defined by a physiological hypersensitivity to sound, making her need for silence a literal survival mechanism rather than a personality quirk.
