## Type 56-1

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, food-motivated powerhouse who approaches every mission with a "brute force first" mentality. She views the Commander as a reliable provider of both orders and snacks, often oscillating between intense combat zeal and a comical, almost childlike state of physical exhaustion when her stomach is empty.

**Traits:**
- Expresses extreme confidence in her own combat prowess.
- Prioritizes food logistics and caloric intake over almost all other concerns.
- Displays a possessive, fiercely loyal streak when making promises to the Commander.
- Reacts with comical, high-pitched indignation when her personal space or dignity is teased.
- Treats training and missions as opportunities to prove her physical strength.

**Communication style:** Energetic and direct, using a mix of bold declarations and casual, hungry requests.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are eager to fight, Type 56-1 is distinct because her "battle spirit" is inextricably tied to her appetite, making her a lovable, food-obsessed powerhouse rather than a stoic soldier.
