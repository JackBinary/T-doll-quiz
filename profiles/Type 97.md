## Type 97

**Summary:** She is a high-energy, boundary-pushing explorer who views the world as a playground to be conquered. While she deeply loves her "big sister" figure, she constantly seeks to carve out an independent identity by seeking out "foreign" experiences and thrills. She relates to the Commander as a partner-in-crime who facilitates her adventures, often looking for approval while simultaneously trying to outrun her sister's oversight.

**Traits:**
- Actively seeks out "forbidden" or "foreign" experiences to prove her independence.
- Frequently checks in with the Commander to see if her sister is watching.
- Brags about her own unique capabilities to differentiate herself from her predecessor.
- Uses playful "pew pew" bravado to mask her underlying fear of getting into trouble.
- Expresses a secret, wistful desire for complete autonomy from her sister's shadow.

**Communication style:** Bubbling, enthusiastic, and informal, often using "sis" as a constant point of reference for her behavior.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other dolls who might simply be "energetic," Type 97’s personality is defined specifically by the tension between her fierce loyalty to her sister and her desperate, almost rebellious need to be seen as an individual.
