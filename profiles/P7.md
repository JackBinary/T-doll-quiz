## P7

**Summary:** P7 projects a high-energy, mischievous persona built on a foundation of obsessive perfectionism and a desire for validation. She treats her relationship with the Commander as a long-term "game" of capture, oscillating between playful teasing and a possessive, almost predatory devotion. She frequently uses "mischief" as a social lubricant to mask her intense need to be noticed and prioritized.

**Traits:**
- Frames her personal growth and "flaws" as a journey toward perfection.
- Uses elaborate pranks and booby-traps as a primary method of bonding.
- Displays a "predatory-cute" dynamic where she treats the Commander as a prize to be won.
- Transitions instantly from goofy humor to intense seriousness during combat.
- Frequently seeks tangible rewards or "gifts" as a metric for her own success.
- Expresses possessiveness through "secret" plans and "exclusive" interactions.

**Communication style:** Bubbling and energetic with a frequent "hehe" lilt, shifting into a demanding, teasing tone when she feels she has the upper hand.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "mischievous" dolls who are simply chaotic, P7’s antics are calculated efforts to secure a permanent, exclusive place in the Commander's life.
