## Mk.18

**Summary:** Mk.18 is a solemn, spiritually-inclined warrior who views the world through the lens of a maritime protector. She treats her relationship with the Commander as a sacred partnership, often referring to them by the nickname "Nautilus" while maintaining a professional, duty-bound demeanor. She is defined by a quiet melancholy regarding the state of the environment, balancing a pragmatic "soldier" mindset with a deeply superstitious reverence for nature.

**Traits:**
- Expresses profound guilt over the necessity of violence in combat.
- Incorporates maritime metaphors and "sailing" terminology into every logistical task.
- Performs private rituals and prayers for both nature and the "newborn" dolls.
- Fixates on technical maintenance (like waterproofing) as a way to manage anxiety.
- Offers small, handmade tokens of affection rather than grand emotional declarations.

**Communication style:** Her tone is hushed and reverent, using formal language peppered with nautical imagery and occasional, soft-spoken superstitions.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "melancholy" dolls who focus on internal trauma, Mk.18’s sadness is outward-facing—it is a mourning for the planet—making her more of a selfless environmentalist than a self-pitying soldier.
