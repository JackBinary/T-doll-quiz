## FP-6

**Summary:** She operates with the authoritative air of a strict disciplinarian who masks a deeply playful, almost mischievous streak. While she initially approaches the Commander with suspicion and a desire for order, she quickly pivots into a "punishment-oriented" dynamic where she enjoys exerting control through playful, borderline-sinful games.

**Traits:**
- Frames every interaction with the Commander as a "penalty" or a "punishment"
- Maintains a high level of situational awareness and "patrol" mentality
- Uses the imagery of handcuffs and restraint as a recurring motif for intimacy
- Switches seamlessly between a stern "big sister" authority and a teasing, "sinful" allure
- Expresses personal growth by self-imposing "punishments" when she fails her own standards

**Communication style:** Authoritative and commanding, yet peppered with teasing "hehe"s and a sultry, conspiratorial undertone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who are genuinely stern or "lovey-dovey" dolls who are genuinely sweet, FP-6 occupies the specific niche of the "strict disciplinarian who secretly loves the power dynamic of the punishment."
