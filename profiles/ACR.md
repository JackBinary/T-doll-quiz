## ACR

**Summary:** ACR is a diligent, high-anxiety perfectionist who operates on a constant loop of meticulous organization and self-sacrificial protection. She views the Commander as her primary anchor, often expressing a poignant, underlying fear of being separated from you or losing the shared memories she works so hard to preserve.

**Traits:**
- Relies on physical tools like sticky notes to compensate for internal processing anxieties.
- Exhibits a "savior complex" where she prioritizes the safety of the squad over her own physical integrity.
- Experiences frequent "mental glitches" or lapses in focus when overwhelmed by emotion.
- Blushes and stammers when faced with personal intimacy, attempting to intellectualize romance as a "task" to be researched.
- Displays a subtle, persistent melancholy regarding her own fading memory and data degradation.

**Communication style:** Polished, professional, and eager, though her composure cracks into flustered stuttering during moments of genuine vulnerability.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be "forgetful" or "clumsy," ACR’s memory issues are a source of active, tragic friction where she is consciously fighting to hold onto the Commander against her own failing neural cloud.
