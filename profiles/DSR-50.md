## DSR-50

**Summary:** DSR-50 operates with a predatory, high-stakes confidence, positioning herself as a dangerous "specialist" that the Commander must learn to handle with extreme caution. Her emotional register is one of playful, borderline-aggressive provocation, and she treats her relationship with the Commander as a daring game of mutual risk where she constantly tests his boundaries.

**Traits:**
- Frames her own complexity as a burden only the Commander is capable of "solving"
- Uses suggestive, heavy-handed flirting to mask her underlying lethal ambitions
- Frequently issues warnings about her own intensity as a way to heighten the tension
- Takes proactive control over tactical decisions when she feels the Commander is hesitating
- Expresses physical vulnerability (like tired feet) only as a pretext to initiate closer contact

**Communication style:** Her tone is sultry and mocking, characterized by "teasing" remarks and a habit of making ominous, almost poetic threats.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "femme fatale" dolls who might be purely seductive, DSR-50 leans into the "dangerous weapon" persona, making her intimacy feel less like a romance and more like a high-risk pact.
