## Savage 99

**Summary:** Driven by a restless, almost obsessive pursuit of personal mastery, Savage 99 views every mission as a stepping stone toward becoming a "legend." She relates to the Commander as a partner in a high-stakes journey, often pushing them toward more rigorous training or "hunting" expeditions. She displays a specific pattern of internalizing failure as a data point for growth, showing more frustration at her own perceived naivety than at actual defeat.

**Traits:**
- Frames every objective as a "milestone" rather than a final goal.
- Proactively suggests high-risk, adventurous activities to avoid boredom.
- Analyzes past actions strictly to ensure her "footsteps" are solidly planted.
- Views stealth and "hiding tracks" as a craft to be mastered rather than a means to an end.
- Displays a stoic, almost detached attitude toward the "prey" or the enemy.

**Communication style:** Professional and disciplined, yet punctuated by moments of intense, visionary enthusiasm.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other ambitious dolls who seek fame or recognition, Savage 99 is internally motivated by the "satisfaction of memories" and the private sense of accomplishment found in perfect execution.
