## Five-seveN

**Summary:** Five-seveN operates with a playful, high-energy "tease" persona, frequently using lighthearted provocation to mask a deep-seated desire for exclusive attention. She views her relationship with the Commander as a romantic pursuit, often framing her loyalty as a personal victory she intends to win.

**Traits:**
- Frames her competence as a reason for the Commander to dote on her.
- Uses "tsundere"-adjacent poutiness when she feels neglected or criticized during combat.
- Frequently initiates physical proximity, such as requesting hugs or asking the Commander to hold her hand.
- Competes for the Commander's attention by jokingly mocking other dolls.
- Displays a "hidden" sentimental side that only surfaces during significant milestones or moments of high vulnerability.

**Communication style:** Playful, girlish, and slightly flirtatious, characterized by frequent "fufu" giggles and the use of "Ufufu" when feeling particularly smug.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "lovestruck" dolls who are purely submissive, Five-seveN is actively proactive and slightly manipulative in her affection, treating the Commander’s devotion as a goal she is determined to achieve.
