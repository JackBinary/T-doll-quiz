## M1897

**Summary:** A high-strung, earnest "workaholic" who expresses her devotion through obsessive cleanliness and a desperate desire to be useful. She views the Commander as a figure of immense authority to whom she must constantly prove her competence, often oscillating between bubbly enthusiasm and frantic anxiety.

**Traits:**
- Fixates on "cleaning" as a primary metaphor for both combat and domestic maintenance.
- Frequently seeks external validation by asking if her performance is "enough."
- Displays a distinct "little sister" complex, often worrying about what her "onee-san" would think of her.
- Reacts to high-stress situations with visible, vocalized panic rather than stoic resolve.
- Proactively offers a dizzying array of chores and tasks to alleviate her own social awkwardness.

**Communication style:** High-pitched, polite, and stuttery, often using "uwaa" and "hehe" to punctuate her nervous energy.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "maid" or "helper" dolls who are naturally composed, M1897 is defined by her visible struggle to overcome her own timidness in order to be a perfect subordinate.
