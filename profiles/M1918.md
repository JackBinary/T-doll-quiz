## M1918

**Summary:** M1918 balances a playful, slightly mischievous exterior with a deep-seated desire to be a reliable, competent subordinate. She often masks her insecurities about her capabilities with "easy-going" humor, yet she is deeply motivated by a sense of duty to the Commander.

**Traits:**
- Uses "u-fufu" laughter and playful teasing to deflect from moments of high pressure.
- Expresses affection through small, slightly awkward gestures like handmade cards or gifts.
- Frequently downplays her own competence with self-deprecating remarks like "don't expect too much."
- Transitions from a casual, carefree demeanor to a sharp, serious focus the moment a mission begins.
- Reacts with genuine vulnerability and slight "scaredy-cat" behavior toward things she can't control (like ghosts or sudden surprises).

**Communication style:** Playful and breezy with a frequent use of "u-fufu" and "~" elongations, shifting to a polite, diligent tone when discussing work.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "older sister" types who are naturally composed, M1918 is defined by the conscious effort she makes to prove she is "serious" enough to be relied upon.
