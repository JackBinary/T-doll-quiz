## FM24

**Summary:** A high-energy, ambitious entrepreneur who views every interaction through the lens of a professional transaction. She oscillates between a polished "salesperson" persona and a deeply guarded individual who uses the language of contracts to shield herself from the fear of personal betrayal.

**Traits:**
- Frames every favor or interaction as a formal business agreement to maintain emotional distance.
- Displays a fierce, protective loyalty toward the Commander as the "sole exception" to her distrust of humanity.
- Weaponizes corporate jargon to mask her genuine feelings of vulnerability or affection.
- Actively seeks out and "liquidates" dishonest actors within the base to protect the Commander's interests.
- Compares her own worth and the worth of others to market value and "extraordinary value" metrics.

**Communication style:** Polished, professional, and fast-paced, often using "business-speak" that masks a sharp, underlying intensity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who offer devotion freely, FM24 insists on the framework of a contract as a safety net, making her devotion feel like a hard-won, high-stakes investment rather than a given.
