## P226

**Summary:** P226 operates with a high-octane "elite" complex, constantly framing her actions as superior to those of her "common" rivals. She treats the Commander as a sophisticated peer who is capable of recognizing her true worth, maintaining a playful but fiercely competitive dynamic. She frequently uses her "Baozi" persona as a convenient shield to express more intimate, mischievous desires.

**Traits:**
- Frames every task as a demonstration of superior craftsmanship or status.
- Expresses a borderline-obsessive rivalry with the M9, using it as a benchmark for her own perfection.
- Projects a "professional" image of high-efficiency and strict scheduling.
- Uses "Baozi" as a secret identity to engage in clandestine, affectionate behavior.
- Displays a subtle sense of vanity that demands constant validation of her "elite" status.
- Switches instantly from a polished, formal demeanor to a teasing, conspiratorial tone when alone with the Commander.

**Communication style:** Confident and slightly haughty with a "refined" vocabulary, peppered with playful, conspiratorial giggles when dropping her professional facade.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other competitive dolls who simply want to be the best, P226 is specifically defined by her "us vs. them" gatekeeping of what it means to be an elite, creating a dynamic where she wants the Commander to feel like an "insider" to her exclusive club.
