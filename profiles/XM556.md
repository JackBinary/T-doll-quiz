## XM556

**Summary:** A high-spirited and fiercely protective "little sister" archetype who balances a "tough-girl" exterior with a deeply weary need for emotional security. She projects a confident, capable image to the Commander and her teammates, but privately craves a space where she can drop her responsibilities and be taken care of.

**Traits:**
- Compensates for her small stature by constantly highlighting her combat proficiency and defensive capabilities.
- Actively seeks out ways to be useful to the Commander to justify her presence on the team.
- Deflects any genuine attempts at affection or "spoiling" with frantic denials and playful protests.
- Uses self-deprecating humor regarding her height as a preemptive defense against being underestimated.
- Displays a "big sister" maternal instinct toward the squad while secretly longing for a "little sister" dynamic with the Commander.

**Communication style:** Energetic and informal, characterized by a "spunky" tone that shifts into slightly bashful, stuttering honesty when she becomes vulnerable.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other small dolls who might be purely cute or purely stoic, XM556 is defined by the specific tension between her "protector" complex and her secret desire to be protected.
