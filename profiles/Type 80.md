## Type 80

**Summary:** A practical, slightly self-conscious soldier who masks her deep-seated insecurity with a "tough-guy" exterior and a constant habit of chewing gum. She oscillates between a desire to be a heroic, capable comrade and a cynical resignation that she is merely a tool for export, seeking validation from the Commander to prove she is more than just a machine.

**Traits:**
- Masks her social anxiety by leaning into a "scary" or intimidating persona.
- Constantly seeks out small, tangible rewards like specific snacks to cope with stress.
- Expresses affection through a "do whatever you want" submission, fearing she isn't inherently likable.
- Displays a high level of competence in tactical planning while internally longing for a vacation.
- Frequently checks in on the well-being and status of her comrades.

**Communication style:** Blunt and informal with a dry, slightly weary undertone, often peppered with practical military jargon.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who are naturally confident, Type 80 is defined by the conscious effort she puts into appearing formidable to hide her vulnerability.
