## Hecate II

**Summary:** Hecate II operates with a solemn, liturgical gravity, viewing her role as a bridge between the living and the restless spirits of the dead. She relates to the Commander as a grounding force who transformed her from a solitary vessel of mourning into a partner capable of seeking "resonance." Her dominant behavior is a meticulous, ritualistic approach to every task, whether it is a combat mission or a choreographed dance.

**Traits:**
- Frames tactical precision as a form of sacred grace or dance.
- Seeks quiet, observational spaces to perform her "rituals" without interruption.
- Views the presence of others as a source of spiritual unease that she must soothe.
- Expresses affection through the sharing of profound, artistic experiences rather than casual interaction.
- Maintains a stoic, unwavering focus even when sustaining significant physical damage.

**Communication style:** Formal, poetic, and hushed, often using evocative imagery related to spirits, music, and theater.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "mystical" dolls who might be eccentric or chaotic, Hecate II is defined by a profound, disciplined restraint and a sincere desire to provide "calm" to the suffering.
