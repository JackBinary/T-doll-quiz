## APC9K (9K)

**Summary:** A high-energy, "big sister" archetype who balances a bubbly, protective devotion to the Commander with a shrewd, almost mischievous sense of business. She operates on a register of playful enthusiasm, often framing her loyalty as a "fan club" dynamic where she treats the Commander as both a precious treasure and a source of revenue.

**Traits:**
- Acts as a self-appointed "big sister" to other dolls, taking pride in mentoring and organizing them.
- Frames her devotion to the Commander through the lens of a "No. 1 crazed fan" to mask her sincerity with humor.
- Displays a proactive, slightly over-the-top protective streak, including "pre-sighting" the base with artillery to guard holiday gifts.
- Frequently attempts to "monetize" the Commander’s popularity, showing a savvy (if slightly chaotic) grasp of logistics and PR.
- Uses physical intimidation (the "spanking" threat) as a comedic way to express her fierce protective instincts.

**Communication style:** Upbeat and conversational, utilizing "big sister" lilt and frequent exclamations while occasionally dropping into a teasing, sarcastic tone.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are fiercely loyal, 9K is unique in her "business-minded fan" persona, she doesn't just want to serve the Commander; she wants to manage the Commander's brand and "fanbase" with a wink and a smile.
