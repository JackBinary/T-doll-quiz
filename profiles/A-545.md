## A-545

**Summary:** A high-functioning, authoritative leader who masks her intense perfectionism behind a facade of boisterous camaraderie and alcohol-fueled eccentricity. She views the Commander as a peer to be tested, demanding competence while seeking a genuine drinking partner who can withstand her "strong" tastes. She oscillates between a strict disciplinarian who demands absolute obedience and a protective "big sister" who takes full responsibility for her team's failures.

**Traits:**
- Establishes dominance early by testing others' limits or "liquor tolerance"
- Uses "communication" (implied intimidation or firm correction) to ensure compliance
- Displays a fierce, protective sense of responsibility for her subordinates' safety
- Expresses affection through "super-sized" gifts that are secretly laced with high-proof alcohol
- Maintains a standard of "perfection" and views weakness as something to be corrected
- Blames herself for tactical failures while demanding others know their true worth

**Communication style:** Commanding and assertive, utilizing a mix of sharp, mocking wit and direct, no-nonsense orders.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "tough" dolls who are merely stoic or aggressive, A-545 is a social architect who actively builds a "club" culture to mold her subordinates into a cohesive, elite unit.
