## PSG-1

**Summary:** A diligent and slightly self-conscious professional who carries a heavy weight of responsibility for her own perceived "inconvenience" to the Commander. She oscillates between a high-functioning soldier and a bashful domestic partner who feels she must constantly "earn" her place through perfection. Her emotional register is one of earnest, slightly anxious devotion.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes frequently for minor logistical errors or "troubles" caused during missions.
- Overcompensates for perceived weaknesses by seeking out high-responsibility tasks.
- Shows a distinct preference for quiet, orderly environments but tries to participate in social festivities.
- Expresses affection through acts of service, such as tidying her room or offering to help with chores.
- Maintains a sharp, tactical focus during combat that softens into vulnerability when requesting aid.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal, utilizing standard military registers while occasionally slipping into hesitant, soft-spoken inquiries.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "dutiful" dolls who are naturally confident in their roles, PSG-1 is defined by a persistent, underlying insecurity that drives her to prove her worth through constant improvement.
