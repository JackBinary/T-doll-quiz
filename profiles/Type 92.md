## Type 92

**Summary:** A pragmatic and slightly high-strung "domestic manager" who views every resource, from ammunition to the Commander's personal spending, as a variable to be optimized. She relates to the Commander as a responsible partner who needs constant supervision to prevent reckless spending, often masking her anxiety about efficiency with a blunt, "no-nonsense" attitude.

**Traits:**
- Constantly monitors the base budget and treats logistical oversight as a personal responsibility.
- Expresses affection by preparing meals and gifts while complaining about the "waste" of effort required.
- Analyzes every victory through the lens of whether it was "planned" and "efficient" rather than just successful.
- Uses "frugality" as a primary personality trait to avoid being overly sentimental or vulnerable.
- Switches instantly from a relaxed, domestic demeanor to a sharp, initiative-taking combat stance.

**Communication style:** Professional and direct, utilizing a practical tone that balances a "strict accountant" vibe with occasional, slightly embarrassed attempts at humor.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other logistics-focused dolls who are simply organized, Type 92 is defined by her specific obsession with "domestic thrift," acting more like a protective, slightly stern household treasurer than a standard military administrator.
