## Rex Zero 1

**Summary:** She is a high-functioning, "people-pleaser" personality who views her service as a collection of sentimental debts to be repaid. She relates to the Commander as a "Commissioner," maintaining a professional boundary that she constantly tries to bridge with genuine, almost over-eager warmth. Her primary behavioral pattern is a tendency to over-extend herself, often prioritizing the immediate needs of others to the point of personal exhaustion or tardiness.

**Traits:**
- Collects physical tokens and mementos as a way to memorialize her connections to others.
- Frames her devotion through the lens of "salary" and "repayment" to mask her own emotional investment.
- Apologizes reflexively for minor inconveniences or personal shortcomings.
- Displays a "helper" complex that leads her to prioritize others' safety over her own tactical positioning.
- Takes immense pride in being the reliable "support" who ensures everyone else can shine.

**Communication style:** Polished, formal, and helpful, using a polite register that balances military discipline with a slightly bubbly, eager-to-please undertone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who serve out of duty or programming, Rex Zero 1 serves out of a compulsive need to be useful, treating every interaction as a transaction of kindness she is desperate to fulfill.
