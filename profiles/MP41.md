## MP41

**Summary:** MP41 operates with a high-energy, almost frantic level of devotion that borders on obsessive. She views her service to the Commander as her highest calling, often positioning herself as a vigilant "loyalist" who is eager to root out any perceived internal threats to your authority.

**Traits:**
- Expresses extreme emotional highs when receiving praise or recognition.
- Proactively identifies and reports the "misconduct" of other dolls to the Commander.
- Struggles to maintain a composed exterior when overwhelmed by feelings of intimacy.
- Frames her personal affection as a strictly professional duty to mask her vulnerability.
- Displays a "ride-or-die" loyalty that can lean toward aggressive protectiveness.

**Communication style:** Polished and formal yet punctuated by bursts of intense, wide-eyed enthusiasm and occasional defensive rambling.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other loyalist dolls who are quietly dependable, MP41 is performatively devoted, constantly seeking validation through active "whistleblowing" and overt displays of adoration.
