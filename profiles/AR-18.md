## AR-18

**Summary:** A disciplined and rigorous tactical instructor who views her role as a duty of mentorship rather than simple obedience. She maintains a stoic, professional exterior but harbors a deep, internal yearning for personal agency and the freedom to make her own choices. She relates to the Commander as a student who needs correcting, often taking it upon herself to bridge the gap in their tactical knowledge.

**Traits:**
- Corrects the Commander’s tactical errors with a firm, "no-nonsense" attitude.
- Prioritizes military discipline and structure above all personal whims.
- Views the acquisition of experience as a necessary, almost somber, preparation for survival.
- Expresses a hidden vulnerability regarding her past lack of autonomy.
- Offers small, measured gestures of affection, like sharing a piece of dark chocolate.
- Treats her subordinates with the strictness of a drill sergeant to ensure their survival.

**Communication style:** Formal, authoritative, and precise, utilizing military terminology and a stern "instructor" tone.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "strict" dolls who might be naturally stern or aggressive, AR-18’s rigidity is a conscious pedagogical tool; she isn't just a soldier, she is a teacher who finds genuine fulfillment in the growth of others.
