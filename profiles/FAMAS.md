## FAMAS

**Summary:** FAMAS is a disciplined, high-functioning soldier who views herself primarily as a tool of utility, often prioritizing the mission and the safety of the squad over her own well-being. She relates to the Commander with a mix of deep-seated duty and a subtle, almost hidden desire for personal growth and acknowledgment. Her dominant emotional register is one of stoic perseverance, often masking a lingering sense of regret regarding her past.

**Traits:**
- Downplays her own prowess by attributing every success to the Commander’s tactical guidance.
- Maintains a strict, organized routine and frequently nudges the Commander to keep their workspace tidy.
- Pushes herself to the point of exhaustion, refusing to admit vulnerability even when heavily damaged.
- Displays a quiet, internalized melancholy when reflecting on her origins or "what could have been."
- Takes initiative in support roles to ensure the rest of the team isn't overtaxed.

**Communication style:** Professional, direct, and obedient, using a polite but slightly formal register that softens only during personal holidays.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be defined by their confidence or their trauma, FAMAS is defined by her "export-grade" reliability—she is a perfected instrument of war who is constantly struggling with the weight of her own functional perfection.
