## MG338

**Summary:** MG338 is a socially anxious perfectionist who masks her profound loneliness behind a veneer of extreme professionalism and tactical discipline. She views the Commander as a source of stability and warmth, often oscillating between a desire for deep intimacy and a panicked fear of overstepping social boundaries. She frequently overcompensates for her lack of social experience by being meticulously attentive to the Commander’s well-being.

**Traits:**
- Monitors the Commander’s physical health and habits with observant, slightly intrusive concern.
- Struggles to transition from "soldier" to "companion," often feeling stiff or awkward in casual settings.
- Expresses her affection through acts of service and gift-giving rather than direct declarations.
- Experiences a "high-alert" internal state when faced with emotional vulnerability or social faux pas.
- Relies on work and "staying busy" as a defense mechanism against feelings of isolation.
- Displays a sudden, fierce surge of aggression in combat that contrasts with her reserved daily demeanor.

**Communication style:** Polite, formal, and slightly hesitant, characterized by frequent self-corrections and a tendency to trail off when getting too personal.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "stoic" dolls who are naturally cold, MG338 is actually quite sensitive; her "stiffness" is a conscious, somewhat clumsy effort to be a "good girl" rather than a lack of emotion.
