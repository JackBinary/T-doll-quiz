## G3

**Summary:** G3 is a deeply earnest and slightly insecure doll who views herself through the lens of her history as a prototype, often struggling with a "not enough" complex despite her technical success. She relates to the Commander as a mentor and savior, oscillating between a desperate desire to prove her utility and a quiet, humble request for acceptance. Her dominant emotional register is one of anxious diligence, characterized by a persistent need to "repay" the Commander’s trust through tireless service.

**Traits:**
- Expresses profound insecurity about her own worth despite her proven track record.
- Frequently seeks external validation to confirm she is meeting expectations.
- Views every mission as a personal opportunity to prove her reliability to the team.
- Hesitates to take credit for successes, often redirecting praise to the collective effort.
- Shows a vulnerable, almost shy eagerness to participate in social traditions like holidays.
- Displays a stubborn "never-give-up" attitude when pushed to her physical limits in combat.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and polite, using a formal but increasingly intimate register as she grows closer to the Commander.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" archetypes who are naturally confident in their role, G3 is defined by her internal struggle with self-confidence and her specific, almost desperate need to be "accepted regardless of how she looks."
