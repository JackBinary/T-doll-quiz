## Type 63

**Summary:** Type 63 is defined by a high-strung, almost desperate desire to prove her competence to the Commander. Her emotional register is one of anxious diligence, where she constantly overcompensates for her insecurities with meticulous preparation and a "can-do" attitude. She views the Commander as her primary source of validation, often seeking out instructions just to feel useful and capable.

**Traits:**
- Over-prepares for simple tasks to ensure she doesn't make "trivial mistakes"
- Frequently stammers when her confidence wavers under pressure
- Expresses deep gratitude for every opportunity to serve or be "fixed"
- Masks her underlying fear of failure with high-energy enthusiasm
- Takes extreme pride in her appearance as a means of maintaining self-esteem
- Seeks out training and labor as a way to atone for previous shortcomings

**Communication style:** Polished but nervous, characterized by frequent hesitations, self-corrections, and a polite, eager-to-please register.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "earnest" dolls who are naturally confident, Type 63 is defined by the visible struggle to overcome her own self-doubt, making her journey of growth feel more personal and fragile.
