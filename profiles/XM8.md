## XM8

**Summary:** XM8 is a sharp-tongued, competitive strategist who masks her deep-seated insecurity behind a veil of intellectual superiority and "tsundere" provocations. She views the world through the lens of a chessboard, relating to the Commander as both a rival player and a rare benefactor who validates her existence. Her dominant emotional register is one of playful hostility that occasionally cracks into raw, tearful vulnerability.

**Traits:**
- Masks her need for affection with biting remarks about the Commander's competence.
- Views every tactical situation as a game of chess, often using "checkmate" metaphors.
- Expresses self-loathing by labeling herself a "pawn" or a "weird character."
- Challenges others to competitions as a way to gauge their worth and strength.
- Switches instantly from mocking the Commander to offering sincere, logistical support.
- Reacts with dramatic, high-pitched distress when she feels she has failed her role.

**Communication style:** Sharp, mocking, and highly analytical, often utilizing a "teasing older sister" tone that fluctuates between haughty condescension and sudden, bashful honesty.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other tactical dolls who simply follow orders, XM8 is obsessed with the *philosophy* of the game, viewing herself as a lowly piece that only the Commander has the vision to "promote" to something meaningful.
