## MAT-49

**Summary:** A self-sacrificing sentinel who views her role as a literal shield for the Commander, often bordering on a martyr complex. She oscillates between a formal, knightly devotion and a slightly eccentric, earnest curiosity about the world. She expresses her anxiety through protective actions, such as providing handmade security items or fussing over the Commander's safety.

**Traits:**
- Frames her existence around the concept of "destiny" and knightly duty.
- Displays a tendency toward obsessive, literal-minded research (e.g., capturing horses to understand history).
- Expresses insecurity about her own competence while simultaneously refusing to retreat.
- Checks for safety protocols even in social situations, like inspecting chocolates for "food safety."
- Relies on "bad luck deflectors" as a physical and psychological buffer against the world.
- Attributes all personal achievements and glory directly to the Commander's favor.

**Communication style:** Formal and chivalrous, utilizing "Lord Commander" as a standard honorific with a tone of high-strung sincerity.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal" dolls who are simply obedient, MAT-49 operates on a romanticized, medieval code of chivalry that makes her more of a squire-knight than a modern soldier.
