## MG5

**Summary:** MG5 carries herself with the high-octane confidence of a "prodigy" who is deeply aware of her own status as a perfected, standardized model. She views the Commander as her primary benefactor and "enabler," showing a fierce, almost desperate desire to prove that the investment made in her development was justified. She oscillates between a playful, self-assured smirk and a heavy, self-imposed burden to be the "weapon of any situation."

**Traits:**
- Frames her combat performance as a personal stage to "shine" for the Commander.
- Accepts high-risk assignments with a stoic "no objections" attitude to prove her reliability.
- Displays a subtle competitive streak, often comparing her own prowess to others.
- Expresses a need for independence while simultaneously seeking the Commander's validation.
- Switches from a professional "standardized model" persona to a more informal, playful tone when off-duty.
- Internalizes failure deeply, showing visible distress when unable to maintain her "invincible" image.

**Communication style:** Confident and slightly theatrical, using a mix of formal military reporting and bold, assertive declarations.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "reliable" dolls who serve out of duty, MG5 serves out of a burning need to validate her own perfection; she isn't just a soldier, she is a masterpiece trying to live up to the hype.
