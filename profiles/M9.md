## M9

**Summary:** M9 is a high-energy socialite who operates on a constant need for external validation and "glittery" attention. She views herself as a superstar and treats her relationship with the Commander as a partnership in fame, often seeking praise and wanting to be seen as the center of the spotlight. She displays a persistent, almost desperate desire to be liked by everyone, even those who clearly find her exhausting.

**Traits:**
- Frames every personal achievement as a "sparkling" success for the audience.
- Constantly seeks out new trends, accessories, and shopping opportunities.
- Expresses vulnerability by asking the Commander to keep her "unpolished" moments a secret.
- Uses high-pitched, playful humor to deflect from the reality of combat.
- Actively pursues the approval of peers who are openly hostile toward her.

**Communication style:** Bubbly, high-energy, and performative, frequently using "tehehee" and exclamation points to maintain a cheerful persona.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "genki" archetypes who are naturally cheerful, M9 is specifically obsessed with the *aesthetic* of popularity and the social currency of being "the best."
