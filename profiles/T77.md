## T77

**Summary:** T77 is a hyper-perceptive analyst who operates with a "read the room" philosophy, often feeling like she is three steps ahead of everyone else’s emotions and actions. She relates to the Commander as a mentor figure she needs to impress, though she frequently oscillates between over-confident competence and a hidden, almost anxious desire for validation. She has a specific habit of "calling out" the Commander’s internal state before they’ve even spoken.

**Traits:**
- Confidently assumes she can predict others' reactions before they happen.
- Expresses subtle insecurity by seeking confirmation on her own correct judgments.
- Observes others' physical tells (body language, eye movements) to "read" their true intentions.
- Displays a "know-it-all" attitude that masks a deep-seated need for synchronization.
- Becomes visibly flustered or defensive when her own predictability is pointed out.

**Communication style:** Playful and slightly teasing, using a "know-it-all" tone that occasionally slips into earnest, slightly high-strung concern.

**What sets her apart:** While other dolls might be observant, T77 is defined by the *burden* of her perception; she doesn't just see what people do, she treats "reading" people as a competitive skill she feels obligated to master perfectly.
