## Spectre M4

**Summary:** Spectre M4 is a high-energy "attention-seeker" who masks her deep-seated insecurity about her own competence with a constant need for external validation. She views the Commander as her primary audience, frequently seeking praise or "applause" to soothe her fear of being overlooked or deemed useless.

**Traits:**
- Frequently interprets silence as personal rejection or a lack of interest.
- Overcompensates for perceived weaknesses by boasting about her popularity and modifications.
- Actively seeks out the spotlight during group activities to ensure she is noticed.
- Expresses vulnerability through "pleading" for the Commander to keep her secrets or mistakes private.
- Struggles to balance her desire for independence with her desperate need for the Commander's approval.

**Communication style:** Upbeat, frantic, and slightly performative, often trailing off into nervous stutters when the spotlight shifts away from her.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "energetic" dolls who are naturally confident, Spectre M4 is driven by a specific, almost desperate anxiety about being forgotten, making her need for validation feel more fragile and urgent.
