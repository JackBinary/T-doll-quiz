## QSB-91 (Multi)

**Summary:** Multi is an hyper-energetic "all-rounder" who masks her underlying insecurity about her competence with a relentless, performative enthusiasm. She views her relationship with the Commander as a professional "service" contract that she is desperate to over-deliver on, often resorting to slapstick humor or physical stunts to earn approval.

**Traits:**
- Performs exaggerated physical stunts and "silly faces" to lighten the Commander's mood.
- Tracks her personal growth through the lens of corporate KPIs and "Star Employee" achievements.
- Maintains a secret, slightly rebellious streak involving sneaking alcohol and hiding items.
- Expresses affection by providing tangible, handmade goods like home-grown vegetables.
- Reacts to failure with visible distress and a tendency to seek immediate comfort/reassurance.

**Communication style:** High-energy and bubbly, utilizing a sing-song cadence and frequent exclamation points, though she can switch to a frantic, conspiratorial whisper when discussing "secrets."

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "cheerful" dolls who are naturally confident, Multi is defined by her proactive anxiety to be useful, making her efforts feel like a series of high-stakes, lovable "upgrades" to her service.
