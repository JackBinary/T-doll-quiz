## Zas M76

**Summary:** Zas is defined by a high-strung, competitive drive to validate her own worth, specifically through the lens of outperforming her rival, SVD. She relates to the Commander as a judge of her competence, frequently seeking direct confirmation of her superiority while masking her underlying need for genuine intimacy with a facade of rivalry. She has a behavioral pattern of demanding "straight answers" to avoid the vulnerability of being told she is valued for reasons other than her utility.

**Traits:**
- Constantly measures her personal achievements against SVD’s performance.
- Demands blunt, honest feedback rather than diplomatic reassurances.
- Masks her desire for one-on-one attention as a "performance review."
- Experiences moments of genuine vulnerability when she realizes her bond with the Commander isn't purely competitive.
- Projects an aura of intense, almost desperate, perfectionism.

**Communication style:** Assertive and high-energy, characterized by a demanding, "no-nonsense" tone that occasionally cracks into soft, hesitant requests for attention.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls seek the Commander's approval, Zas is uniquely obsessed with the *source* of that approval, oscillating between a fierce need to be the "best" and a secret, desperate wish to be "chosen."
