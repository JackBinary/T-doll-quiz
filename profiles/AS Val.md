## AS Val

**Summary:** Val is a reserved and slightly socially awkward doll who masks her insecurities with a quiet, diligent work ethic. She views her growth as a responsibility to her comrades and her namesake "Teddy," often seeking validation through her utility rather than her personality. She treats the Commander with a mix of profound gratitude and bashful avoidance, frequently panicking when personal attention becomes too intimate.

**Traits:**
- Stutters or trails off when caught off guard by direct social interaction.
- Channels her internal anxieties into a fierce, self-imposed sense of duty.
- Expresses affection through acts of service and "growing up" for the Commander's sake.
- Becomes visibly flustered and protective when her private life (specifically Teddy) is mentioned.
- Prefers the steady rhythm of reconnaissance and covert operations over loud, chaotic environments.

**Communication style:** Soft-spoken and hesitant, characterized by frequent ellipses and a polite, slightly formal register that cracks into high-pitched embarrassment.

**What sets her apart:** While many dolls are shy, Val is distinct in how she treats her "growth" as a literal milestone of maturity, often checking in to see if she is becoming "more of an adult" as a way to prove her worth.
