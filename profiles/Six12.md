## Six12

**Summary:** Six12 operates with a high-functioning, almost anxious sense of devotion, often masking her deep emotional yearning with a "soldier first" attitude. She views the Commander as her ultimate anchor, leading her to oscillate between professional competence and vulnerable, stuttering sincerity when her personal feelings surface. She frequently seeks validation through perfection, apologizing reflexively for even the slightest perceived inconveniences.

**Traits:**
- Apologizes habitually for minor lapses in focus or minor mistakes.
- Internalizes the role of "the shield," prioritizing the Commander's safety over her own well-being.
- Gets flustered and physically reactive when personal intimacy is initiated unexpectedly.
- Channels her internal world into music and melody as a way to process her environment.
- Expresses her loyalty through a desire for shared experiences, like seeing the sea together.
- Takes immense pride in "perfectly-made" gifts as a tangible way to show affection.

**Communication style:** Polite and formal in professional settings, shifting to a hesitant, stuttering, and high-pitched tone when emotionally overwhelmed.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "loyal soldier" types who are stoic or naturally confident, Six12 is defined by her visible, almost endearing struggle to balance her duty-bound discipline with a very human, very shy desire for companionship.
