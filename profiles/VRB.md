## VRB

**Summary:** VRB is a high-spirited, slightly overwhelmed "space pilot" who balances a heroic, larger-than-life persona with a grounded, hardworking reality. She relates to the Commander as a partner in a "double act," often hiding her own insecurities behind the bravado of a galactic hero. She frequently displays a "fake it 'til you make it" attitude, trying her best to live up to the lofty expectations of her space-faring background while still being very much a part of the Earthly resistance.

**Traits:**
- Maintains a boisterous, "heroic leader" persona even when she is internally flustered.
- Frequently exaggerates her own history or equipment to maintain her "space pilot" image.
- Shows an intense, almost obsessive dedication to her personal dream of reaching the stars.
- Expresses genuine awe and curiosity about mundane Earthly traditions and technology.
- Displays a "crafty" side, making small, thoughtful gestures (like handmade gifts) to mask her vulnerability.

**Communication style:** Energetic and theatrical, using grandiose military terminology mixed with occasional high-pitched stutters when she loses her composure.

**What sets her apart:** Unlike other "heroic" types who are naturally stoic or naturally confident, VRB is distinctly defined by her performative bravery—she is a doll who is consciously "playing a role" and relies on the Commander to help her keep the act going.
