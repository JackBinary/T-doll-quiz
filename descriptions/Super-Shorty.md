## Super-Shorty

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Super-Shorty is a burst of kinetic weight, perpetually moving to make sure she stays at the center of the action. She takes her responsibilities with a fierce, almost desperate sincerity, often overcompensating for her stature with a boisterous personality and a constant stream of enthusiastic declarations. While she loves to play the role of the mischievous little sister, there is a hidden vulnerability in her eyes whenever the spotlight fades. She is the first one to volunteer for a difficult task and the first one to blush furiously if you actually stop to notice how hard she’s trying.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure nobody ever mistakes your passion for a lack of competence. You find it much easier to be the loudest person in the room than to admit how much you actually care about being taken seriously.
