## SPR A3G

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
She leads with a vibrant, theatrical weight, often framing her constant presence as a necessary duty of supervision. While she loves to tease and maintain a playful cat-and-mouse dynamic, her mischievous laughter masks a profound, measured care to the person she protects. She is the type to notice the smallest changes in your way, to herself celebrating your growth while offering rewards with a flourish of dramatic flair. Deep down, she is a weary guardian who would rather be your most dedicated confidante than just another soldier.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of pushing yourself until you're running on empty, and you privately find comfort in the idea of someone who actually notices the effort you try so hard to hide.
