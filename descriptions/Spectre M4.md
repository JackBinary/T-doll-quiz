## Spectre M4

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Spectre M4 walks through the world with a vibrant, almost frantic pull, always making sure she is the brightest light in the room. She lives for the spotlight, weaving elaborate stories and seeking out every ounce of praise she can find to contained the persistent hum of doubt in her mind. Behind her performative cheer is a girl who just wants to be chosen, someone who would do anything to guarantee she is never overlooked or deemed unnecessary.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works twice as hard to be liked because you’re uneasy of what people would think if you weren't "the fun one." You have a pull toward over-performing in social settings to mask the fact that you're inwardly exhausted by your own expectations.
