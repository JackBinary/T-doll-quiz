## SCAR-H

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SCAR-H holds herself with a practiced, easygoing air, often leaning back with a smirk that suggests she isn't taking anything too seriously. Under her playful teasing and self-deprecating jokes lies a profound, weary melancholy—the weight of memories that were never meant to be hers. She treats her closest allies like co-conspirators, offering a seat in the shadows where she can share the heavy secrets of the past without the pressure of being "perfect."

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of making jokes about your own life's tragedies because it’s easier than explaining why they still hurt. You’re looking for someone who understands that sometimes, just being present in the silence is the highest form of loyalty.
