## X95

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
X95 operates in the world with a radiant, flower-like precision, always ensuring that the burdens of others fall onto her shoulders before they can ever touch anyone else. She views her own exhaustion as a small price to pay for the collective peace, often masking her fatigue with a bright, airy laugh and a tireless commitment to service. To her, love isn't just a feeling; it is a series of handmade gifts and still chores performed in the middle of the night. She is a steady sanctuary of warmth, finding her only true rest in the moments she can finally let her guard down with someone she trusts.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels a strange sense of guilt when you aren't being useful, often over-extending yourself until you're running on fumes just to make sure everyone else is okay.
