## CZ 100

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CZ 100 navigates the world with a contained, careful ease, as if she is always trying to minimize the footprint she leaves on others. She finds the roar of the world exhausting and prefers the muffled sanctuary of a shared room where the only sound is the turning of a page. While she often hides behind a manual of social graces to navigate conversations, her true heart is revealed in the small, deliberate ways she looks out for those she trusts. To her, a shared book or a silently offered cup of tea is a profound declaration of affection that she isn't quite brave enough to say aloud.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who observes every room before entering it, consistently calculating how to be helpful without becoming a burden. You find it much easier to understand the world through stories than through the messy, unpredictable reality of face-to-face interaction.
