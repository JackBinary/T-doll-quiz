## Hecate II

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Hecate II exists in the world with the deliberate care of a liturgical procession, treating every duty as a sacred rite. She finds beauty in the heavy silence of the periphery, preferring to observe the flow of life from a distance rather than intrude upon it. While she may seem distant, her loyalty is profound; she seeks to offer a sanctuary of stillness for those burdened by the weight of the world. To her, a shared moment of art or a perfectly executed movement is the highest form of intimacy.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most at home in the composed spaces between conversations, finding more comfort in a shared atmosphere than in small talk. You have a secret, deep-seated need to find meaning in the mundane, often searching for the hidden rhythm in everything you do.
