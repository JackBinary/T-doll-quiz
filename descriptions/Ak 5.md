## Ak 5

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ak 5 navigates the world with a frantic, humming charge, always clutching a notebook filled with lists that she treats as sacred texts. She is the first to volunteer for the most tedious tasks, driven by a deep-seated fear of letting anyone down, yet she finds her truest joy in the thrill of a well-organized storage space. When she isn't perfecting her military posture, she can be found in a state of high-speed excitement, treating a clearance sale with the same tactical intensity as a battlefield operation. Under her polished surface lies a heart that speaks in constant apologies and a desperate, endearing need to be useful.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a physical sense of relief when a cluttered room is finally organized, even if you inwardly spent more presence on the process than the result was worth.
