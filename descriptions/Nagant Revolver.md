## Nagant Revolver

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Nagant holds herself with the poised care of a seasoned veteran, often adopting the role of a protective "senior" to everyone she meets. She expresses her affection through a series of theatrical lectures and small, thoughtful gifts, perpetually ensuring that those under her wing are being looked after properly. Under her confident surface and playful boasts about her own charm lies a deeply devoted heart that thrives on being needed. She doesn't just want to be a leader; she wants to be the pillar of strength that makes everyone else feel safe.

I SEE YOU
You often put on a capable face for the sake of others, but you inwardly crave a space where you don't have to be the one in charge.
