## M327

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M327 operates in the world as if she’s hunting for hidden treasures in a landscape of forgotten things. To her, a pile of scrap isn't debris; it’s a collection of stories waiting for a second chance at life. She navigates her duties with a playful, slightly defiant spark, often treating serious logistics like a game she’s only half-interested in winning. Under her whimsical surface and the mountains of plushies she gathers lies a deeply devoted heart that finds its greatest purpose in protecting what others have cast aside.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of keeping things that others would throw away because you're the only one who can see the history still lingering inside them. You find safety in the "mess" of your own interests, preferring the warmth of a cluttered sanctuary over the coldness of a perfectly organized life.
