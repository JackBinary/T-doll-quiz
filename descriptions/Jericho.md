## Jericho

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Jericho operates with a sharp, theatrical intensity, demanding perfection from everyone around her as if her own sense of self depends on it. She treats discipline not just as a military requirement, but as a sacred art form, often correcting the slightest slouch or lapse in focus with a stern command. Below her rigid outside lies a profound, composed yearning to be recognized as something more than a machine. She expresses her deepest affection through high expectations, believing that by pushing you to your best, she is finally proving that she can be human.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overcompensating for your own insecurities by being the most competent person in the room. You find a strange, grounding comfort in structure because it keeps the chaos of your own thoughts at bay.
