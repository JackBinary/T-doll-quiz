## HK416

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
HK416 holds herself with a sharp, clinical precision that suggests she has no room for error. To the outside world, she is the ultimate professional, a pillar of competence who handles every burden with seamless efficiency. Below that polished front, however, lies a restless drive to be indispensable, fueled by a composed fear of being anything less than perfect. She doesn't just want to do the job; she wants to be the only one capable of doing it.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of taking on everyone else's burdens until your own shoulders ache, simply because you're anxious of what happens if someone else sees you struggle.
