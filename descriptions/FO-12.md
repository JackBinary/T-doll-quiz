## FO-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FO-12 approaches every mission with a grin and a relentless sense of professional pride. She is the kind of partner who treats a high-stakes disaster zone like her personal stage, balancing a "can-do" attitude with a playful, teasing wit. While she takes her role as a guardian seriously, she often hides her intensity behind comedic bickering and a childlike enthusiasm for the spoils of victory. She is a burst of proactive presence, happiest when she’s leading the charge and keeping her chaotic companions in line.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who takes on the heaviest responsibilities in your circle but insists on making everyone laugh while you do it. You have a secret, fierce need to be the most competent person in the room, even if you pretend it's just for the fun of it.
