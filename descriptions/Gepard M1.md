## Gepard M1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Gepard M1 is the first person to arrive at the office and the last one to leave, usually nursing a cup of coffee and a growing sense of exhaustion. She walks with a composed, weary dignity, often masking her deep-seated insecurities with self-deprecating jokes and playful complaints about her endless overtime. While she perpetually sighs for a vacation that never seems to come, her actions reveal a fierce, protective heart that views her comrades as her only true home. She doesn't seek glory or medals; she simply wants to be reliable enough to make sure everyone else can rest easy.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late not because you enjoy the work, but because you’re afraid of what might happen if you let anyone else take the lead. You have a pull toward making yourself indispensable just so you can feel like you have a reason to stay.
