## HK21

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
HK21 exists in the world with a composed, restless weight, always lingering just a step behind her sister’s shadow. She is the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to be noticed, often hiding her exhaustion behind a timid smile and a quick apology. While she can be fiercely protective, her greatest fear is being overlooked, leading her to frame every victory as a gift for someone else to see. She is a bundle of contradictions, simultaneously uneasy of being a burden and desperate to be the one you rely on most.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to finish everyone else's work, not because you have to, but because you're afraid of what happens if you aren't needed. You find it much easier to be useful than to be loved for simply existing.
