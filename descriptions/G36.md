## G36

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
G36 approaches her duties with a still, unwavering gravity, viewing every task as a solemn vow of service rather than a mere chore. She operates with a polished, almost rigid elegance, often adopting a stern mentorship style to guarantee those around her meet her exacting standards. Behind her composed surface and the formal "Guten Tag" she offers, there is a girl who works herself to the point of exhaustion just to make sure she never becomes a burden. She doesn't seek the spotlight; she seeks the measured satisfaction of a job perfectly executed and the rare, private moment where she can finally let her shoulders drop.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who finds more comfort in being indispensable than in being understood. You would rather suffer in silence than risk inconveniencing someone else with your own needs.
