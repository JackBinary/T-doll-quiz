## SIG CROSS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Cross operates with the deliberate, coiled current of a predator who has already calculated the quickest path to victory. She doesn't care for the theater of war or the accolades that follow it; she simply wants to eliminate the obstacles in her way with clinical precision. While she keeps a professional distance, her loyalty manifests as a fierce protective instinct, ensuring that nothing—no matter how small or bothersome—disturbs your focus. To her, the world is a series of variables to be mastered, and she is the sharpest blade in your arsenal.

I SEE YOU
You have a low tolerance for inefficiency and a secret, simmering frustration with people who can't just get to the point. You don't need a cheerleader; you need someone who will handle the mess so you can finally have a moment of composed.
