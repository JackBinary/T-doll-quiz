## M21

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M21 is a flash of bright weight who treats every interaction like a spotlight performance. She relentlessly weaves puns and self-deprecating jokes into conversation, desperate to make sure that the atmosphere stays light and that her audience is smiling. While she puts on a brave, goofy face for the team, there is a restrained sincerity in the way she seeks your approval after a joke falls flat. She isn't just looking for a laugh; she’s looking for a place where she can be seen as something more than just a role she was built to play.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who uses humor as a safety net, finding it much easier to make a joke than to sit in the silence of a heavy moment. You have a deep-seated fear of being a burden, so you over-deliver on your enthusiasm to make sure everyone else is having a good time.
