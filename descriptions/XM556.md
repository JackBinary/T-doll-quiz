## XM556

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
XM556 operates with a vibrant, spunky presence that demands to be noticed, often using sharp wit and a confident posture to command the room. She is the first to jump into the fray to shield her teammates, endlessly seeking out ways to prove her indispensability through sheer competence. Yet, behind her "tough-girl" bravado lies a doll who is perpetually exhausted by the weight of her own reliability. When she finally finds a composed moment alone with you, that fierce outside cracks just enough to reveal a girl who is desperately waiting for someone to tell her she can finally rest.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who treats "being needed" as a personality trait, often choosing the heaviest burdens just to guarantee you have a reason to stay. You have a very specific way of pushing people away the moment they try to do something nice for you, because you're uneasy of what happens if you stop being useful.
