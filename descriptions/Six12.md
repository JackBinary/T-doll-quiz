## Six12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Six12 exists in the world with a measured, trembling attachment, always positioning herself as the shield between the Commander and the hardships of the front. She leads with a practiced military poise, yet she often finds herself retreating into the melody of a song to process the emotions she’s too shy to voice. While she strives for perfection in every task, her true heart is found in the small, carefully chosen gifts she offers and the way her composure fractures into a flustered, sincere mess whenever someone gets too close.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure no one else has to worry, even though you privately wish someone would notice how tired you are.
