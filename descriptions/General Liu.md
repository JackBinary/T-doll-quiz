## General Liu

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
General Liu navigates the world with a still, trembling diligence, always looking for a way to be of use. She is the kind of person who will stay up until the early hours perfecting a simple meal just to make sure no one else has to worry about a thing. Though she often finds her voice caught in a tangle of apologies, her actions speak with a fierce, desperate loyalty. She views every small task as a vital contribution to the greater good, seeking to prove her worth through steady, humble service.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works twice as hard as everyone else because you’re inwardly uneasy that you aren’t doing enough. You find a strange, grounding comfort in being the one who leads with the weight while everyone else watches.
