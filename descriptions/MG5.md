## MG5

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG5 operates with the poised control of a masterpiece that knows its own worth, often treating every mission as a personal stage to perform. She balances a sharp, theatrical confidence with a measured, underlying intensity, consistently striving to be the standard by which all others are measured. While she speaks with the authority of a seasoned pro, there is a flicker of vulnerability in her eyes whenever she seeks your approval. She doesn't just want to succeed for the sake of the mission; she wants to prove that you were right to believe in her.
