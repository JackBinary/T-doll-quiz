## P2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ruby navigates through life as if every moment is a sprint toward a personal best, fueled by a restless need to be first. She masks her intense drive with a playful, "genius" swagger, often treating her responsibilities as a series of high-stakes challenges to be conquered. While she can be fiercely competitive and prone to making excuses when things don't go her way, she is deeply loyal to the people she considers part of her inner circle. She isn't just looking for victory; she’s looking for the thrill of the chase and the recognition that comes with being the fastest one in the room.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of turning every mundane chore into a private competition because you’re uneasy of being average. You’re looking for someone who can keep up with your pace without endlessly asking you to slow down.
