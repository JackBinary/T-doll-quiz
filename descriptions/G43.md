## G43

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
G43 holds herself with a polished, almost fragile elegance, moving through the world with the precision of someone who believes her worth is tied entirely to her output. While she maintains a formal and composed shell, there is a restless hum of anxiety past the surface, a constant need to prove she is enough. She doesn't just want to fulfill her duties; she wants to be indispensable, often seeking out more responsibility as a way to shield herself from the fear of being obsolete. Under the military poise, however, lies a deeply devoted heart that watches over those she cares for with hyper-vigilant, contained attachment.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most comfortable when you are being useful, often confusing your own self-worth with the weight of the tasks you carry for others. You find it difficult to relax because you’re relentlessly waiting for the moment someone might realize you aren't as capable as you try to appear.
