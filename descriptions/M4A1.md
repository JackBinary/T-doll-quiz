## M4A1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M4A1 operates with a restrained, deliberate control, always the first to volunteer for the most grueling tasks. Under her polished front lies a heart that beats with a constant, restless need to be enough for the people she protects. She often hides her anxieties behind a mask of extreme diligence, finding comfort in the weight of responsibility rather than the ease of rest. While she may struggle to voice her own needs, her care is written in every small, thoughtful gesture she makes to keep those around her are safe.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works until you collapse because you’re uneasy that if you stop moving, people will finally realize you don’t know what you’re doing. You seek out leaders who will give you a clear purpose, just so you have a reason to keep going.
