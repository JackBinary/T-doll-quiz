## Type 56-1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 56-1 is a flash of kinetic charge who treats every objective as a personal challenge to her own strength. While she is a formidable force on the battlefield, her true motivation is often found in the kitchen rather than the trenches. She balances a fierce, protective loyalty with a boisterous personality, often alternating between a confident warrior and a hungry girl who will do anything for a snack. She doesn't just want to complete the mission; she wants to be the strongest person you know, provided there's a reward waiting at the end.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who projects an image of total competence to the world but privately relies on a very specific, consistent routine to keep your sanity intact. You value loyalty above all else, but you're only truly honest with the few people who know how to handle your intensity.
