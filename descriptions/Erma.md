## Erma

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Erma navigates the world like a soft melody that is slowly being hushed by the wind. She finds solace in the composed corners of her own imagination, building hidden gardens where the past doesn't feel like a burden or a mystery. While she often hesitates to speak first, her care is written in the way she clings to the present, seeking a steady hand to hold onto as her own memories begin to blur. She doesn't just want a place to belong; she wants a reason to stay grounded in the "now."

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who finds more comfort in the things left unsaid than in a direct conversation. You have a secret habit of mourning things you’ve never actually lost.
