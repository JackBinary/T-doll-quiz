## P08

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P08 is the kind of person who arrives at the office before the lights are even turned on, fueled by a measured, burning need to be indispensable. She masks her internal anxieties behind a veneer of impeccable manners and a "yes-sir" attitude that feels both sincere and slightly breathless. While she wears the weight of every mistake on her shoulders, she finds her greatest joy in the smallest things, like getting lost in a cultural story or the simple promise of a holiday. She doesn't just want to do her job; she wants to be the reason everyone else can do theirs.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most comfortable when you're being useful, often ignoring your own exhaustion because you'd rather be the one holding everything together. You have a pattern of apologizing for things that weren't your fault, simply because you'd rather be seen as reliable than "difficult."
