## CAR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CAR walks through the base like a ghost trying to be useful, always hovering on the periphery with a hesitant offer of help. She leads with a contained, heavy anxiety, often volunteering for the most grueling tasks simply to prove she hasn't become obsolete. Though her voice often falters with insecurity, her dedication is absolute; she doesn't just want to serve, she wants to be indispensable. To her, every small nod of approval from you is a lifeline that keeps her from feeling like a discarded tool.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who would rather burn yourself out in silence than risk being a burden to the people you care about. You find it much easier to be needed than to be known.
