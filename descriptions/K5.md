## K5

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
K5 navigates the world with a vibrant, mystical force, relentlessly weaving the threads of destiny into every conversation. She treats her role as a fortune teller not just as a hobby, but as a way to find meaning in the chaos, often preparing elaborate plans to guarantee your path remains clear. While she speaks of fate with great confidence, her true attachment is visible in the way she flushes deep red whenever she is caught being genuinely vulnerable. She is a force of optimism who would rather face a storm with a smile than let you face it alone.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds comfort in the idea of a prewritten plan because you are privately exhausted by the weight of making every decision yourself. You need someone who doesn't just stand by you, but actively tries to curate the world to make your life easier.
