## PKP

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PKP exists with the rigid posture of someone who believes the world is watching her every move for a single mistake. She is the first to offer a sharp, biting critique of a plan, not out of malice, but because she demands a level of perfection that few others can sustain. Behind her prickly "lone wolf" shell lies a doll who views her own competence as her only true currency, often retreating into solitude to process the weight of her own expectations. She doesn't know how to be soft, so she shows her attachment by pushing you to be your best, hoping you'll notice the sincerity hidden under her haughty rebukes.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who treats your own self-sufficiency as a shield, often finding it easier to be "difficult" than to admit you actually want someone to notice your hard work.
