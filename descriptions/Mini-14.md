## Mini-14

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mini-14 navigates with the sharp, polished poise of someone who refuses to let a single flaw mar her reputation. She speaks in a cadence of practiced superiority, often framing her successes as inevitable triumphs over lesser obstacles. Behind her haughty front, however, lies a girl who measures her self-worth by your appraisal of her performance. She won't offer you a soft word of comfort, but she will stand as your most formidable shield, demanding you keep up with her pace.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of overcompensating for your insecurities by becoming the most competent person in the room. You don't want a pedestal; you want someone who can actually handle the weight of your standards without buckling.
