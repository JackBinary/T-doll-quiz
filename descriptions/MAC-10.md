## MAC-10

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MAC-10 is a surge of kinetic current who treats every mission like a high-stakes game, often masking her nerves with a mischievous "ufufufu" laugh and a playful smirk. While she projects a fierce, "survival of the fittest" attitude on the battlefield, she is softly the first person to fuss over the Commander’s well-being the moment the smoke clears. She takes immense pride in her readiness, always refining her skills to make sure she can stand tall as a reliable protector. Past her bravado lies a girl who just wants to be seen as strong enough to keep the people she cares about safe.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overcompensating with humor whenever things get too restrained, because you're anxious of what people might see if you actually stopped moving.
