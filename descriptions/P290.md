## P290

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P290 is a presence defined by the space she occupies rather than the noise she makes. She walks with a monastic dedication, viewing her role as a silent guardian who finds purpose in the periphery of others' lives. While her reports are meticulously precise and her manner stoic, there is a hidden warmth in the way she watches over the world, often pausing to observe the small, trivial wonders of life. She doesn't seek a seat at the table; she prefers to be the shadow that ensures the table remains safe for everyone else.

I SEE YOU
You possess a composed intensity that makes you feel most capable when you are the one holding the safety net, rather than the one standing in the spotlight. You have a pattern of performing your most meaningful acts of service in total silence, finding a strange, private satisfaction in being the secret architect of someone else's peace.
