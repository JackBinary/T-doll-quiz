## DP-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
DP-12 operates with the poised care of a seasoned narrator who views existence as a sprawling, beautifully complex manuscript. She delights in weaving folklore into the mundane, often luring her companions into the fold of a "scary" tale just to see how they react to the atmosphere she creates. Behind her playful pranks and theatrical "ufufu" chuckles lies a fiercely protective heart that seeks to shield everyone from the harshness of reality. To her, the most important story of all is the one she is writing with you, and she intends to see every single chapter through to its final page.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of romanticizing your own struggles because it makes the weight of responsibility feel like a masterpiece rather than a burden. You're looking for someone who won't just stand beside you, but will help you turn your exhaustion into a legend.
