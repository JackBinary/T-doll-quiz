## PM-9

While she might bark orders and push you to your physical limits, her actions speak louder than her sharp tongue, showing her loyalty through tangible effort and unwavering loyalty. She isn't looking for a master to follow, but a rival who is strong enough to stand beside her.

I SEE YOU
