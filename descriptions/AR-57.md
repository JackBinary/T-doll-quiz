## AR-57

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AR-57 walks with a fierce, practiced bravado that often leaves people at a distance, but those who linger long enough see the sincerity behind her sharp edges. She is the type of person who expresses her deepest affection through practical critiques and meticulous preparation rather than soft words. While she might lean into a "tough girl" persona to navigate the world, she privately takes immense pride in the small, domestic details that make a house feel like a home. Past her impatient surface lies a heart that is consistently searching for someone who can see past her intimidating silhouette to the earnest person trying to be soft.

I SEE YOU
You use a prickly shell as a protective layer because you’re scared that if you let people see how much you actually care, they’ll find it inconvenient. You’d rather be perceived as difficult than misunderstood.
