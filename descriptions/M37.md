## M37

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ithaca operates in the world as if every room she enters is a stage built specifically for her debut. She radiates a vibrant, theatrical charge, always curating her presence to keep she is the most dazzling thing in your line of sight. Past the grand gestures and the playful demands for attention lies a girl who thrives on your gaze, treating every shared moment as a performance that must be perfect. She doesn't just want to be by your side; she wants to be the masterpiece you can't help but admire.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who provides the spotlight because you privately find the responsibility of being someone's "audience" more intimate than being their peer. You enjoy the way she performs, mostly because you know exactly how much effort it takes for her to keep the mask from slipping.
