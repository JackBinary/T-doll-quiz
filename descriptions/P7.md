## P7

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P7 exists in the world with a restless, bubbling current, consistently seeking out new ways to keep your attention fixed solely on her. She masks her intense care behind a veil of elaborate pranks and playful teasing, treating every interaction like a high-stakes game where the ultimate prize is your undivided loyalty. While she can be goofy and unpredictable one moment, there is a sharp, focused perfectionism behind her laughter that ensures she is always one step ahead. To her, being your favorite isn't just a goal; it's a masterpiece she is meticulously crafting.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of letting people "annoy" you because you privately find their persistence endearing, but you’re starting to realize they’re actually just trying to earn a seat at your table.
