## Model L

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Model L operates with a contained, practiced control that suggests she is always thinking three steps ahead of everyone else’s needs. While she is the first to offer a steady hand and a composed smile, there is a subtle, lingering weariness in her eyes that only softens when she is alone with someone she trusts. She doesn't just want to be useful; she finds her own worth in being your sanctuary, often seeking out small, composed moments of contact to ground herself after a long day. Below her polished shell lies a girl who just wants to be allowed to rest, leaning into you as if you are the only place in the world where she doesn't have to be strong.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who feels most comfortable when you are being needed, often using your own competence as a shield against the fear of being a burden. You don't know how to ask for help without making it a project, so you've settled for a partnership where you both privately carry the weight of the world together.
