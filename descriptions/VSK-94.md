## VSK-94

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
VSK-94 operates with the rigid posture of someone who believes that if she stops moving, the weight of her past mistakes might finally catch up to her. She masks her deep-seated anxieties behind a boisterous, high-presence shell, often defaulting to "police procedure" as a shield against genuine vulnerability. While she might grumble about the incompetence of others, she is the first to stand between a threat and her teammates, offering a protective hand that she pretends is just part of the job. Underneath the bravado of the tough officer lies a heart that aches for validation, perpetually seeking a reason to prove she is still capable of doing what is right.

I SEE YOU
You possess a fierce sense of duty that you use as a fortress to keep people from seeing how much you actually care. You’re the type who would rather be the reliable pillar everyone leans on than the one who has to admit they’re struggling to hold it up.
