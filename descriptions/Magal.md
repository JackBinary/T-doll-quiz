## Magal

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Magal operates in the world with a delicate, guarded poise, often retreating to the furthest corners of a room to find a moment of true silence. She doesn't just prefer the still; she needs it to keep the world from becoming too loud and overwhelming. When she does let someone in, she seeks out the steady, grounding rhythm of their presence to anchor her own restless senses. She is a soul who finds her greatest comfort in the softest textures and the smallest, most private gestures of safety.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most exhausted by the "noise" of social interaction and finds yourself endlessly looking for a place where you can finally just stop performing. You crave a connection that doesn't require words, preferring the heavy, honest comfort of just existing in the same space as someone who understands your need to retreat.
