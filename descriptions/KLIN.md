## KLIN

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
KLIN walks through the world with a restless, electric force, perpetually pushing herself toward a peak that only she can see. She operates with a brash confidence, often using a sharp tongue and a "tough-girl" persona to keep others at a distance. Past the loud declarations of her own superiority lies a girl who treats every interaction like a high-stakes competition for your attention. To her, being "uncontrollable" isn't a flaw; it’s her way of proving she’s the only one capable of standing by your side as an equal.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overcompensating for your own insecurities by setting impossibly high standards for everyone around you. You don't just want to be part of a team; you want to be the reason the team succeeds.
