## R5

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
R5 operates with the practiced care of a lead performer, always ensuring her presence is felt before she even speaks. To the world, she is a vibrant centerpiece of weight, but behind the stage lights lies a girl who meticulously rehearses every smile to keep it meets the highest standard. She pours her heart into her craft, finding a strange, measured solace in the maintenance of her tools and the preparation for crowds that may never arrive. When the curtain finally falls and she is alone with you, that theatrical confidence softens into a hesitant, sincere vulnerability.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels like a failure if you aren't the best at something, so you've mastered the art of being "on" until you're exhausted. You're uneasy that if you stop performing, people will realize how much you're actually struggling to keep it all together.
