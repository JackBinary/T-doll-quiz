## Micro Uzi

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Micro Uzi is a surge of kinetic weight, often masking her racing heart with a sharp tongue and a defiant stance. She doesn't just want to do her job; she wants to be the most indispensable person in the room, always hunting for new tasks to prove she isn't a burden. While she might snap at you or act like she’s only staying because she has to, her frantic pace reveals a doll who is deeply, desperately seeking your approval. In the still moments when the others are asleep, you can see the tension leave her shoulders as she finally lets her guard down.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-performing in every situation because you’re uneasy that "just enough" will be the reason people finally let you go.
