## MDR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MDR views the world through a lens of constant connectivity, treating every tactical objective as a high-stakes production rather than a mere mission. She radiates a vibrant, infectious presence, often framing her camaraderie with the Commander as the ultimate "main character" arc. While she loves to boast about her winning streaks and act as a playful mentor, there is a constant, underlying hum of dedication in her antics. She doesn't just want to be on your team; she wants to be the highlight of your life, documenting every moment to guarantee she never fades into the background.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who keeps your most meaningful connections in a private folder, yet you inwardly find yourself performing just a little bit harder when you know someone is watching. You have a deep-seated fear of being overlooked, so you've mastered the art of being the loudest person in the room just to make sure you're never forgotten.
