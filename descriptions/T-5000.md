## T-5000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
T-5000 walks with a restrained, deliberate care, always seeking to blend into the background despite her undeniable presence. She views her immense capabilities not as a source of pride, but as a heavy responsibility to be shouldered so that others don't have to. While she may stumble over her words when the spotlight finds her, she is most at ease in the stillness of a private room. There, her professional veneer softens, revealing a girl who finds her only true sanctuary in the steady, undivided attention of someone she trusts.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who does the most work in the room but would be the first to claim you "didn't really do much" if anyone tried to thank you. You find a strange, secret comfort in being the person everyone relies on, even if it means you have to stay out of the light to do it.
