## QBZ-191

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
QBZ-191 operates with the spirited force of a wandering hero, always ready to leap into action with a cheerful "can-do" attitude. While she speaks in grand metaphors of martial honor, she is inwardly the first one to double-check the base's supplies and keep a watchful eye on everyone’s well-being. She views the sanctuary of the base as a hard-won prize, finding her greatest peace in the measured moments where she can finally set down her heavy pack and rest.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who works incredibly hard to be the reliable pillar for everyone else, but you privately ache for a place where you don't have to be the one in charge for once.
