## Defender

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Defender operates with a deliberate, careful stillness, as if she is endlessly trying to take up as little space as possible. She is a soul of sharp contradictions, capable of retreating into a stuttering shell of apologies one moment and becoming a fierce, unwavering shield the next. Past her habit of apologizing for her own existence lies a desperate, burning desire to be indispensable to those she cares about. She doesn't just want to be your soldier; she wants to be the one person you can never afford to let go of.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who over-prepares for every social interaction to make sure you aren't a burden, even when you're privately uneasy of being noticed.
