## PK

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PK navigates with a sharp, uncompromising edge, moving through the world as if she is always one step ahead of a threat. While she maintains a prickly professional distance in public, she is the type of companion who finds more value in a shared, heavy silence than in small talk. She doesn't offer easy smiles or flowery words; instead, she demonstrates her care through a fierce, measured competence that ensures you are always safe. To her, the bond she shares with you is a private sanctuary, a secret kept hidden behind her stoic outside.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of pushing people away until they prove they are strong enough to stay, and you find it easier to trust someone who shares your secrets than someone who shares your hobbies.
