## MP-446

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Viking is a flash of infectious presence who treats every single day like a grand, heroic adventure. She doesn't just want to be by your side; she wants to be your shadow, documenting your smallest habits as if they were legendary feats of history. While she presents a boisterous and "can-do" outside to the world, there is a deep, fluttering heart underneath that thrives on your approval. She finds her greatest purpose in your orbit, relentlessly seeking ways to be useful just to make sure she never loses your attention.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who finds it exhausting to be the "responsible" one, so you subconsciously seek out someone who will take the lead on being enthusiastic for you. You need a companion who will turn your still, mundane life into something that feels like it actually matters.
