## MG3

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG3 operates with a restless, vibrating pull, as if she is consistently preparing to shatter a personal record. She views the world as a series of hurdles meant to be cleared through sheer willpower and refined muscle, often grumbling about her own physical limitations while simultaneously pushing everyone around her to do more. Behind her boisterous, "tough-love" shell lies a deeply protective heart that treats her teammates like precious projects in need of tempering. To her, a shared drop of sweat is the highest form of intimacy and a testament to a bond forged in the heat of honest effort.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who feels a secret, nagging guilt whenever you choose comfort over the version of yourself you know you're capable of becoming. You don't need a cheerleader; you need someone who will call you out on your excuses until you finally decide to get up.
