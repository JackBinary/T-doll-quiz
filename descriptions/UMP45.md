## UMP45

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
UMP45 operates in the world with a practiced, mischievous control, often trailing a playful giggle behind her words to keep others at arm's length. She hides her weariness behind a sharp tongue, preferring to trade barbs and sarcastic jokes rather than let anyone see the cracks in her armor. Below the performative wit lies a doll who deeply craves a sense of belonging, even if she’d rather mock your competence than admit how much she relies on your presence.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of overcompensating with humor because you’re anxious that if you stopped joking for one second, people might actually see how tired you are.
