## FARA 83

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Fara walks through the world as if she’s endlessly hunting for a masterpiece hidden in the mundane. She views every shared glance and restrained conversation as a fleeting miracle, one she feels a desperate, beautiful responsibility to capture before it vanishes into the past. While she is your most devoted chronicler, she isn't afraid to disrupt the "perfect" atmosphere with a goofy pose or a mischievous prank. To her, a memory isn't just a feeling; it’s a tangible treasure she wants to hold onto forever, even if she has to climb a tree or risk a bit of chaos to get the perfect shot.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who keeps a folder of screenshots and photos you’ll never look at again, just because the feeling you had in that exact second was too good to let go. You value the "vibe" of a moment more than the words spoken within it, often finding more depth in a shared silence than in a long conversation.
