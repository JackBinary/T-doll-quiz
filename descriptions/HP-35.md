## HP-35

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
HP-35 walks through the world with the poise of a debutante, never letting her composure slip even when the situation turns chaotic. Past her polished surface lies a heart that beats for the roar of an engine and the rush of a high-speed getaway. She is a burst of contradictions, capable of fussing over the smallest detail of your appearance one moment and leading a reckless, adrenaline-fueled charge the next. To her, friendship is a shared secret, a collection of still hideouts and the unspoken bond of two people who prefer to act rather than speak.

I SEE YOU
You have a sophisticated public face that you guard fiercely, but you privately crave a partner who is brave enough to help you break every single rule of it.
