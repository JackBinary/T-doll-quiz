## Ameli

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ameli operates with a composed, almost trembling precision, as if she is always trying to prove her own worth through perfect adherence to protocol. She finds comfort in the tangible, often spending her downtime meticulously sewing buttons onto her uniform to soothe her anxieties about being "faulty." While she speaks in formal reports and requests permission for every small action, there is a profound, hidden yearning in her eyes for someone to tell her that she is enough exactly as she is.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for every interaction because you’re uneasy that if you don't follow the "correct" script, people will see how much you're actually struggling.
