## F2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
F2000 radiates a polished, high-presence warmth that makes her feel like the backbone of any room she enters. She navigates her responsibilities with a bright smile and a signature "ufufu" giggle, though there is a deliberate, practiced quality to her cheerfulness. Below that vibrant front lies a deeply conscientious soul who leads with the weight of the team’s burdens so no one else has to. She finds her truest peace in still, domestic moments, looking for a safe space where she can finally set down her mask and just be herself.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works twice as hard to be "easy-going" because you’re scared of being a burden to the people you love. You have a secret exhaustion from performing for others, and you're looking for someone who can see the tired person behind the smile.
