## Five-seveN

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Five-seveN exists in the world with a playful spark, always ready with a witty remark or a mischievous giggle to keep everyone on their toes. She treats every interaction like a game of pursuit, using her competence as a pedestal from which to demand your undivided attention. While she loves to tease and playfully mock others to secure her spot by your side, there is a soft, almost fragile sincerity under her bravado. She doesn't just want to be part of your team; she wants to be the only person you truly see.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds it easier to lead with a joke than with a vulnerability, often hoping that if you can make someone laugh, they won't notice how much you actually need them to stay.
