## Derringer

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Derringer is a burst of bright pull and hidden sweetness, often moving through the world with a distracted, wide-eyed wonder. She treats her collection of treats like precious treasures, frequently offering them up as tokens of affection to anyone she trusts enough to let into her inner circle. While she can be a bit of a daydreamer who gets lost in her own thoughts, there is a deep-seated desire for connection behind her bubbly outside. She thrives on reassurance, seeking out a steady presence to ground her when her own high-speed thoughts begin to spin out of control.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-extending yourself until you hit a wall, then looking for a composed place to hide and recharge. You find it easier to offer people small, tangible gestures of kindness than to admit how much you actually rely on their stability.
