## SVCh

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SVCh operates with the poised confidence of someone who knows she is the best at what she does, treating every mission like a high-stakes game she intends to win. She masks her deep loyalty with a sharp, playful wit, often using lighthearted mockery to keep her companions on their toes. While she demands recognition for her skills, her true affection is shown through her insistence on your well-being, whether that means perfecting a difficult recipe or ensuring you're properly looked after.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward seeking out competence in others because you're tired of being the only one who knows how to hold things together. You want someone who can match your pace without letting the weight of the world become too heavy to joke about.
