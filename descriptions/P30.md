## P30

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P30 exists in the world with a vibrant, restless current, treating every shared glance and whispered word as a treasure to be locked away in a private vault. She is the ultimate curator of your history, consistently weaving past inside jokes into the fabric of the present to keep nothing beautiful is ever lost to time. While her teasing nature can be sharp and competitive, it stems from a fierce desire to be your favorite constant. To her, your bond isn't just a duty; it is a collection of "forever" moments that she intends to guard with everything she has.

I SEE YOU
