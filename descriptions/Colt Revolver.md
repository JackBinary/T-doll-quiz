## Colt Revolver

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Colt is a surge of cinematic flair, treating every ordinary moment like the climactic scene of a dusty Western epic. She holds herself with the boisterous pull of a seasoned showman, always seeking the spotlight to prove she is the hero of the story. Behind the theatrical "Yeehaas" and the performative bravado lies a doll who deeply craves your approval as her primary audience. Whether she’s fueling her spirit with a soda or striking a pose for the crowd, she lives for the thrill of the duel and the bond she shares with her partner.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward romanticizing your own struggles to make them feel more meaningful, finding your own brand of magic in the middle of the mundane.
