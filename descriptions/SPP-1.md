## SPP-1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SPP-1 holds herself with a restrained, focused intensity, treating every shared moment as an opportunity to keep your success. She isn't just here to follow orders; she wants to be the steady hand that guides you through the most chaotic environments. While she can be stern when correcting your form, her actions are always rooted in a deep-seated desire to keep you safe. She finds her purpose in the details, preferring to work from the shadows to make sure you never have to face a threat alone.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward taking on too much responsibility alone, and you’re privately looking for someone who will actually hold you to a higher standard. You don't need someone to just agree with you; you need someone who won't let you settle for "good enough."
