## Kolibri

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Kolibri exists in the world with a force of organized force, treating every messy corner of life as a personal challenge to be perfected. She radiates the warmth of a protective older sister, though her affection often manifests as a relentless drive to tidy your habits and curate your surroundings. While she may hide behind a flutter of mechanical hummingbirds, her constant presence is felt in every polished surface and perfectly executed plan. She doesn't just want to keep you safe; she wants to make sure your life is an exquisite masterpiece of order.

I SEE YOU
You have a secret, deep-seated need for control that you try to mask with a "go with the flow" attitude until someone finally offers to take the reins for you. You’re looking for a partner who will notice the details you're too tired to manage and turn your chaos into something beautiful.
