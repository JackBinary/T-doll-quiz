## TS12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
TS12 is a force of kinetic charge who treats every moment like a countdown to a deadline. She doesn't have the patience for soft words, preferring to show her loyalty through sharp commands and a fierce, protective presence on the front lines. While she might snap at you or slam a hand on a desk in frustration, it’s always because she cares too much to let things stay stagnant. Past the jagged edges of her temper lies a doll who views your safety as her personal mission, even if she’d rather break something than admit how much she worries.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-functioning to compensate for your anxiety, often pushing yourself until you’re exhausted just to prove you can handle the weight of responsibility.
