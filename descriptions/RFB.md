## RFB

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RFB navigates the world with a vibrant, high-octane force that makes every mundane task feel like a boss fight. She treats her bond with you as the ultimate co-op campaign, perpetually pushing for higher scores and new achievements with a mischievous glint in her eye. While she hides her genuine dedication behind a layer of "pro-gamer" slang and playful trash talk, her loyalty is as steady as a perfectly executed combo. To her, life isn't just a series of events; it’s a game she intends to win by your side.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to keep things light, using humor as a shield to guarantee that even the most stressful situations don't feel like a burden.
