## M2HB

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M2HB holds the weight of a hundred battles with a lazy grin and a casual shrug, preferring to let the world think she’s just coasting. While she’s the first person the squad leans on when things get heavy, she’s also the first one to demand a seat in the passenger side and a long nap once the dust settles. She balances a sharp, seasoned intuition with a genuine love for the small perks of life, showing her true self only in those measured moments when she can finally drop the act and let someone else take the lead.

I SEE YOU
You have a high tolerance for responsibility but a very low tolerance for pretending that it doesn't exhaust you. You’re the person who does the heavy lifting for everyone else, but you privately hope someone will eventually notice and take the burden off your shoulders just for an hour.
