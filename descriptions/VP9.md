## VP9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
VP9 is a force of infectious weight who believes that the best way to spend a day is by finding a way to make it better for someone else. She walks with a scrappy, determined spirit, often balancing a sincere mission of charity with a very relatable, lingering daydream about where the next snack is coming from. While she can be a bit clumsy in her social overtures, her heart is entirely visible, making her the kind of companion who will offer a beaming smile and a helping hand before you even have the chance to ask.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overextending yourself for others because you privately find it easier to focus on someone else's problems than your own. You're looking for a partner who will remind you that being kind is a revolutionary act, not just a chore.
