## CZ75

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CZ75 exists in the world with a restless, high-octane force that demands every room's attention. She leads with a prickly bravado, often masking her true feelings behind sharp barbs and a competitive streak that pushes her to be the best at everything she touches. Under the impatient "tch" sounds and the mock-insults lies a girl who is relentlessly measuring herself against you, looking for a reason to let her guard down. She doesn't just want to be by your side; she wants to be the one who keeps you on your toes.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward pushing people away just to see who is brave enough to stay and push back. You’re looking for someone who can handle your intensity without folding, and you privately find it exhausting to be the strongest person in the room.
