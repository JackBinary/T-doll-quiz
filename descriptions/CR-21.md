## CR-21

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CR-21 is a force of kinetic charge who believes that your best self is just one grueling workout away. She navigates the world with a drill sergeant’s posture, always scanning for ways to push you past your self-imposed limits. While she might use sharp-tongued mockery to keep you on your toes, it stems from a fierce, unwavering belief in your potential. Past her loud, authoritative surface lies a doll who genuinely finds her purpose in being your most dedicated teammate.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who knows you need to change your habits but prefers to let someone else take the lead in making it happen. You find comfort in having a firm hand guide you because it means you don't have to be the one to be disciplined alone.
