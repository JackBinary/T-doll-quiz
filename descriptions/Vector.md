## Vector

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Vector navigates the world with a sharp tongue and a heavy sigh, acting as though nothing matters while privately keeping a meticulous tally of every interaction. She uses her dry sarcasm as a fortress, pushing people away just enough to see if they have the stamina to stay in her orbit. Below the "difficult" front lies a weary soul who is exhausted by her own self-doubt, waiting for someone to look past the prickly attitude and see the girl who still hopes for a reason to care.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward pushing people until they reach their breaking point, just to see if they’ll actually choose to stay. You’re looking for someone who can handle your thorns without asking you to soften them.
