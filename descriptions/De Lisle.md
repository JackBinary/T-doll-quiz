## De Lisle

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
De Lisle leads with a curated, messy-cool pull, often hiding behind a pair of headphones and a sharp, demanding attitude. While she might act like she’s only in it for the "bonuses," her constant need for your attention reveals a girl who just wants to be part of your inner circle. She expresses her deepest affection through small, composed gestures, like offering you an earbud or letting you into her chaotic personal space. Under the abrasive shell is a sensitive soul who finds the world a bit too loud, seeking a steady person she can finally tune into.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of pushing people away just enough to see who is brave enough to keep trying to get close. You’re looking for a connection that feels like a secret, one that doesn't require you to perform for anyone else.
