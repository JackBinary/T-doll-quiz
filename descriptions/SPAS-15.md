## SPAS-15

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SPAS-15 is a spark of kinetic current who treats every moment like it’s the opening night of a grand performance. She thrives on the hum of shared interests and the electric spark of a well-timed joke, often using a playful "just kidding" as a safety net for the things she’s too brave to say out loud. While she acts like the life of the party, there is a sincere, fluttering yearning in her eyes whenever the music stops and it’s just the two of you. She doesn’t just want to stand by your side; she wants to make sure you’re laughing, reacting, and feeling every bit of the intensity she holds.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who pretends you don't mind the chaos, but you inwardly find a strange sense of peace in being the only one who can keep up with it. You have a way of looking for the spark in the mundane because you're anxious of things becoming too measured.
