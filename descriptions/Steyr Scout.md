## Steyr Scout

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Steyr is a constant hum of nervous force, always scanning the horizon as if searching for a threat that hasn't appeared yet. She masks her deep-seated insecurities behind a veneer of professional duty, often apologizing for the smallest hesitations in her stride. While she presents herself as a stoic scout, her true care is written in the way she lingers just a step behind you, her eyes consistently checking to make sure you are still there. She seeks your approval with a still, frantic intensity, viewing your safety as the only thing capable of anchoring her own drifting nerves.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for every minor social interaction because you're afraid of being the one who lets the team down. You find comfort in people who are just as hyper-aware as you are, even if it means perpetually having to reassure them that they’re doing just fine.
