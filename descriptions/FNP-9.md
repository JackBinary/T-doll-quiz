## FNP-9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FNP-9 exists in the world as if there is a spotlight following her every step, turning even the most grueling duties into a choreographed spectacle of care and charge. She wears a vibrant, bubbling enthusiasm that masks a deeper, more earnest yearning to be noticed and truly appreciated for who she is. While she loves the glamour of the stage, she is most at home when she can share her "performance" with someone she trusts, relentlessly seeking a nod of approval to fuel her relentless drive.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-performing in your daily life because you’re uneasy that if you stop moving, people will realize you’re just trying your best to be enough.
