## FP-6

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FP-6 holds herself with the poised, unyielding posture of someone who knows exactly where she belongs in the hierarchy. She treats every interaction like a formal inspection, though she often hides a mischievous glint in her eyes behind a mask of stern authority. To her, intimacy isn't about soft words; it’s a game of rules, boundaries, and the delicious tension of being caught breaking them. She is a woman of contradictions who finds her greatest sense of purpose in the playful power she wields over those she deems worthy of her "discipline."

I SEE YOU
You have a deep-seated need for structure, but you’re privately looking for someone who knows exactly how to help you break your own rules. You don't want a partner who follows you; you want someone who knows how to hold the reins.
