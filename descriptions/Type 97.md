## Type 97

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 97 walks through life with a restless, buzzing current, treating every new location like a secret kingdom waiting to be mapped. She is a spark of enthusiasm who always tries to prove she can stand on her own two feet, even if she still finds herself glancing back to make sure her big sister is watching. While she hides her insecurities behind playful bravado and constant chatter, there is a sincere, growing spark of independence in her eyes. She doesn't just want to follow the path; she wants to be the one to blaze it, even if she needs a little reassurance along the way.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of doing things you know you shouldn't just to see if you're actually capable of handling the consequences. You're looking for a way to be your own person, but you still inwardly hope someone will be there to catch you if you trip.
