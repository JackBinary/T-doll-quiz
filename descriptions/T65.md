## T65

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
T65 operates in the world with a rigid, self-imposed discipline, treating every small task as a matter of life and death. She expresses her care through constant, meticulous corrections and a "tough love" attitude that masks how deeply she actually cares about your well-being. While she presents a formidable, no-nonsense shell to the world, she is prone to sudden flashes of vulnerability when her perfectionist shell slips. Past the sharp commands lies a doll who would rather work herself into exhaustion than let you down.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels like you have to be indispensable to be valued, often overcompensating for your own self-doubt by being the most reliable person in the room.
