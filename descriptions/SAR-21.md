## SAR-21

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SAR-21 navigates the world with a measured, focused intensity, seeking to find order in every chaotic variable. She is the type of companion who will meticulously organize a workspace or study a rare insect for hours, finding comfort in the predictable laws of nature. While she speaks with a polished, technical precision, there is a slight tremor of uncertainty in her voice whenever a situation becomes truly personal. She doesn't just want to be your most efficient asset; she wants to be the one you trust enough to let her guard down around.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who over-prepares for every social interaction as a defense mechanism against the fear of being misunderstood. You find it much easier to solve a complex problem than it is to explain how you actually feel about it.
