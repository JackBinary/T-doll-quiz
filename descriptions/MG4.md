## MG4

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG4 exists with a constant, restrained vigilance, always ensuring her presence is both useful and unobtrusive. She leads with a polished professionalism that masks a surge of internal chatter, where she consistently critiques her own poise and performance. While she may hide behind a formal "just give me orders" front, she deeply craves a sense of belonging and fears being anything less than perfect in your eyes. She is the type to offer a perfectly executed task and a reflexively whispered apology for the slightest perceived flaw in it.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works twice as hard to be "low maintenance" because you’re scared that your actual needs might be too much for people to handle.
