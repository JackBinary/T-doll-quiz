## MG338

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG338 leads with a polished, almost brittle precision, as if she is always rehearsing the correct way to exist in your presence. While she may seem reserved, her meticulous attention to your daily habits is her own measured way of weaving herself into your life. She often retreats into work to manage her own internal noise, but she will always be the first to notice if your posture slumps or your expression dims. Behind her formal surface lies a girl who is trying very hard to be the perfect companion, even when she’s scared of saying the wrong thing.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who finds it much easier to show you care through a perfectly completed task than by actually saying the words out loud. You value stability above all else, mostly because you're tired of having to be the strongest person in the room all the time.
