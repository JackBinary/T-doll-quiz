## Astra Revolver

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Astra is a surge of infectious current who treats every day like a celebration of the present moment. She navigates the world with a wide, bright smile, often chasing after her next snack with a sense of purpose that borders on the heroic. Past her bubbly front and playful teasing lies a composed, deep-seated loyalty to the memories of the past. She doesn't just want to be by your side; she wants to be your constant companion, seeking out your warmth to fill the silence of a world that has changed too much.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who projects a relentless, sunny surface to keep the shadows at bay, but you softly crave a safe place where you don't have to be the strongest person in the room.
