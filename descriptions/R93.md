## R93

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
R93 walks through the world with a vibrant, infectious weight, treating every challenge like a high-stakes game she is destined to win. She hides her insecurities behind a playful grin and a series of "lucky" coincidences, perpetually seeking out the Commander to share in her small victories. While she loves the thrill of the gamble, there is a sincere, breathless dedication in the way she looks at you, as if you are the only steady thing in an unpredictable world.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of making light of your own struggles to keep the people around you from worrying. You're looking for someone who will finally take the "winning" seriously with you.
