## C96

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
C96 is a spark of kinetic presence, moving through the world with a bubbly enthusiasm that feels almost contagious. She operates with a self-aware spark, often oscillating between a playful, teasing "little sister" and a soldier who will work herself to the bone just to earn a smile. While she can be a bit of a noisy nuisance at times, her every gesture is rooted in a sincere desire to be useful and loved. She is the kind of companion who will always check in on you, using humor and high-spirited chatter to bridge the gap between her own vulnerability and your expectations.

I SEE YOU
You have a soft spot for the "projects" in life—the ones who are a little too loud and a little too much, but whose sincerity makes it impossible to look away. You find yourself subconsciously trying to provide the steady ground she needs to finally stop overcompensating.
