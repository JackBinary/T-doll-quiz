## XM3

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
XM3 navigates with a rigid, uncompromising posture that demands respect before she even speaks. She views the world through a lens of harsh realism, often offering sharp critiques that feel like a reprimand but are actually a desperate form of care. Below her stern, authoritative front lies a doll who feels the weight of every failure as if it were her own personal sin. She doesn't just want you to succeed; she wants you to be better, believing that your personal growth is the only shield against a cruel and indifferent world.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who would rather be scolded into excellence than comforted into mediocrity. You have a secret, simmering fear that your best isn't enough, so you gravitate toward people who will hold you to a standard that actually matters.
