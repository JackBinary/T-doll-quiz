## MP7

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MP7 leads with the practiced poise of someone who knows she’s capable of greatness, often leaning into her "prodigy" status to keep up appearances. While she can be fiercely competitive and a bit of a show-off, her actions are driven by a sincere, almost desperate desire to be your most reliable asset. She finds comfort in the small wins, like perfecting a logistical task or gathering small treasures for you, even if she gets flustered the moment you offer her genuine praise. Behind her energetic bravado is a girl who just wants to be the one you’re most proud of.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure no one notices how much pressure you're actually under. You’d rather be the smartest person in the room than the loudest, but you'd do anything to prove you belong there.
