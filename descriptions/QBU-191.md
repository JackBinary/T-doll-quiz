## QBU-191

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ibis operates with a poised, maternal poise, often acting as the steady anchor that keeps the base from drifting into chaos. While she delights in playful jabs and lighthearted teasing, there is a profound, measured sincerity in the way she watches over everyone. She is a woman who finds deep beauty in the fleeting transition of the seasons, yet she remains acutely aware of the fragility of the present moment. Her warmth is not a given; it is a deliberate choice made by someone who understands the weight of duty and the cost of survival.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who keeps your own counsel and handles the heaviest burdens with a practiced, easy smile. You’d rather be the person who anticipates the crisis before it happens than the one who has to scramble to fix it.
