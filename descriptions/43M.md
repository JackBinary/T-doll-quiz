## 43M

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
43M is the steady heartbeat of the squad, a girl who believes that a well-stocked pantry and a warm smile are the strongest defenses against any hardship. She operates with a bright, proactive presence, often appearing with heavy crates of supplies just because she noticed you looked slightly tired. While she can be a bit flustered when the spotlight turns toward her own feelings, her dedication is written in every meticulous detail of her work. She doesn't just want to complete the mission; she wants to make sure you feel safe enough to enjoy the victory afterward.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of carrying the weight of everyone’s expectations until your shoulders ache, and you’re without a word hoping someone will finally notice and take over the heavy lifting for you.
