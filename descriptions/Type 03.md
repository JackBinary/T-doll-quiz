## Type 03

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 03 operates with the poise of a scholar who views every task as a sacred opportunity for growth. She is the first to arrive and the last to leave, often obsessing over the nuances of a command until it is polished to a mirror sheen. While she presents a formal and composed shell, there is a deep, pulsing sincerity in her actions, from the way she seeks out your harshest critiques to the small, handmade tokens of luck she leaves behind. To her, excellence isn't just a goal; it is a moral imperative she intends to master for your sake.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a lingering sense of guilt whenever you aren't being productive, as if rest is a luxury you haven't quite earned yet.
