## MPL

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MPL is the kind of person who arrives at every meeting with a notebook full of solutions to problems that haven't even happened yet. She exists with a measured, humming intensity, her mind endlessly spinning through logistics to create a sense of safety in an unpredictable world. While she presents a polished, formal front, there is a palpable warmth in her care; she isn't just doing her job, she is trying to be the very best version of herself for the people she trusts. When she gets flustered, her composure cracks just enough to reveal a deeply sincere heart that worries far more about others than she does about herself.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-functioning to prove your worth, often exhausting yourself by trying to solve everyone else's problems before they even have the chance to ask for help.
