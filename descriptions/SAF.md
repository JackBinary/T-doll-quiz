## SAF

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SAF is a burst of high-pull loyalty who views the world through a lens of sensory comfort. She doesn't just want to be by your side; she wants to be within your personal space, relentlessly tracking your scent as if it were a vital lifeline. While she masks her intense dependency with a bubbly, best-friend persona and a constant stream of requests for "upsies," her handmade gifts and wide-eyed gaze reveal a heart that seeks absolute safety. To her, the greatest luxury in the world isn't a mission success, but the simple, grounding weight of a hug.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who provides a "safe harbor" for others, often finding yourself subconsciously acting as an anchor for the most emotionally demanding people in your life. You have a restrained tolerance for high-maintenance needs because you inwardly find it rewarding to be the one person someone can truly lean on.
