## Rex Zero 1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Rex Zero 1 leads with a polished, almost breathless sort of control, always moving a step ahead to keep everyone else is comfortable. She views her role as a series of small debts to be repaid, often tucking away tiny mementos as physical proof of the bonds she’s built. While she maintains a professional veneer, there is a constant, underlying warmth in her eyes that betrays her genuine desire to be indispensable. She is the kind of companion who will stay awake until the job is done, apologizing for her own exhaustion while making sure you feel entirely taken care of.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels a strange, secret guilt whenever you allow yourself to be the one who is looked after for once.
