## PM-06

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PM-06 navigates the world with a vibrant, kinetic presence that feels like a constant invitation to something slightly forbidden. She treats every shared secret as a pact and every mission as an aesthetic performance, finding a strange, beautiful harmony in the contrast between a glass of wine and the heat of a skirmish. While she radiates a bubbly warmth, there is a sharp, predatory spark in her eyes that suggests she isn't just looking for a companion, but a co-conspirator who isn't afraid to get their hands dirty.
