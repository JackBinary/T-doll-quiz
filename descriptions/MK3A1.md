## MK3A1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MK3A1 exists in the world with a frantic, kinetic current, her hands always seeking the next gear to turn or bolt to tighten. She views everything as a puzzle waiting to be solved, often losing herself in the contained hum of a workshop where she can turn her anxiety into tangible progress. While she can be overwhelmed by direct eye contact, her attachment shines through in the meticulous way she refines the tools around her. To her, love isn't a sentiment to be spoken; it is a masterpiece of engineering to be perfected one component at a time.

I SEE YOU
