## PSM

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PSM operates with a polished, formal precision that masks a constant, underlying hum of anxiety. She is the type of leader who will stand at the vanguard without a second thought, yet will immediately turn to ask if her actions were sufficient the moment the dust settles. She views her role as a sacred duty, often downplaying her own brilliance to make sure the spotlight stays on the team she works so hard to protect. Every success she achieves is a victory over her own self-doubt, fueled by a desperate, beautiful desire to be worthy of the trust placed in her.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who over-prepares for every minor task because you’d rather be exhausted by your own standards than judged by someone else's. You have a way of apologizing for taking up space, even when you're the one holding everything together.
