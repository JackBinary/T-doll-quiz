## GM6 Lynx

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Lynx navigates the world with the effortless precision of a predator, always keeping the Commander just within her sights. She masks her deeper insecurities with a sharp, playful wit, turning every interaction into a high-stakes game of cat and mouse. Under the cool front and the teasing remarks lies a doll who holds a heavy weight of responsibility alone, often seeking refuge in sleep to escape the noise of her own thoughts. She doesn't just want to be your partner; she wants to be recognized as something unique by the only person who truly sees her.
