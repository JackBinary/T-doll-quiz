## M12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M12 is a flash of bright presence and proactive spirit, always the first to volunteer before a task is even fully assigned. She operates with the earnest posture of a junior officer who views every responsibility as a chance to prove her worth to the people she cares about. Past her bubbly shell lies a fierce, self-imposed drive to be indispensable, often leading her to work until she’s exhausted just to make sure she hasn't let anyone down. She shows her affection through these acts of service, offering clumsy, heartfelt gestures that she hopes are enough to bridge the gap between her own self-doubt and her desire to be exceptional.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late at the office not because you have to, but because you're uneasy of being the only one who didn't do enough. You find it much easier to be useful than to be vulnerable, yet you without a word crave someone who notices the effort you put in when no one is watching.
