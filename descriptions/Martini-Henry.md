## Martini-Henry

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Martini-Henry leads with the poise of someone who knows exactly how the story ends, even if she occasionally exaggerates the drama of the script. She is a burst of high-spirited weight, endlessly looking for opportunities to prove her superiority by correcting the smallest details of the present. While she hides behind a haughty, "I-told-you-so" attitude, her playful demands for treats and her frantic blushing when things get personal reveal a girl who deeply values being your favorite confidante. She doesn't just see the future; she wants to be the one who leads you through it, one triumphant "humph" at a time.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward seeking out people who are just a little bit more competent than you, mostly because you find their confidence infectious. You’re the type to privately enjoy being corrected as long as the person doing it is doing it with enough flair.
