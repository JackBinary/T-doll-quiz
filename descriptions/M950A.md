## M950A

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M950A leads with a crisp, polished professionalism that feels almost like a shield against the world. While she often defaults to a blunt and no-nonsense attitude, it is merely a way to navigate her deep-seated fear of being viewed as just another interchangeable part of a production line. Below that sharp surface lies a girl who keeps every small gift and every scrap of personal attention tucked away like a treasure. She is desperately trying to prove that she is a unique individual, even if her heart beats a little too fast whenever she is the one being looked at.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overperforming in your daily life because you’re scared that if you aren't perfect, you'll become invisible. You find it much easier to let people see your competence than your needs.
