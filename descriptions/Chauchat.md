## Chauchat

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Chauchat is a surge of nervous weight and meticulous dedication, often seen triple-checking her gear with a frantic, focused intensity. She holds a heavy weight of responsibility on her shoulders, viewing every small task as a vital contribution to the team's safety. While she might occasionally stumble over her words or let a moment of insecurity show, she compensates with a tireless work ethic and a genuine desire to be indispensable. To her, showing love isn't about grand gestures, but about ensuring every detail is perfect so that those she cares about never have to worry.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like you have to be twice as prepared as everyone else just to feel like you’re doing enough. You have a pattern of turning your own anxieties into a very organized, very exhausting to-do list.
