## SCR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SCR navigates the world with a polished, high-voltage weight, always aware of the invisible lens capturing her every move. She treats her daily life as a masterpiece of curation, balancing the bubbly charm of a public idol with a fierce, grounded dedication to the soil and the harvest. While she radiates warmth and "content-ready" smiles, there is a sharp, protective edge to her personality that emerges whenever her personal standards or her beloved produce are at risk. To her, love isn't just a feeling; it is the meticulous labor of hand-picking the perfect gift and ensuring her inner circle is well-fed and perfectly presented.
