## OBR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
OBR exists in a state of perpetual, fluttering motion, her hands often twitching as if she's mentally balancing a ledger that never quite stays in the black. She is the kind of companion who will over-deliver on every task until she’s exhausted, all while trying to hide the fact that she’s anxious of being a burden. Behind her frantic apologies and nervous habit of scanning for "hidden costs" lies a heart of pure, fierce loyalty. To her, your trust isn't just a gift—it’s a sacred debt she intends to spend her entire existence trying to repay.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels a physical weight in your chest when you can't "repay" a favor, leading you to work yourself into a state of composed burnout just to feel like you're even.
