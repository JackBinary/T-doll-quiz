## FAL

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FAL holds herself with the poised authority of someone who has seen every possible outcome and decided she won't let any of them be messy. She is the kind of presence who instinctively reaches out to straighten your collar or reorganize your desk before you even realize things are out of place. While her front is defined by sharp standards and a "tough love" mentorship, there is a fierce, protective dedication in every correction she makes. She doesn't just want to follow your lead; she wants to guarantee you are the most capable version of yourself.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of taking on too much weight alone, so you gravitate toward someone who will step in and take the reins of the details you're too exhausted to handle. You don't want a subordinate; you want a partner who is brave enough to tell you when you're making a mistake.
