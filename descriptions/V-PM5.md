## V-PM5

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PM5 is the kind of girl who keeps her head down, consistently smoothing out her uniform as if trying to make herself smaller. She views every task as a mountain to climb, often apologizing for the mere space she occupies while she meticulously double-checks every detail. Despite her trembling hands and constant self-doubt, there is a profound warmth in her desire to be helpful, often channeling her nervous presence into the restrained, domestic art of cooking. She doesn't want to be a hero; she just wants to be someone you can rely on, even if she has to find her own footing one shaky step at a time.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like a burden for having needs, so you've perfected the art of trying to be invisible while doing everything right. You'd rather overthink a plan until it's perfect than risk making a mistake that someone else has to fix.
