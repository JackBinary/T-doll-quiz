## RMB-93

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RMB-93 walks with a sharp, polished control, approaching every interaction as a matter of professional excellence. She expresses her loyalty through the meticulous upkeep of her duties, often framing her care for others as a matter of "proper compensation" or "contractual obligation." Under her authoritative shell and her habit of correcting every minor oversight, there is a doll who takes immense pride in being indispensable. She is the first to offer a stern critique and the last to admit that she privately thrives on being the one you rely on most.

I SEE YOU
