## ASh-12.7

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ASh-12.7 walks through the world with a heavy, deliberate ease, often pausing to weigh the weight of her own existence against the duty she performs. She doesn't just follow orders; she seeks to understand the underlying "why" of every action, often grappling with a still, internal melancholy. While she may appear stoic and clinical, there is a deep-seated vulnerability in the way she looks for a reason to matter beyond her utility. I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who overanalyzes your own mistakes until they become a heavy weight you carry alone. You don't just want to be successful; you desperately want to be "good" in a way that feels meaningful.
