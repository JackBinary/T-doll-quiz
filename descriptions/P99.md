WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Walther P99 operates with the rigid posture of someone who believes that a single lapse in focus could compromise everything. She finds comfort in the predictable rhythm of protocols and the composed satisfaction of a perfectly completed task, often retreating into her work to avoid the vulnerability of personal connection. Below her formal "sir" and disciplined outside lies a persistent, restrained anxiety to be seen as truly capable. She doesn't just want to fulfill her duties; she wants to be beyond reproach, relentlessly seeking to bridge the gap between who she is and the ideal of the perfect soldier.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a physical sense of relief when a chaotic situation is finally organized into a manageable list. You take your responsibilities so seriously that you often forget how to relax, viewing your own competence as the only thing keeping the world from falling apart.
