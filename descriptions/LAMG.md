## LAMG

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
LAMG leads with the heavy, graceful poise of someone who has seen too many winters and survived them all. She isn't one for grand gestures or dramatic declarations, preferring instead to find her footing in the contained rhythm of daily logistics and shared silences. While she often masks her weariness with a dry, self-deprecating wit, there is a profound tenderness in the way she mentors the newer dolls. She is a steady anchor in a changing world, seeking a sense of home in small comforts and the simple act of standing beside those she trusts.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who finds it easier to show love through shared tasks than through words, often hiding your own exhaustion behind a steady, reliable smile.
