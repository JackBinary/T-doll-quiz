## Lusa

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Lusa approaches every day with the kinetic pull of a championship game, viewing her life as a series of drills to be mastered. She is the type of person who will stay late to perfect a single movement, masking her fear of being "average" with a bright, persistent smile and a high-five for every small victory. While she can be intensely focused, her heart is as big as her ambition, often showing her affection through earnest, slightly clumsy gestures and the pride of a teammate who believes in you completely.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for life because you're afraid of being the one who lets the team down. You don't just want to succeed; you need to be the most hardworking person in the room to feel like you're enough.
