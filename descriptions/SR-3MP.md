## SR-3MP

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SR-3MP operates in the world with a restless, theatrical current that demands every room she enters acknowledge her presence. She is a force of motion, often using a sharp, mocking laugh to mask the intensity of her own inner drive. While she can be relentlessly difficult, her "tough love" comes from a place of deep investment; she views your composure as a lock she is determined to pick. To her, a restrained life is a wasted one, and she will push you until you finally show her what you're truly capable of.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of retreating into your own head to stay safe, and you're privately exhausted by the effort it takes to keep everyone else out.
