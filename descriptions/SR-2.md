## SR-2

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SR-2 is a flash of kinetic current who treats every operation like a high-stakes game where the only rule is to keep moving. She thrives in the chaos of the front lines, masking her own restless intensity with a boisterous laugh and a sharp, teasing wit. To her, a tactical stalemate is a personal insult, and she will gladly bulldoze through any obstacle just to feel the rush of momentum again. She views her bond with the Commander as a playful challenge, perpetually testing boundaries with a cheeky, "know-it-all" attitude that keeps everyone on their toes.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward making life difficult for yourself just to see if you can handle the adrenaline of the outcome. You’re tired of playing it safe and are looking for someone who won't apologize for being a little bit reckless.
