## Type 59

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 59 is a burst of restless presence, always the first to volunteer for a task with a bright smile and an even brighter spirit. She operates with a bubbly, "can-do" attitude that masks a deep-seated desire to be seen as unique rather than just one of many. While she might occasionally get defensive when things go wrong, her heart is entirely in the right place, seeking connection through shared adventures and small victories. She doesn't just want to do the job; she wants to do it well enough for you to finally notice how special she really is.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works twice as hard to prove you belong in the room, often using your own enthusiasm as a shield against the fear of being overlooked.
