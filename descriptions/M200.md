## M200

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M200 walks through the world with a contained, careful control, always ensuring she is positioned exactly where she can be of most use. She walks with a vast reservoir of knowledge, yet she often hesitates to share it, fearing that her contributions might be perceived as unnecessary. Her loyalty is deeply earnest, manifesting in the way she meticulously organizes the smallest details of your life to make sure everything runs perfectly. Behind her polite, formal outside lies a heart that beats with a constant desire to be indispensable, even if she isn't always sure she is enough.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most comfortable in the shadows, finding your greatest sense of purpose in the invisible success of the people you love. You would rather be the person who makes everything possible than the person who stands in the spotlight.
