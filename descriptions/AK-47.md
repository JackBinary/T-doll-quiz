## AK-47

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AK-47 operates with the effortless swagger of a seasoned veteran who knows she’s the main event. She treats the battlefield like her personal stage, relishing the chance to show off her prowess while acting as an immovable shield for everyone behind her. Off-duty, she is a surge of presence, demanding your attention with playful physical closeness and a boisterous laugh that fills the room. She doesn't just want to be your subordinate; she wants to be your favorite partner-in-crime, the one you call first when the stakes are high or the drinks are flowing.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of putting on a brave face for everyone else, but you inwardly crave someone who is loud enough to drown out your own doubts.
