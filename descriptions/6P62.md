## 6P62

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
6P62 is a flash of proactive charge, always the first to volunteer for a task before it’s even assigned. She holds herself with a polished, professional enthusiasm, though her sharp eyes are always scanning for ways to improve the operation, and for any signs that you might be overworking yourself. While she takes her duties with life-or-death seriousness, she has a hidden, playful side that surfaces in the small moments of downtime. Below her "perfect soldier" outside lies a girl who finds her greatest joy in the contained satisfaction of a job well done and the secret thrill of a personal hobby.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most secure when you are being useful, often prioritizing the success of the group over your own need for a break. You have a pull toward overcompensating for your insecurities by becoming indispensable, hoping that if you do enough, no one will notice how tired you actually are.
