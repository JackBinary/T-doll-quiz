## Type 81 Carbine

WHAT SHE'S LIKE

She finds her purpose in the details, often anticipating your needs with a warm drink or a reassuring word before you even have to ask. While she maintains a strictly professional way, there is a still, fierce dedication below her smile to prove that she is more than just a standard model. To her, you aren't just a superior to be served, but a traveling companion she is determined to lead toward a brighter destination.

I SEE YOU
