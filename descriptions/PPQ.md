## PPQ

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PPQ approaches every interaction as a complex blueprint to be decoded and mastered. She finds comfort in the rigid structure of technical specifications, often attempting to translate human warmth into manageable logistical data or precise measurements. While she maintains a stoic, by-the-book front, there is a composed, earnest determination below her surface to succeed in her "humanity project." She doesn't just want to follow orders; she wants to execute them with such flawless precision that her competence becomes her most sincere form of affection.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most secure when there is a clear system to follow, even if you inwardly spend your nights wondering if you're doing it the "right" way. You value competence over charisma, and you'd rather be told you're useful than told you're special.
