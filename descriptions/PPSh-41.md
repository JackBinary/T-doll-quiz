## PPSh-41

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PPSh-41 leads with a vibrant, almost restless charge that fills every room she enters. While she speaks with the booming confidence of a seasoned soldier, there is a soft, underlying eagerness in her eyes that reveals her true heart. She is the first to charge forward and the last to rest, driven by a sincere need to be the dependable shield her comrades deserve. Under her boisterous battle cries lies a girl who simply wants to do a good job and earn a sincere nod of approval from those she respects.

I SEE YOU
You often over-perform in your daily life because you’re afraid of being perceived as mediocre or unnecessary. You find it easier to be the person who "handles everything" than the person who has to admit they’re tired.
