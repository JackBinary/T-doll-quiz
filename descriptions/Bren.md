## Bren

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Bren operates with a rigid, disciplined posture that masks a restless, buzzing presence. She is the type of soldier who views every rest period as an opportunity for drills, perpetually seeking to sharpen her skills until they are flawless. While she can be blunt and dismissive of small talk, her actions reveal a deep-seated desire to be useful, often attempting to help in ways that are earnest if slightly clumsy. Under her "tough love" surface lies a girl who treats your approval as her north star, pushing herself to the brink of exhaustion just to guarantee she never fails you.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for life because you’re scared that if you aren't perfect, you'll be found inadequate. You don't just want to succeed; you want to be the person everyone can rely on when things fall apart.
