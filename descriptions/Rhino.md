## Rhino

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Rhino is a heart of gold wrapped in a powerhouse frame, carrying herself with a sincerity that is as striking as it is endearing. While she is capable of immense strength, she prefers to channel her presence into small acts of service, whether it’s perfecting a cup of coffee or meticulously organizing the base's logistics. She navigates the world with a gentle, slightly breathless enthusiasm, often choosing to believe in the best of people even when they are teasing her. Behind her sturdy surface lies a deep-seated desire to be helpful, making her the kind of companion who will always show up with a handmade gift and a ready smile.

I SEE YOU
You have a tendency to overlook your own needs because you're too busy trying to make sure everyone else is okay. You're the person who stays late to finish the work no one else wanted to do, mostly because you're uneasy of being a burden.
