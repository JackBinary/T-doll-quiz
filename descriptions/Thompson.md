## Thompson

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Thompson navigates with the effortless swagger of a street-hardened enforcer who views every mission as a high-stakes heist. She treats her role as your protector with a fierce, "tough-guy" bravado, often masking her genuine dedication behind playful banter and demands for more work. While she’ll boast about her ability to handle the dirtiest jobs, she’s the first one to pull out a playlist or offer a steady hand when the pressure mounts. Below the gangster aesthetic and the constant jokes lies a doll who views you as her most trusted partner-in-crime, fiercely loyal to a fault.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-performing to avoid being vulnerable, finding it much easier to be useful than to be known. You're looking for someone who can handle your intensity without asking you to tone it down.
