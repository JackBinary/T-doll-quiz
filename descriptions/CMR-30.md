## CMR-30

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CMR-30 holds herself with a boisterous, high-octane pull that fills any room she enters. She is the kind of mentor who believes that the best way to show someone you care is to push them past their perceived limits, often using "training from hell" as her primary love language. Past her demanding, no-nonsense front lies a protective streak that manifests in small, practical gestures of kindness. She doesn't just want you to succeed; she wants you to be someone you're proud of, even if she has to be the loudest person in the room to make it happen.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who feels most comfortable under pressure because it’s the only time you can stop overthinking and just act. You’re tired of being coddled and are inwardly looking for someone who will hold you to a higher standard than you hold yourself.
