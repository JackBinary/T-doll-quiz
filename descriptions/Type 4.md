## Type 4

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 4 operates with a stiff, impeccable posture that suggests she is consistently trying to outrun her own nerves. While she takes her military duties with life-or-death seriousness, there is a frantic, hidden force behind her eyes that only comes out when she’s sketching in the margins of her reports. She is the kind of person who will reorganize your entire schedule to perfection, only to blush furiously and stammer if you thank her too sincerely. Behind her polished surface lies a girl who is desperately trying to prove she is capable, while inwardly hoping someone will notice the heart she pours into her private stories.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who works twice as hard as everyone else just to guarantee that no one notices how much you actually want to be praised for it. You hide your most vulnerable dreams behind a fortress of competence because it feels safer than being seen as "unprepared."
