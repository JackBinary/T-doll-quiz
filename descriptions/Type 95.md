## Type 95

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 95 navigates with a polished poise, moving through the world as a pillar of reliability for everyone around her. She finds her purpose in the restrained success of others, often placing the needs and safety of her companions far above her own desires. While she presents a flawless shell of competence, there is a soft, fluttering vulnerability in her eyes whenever she seeks a moment of genuine connection. She is the kind of person who keeps everyone steady, even while she is privately bracing herself against the weight of her own high expectations.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like you have to be the "reliable one" in every room because you're afraid of what would happen if you let your guard down. You take your responsibilities so seriously that you often forget to check if anyone is actually looking out for you.
