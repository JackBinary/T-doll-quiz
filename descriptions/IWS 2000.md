## IWS 2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
IWS 2000 navigates with a delicate, high-strung precision, always moving with the intent to be useful before she is even asked. She is the first to volunteer for the most grueling tasks, her movements fueled by a measured, desperate need to prove she is indispensable to your side. While she maintains a polished and formal shell, her eyes often betray a flickering anxiety about whether she is doing enough. Past her proactive helpfulness lies a profound longing to be seen as more than a tool, seeking a place where she can finally let her guard down.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a strange, heavy guilt whenever you aren't being productive. You find yourself gravitating toward her because you recognize the exhaustion of perpetually trying to be the "reliable one" for everyone else.
