## M60

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M60 is the kind of companion who arrives before you’ve even finished making your plans, radiating a restless, "can-do" current that keeps the team moving forward. She takes immense pride in her reliability, often treating every successful mission as a personal victory to be celebrated with tangible rewards. Behind her brisk, soldierly shell lies a playful streak of stubbornness, and she isn't afraid to call you out when she thinks you're getting a bit too comfortable. Despite her high-octane pace, she possesses a hidden, still appreciation for the moments when the world slows down and the work is finally done.

I SEE YOU
You pride yourself on being the person everyone can lean on, but you privately find yourself looking for an excuse to let someone else take the lead for once.
