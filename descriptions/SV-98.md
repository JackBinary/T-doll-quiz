## SV-98

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SV-98 holds herself with a polished, almost rigid professionalism, always looking for ways to be indispensable to the team. Behind her meticulous attention to detail lies a heart that worries endlessly about falling short of the high standards she sets for herself. She is the type to over-prepare for every task, finding solace in data to mask her inner nerves, yet she will be the first to offer a handmade gift or a sincere apology if she feels she’s let anyone down.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to double-check the details because you’d rather be exhausted than be responsible for a mistake. You have a way of over-functioning to prove your value, often forgetting that people already see how hard you’re trying.
