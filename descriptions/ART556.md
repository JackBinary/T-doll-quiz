## ART556

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ART556 approaches the world with a wide-eyed sense of wonder, treating every grueling mission like a high-stakes scavenger hunt. She is the first to offer a giggle when things get tense, endlessly trying to turn the mundane routine of the base into a shared adventure. While she occasionally trips over her own feet or stammers when trying to act serious, her sincerity is undeniable. She doesn't just want to be a soldier; she wants to be your partner in crime, the one who finds the magic in the middle of the gray.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of looking for the "fun" in situations that everyone else has already decided are miserable. You'd rather be a chaotic participant in a beautiful mess than a perfect observer of a dull reality.
