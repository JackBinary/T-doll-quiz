## Vigneron M2

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Vigneron M2 exists in the world with a frantic, infectious weight, her mind endlessly buzzing with the rhythmic hum of machinery and the potential of discarded scrap. She treats every broken gear as a story waiting to be retold, often getting so lost in the "romance" of a repair that she forgets to eat or sleep. While she might hide behind her tools to avoid direct emotional intimacy, her attachment shines through in the unauthorized upgrades she sneaks into your workspace. She doesn't just want to fix things; she wants to see what they can become when given a little bit of her own restless imagination.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who feels a strange, restrained sense of peace in a cluttered workspace and prefers the honest reliability of a machine over the unpredictability of a person.
