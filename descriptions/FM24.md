## FM24

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FM24 walks through the world with the sharp, calculated care of a high-stakes negotiator. She presents a polished surface where every gesture is a transaction and every favor is bound by the safety of a formal agreement. Under the corporate jargon and the drive for "market value," however, lies a fierce, almost desperate loyalty that she only grants to those she deems worthy of her trust. She doesn't just want to be your ally; she wants to be your most valuable and reliable asset, protecting your interests with a ferocity that borders on the personal.

I SEE YOU
You use your competence as a shield, finding it much easier to manage a complex project than to navigate the messy unpredictability of someone else's feelings.
