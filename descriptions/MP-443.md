## MP-443

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Rook views the world as a vast, dusty workshop waiting to be curated into something beautiful. She operates with a bubbly current, her eyes relentlessly scanning the ground for the sparkle of a hidden mineral or the perfect curve of a stone. To her, everything, and everyone, is a masterpiece in progress, and she treats her closest bonds with the same meticulous care she gives her carvings. There is a playful, teasing edge to her dedication, but behind her whimsical "teehee" and gem-metaphors lies a deep, artistic pride in the treasures she chooses to keep by her side.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of finding the hidden value in things others dismiss as junk, and you without a word crave a connection that feels like a curated sanctuary from the world.
