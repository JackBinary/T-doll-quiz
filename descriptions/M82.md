## M82

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M82 holds herself with a polished, diligent poise that often masks the frantic humming of her internal anxieties. She is the first to arrive and the last to leave, consistently seeking out new ways to be indispensable because she fears that her value might one day expire. While she may struggle to voice her own needs, her attachment is written in the small, meticulous details of her actions, like an over-prepared gift or a perfectly executed task. Past her dutiful surface lies a whimsical heart that occasionally flickers to life, showing a girl who just wants to be certain that she has a permanent place by your side.

I SEE YOU
