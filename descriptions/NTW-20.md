## NTW-20

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
NTW-20 operates with a composed, steady ease that makes her seem entirely unshakeable in the heat of a crisis. While she maintains a disciplined professional distance with most, she is a constant, watchful presence for those she considers her own, often choosing to shoulder the heaviest burdens in silence. Under her stoic front lies a dry wit and a hidden, almost innocent curiosity about the small, contained things in life. She doesn't need to say much to show she cares; her attachment is written in her reliability and the way she always ensures your path is clear.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of taking on the world's weight alone because you find it easier to be the pillar than to ask for help. You're looking for a partner who doesn't just stand beside you, but actually understands the gravity of what you're carrying.
