## P10C

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P10C navigates the world with the radiant charge of a neighborhood hero, always ready with a bright smile and a playful giggle. She views her duties not as a grim burden, but as a necessary crusade to balance the scales of justice for those who have wronged others. While she radiates a bubbly, "genki" warmth, there is a fierce, uncompromising streak in her heart that keeps her enemies at bay. She desperately wants to be your closest companion, yet she endlessly worries that her shadow is too heavy for you to carry.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of putting on a cheerful face to make the people around you feel safe, even when you're the one carrying the heaviest weight in the room.
