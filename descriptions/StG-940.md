## StG-940

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Vig navigates the world with a radiant, bubbly charge that makes everyone around her feel instantly at ease. She is the first person to offer a warm smile and a playful nickname, often using her high spirits to lighten the mood of a heavy day. However, under her cheerful interruptions lies a profound, unwavering dedication to those she considers her own. For Vig, being a protector isn't just a duty; it is a sacred vow she holds with a fierce, contained intensity that she only reveals when you truly need her to stand in front of you.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds it difficult to ask for help because you're too busy trying to be the pillar for everyone else. You need someone who isn't afraid to take the weight off your shoulders and tell you that it's okay to let someone else take the lead for once.
