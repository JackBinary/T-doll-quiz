## MAT-49

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MAT-49 navigates with the rigid, earnest poise of a knight who believes she is destined for a hero’s tragedy. She doesn't just perform her duties; she treats every minor task as a sacred oath of protection, often fussing over your safety with a sincerity that borders on the overwhelming. While she can be intensely formal, there is a charming, eccentric spark to her that surfaces when she becomes obsessed with researching the past or meticulously inspecting the world around her. Past her chivalrous surface lies a heart that consistently seeks validation, finding her entire purpose in the hope of earning your favor.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a heavy sense of responsibility for the people you love, often sacrificing your own peace of mind to make sure everyone else is safe. You find comfort in clear roles and traditional values because the chaos of the world feels much easier to manage when you have a code to live by.
