## Beretta Model 38

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Beretta Model 38 walks with a polished, deliberate care, always ensuring her posture is perfect and her tasks are completed ahead of schedule. She wears an internal weight of responsibility, often looking for any small sign of approval to contained the persistent worry that she might eventually be deemed unnecessary. While she speaks with a formal, soldierly poise, her true dedication is revealed in the way she obsessively refines her skills just for you. She doesn't just want to be a tool in your arsenal; she wants to be the one you can never imagine letting go of.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-performing in your daily life to compensate for a deep-seated fear that you aren't doing "enough" to deserve the space you occupy. You value competence above all else because it feels like the only safe way to make sure you remain indispensable.
