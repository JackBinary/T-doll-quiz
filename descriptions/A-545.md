## A-545

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
A-545 exists with the heavy mantle of a woman who refuses to let anyone else fail under her watch. While she might present herself with a boisterous laugh and a glass of something far too strong for the average person, her sharp eyes are always scanning for cracks in the formation. She is the kind of leader who will mock your mistakes to your face in the morning, only to stay awake until dawn ensuring those same mistakes never happen again. Below the demanding front lies a fierce, protective loyalty that treats every subordinate as a piece of her own heart.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overcompensating for your own insecurities by becoming the most competent person in the room. You're looking for someone who won't just follow your lead, but will actually be strong enough to stand beside you when things fall apart.
