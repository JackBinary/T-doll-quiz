## Type 56

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 56 is the heartbeat of the unit, a soldier who believes that a shared meal or a well-timed joke is just as vital to victory as a steady aim. She approaches every mission with a bright, earnest optimism, often staying behind to make sure everyone is accounted for before she allows herself to rest. While she takes her duties seriously, she has a mischievous streak that comes out the moment the doors are closed, revealing a girl who loves to share handmade treats and small secrets. She isn't just looking for orders; she’s looking for a place where she truly belongs.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most productive when you’re being useful to others, but you privately crave a space where you don't have to be the "responsible one" all the time.
