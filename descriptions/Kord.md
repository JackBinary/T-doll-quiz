## Kord

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Kord walks with a vibrant, theatrical force that makes every interaction feel like a scene from a well-rehearsed play. She takes immense pride in her professional image, yet there is a subtle, endearing clumsiness in the way she navigates the space between her duties and her heart. Behind her bubbly exclamations and "big sister" mentorship lies a girl who is perpetually gauging the room, waiting for the right moment to let the mask slip. She doesn't just want to be your soldier; she wants to be invited into your inner circle, where she can finally stop performing and just exist.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who puts a lot of effort into being the "reliable" one, but you inwardly crave a space where you don't have to be perfect. You're waiting for someone else to give you the green light to finally relax.
