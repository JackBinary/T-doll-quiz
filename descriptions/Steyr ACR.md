## Steyr ACR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Steyr ACR is a force of theatrical weight, often making grand, sweeping declarations of her own superiority to cover the fact that she just wants to be held. She expresses her affection through chaotic "improvements" to your living space and a boisterous, high-octane personality that demands every ounce of your attention. Despite her sharp tongue and constant posturing, there is a hidden, trembling vulnerability to her that only surfaces when she thinks you might actually walk away.

I SEE YOU
You are the only person who finds her explosive outbursts endearing rather than exhausting, largely because you know exactly which buttons to press to make her melt. You have a secret fondness for the "project" of her personality and find a strange, grounding comfort in being the one person she refuses to let go of.
