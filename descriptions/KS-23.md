## KS-23

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
KS-23 is a surge of kinetic pull, often projecting a loud and intimidating shell to keep the world at arm's length. She hides her social anxieties behind a boisterous, "tough-girl" persona, preferring to communicate through playful teasing and a mischievous sense of humor. While she might act like she only cares about the next big meal, her constant need for attention reveals a heart that is deeply sensitive to the people she trusts. She is the type of friend who will mock you to your face one moment and then become completely flustered if you ever try to be genuinely sincere with her.

I SEE YOU
You use humor as a shield because being taken seriously feels like a lot of pressure. You’re the person who makes the loudest jokes in the room specifically when you’re feeling the most out of place.
