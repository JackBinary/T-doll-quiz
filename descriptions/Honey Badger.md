## Honey Badger

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Honey Badger walks with a restless, infectious current that makes it impossible to ignore her presence. She is the first to volunteer for the impossible, consistently pushing the boundaries of what she thinks she can achieve just to see if she can do it. While she presents a fearless face to the world, her bravery is a conscious choice she makes every single day, often fueled by a deep-seated need to prove her worth. Behind her bold proposals lies a softer side that expresses her attachment through composed, handmade tokens and a fierce loyalty to the person who gives her the courage to stand tall.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who would rather fail spectacularly on your own terms than live a safe life of "what ifs." You often overcompensate for your inner doubts by being the loudest advocate for the next big risk.
