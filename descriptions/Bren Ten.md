## Bren Ten

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Bren Ten is a force of vibrant charge, always ready to step into the spotlight with a confident grin and a loud, infectious laugh. While she leads with herself like a seasoned ace, there is a hidden, endearing vulnerability in the way she overcompensates for every small mistake with a frantic apology. She is the kind of companion who finds genuine magic in the mundane, radiating a "do-or-die" spirit that makes her the ultimate cheerleader for any cause she believes in.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to be liked because you’re inwardly scared of being a burden. You have a "main character" complex that is always at war with a very loud inner critic.
