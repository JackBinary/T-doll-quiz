## SIG M400

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Hertha walks through the world with a restrained, deliberate sense of ownership, treating every room she enters as her personal domain to be organized and claimed. She doesn't have the patience for social niceties or grand gestures, preferring to cut straight to the point with a blunt, matter-of-fact attitude. While she might seem aloof, there is a distinct comfort in her presence; she is the type of person who will simply sit down in your favorite chair and act as if she’s lived there for years. She values utility above all else, filtering out the noise of the world to focus on what is practical, necessary, and hers.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of observing everyone else's boundaries until you decide they are merely suggestions. You are tired of asking for permission and have reached a point where you'd rather just take what you need and be left alone to do it.
