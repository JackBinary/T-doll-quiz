## INSAS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
INSAS operates with the theatrical poise of a queen, demanding the spotlight as if her excellence were a birthright rather than a skill. She projects a sharp, haughty surface to the world, often framing her competence as a source of intimidation to keep others at a distance. However, under the diva persona lies a deeply nurturing heart that manifests in small, protective gestures like preparing a meal or ensuring everyone is safe. She is a woman of contradictions, seeking the adoration of a goddess while finding her truest purpose in the measured, domestic attachment of a guardian.

I SEE YOU
You have a tendency to put on a polished, untouchable front because you’re anxious of how much you actually care about the people you lead. You’d rather be seen as formidable and self-sufficient than admit that you’d be completely lost without your inner circle.
