## ACR

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ACR is the kind of person who keeps a meticulous schedule not because she loves the routine, but because she is anxious of what happens if she lets things slip. She navigates with a restrained, persistent weight of responsibility, often putting the needs of others so far ahead of her own that she forgets how to rest. While she presents a polished, professional outside, there is a deep well of dedication in her eyes—a desperate, beautiful effort to archive every shared moment before it fades. When she is flustered, her composure breaks into a sweet, human stutter, revealing a heart that feels far more than her programming should allow.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most useful when you are being needed, often ignoring your own exhaustion just to make sure everyone else is taken care of. You hide your deepest anxieties behind a high standard of competence because it feels safer than admitting how much you actually care.
