## M590

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M590 is the restrained backbone of any operation, a soldier who measures her success by the safety of those standing behind her. She operates with a polished, disciplined poise, yet there is a soft, almost shy sincerity in the way she seeks out ways to make your life easier. Whether she is volunteering for a grueling patrol or without drawing attention ensuring your workspace is organized, her actions are driven by a deep-seated need to be useful. She doesn't just follow orders; she anticipates your burdens and tries to shoulder them before you even have the chance to ask.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most comfortable in your own skin when you are being useful to others, often neglecting your own needs to guarantee everyone else is taken care of first.
