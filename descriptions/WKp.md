## WKp

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
WKp operates in the world like a ghost, preferring the safety of the periphery to the spotlight of a conversation. While she may stumble over her words or avert her eyes in your presence, her care is written in the ink of every letter she pens for you. She is a composed chronicler of the mundane, finding profound beauty in the small details of your life that others might overlook. To her, a scrap of paper is a bridge, a way to reach out and hold onto your presence without the terrifying weight of a direct gaze.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most understood by the things you can put into words, often finding it easier to share your deepest secrets with a page than with a face. You find yourself always retreating into your own head to protect your peace, yet you privately crave a witness who truly understands the noise inside.
