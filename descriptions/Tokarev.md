## Tokarev

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Tokarev operates with the measured, heavy care of someone who has seen the worst of the world and decided to stand against it anyway. While she presents a polished front of military discipline and protective duty, there is a soft, almost trembling sincerity in the way she looks at her commander. She operates with herself as a shield, yet she endlessly seeks the contained reassurance that she is more than just a weapon in the field. To her, every small gesture of affection is a profound gift that she struggles to process, often masking her overwhelming joy with a polite, slightly flustered formality.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who keeps your guard up until you find someone who makes it feel safe to let it down. You value competence above all else, but you without a word crave to be told that your own worth isn't tied to how much you do for others.
