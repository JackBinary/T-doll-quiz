## PTRD

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PTRD holds herself with the heavy, grounded posture of someone who has seen too many long nights and knows exactly how much effort it takes to keep moving forward. She doesn't care for the glory of the battlefield, preferring to view her role as a necessary duty that she fulfills with contained, steady competence. While she maintains a sharp, professional surface in public, she finds her true relief in the restrained moments of decompression, trading her soldierly mask for a playful, tactile warmth. She is the kind of companion who will shoulder the heaviest load for you, provided you're willing to let her lean on you just as much when the work is done.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overextending yourself until you're running on fumes, then acting like it was no big deal just to keep the peace. You don't need a hero; you need someone who understands that the most rewarding part of a hard day is finally being allowed to let go.
