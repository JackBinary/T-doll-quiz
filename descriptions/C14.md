## C14

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Timberwolf leads with the restrained, heavy gravity of a predator who knows exactly where her next meal—or objective—is coming from. While she is a master of clinical analysis on the battlefield, she is far from a cold machine; she thrives on the shared pulse of a team and the warmth of a collective goal. She expresses her care not through soft words, but through the steady rhythm of shared labor and the playful, mischievous presence she brings to the pack after the dust has settled. To her, loyalty is a physical thing, woven into every joint and circuit as she balances her fierce independence with a deep, unspoken need to belong.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most capable when you have a team to lead, yet you without a word find it exhausting to be the only one in the room holding the weight of the world. You don't want to be a hero in a vacuum; you want to be the heartbeat of a group that actually gives a damn about you.
