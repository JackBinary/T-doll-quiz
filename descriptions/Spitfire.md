## Spitfire

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Spitfire holds herself with the bright, restless charge of someone who is always trying to outrun her own shadow. She approaches every task with a wide-eyed intensity, treating her duties less like a job and more like a performance intended to earn a nod of approval. While she can be playfully affectionate, there is an underlying earnestness in her movements, as if she is perpetually trying to prove she is capable of standing tall. She doesn't just want to succeed; she wants to be worthy of the legacy she wears.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else specifically because you’re uneasy of being perceived as average. You don't just want to be good; you want to be "enough" in a way that finally makes the people you admire stop looking past you.
