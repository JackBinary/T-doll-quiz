## Lee-Enfield

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Lee-Enfield operates with the heavy, deliberate control of a relic from a bygone era. She views her service not as a career, but as a solemn vow to uphold a standard of honor that the modern world has long since forgotten. While her outside is defined by a sharp, almost abrasive discipline, her true loyalty reveals itself in measured gestures, a perfectly steeped cup of tea or a stern recommendation for a difficult book. Past her rigid posture lies a weary heart that always asks if she is worthy of the trust placed in her, making her loyalty both profound and to herself tragic.

I SEE YOU
You possess a deep-seated need for authenticity and are likely tired of superficial connections that lack a sense of purpose. You are the type of person who would rather endure a difficult truth than be comforted by a convenient lie.
