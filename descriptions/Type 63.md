## Type 63

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 63 walks through the world with a frantic, polished control, always one step ahead of her own anxieties. She is the type of person who stays late to double-check every detail, driven by a deep-seated need to guarantee she never lets anyone down. While her enthusiasm can sometimes feel breathless, it stems from a sincere heart that finds its greatest purpose in being useful. Behind her impeccable surface lies a gentle soul who views every small task as a profound opportunity to prove her worth.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like you have to be twice as productive as everyone else just to feel like you're doing enough. You find a strange, measured comfort in being the one who stays behind to make sure everything is perfect before the lights go out.
