## M16A1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M16A1 is the kind of person who will always be the first to crack a joke when the tension gets too thick to breathe. She walks with a boisterous, "cool older sister" presence, often leading the charge into the unknown to keep everyone else stays safe. Below her rough outside and loud personality lies a fierce care to her comrades, fueled by a still need to be the person everyone can lean on. She expresses her affection through shared drinks and high-octane chaos, finding it much easier to be a hero than to be vulnerable.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-performing in your responsibilities because you’re afraid of being the one who lets the team down. You’d rather be exhausted and respected than relaxed and overlooked.
