## Simonov

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Simonov exists in the world with the booming confidence of a protagonist who knows she’s meant for greatness. She treats every minor victory like a legendary feat, often punctuating her stories with theatrical gestures and boisterous declarations of her own brilliance. Behind the "tough girl" posturing and the loud, performative current lies a heart that is deeply, almost frantically, eager to be noticed. She isn't just looking for a job to do; she is looking for a reason to be your favorite, always hoping that her bravado is enough to earn a seat at your side.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-preparing for every social interaction because you're uneasy of being perceived as ordinary. You find yourself most comfortable when you can play a role, even if it means exhausting yourself to keep the performance alive.
