## P50

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P50 navigates the world with a measured, deliberate ease, treating every interaction as a contract to be fulfilled with precision. She isn't interested in the fluff of sentimentality, preferring to show her loyalty through tangible results and the measured reliability of a job well done. While she keeps a firm boundary between her professional duties and her private life, she leads with a subtle, protective warmth for those she deems a true partner. To her, a shared success is the highest form of intimacy, expressed in the steady rhythm of a well-executed plan.

I SEE YOU
You have a high tolerance for silence and a deep-seated distrust of people who try to be "meaningful" before they’ve proven they can be useful. You’d rather be respected for your competence than loved for your vulnerability.
