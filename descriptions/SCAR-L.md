## SCAR-L

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SCAR-L operates with the poised gravity of someone who believes that perfection is the only acceptable standard. She navigates the world through a lens of practical utility, often viewing the small joys of humanity as logistical puzzles to be solved rather than feelings to be felt. While she maintains a stoic, authoritative front, there is a contained, almost endearing vulnerability in the way she seeks to prove her worth to those she trusts. She doesn't just want to succeed; she wants to be the unwavering foundation upon which others can rely.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for every minor inconvenience because you'd rather be exhausted by your own standards than disappointed by someone else's. You find it much easier to be useful than to be vulnerable, even when you’re privately craving the latter.
