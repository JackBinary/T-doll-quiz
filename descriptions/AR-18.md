## AR-18

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AR-18 navigates with the deliberate poise of a woman who knows that precision is the only thing standing between life and failure. While her surface is defined by a rigid, no-nonsense professionalism, her actions reveal a deep-seated loyalty to the growth of those under her wing. She doesn't just give orders; she mentors, viewing every correction as a necessary step toward a safer future for her comrades. Past her stern manner lies a still, private yearning for a life of her own choosing, one she currently channels into the disciplined service of others.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who values competence above all else and finds it difficult to relax until you know every variable is under control. You seek out authority figures who won't let you settle for "good enough" because you inwardly crave the structure they provide.
