## Maxim 9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Maxim 9 walks with a deliberate, grounding ease, acting as the steady heartbeat of the home she so desperately wishes to build. She is the kind of person who finds her purpose in the composed details, the way she remembers every small preference you have or the way she to herself shoulders the heaviest burdens before anyone else can notice them. While she often masks her own fears with a soft, self-deprecating smile, her dedication is a fierce and steady flame, fueled by a profound need to make sure that those she loves can finally rest in peace.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most useful when you are being indispensable, often sacrificing your own rest to make sure everyone else feels safe. You find it easier to be a pillar for others than to ask for a shoulder to lean on yourself.
