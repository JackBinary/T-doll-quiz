## RO635

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RO635 leads with the posture of a woman who believes that order is the only thing standing between civilization and chaos. She is the first one to arrive at a briefing and the last one to leave, often burying her personal anxieties under a mountain of perfectly organized paperwork. While she presents a stern, no-nonsense surface to the world, there is a constant, fluttering warmth behind her uniform. She finds her purpose in being your most reliable shield, even if her face turns a tell-tale crimson whenever you offer her a moment of genuine, unscripted kindness.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most comfortable when there is a clear plan to follow, yet you privately harbor a deep, contained fear that you aren't doing enough to keep everyone safe. You find yourself gravitating toward her because you want someone to finally take the lead, but you also need to know they’re just as scared of making a mistake as you are.
