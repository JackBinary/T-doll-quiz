## Type 97 Shotgun

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 97 Shotgun is a burst of proactive weight, always the first to step into the line of fire with a determined grin. She views her role as a protector not just as a duty, but as a personal point of pride, often burying her own exhaustion under a layer of "mission-first" enthusiasm. While she can be incredibly earnest and bold in the face of a challenge, she leads with a hidden, bashful side that surfaces whenever the focus shifts from the battlefield to her own feelings. She would rather prove her worth through a brave action than a soft word, endlessly striving to be the shield you can always rely on.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-performing in your daily life to keep no one has to worry about you, even when you're inwardly running on fumes. You'd rather be the person who handles the crisis than the one asking for help.
