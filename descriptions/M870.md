## M870

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M870 operates with the practiced poise of someone who knows she’s the smartest person in the room, often hiding her genuine dedication behind a veil of sharp-tongued sarcasm. She treats every interaction like a playful power struggle, demanding small "tributes" of snacks or drinks just to keep her protective instincts from becoming too obvious. Under her constant teasing and mocking nicknames lies a fierce, almost maternal loyalty to those she considers her responsibility. She won't admit it, but she would rather spend all day correcting your mistakes than let you face a single one alone.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of taking on far too much responsibility solo, and you inwardly crave someone who is competent enough to tell you when you're being reckless. You need a partner who will mock your stubbornness while simultaneously making sure you don't burn the world down in the process.
