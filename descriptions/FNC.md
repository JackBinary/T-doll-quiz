## FNC

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FNC is a spark of kinetic current who views every completed task as a hard-earned ticket to her next favorite dessert. She walks with her history of mechanical setbacks with a defiant sort of pride, transforming her resilience into a bubbly, high-spirited personality. While she might protest the start of a long shift with a playful pout, she is fiercely loyal to the person who keeps her pantry stocked. To her, affection isn't found in grand declarations, but in the measured, shared joy of a stolen snack during a well-deserved break.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works with frantic intensity specifically so you can justify doing absolutely nothing for the rest of the day. You value tangible rewards over empty promises, and you're looking for someone who understands that "productivity" is just a means to an end.
