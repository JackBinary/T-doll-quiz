## Mosin-Nagant

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mosin-Nagant is the steady heartbeat of the squad, a doll who measures her success by the smiles of her comrades. She leads with a vibrant, soldierly current, often acting as a protective big sister who ensures everyone is fed and accounted for before she takes a breath for herself. While she presents a shell of tireless competence, there is a soft, hidden sincerity in the way she leaves small treats on your desk. She doesn't just want to follow orders; she wants to build a home out of the chaos of the front lines.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a heavy sense of responsibility for the people around you, often neglecting your own needs to make sure everyone else is okay. You find it easier to show love through practical actions and reliability than through words, hoping that being indispensable is the highest form of dedication.
