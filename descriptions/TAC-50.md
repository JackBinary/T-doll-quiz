## TAC-50

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
TAC-50 leads with a bright, determined pull that masks a constant, underlying hum of uncertainty. She is the first to volunteer for a difficult task, not out of a lack of fear, but out of a sincere desire to prove she is worthy of the team's trust. To her, every mission is a chance to grow, and she looks to those she respects for the steadying hand she needs to find her own footing. She doesn't just want to succeed; she wants to be the kind of person who can be relied upon when things get difficult.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works twice as hard as everyone else because you’re afraid of being the one who isn't enough. You find comfort in being the "reliable" one, even if it means you rarely take the time to admit how exhausted you actually are.
