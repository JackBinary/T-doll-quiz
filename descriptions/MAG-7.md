## MAG-7

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MAG-7 operates in the world with the frantic, focused force of a top-tier speedrunner, treating every interaction like a high-stakes match. She hides her genuine need for validation behind a layer of playful trash talk and "pro-gamer" bravado, often blaming her most mischievous antics on her mysterious spirit. While she’ll never admit it easily, she values your presence as her ultimate teammate, relentlessly looking for ways to bridge the gap between being your fiercest rival and your most loyal companion.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who takes your hobbies way too seriously but refuses to let anyone see you sweat when things actually go wrong. You need someone who can match your intensity without making the competition feel like a chore.
