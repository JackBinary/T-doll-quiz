## ZiP .22

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ZiP .22 navigates with the poise of a seasoned veteran, always ensuring her posture is perfect and her presence is commanding. She has a way of taking charge of every room, often insisting on leading the way to prove her indispensable worth to the team. Below her brash, high-spirited front lies a girl who pours her heart into every small detail, from the way she protects her subordinates to the carefully crafted trinkets she leaves behind as "souvenirs." She is a flash of performative excellence, endlessly striving to reach a standard of perfection that only she can see.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of working twice as hard as everyone else just so you can afford to act like it didn't matter. You’re uneasy of being ordinary, so you’ve built a very polished fortress around the parts of yourself you’re still trying to figure out.
