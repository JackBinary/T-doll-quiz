## Springfield

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Springfield leads with the gentle control of someone who has seen the worst of the world and decided to build something beautiful in its place. She finds her greatest purpose in the restrained moments, whether she is perfecting a handmade treat or ensuring everyone else is tucked away in safety. Her soft giggles are a deliberate choice, a way to keep the shadows of the past at bay while she offers a steady hand to those she loves. To her, being a protector isn't just a duty; it is a nurturing sanctuary she builds for her found family.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who puts everyone else’s comfort before your own needs, often assuming that if you just work a little harder, no one else will have to feel the weight of the world.
