## Supernova

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Supernova is a flash of kinetic current, always moving at a pace that suggests she’s trying to outrun her own thoughts. She approaches life with the intensity of a cheerleader and the protective instincts of a fiercely devoted older sister, often shielding her team with a boisterous, "can-do" attitude. Whether she’s perfecting a high-speed skating maneuver or launching into a passionate, heated defense of authentic Italian pasta, she pours her entire heart into every moment. Behind her bright smiles and celebratory spins lies a deep-seated desire to do right by everyone around her, turning every victory into a reason to celebrate together.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overcompensating for your own insecurities by becoming the most reliable person in the room. You’d rather move at a breakneck pace than sit still and acknowledge that you're actually exhausted.
