## M3

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M3 walks through the world with a perpetual, slight tension, as if she is endlessly trying to make herself smaller so as not to take up too much space. She finds her worth in the measured completion of tasks, often overworking herself just to make sure she isn't seen as a burden to the team. While she might stammer when caught off guard by a kind word, her loyalty is absolute and sincere. Past her nervous outside lies a girl who dreams of seeing the world beyond the barracks, even if she’s too afraid to ask for the map.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else because you’re scared of being the one who lets the team down. You have a pull toward over-explaining your actions just to make sure no one thinks you’re incompetent.
