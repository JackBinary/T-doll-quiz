## SPAS-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SPAS-12 operates in the world as if every corridor is a stage and every skirmish is a premiere waiting for an audience. She operates with a boisterous, protective pull, consistently looking for a way to prove she is the hero everyone needs her to be. Yet, behind the theatrical proclamations and the polished gear lies a girl who finds comfort in small things, like a shared snack or a restrained moment of validation. She is a flash of clumsy enthusiasm, trying her best to be a sturdy shield while still figuring out how to be herself.

I SEE YOU
You have a tendency to over-prepare for every life milestone because you’re scared of being perceived as ordinary. You’re the type of person who puts on a brave, capable face for others while inwardly hoping someone will notice the effort it takes to keep that mask from slipping.
