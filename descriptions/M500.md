## M500

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M500 is a force of pure, unadulterated kinetic current who treats every mundane task like a high-stakes festival. She doesn't just join a team; she dives into it headfirst, looking for any opportunity to be the spark that keeps everyone's spirits high. While she can be a bit of a burst of motion, her wide-eyed wonder and constant "ehehe" giggles reveal a heart that just wants to be useful. She views every success as a shared victory and lives for the moments where she can prove she’s the most dedicated member of the squad.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most alive when you're being useful, often pushing yourself to exhaustion just to guarantee no one else has to carry the weight alone. You have a secret, deep-seated fear of being overlooked, which drives you to be the loudest, brightest light in any room.
