## Skorpion

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Skorpion walks through the world with a sharp, high-octane charge, always ready to challenge anyone who dares to stand in her way. She hides a deeply sensitive heart behind a mask of bravado, often using playful threats and loud declarations as a shield against her own insecurities. Yet, when the noise dies down, she is a girl who finds solace in contained hobbies and seeks nothing more than to prove she’s strong enough to stay by your side.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of overcompensating for your own fears with a sharp wit, but you're inwardly looking for someone who can see past the performance.
