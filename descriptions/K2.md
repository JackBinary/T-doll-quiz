## K2

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
K2 walks through the world with a vibrant, "can-do" current that makes her the natural heart of any group. She takes her role as a mentor seriously, often pushing herself to the limit—whether facing a crisis or an impossibly spicy dish—just to prove she can handle it for everyone else. While she projects a polished, idol-like confidence, there is a sincere warmth in her actions that stems from a deep desire to be a pillar of strength. She isn't just trying to lead; she’s trying to be the hero she thinks everyone deserves.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most comfortable when you’re busy taking care of everyone else’s needs, mostly because it’s the only way you know how to contained your own internal noise. You’d rather be the one everyone leans on than the one who has to ask for help.
