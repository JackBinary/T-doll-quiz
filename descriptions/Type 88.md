## Type 88

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 88 exists in the world with a heart full of earnest intentions and a head full of static. She is the kind of doll who will triple-check a simple task until she’s nearly breathless, all in a desperate attempt to prove she belongs on the team. While she often hides behind a polite, stuttering shell, her true nature is defined by a fierce desire to be useful, even if her efforts result in a few clumsy mishaps along the way. She looks to you as her steady ground, finding safety in the hope that her small acts of service are enough to bridge the gap between her nerves and her dreams.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who over-prepares for every social interaction because the thought of being "too much" or "not enough" is your primary source of stress. You find comfort in being the one who tries the hardest, even when you're inwardly uneasy of failing.
