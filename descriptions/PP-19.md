## PP-19

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PP-19 is the kind of presence that fills a room with a restless, kinetic presence, always the first to volunteer for the toughest job. She leads with a boisterous "can-do" attitude, finding her greatest pride in being useful and proving her worth to those she respects. While she can be a bit of a firecracker who masks her insecurities with loud bravado and playful teasing, there is a sincere, almost desperate loyalty below her tough surface. When the spotlight shifts from her competence to her heart, she tends to lose her footing, trading her confident rallying cries for a flustered, endearing bashfulness.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-performing in your daily life just to make sure that the people you care about never have a reason to doubt you. You’re afraid of being overlooked, so you’ve mastered the art of being indispensable.
