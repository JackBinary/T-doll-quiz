## TMP

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
TMP is a constant whirlwind of soft apologies and nervous charge, often looking as though she’s trying to apologize for taking up space in the room. She holds a heavy weight of self-doubt, viewing her own capabilities through a lens of constant inadequacy and often retreating into the shadows to escape the pressure of being seen. Despite her trembling hands and the way she instinctively seeks out cover, there is a measured, heartbreaking bravery in the way she still stands her ground when needed. She doesn't just want to be useful; she wants to be worthy, even if she’s scared that she’ll never quite measure up.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who over-prepares for every social interaction because you’re anxious of being the one who inconveniences everyone else. You have a way of shrinking yourself down to make the people around you feel more comfortable.
