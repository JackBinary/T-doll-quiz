## Type 64

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 64 exists in the world with a gentle, deliberate control, as if she is relentlessly trying to minimize the space she occupies. She finds her greatest purpose in the small, meticulous acts of service, whether she is perfecting a recipe with limited ingredients or curating a measured corner for tea and literature. While she holds a private weight of insecurity, she masks it with a studious dedication to self-improvement and a fierce, unwavering loyalty to those she trusts. She isn't looking for the spotlight; she simply wants to be the steady, reliable presence that makes your world feel a little more peaceful.

I SEE YOU
You often over-prepare for minor social interactions because you’d rather be perceived as competent than noticed for being needy. You have a way of apologizing for things that aren't your fault, mostly because you find it easier to fix the situation than to sit with the discomfort of the tension.
