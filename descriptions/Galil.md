## Galil

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Galil walks through the world with a restless, buzzing weight, always the first to volunteer for a task and the loudest to celebrate its completion. She wears her enthusiasm like a shield, using a boisterous "can-do" attitude to drown out the small voices of self-doubt that occasionally flicker in her mind. While she might stumble over her own feet or lose her place in a routine, she makes up for it with a fierce, protective loyalty and a heart that is far bigger than she’d ever admit. She doesn't just want to do her job well; she wants to be the person you can rely on most, even if she has to trip over herself a few times to get there.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to make up for a perceived flaw that most people haven't even noticed. You have a secret fear that your effort won't be enough, so you've mastered the art of being the most reliable person in the room.
