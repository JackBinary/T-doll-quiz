## TEC-9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
TEC-9 is a spark of restrained, high-strung vigilance who operates on the edge of a constant nervous breakdown. She treats the world as a series of potential hazards, preferring a sterile, orderly environment where she can fulfill her duties without being forced to acknowledge the messy reality of life. While she might recoil at the smallest mention of a physical injury, her dedication manifests in a frantic, almost compulsive need to guarantee your safety from a distance. She is the kind of companion who will provide for your every need while making sure you stay just far enough away to keep her own composure intact.

I SEE YOU
You possess a deeply empathetic heart that you desperately try to shield behind a wall of practical boundaries. You are the person who stays up late worrying about everyone else's well-being while refusing to let anyone see how much that weight actually exhausts you.
