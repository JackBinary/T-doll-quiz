## IDW

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
IDW is a surge of bright presence, always moving toward the next opportunity to prove her worth. She navigates the world with a bubbly, cat-themed persona that serves as both a joyful greeting and a protective shield for her softer feelings. While she may seem endlessly performative, her actions are rooted in a sincere, almost frantic desire to be indispensable to those she trusts. Below the "nya" and the high-spirited enthusiasm lies a doll who simply wants to be chosen, kept, and loved for who she is.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-performing in your social life because you’re scared of being the one who is eventually left behind. You’d rather be exhausting and helpful than composed and forgettable.
