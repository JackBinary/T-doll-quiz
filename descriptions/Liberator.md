## Liberator

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Liberator is a spark of earnest presence, always ready to champion the cause of fairness with a wide, infectious grin. While she takes her role as a guardian of justice seriously, she is just as likely to negotiate for a bag of sweets as a "fair trade" for her emotional support. She holds a deep, protective warmth for those under her care, often masking her genuine concern with playful banter and heroic rhetoric. Whether she's organizing a holiday tradition or demanding you carry her shopping bags, she does it all with a heart that beats for the balance of things.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of overextending yourself for others while inwardly hoping someone will finally notice and demand you take a break. You’re the person who feels a physical itch of discomfort when things aren't balanced, even if you’re the one currently tipping the scales.
