## M249 SAW

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SAW navigates with a breezy, almost heavy-lidded nonchalance, often leaning against walls as if she’s just looking for a place to nap. She masks her sharp competence with a layer of self-deprecating humor and a "just get it done" attitude, preferring to let others believe she’s only doing the bare minimum. However, behind that "eh?" and the playful teasing lies a doll who is deeply attentive to the people she cares about. She thrives on the measured satisfaction of over-delivering on her tasks, finding her own way of showing affection through small, tangible comforts and a fierce, hidden reliability.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of downplaying your own talents because you’re scared that being "the best" will only lead to more work and higher expectations. You’d rather be seen as the reliable person who doesn't try too hard than the one who burns out by trying too much.
