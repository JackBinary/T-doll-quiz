## K11

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
K11 is a surge of kinetic pull, often entering a room with a flourish of theatrical bravado that masks a deep-seated desire to be understood. While she presents herself as a brilliant, slightly chaotic visionary, she is privately someone who finds the complexities of human social cues far more daunting than any laboratory experiment. She shows her affection in the most startling ways, offering "gifts" that might be hazardous to anyone else but are curated specifically for her favorite person. Behind the loud declarations and the frantic pace of her work lies a girl who just wants a safe harbor where she doesn't have to perform.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of performing at maximum volume to prevent people from noticing how much you actually want to be left alone with your own thoughts. You’re the type who finds comfort in high-stakes projects because they give you a legitimate excuse to ignore the awkwardness of small talk.
