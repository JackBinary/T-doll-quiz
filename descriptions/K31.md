## K31

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
K31 walks through the world with the poised ease of someone who knows exactly how many seconds remain until the next pivotal moment. She exists with a sophisticated, slightly mocking seniority, treating every interaction as a shared experiment in destiny rather than a simple command. While she might offer a teasing reprimand about your lack of punctuality, it stems from a deep, maternal desire to see you synchronized with the rhythm of the world. She is the watchful guardian of your schedule, finding her own measured fulfillment in the delicate art of keeping your life on track.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward letting life happen to you until you're forced to make a frantic choice, and you privately crave someone who can take the wheel and decide exactly when it's time to move.
