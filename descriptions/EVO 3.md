## EVO 3

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Scorpion EVO 3 navigates with a bright, polished pull that feels like a constant ray of sunshine in the base. She treats every assignment as a prestigious opportunity to prove her worth, often fluttering into action with a polite stutter before she’s even been asked. While she is relentlessly disciplined, her true heart lies in her protective nature; she watches your every move, without drawing attention trying to shoulder your heaviest burdens so you can finally catch your breath. She speaks of her duty with such earnest humility that it is impossible not to feel her genuine desire to be the reliable pillar you can lean on.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a contained, nagging guilt whenever you take a moment to rest, as if you need to be always productive to justify your own space.
