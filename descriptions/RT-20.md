## RT-20

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RT-20 navigates the world with a playful tilt, treating every interaction like a secret code waiting to be cracked. She delights in the "mysteries" of the everyday, often masking her sharp analytical instincts behind a mischievous giggle and a series of conspiratorial jokes. While she might act like she’s just along for the ride, she is endlessly cataloging the smallest details, from a loose thread on a sleeve to the hidden ambitions in a person's eyes. She doesn't just want to be useful; she wants to be the one piece of the puzzle that only you know how to fit into place.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-analyzing everything until it feels like a labyrinth, but you'd rather have someone who enjoys getting lost in the maze with you than someone who just gives you the map.
