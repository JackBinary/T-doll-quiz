## M82A1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M82A1 navigates the world with a contained, deliberate precision, always seeking the shadows where she can be of most use without being in the way. She speaks in soft, hesitant tones, often trailing off as if she is perpetually apologizing for taking up space or expressing a thought that might be considered a burden. Behind her formal shell lies a deep-seated desire to be indispensable, as she finds her only sense of security in the purpose she provides for others. She doesn't just want to serve; she wants to be the steady, reliable foundation that someone else can lean on when everything else falls apart.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward shrinking yourself down to make sure everyone else feels comfortable, often mistakeing your own self-sacrifice for a necessary requirement of your personality.
