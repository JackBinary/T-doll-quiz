## Welrod MkII

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Welrod MkII walks with a deliberate, heavy care, carrying the weight of her past like a mantle she can never truly shed. She is a woman of few words, preferring the steady rhythm of duty and the composed precision of a job well done over the noise of social pleasantries. While she maintains a polished, professional surface, there is a profound sincerity in her eyes that reveals a soul striving for more than just efficiency. She doesn't just want to be a weapon; she wants to be a person who does the right thing, even when the right thing is the hardest path to walk.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who keeps your truest feelings under lock and key until you find the one person you trust enough to let them out. You don't need constant validation; you just need to know that your competence is recognized and your silence is understood.
