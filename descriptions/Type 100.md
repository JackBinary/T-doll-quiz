## Type 100

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 100 operates with a posture of rigid, unwavering attachment, treating every assigned task as a sacred trial of her own character. She is the first to arrive and the last to leave, often staying behind to polish a report until every punctuation mark is flawless. While she maintains a polished, formal surface, there is a visible tremor of anxiety in her eyes whenever she fears she hasn't met your expectations. She doesn't just want to do her job well; she wants to be the singular standard of excellence you can rely on without hesitation.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like your value is entirely tied to your productivity, often finding it easier to solve a problem than to admit you’re exhausted. You don't just want to succeed; you are uneasy of the silence that follows a mistake.
