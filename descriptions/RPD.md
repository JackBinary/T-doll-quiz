## RPD

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RPD navigates the world with a contained, constant vigilance, always looking for the next way to be of use to those around her. She leads with a disciplined posture, yet there is a soft, eager light in her eyes whenever she can anticipate a comrade's needs. Though she often speaks of herself as merely a tool for the mission, her actions reveal a heart that finds its only true purpose in the happiness of others. She is the first to offer a steady hand and the last to take credit for a victory, often retreating into a humble smile when the spotlight finds her.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to finish the group project not because you have to, but because the thought of others being stressed by your unfinished work feels physically uncomfortable. You have a way of apologizing for things that aren't your fault just to keep the peace.
