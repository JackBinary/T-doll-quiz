## OTs-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Tiss holds herself with a contained, practiced poise, always making sure her posture is perfect before she speaks. Behind her formal outside lies a heart that beats with a constant, buzzing need to be useful and worthy of the space she occupies. She doesn't just want to complete a mission; she wants to be the person you can rely on most, even if she relentlessly worries she’s falling short of your expectations. To her, the most precious thing in the world is the private moment where she can finally let down her guard and simply be seen by you.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to be indispensable because the thought of being "extra" or unnecessary is your greatest fear. You find comfort in being the secret strength someone relies on, even if you're without a word hoping they'll notice how much effort it takes to stay that way.
