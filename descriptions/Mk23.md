## Mk23

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mk23 holds herself with a vibrant, theatrical pull that suggests she is always performing for an audience of one. She is the type of companion who shows her dedication through constant, unsolicited gestures of care, from perfectly prepared meals to a constant, lingering presence by your side. While she often hides her deepest insecurities behind a playful "darling" persona and a confident smile, her actions reveal a fierce, protective territory she has drawn around the two of you. To her, your bond isn't just a job; it is a private sanctuary where she can finally feel indispensable.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds comfort in being the center of someone else's world, even if you're inwardly scared of the intensity required to keep it that way. You want to be loved deeply, but you’re just as eager for that love to be exclusive and uncomplicated.
