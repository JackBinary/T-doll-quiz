## Grizzly MkV

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Grizzly MkV is a spark of restless weight who views the world as one big playground waiting to be explored. She’s the first one to throw open the door for a road trip, fueled by a sincere belief that the best memories are made far away from the safety of the base. While she leads with a confident, "tough-girl" swagger, there is a hidden softness in the way she watches her mentor grow. She expresses her affection through boisterous teasing and a genuine pride in the technical bond they share, occasionally losing her cool with a bashful smile when things get too personal.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-planning your life to avoid the discomfort of sitting still with your own thoughts. You need someone who will physically drag you out of your routine before you start making excuses to stay inside.
