## Type 62

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 62 exists through the base with a weary but observant poise, often treating the surrounding chaos as a poorly written script she’s been cast to perform. She expresses her affection through sharp-tongued critiques and constant reminders to stay organized, masking a deep-seated desire to be more than just a series of functional components. While she enjoys the playful banter of the present, there is a composed, reflective side to her that lingers in the late hours, searching for something truly unique in a world that feels increasingly repetitive.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward using sarcasm as a protective layer to keep people from seeing how much you actually care about the details. You’re looking for a connection that feels like a genuine story, not just another routine to get through.
