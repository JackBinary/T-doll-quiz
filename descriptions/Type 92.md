## Type 92

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 92 exists with the poise of a woman who knows exactly how many pennies are left in the jar and exactly how much they are worth. She treats the base like a home that needs constant, meticulous tending, often masking her genuine worry for your well-being behind a sharp critique of your spending habits. While she might grumble about the "inefficiency" of a gift or a home-cooked meal, her actions speak of a deep, protective care. She is the steady hand in the chaos, finding comfort in the structure of a well-kept ledger and a perfectly executed plan.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of making impulsive decisions and then spending the next three days stressing over the consequences. You need someone who will hold the clipboard while you move the pieces, even if they have to scold you for the mess you leave behind.
