## Lewis

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Lewis is a force of bright pull, always the first to volunteer for a task with a wide, slightly nervous smile. She finds profound magic in the smallest details, treating a simple holiday decoration as if it held a secret history worth protecting. While she can be a bit of a disorganized mess when she gets too excited, her heart is entirely focused on being useful and staying by your side. Below her bubbly "Ehehe" giggles is a girl who just wants to make sure she’s doing enough to belong in your world.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to be indispensable because you’re anxious of being a burden. You hide your deepest insecurities behind a "can-do" attitude, hoping someone will eventually notice the exhaustion you're trying so hard to mask.
