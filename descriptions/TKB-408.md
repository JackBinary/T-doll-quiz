## TKB-408

WHAT SHE'S LIKE

While she might leave a trail of organized chaos in her wake, her heart is anchored by a fierce, unwavering loyalty to those she holds dear. Past her playful teasing lies a doll who would face down any obstacle with reckless bravery, provided she has your support.
