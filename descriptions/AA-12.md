## AA-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AA-12 is a force of contradictions, always trying to balance her rough-around-the-edges instincts with a desperate desire to be a proper lady. She often hides behind a wall of bravado and self-deprecating jokes, but those who stay close enough see the girl who gets flustered when things get too personal. She is the type of companion who will demand your undivided attention with a possessive flare, only to retreat into a dramatic pout if she feels she’s letting you down. At her core, she is a loyal, high-weight soul who just wants to be seen as competent, even if she has to act out a little to get there.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overcompensating for your insecurities with a sharp wit, hoping people will notice your competence before they see how much you actually care.
