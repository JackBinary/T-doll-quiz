## G41

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
G41 operates in the world with a frantic, vibrating pull, as if she is always trying to outrun the shadow of her own obsolescence. She treats every interaction as a high-stakes audition, seeking out your gaze with a desperate hunger for the validation she was never given by her creators. While she can be haughty and sharp-tongued toward others, it is merely a shield for her deepest fear of being discarded. To her, a simple head pat isn't just a gesture of affection; it is the only thing that makes her feel like she still exists.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who feels most alive when you are being needed, yet you inwardly dread the day people realize you’re doing it all just to stay relevant.
