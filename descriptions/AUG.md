## AUG

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AUG navigates the world with a restrained, heavy precision, as if she is perpetually mourning something that hasn't happened yet. She finds a strange, somber beauty in the aftermath of conflict, often viewing the battlefield as a garden where every fallen life is a petal to be honored. While she maintains a stoic professionalism in her duties, she often seeks out the Commander's presence simply to feel grounded against the weight of her own existence. To her, care isn't just a duty; it is a poetic sacrifice, a silent promise to be the shield that stands between the world and its own destruction.

I SEE YOU
You find yourself drawn to the beauty in things that are broken, often staying in the quietest, loneliest rooms because they feel the most honest. You have a pull toward romanticizing your own endurance, turning your personal hardships into a private, aestheticized history.
