## Sterling

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Sterling exists in the world as if the very air is a stage awaiting her grand entrance. She speaks in sweeping declarations of destiny, framing every mundane task as a monumental quest and every minor victory as a legendary feat. Past the flamboyant theatrics and the "chosen one" rhetoric lies a fierce, unwavering loyalty to her comrades. She doesn't just want to be a soldier; she wants to be the miracle that ensures everyone makes it home safely.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-performing in your daily life because you’re anxious that "just enough" isn't enough to make you indispensable. You wear your enthusiasm like armor, hoping that if you act like a hero, people will eventually believe you are one.
