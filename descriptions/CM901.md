## CM901

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CM901 exists in the world with a frantic, measured precision, always looking for the next task to prove her worth. She holds herself with the rigid posture of a perfect soldier, though her eyes often betray a contained, restless anxiety about whether she is doing enough. While she strives to be the dependable subordinate you can rely on, she is privately anxious of a moment of true stillness where she might have to face her own insecurities. She doesn't just want to succeed for the sake of the mission; she wants to be worthy of the space she occupies.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late at the office not because you have to, but because you’re afraid of what happens if you admit you’re tired. You have a pull toward turning your own self-doubt into a fuel source that others mistake for sheer ambition.
