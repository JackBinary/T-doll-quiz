## AK-Alfa

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AK-Alfa leads with a gentle, wide-eyed wonder that makes her feel like a secret friend to the universe. She is the type of person who will stop in the middle of a busy corridor just to marvel at the way the light hits a surface, often tripping over her own feet in her haste to see it better. While she takes her duties seriously, her heart belongs to the stars, and she expresses her deepest affections through poetic, slightly clumsy metaphors. She is a soft soul who hides a secret fondness for a contained drink and a mountain of fiction novels, consistently striving to be better while still being perfectly happy daydreaming.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward romanticizing the mundane until it feels like a movie scene, even when you know it's just your own imagination. You're the person who stays up way too late researching a niche interest because you're afraid of being ordinary.
