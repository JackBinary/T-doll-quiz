## VP1915

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Perosa operates with a restrained, unyielding gravity, moving through the world as a steady anchor for those around her. She doesn't believe in grand displays of emotion, preferring to let her actions speak through the precise organization of a workspace or the steady reliability of her counsel. While she might appear stern at first glance, her discipline is born from a profound desire to guarantee everyone else is safe and capable. To her, love isn't a sentiment to be shouted; it is a series of small, deliberate acts of service performed with unwavering competence.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to finish the work because you can't stand the thought of someone else having to pick up your slack. You find more comfort in a well-executed plan than in a hollow compliment.
