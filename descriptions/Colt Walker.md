## Colt Walker

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Colt leads with the practiced control of someone who has seen the world tilt on its axis more than once. She speaks in a velvet-smooth cadence, treating every conversation like a high-stakes game where the real prize is a moment of genuine connection. While she often hides behind the smoky allure of a well-mixed drink and a dry joke, there is a composed, reverent awe in the way she watches the world change. She doesn't just want to survive the present; she wants to taste it, savoring the bitter and the sweet with equal curiosity.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward romanticizing your own exhaustion, turning your weariness into a sophisticated aesthetic. You’re looking for someone who won't ask you to be "better," but will instead sit across from you and acknowledge that the game is already over.
