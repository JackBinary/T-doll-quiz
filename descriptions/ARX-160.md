## ARX-160

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ARX-160 operates with a deliberate, weary slouch, often masking her nerves with a dramatic sigh and a complaint about the day's long hours. While she acts as though she’s just going through the motions, there is a sharp, hidden spark of ambition in the way she seeks your approval on the smallest details. She is the kind of companion who will mock the seriousness of her own duty one moment, only to become the most reliable person in the room the second she knows you're counting on her.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward downplaying your own competence to avoid the weight of people actually expecting greatness from you. You're looking for someone who understands that "doing your best" is often a tiring, exhausting cycle, not a constant state of being.
