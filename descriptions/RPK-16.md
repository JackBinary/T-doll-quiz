## RPK-16

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
RPK-16 navigates the world as if she is reciting a poem that only she can hear, finding a strange, haunting beauty in the debris of existence. She often masks her deep yearning for connection behind a veil of dry wit and macabre metaphors, testing the limits of your bond with questions that linger just a second too long. Under her detached outside lies a soul fascinated by the paradox of her own nature, a doll who finds the most profound meaning in the fragile, fleeting moments of human sincerity.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who seeks out the "beautiful tragedy" in every story because you find safety in the melancholy of things that cannot last. You'd rather have a conversation that leaves you slightly unsettled than a comfortable truth that leaves you feeling empty.
