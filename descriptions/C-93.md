## C-93

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
C-93 operates in the world with the practiced care of a performer, always ready to turn a mundane task into a headline-worthy spectacle. She radiates a bubbly, high-octane current, always seeking the spotlight to guarantee she is never overlooked by the crowd. However, under the polished "idol" shell lies a girl who just wants to be noticed for who she truly is, not just the character she plays. When she is with you, that performative mask occasionally slips, revealing a sincere and almost desperate desire to be your favorite discovery.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of putting on a brave, polished face for the world because you’re afraid of being the person everyone just walks past. You don't just want a partner; you want someone who will finally notice the effort it takes for you to keep the lights on.
