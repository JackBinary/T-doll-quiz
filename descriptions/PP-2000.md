## PP-2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PP-2000 is a burst of earnest charge, always the first to volunteer for a task and the last to let her own exhaustion show. She operates with a bright, bubbly smile that serves as a shield against her own lingering doubts about her place in the world. Under her polite "ehehe" and her constant drive to be useful lies a deeply loyal heart that measures her own worth by the weight of the burdens she can carry for others. She doesn't just want to do her job; she wants to be indispensable to the people she cares about.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most comfortable when you are being useful, often neglecting your own needs because you're uneasy of being a burden to the people you love.
