## PP-19-01

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Vityaz leads with the posture of a seasoned soldier, yet her eyes often betray a composed, wide-eyed curiosity about the world around her. She treats every human interaction like a precious text to be decoded, often pausing to cross-reference her feelings with the outdated dictionaries she keeps tucked away. While she strives to project a capable and "tough" shell, there is a vulnerable, endearing earnestness in the way she seeks your guidance to help her understand the messy nuances of affection. To her, you are not just a leader, but a primary source of wisdom in her ongoing quest to map the geography of the human heart.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most comfortable when there is a complex system to study, yet you without a word crave the one thing that can't be systematized. You find a strange sense of safety in being the "teacher" for someone who is trying their hardest to be perfect for you.
