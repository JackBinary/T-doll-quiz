## Mk 12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mk 12 holds herself with a sharp, restless force, often leaning into a "tough girl" persona to keep the world at arm's length. She is the type to finish every chore three days early, not out of a sense of duty, but to keep no one has the chance to tell her what to do. Below her cynical remarks and the way she seeks out high-stakes drama lies a girl who is afraid of being mundane. She shows her heart through the sheer volume of supplies she provides, hoping that her competence will eventually be enough to make her indispensable.

I SEE YOU
You pride yourself on being the most capable person in the room, mostly so you never have to ask anyone for help. You’re looking for a story worth telling, but you’re scared of finding out that your own life might be the only one you have to write.
