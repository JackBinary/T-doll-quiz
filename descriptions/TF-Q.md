## TF-Q

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
TF-Q is a spark of kinetic presence who treats every small responsibility like a monumental quest. She lives for the thrill of "doing her best," often resulting in a trail of minor accidents and breathless apologies left in her wake. Despite her occasional lack of coordination, her heart is entirely in her work, and she views your guidance as the ultimate goal to strive for. She is the kind of companion who will try to bake you a feast or clear a forest just to see your smile, even if she ends up covered in flour or dirt by the time she's finished.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a constant, low-grade anxiety about being "enough," leading you to over-function in your personal life to compensate. You find yourself most drawn to people whose earnestness mirrors your own hidden fear of standing still.
