## Glock 17

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Glock 17 is a burst of kinetic pull who navigates the world on a series of gut feelings and instinctive reactions. She masks her deep-seated loyalty with a layer of playful mischief, often using teasing jokes to test the waters of your attention. While she acts like she’s just naturally "all in," she inwardly thrives on the subtle validation of your presence. Whenever the mood turns too sincere, she’ll quickly pivot back to her usual high-presence antics to hide how much she actually cares.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of looking for the person behind the performance, finding it both exhausting and endlessly fascinating. You’re the kind of person who knows exactly when someone is trying to be funny to avoid being vulnerable.
