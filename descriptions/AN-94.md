## AN-94

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AN-94 holds herself with a still, almost painful poise, often moving as if she is trying to take up as little space as possible. She is the type of person who finds solace in meticulous preparation, believing that if she can just work hard enough, she can eventually earn the right to rest. Behind her soft-spoken front and constant apologies lies a deep well of care, where every task completed is a silent offering of loyalty. She doesn't just want to fulfill her duties; she wants to be worthy of the person standing beside her.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to make sure every detail is perfect, not because you enjoy the work, but because you’re scared of what happens if you let anyone down. You have a pattern of apologizing for things that aren't your fault, often because you feel like your mere presence is an imposition.
