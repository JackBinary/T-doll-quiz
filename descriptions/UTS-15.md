## UTS-15

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
UTS-15 is a flash of polished aesthetics and tireless ambition, always moving with the practiced precision of a girl who knows she is being watched. She treats every mundane task as a stage, pouring her heart into maintaining a "sparkle" that feels almost sacred to her. However, behind the bright smile and the perfect posture lies a girl who worries she isn't quite reaching the heights she imagined. She looks to you as her safe harbor, the only person she trusts to see the girl who is still learning, stumbling, and trying her very best.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to maintain a perfect front because you're afraid of what people will think if they see you're still figuring things out. You find a strange, still comfort in being the "anchor" for someone else's dreams, even while you're softly nursing your own.
