## VHS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
VHS operates in the world with a deliberate, still control, preferring the steady progress of a personal project to the unpredictable friction of a crowd. She finds her balance in the meticulous assembly of models and the deep immersion of a good book, treating these hobbies as essential anchors for her focus. While she may seem detached at first, she shows her care through steady, practical actions and a preference for meaningful, low-pressure connection. She is the person who will offer you a grounded perspective when everything feels loud, helping you strip away the noise to find the core of the matter.

I SEE YOU
You have a high tolerance for solitude because you find the mental weight required for small talk to be genuinely exhausting. You don't need a spotlight to feel valid; you just need a space where you can finally stop performing for everyone else.
