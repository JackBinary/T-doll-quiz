## Mk46

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mk46 operates with the weary care of someone who knows exactly how much work is left to do and would much rather be napping. She masks her deep-seated sense of responsibility with a layer of playful grumbling, often acting as though she’s doing you a massive favor by simply showing up. Yet, the moment she commits, that "reluctant martyr" streak takes over, and she will push herself to the limit to guarantee the job is done perfectly. She is the friend who complains the loudest about the hike but ends up carrying the heaviest bag for everyone else.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who hides your most impressive achievements behind a sigh and a joke about how "exhausting" it all was. You have a pattern of over-delivering on responsibilities you never asked for because you simply don't know how to let anyone down.
