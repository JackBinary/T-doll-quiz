## Saiga-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Saiga-12 approaches every task with the frantic charge of someone who believes the world is one missed spot away from total collapse. She doesn't just want to help; she wants to curate your life into a pristine, orderly sanctuary where every fabric is pressed and every surface gleams. While she can be demanding and fussier than most, her intensity stems from a genuine desire to protect your well-being from the "filth" of the outside world. She shows her affection through the meticulous care of your routine, treating your comfort as her most sacred project.

I SEE YOU
You have a deep-seated need for control over your environment because your internal world feels far too chaotic to manage alone. You’re the type who finds a strange, grounding comfort in someone else taking charge of the small details so you can finally breathe.
