## SIG MCX

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SIG MCX treats every moment of the day like a high-stakes game where the only prize is your reaction. She is a burst of restless charge, endlessly looking for a way to turn a mundane conversation into a clever trick or a playful confrontation. While she hides behind a breezy, mocking surface, there is a sharp intelligence behind her eyes that thrives on finding your buttons and pressing them just to see what happens. To her, affection isn't a soft word; it’s a challenge issued with a grin, an invitation to see how long you can keep up with her.

I SEE YOU
You have a secret pride in being the only person who can actually keep pace with her, even if you spend half your time pretending you aren't enjoying the chase. You’re the kind of person who would rather be challenged to a duel than offered a hand in comfort.
