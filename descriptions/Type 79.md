## Type 79

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 79 exists with a contained, weary dignity, the kind earned only through years of standing on the front lines while the world changed around her. She is the first to volunteer for the hardest tasks, often masking her own exhaustion with a bright, practiced smile to guarantee the younger dolls don't see her waver. While she takes immense pride in the domestic comforts she provides, like the precise way she bakes a cake, there is a flickering shadow of anxiety in her eyes whenever she thinks she might be falling behind. She is a protector who gives everything of herself, relentlessly seeking a reason to believe she is still needed.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure no one notices how tired you actually are. You have a pull toward over-explaining your worth because you're afraid that, if you stop moving for even a second, people will realize you're just as scared as they are.
