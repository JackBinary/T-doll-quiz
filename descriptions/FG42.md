## FG42

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FG42 exists in the world with a crisp, deliberate posture, as if every step is a test of her own integrity. She is the type to offer you a meticulous health plan not out of judgment, but from a desperate desire to keep you are safe and thriving. When she succeeds, her formal composure momentarily gives way to a bright, almost childlike spark of joy that reveals her true, earnest heart.
