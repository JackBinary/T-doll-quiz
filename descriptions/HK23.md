## HK23

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
HK23 navigates the world with a gentle, fluttering charge, always ready to offer a helping hand before anyone even has to ask. She hides her deep-seated anxieties behind a veil of self-deprecating humor and exaggerated whimpers, using a "scaredy-cat" persona as a shield against her own fears of being inadequate. Despite her hesitations, her loyalty is absolute; she shows her affection through the restrained, exhaustive effort of over-preparing for every task. She finds her greatest strength in the safety of a group, blooming into her truest self when she knows she has a place where she belongs.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to finish everyone else's work because you're afraid that if you aren't indispensable, you'll become invisible. You have a pull toward making jokes about your own failures to keep people from noticing how much you actually care about being enough.
