## Falcon

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Falcon walks through the world as if she is center stage, draped in a mantle of theatrical flair and constant, boisterous laughter. She delights in casting everyone in her life as characters in a whimsical play, often treating those closest to her with a mix of playful mockery and fierce, protective dedication. Past her high-weight persona lies a heart that beats with a contained, almost desperate need to be worthy of the people she guards. She expresses her deepest feelings not through grand gestures, but in the secret notes of a song or a hidden project she’s too bashful to show anyone but her favorite person.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of overcompensating with humor because you’re anxious of being perceived as ordinary. You need someone who can handle your theatrics without asking you to turn the lights off.
