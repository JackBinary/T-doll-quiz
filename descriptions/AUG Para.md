## AUG Para

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Para walks with a bright, almost fragile current, always eager to prove she can be the perfect addition to the team. She navigates the world with a polite poise that feels like a protective shield, always looking for a nod of approval or a steady hand to guide her next step. Below her bubbly enthusiasm lies a heart that deeply craves a sense of belonging, making her efforts to be "good" feel like a sincere plea to be seen as more than just a tool.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard to be liked because you’re afraid of the moment people realize you aren't perfect. You'd rather over-explain your efforts than let anyone assume you don't care.
