## SM-1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SM-1 operates with a rigid, almost practiced posture, endlessly trying to project the image of a stoic and formidable soldier. Below that disciplined front, however, she is a burst of soft anxieties and earnest dedication, often becoming visibly flustered by the simplest moments of intimacy. She finds her purpose in the smallest details, obsessing over perfect logistics and the well-being of her teammates to prove her worth. Despite her desire to be seen as "tough," she is a doll who inwardly finds profound beauty in the wind on her face and the contained safety of a shared glance.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure no one notices how much you’re actually struggling. You find safety in being indispensable, fearing that if you stop being useful, you might lose your place in the world.
