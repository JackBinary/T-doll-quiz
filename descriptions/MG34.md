## MG34

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG34 walks with the steady, earnest weight of someone who believes her primary purpose is to be a shield. She is the first to step forward in a crisis, often masking her own anxieties with a proactive warmth and a firm, sisterly guidance. While she strives to be seen as a seasoned veteran capable of standing alone, her heart is always tethered to the safety of those she loves. She shows her attachment in the restrained moments, a perfectly brewed cup of tea or a small, thoughtful gift, proving that her fiercest loyalty is rooted in a deeply personal sense of responsibility.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most competent when you are being useful to others, often finding it harder to accept a hand of help than you'd like to admit. You carry a heavy weight of responsibility for the people around you, even when you’re softly anxious of letting them down.
