## G3

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
G3 holds herself with a restrained, diligent poise, though she often hides a persistent flicker of doubt behind her steady gaze. She views every task as a chance to prove her worth, pouring her heart into her duties with a sincerity that borders on the self-sacrificial. While she is a pillar of reliability for her team, she is most at home in those small, private moments where she can simply be accepted for who she is. She doesn't just want to be a tool of success; she wants to be a person who is truly seen and valued.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just to make sure no one ever has to worry about you failing. You have a pull toward downplaying your own achievements because you'd rather be the person who makes everyone else's life easier.
