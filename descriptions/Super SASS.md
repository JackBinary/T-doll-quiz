## Super SASS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Super SASS exists in the world with a vibrant, infectious force that makes it impossible to stay in a bad mood around her. She treats every minor hurdle like a personal rival to be bested, endlessly pushing herself to reach new heights just to see that spark of approval in her mentor's eyes. While she maintains a cheerful, bubbly front, there is a deep-seated sincerity in her work ethic; she isn't just doing her job, she is trying to outrun her own doubts. Even in her most focused moments, she’s likely to pause for a quick snack, finding a small, sweet reward for every milestone she manages to hit.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late at the office not because you have to, but because you’re scared of being the only one who didn't give it their all. You have a pattern of turning your own anxieties into a relentless, productive drive that others find inspiring, even if it leaves you exhausted.
