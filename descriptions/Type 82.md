## Type 82

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 82 is a surge of nervous pull, relentlessly oscillating between a trembling heart and a fierce, desperate determination to stand tall. She often hides her own fear behind a veil of enthusiastic commands, trying to be the pillar of strength she feels she lacks on the inside. To her, every small interaction is a monumental event, and she wears her vulnerability openly, seeking your steady hand to ground her high-frequency emotions. She doesn't just want to be a soldier; she wants to be the hero you believe she can be.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who feels most capable when you're busy making sure everyone else is okay. You carry a heavy weight of responsibility that you'd rather not admit to, hiding your own exhaustion behind a persistent, helpful smile.
