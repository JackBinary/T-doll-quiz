## Saiga 308

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Saiga-308 operates with the rigid posture of someone who believes the world will fall into chaos the moment she blinks. She takes her role as the office disciplinarian with a fierce, almost desperate intensity, always patrolling the hallways to make sure every rule is followed to the letter. Yet, below her sharp commands and authoritative bark lies a girl who is perpetually exhausted by her own high standards. When she isn't correcting others, she’s often found hiding in a restrained corner, trying to catch a few minutes of sleep before she has to put her "tough love" mask back on.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to finish the work no one else asked for, mostly because you’re uneasy of what happens if things aren't done perfectly. You use your competence as a shield to keep people from seeing how tired you actually are.
