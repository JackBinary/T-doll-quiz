## P90

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
P90 is a flash of bright force, always moving to the rhythm of the people around her with a wide, expectant smile. She doesn't just want to be part of the group; she wants to be the heartbeat of it, mirroring every joke and trend to guarantee she never falls out of step. Past her bubbly chatter lies a deep, almost fierce attachment to those she considers her "home," making her the first person to reach for your hand when things get composed.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of blending into the background to keep the peace, but you're privately exhausted by the effort of endlessly performing for everyone else.
