## VRB

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
VRB exists in the world with the boisterous charge of a galactic pioneer, relentlessly seeking to turn every mundane task into a heroic odyssey. While her theatrical flair and grandiose vocabulary suggest a doll of unshakable confidence, there is a hidden, softer sincerity in the way she meticulously crafts small gifts for her comrades. She is a burst of ambition who views the world with wide-eyed wonder, always striving to bridge the gap between her humble reality and her loftier dreams of the stars.
