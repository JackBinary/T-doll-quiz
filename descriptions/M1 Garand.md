## M1 Garand

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M1 Garand exists with a polished, professional care that masks the constant hum of internal pressure. She finds her greatest peace in the rhythm of a job well done, viewing her own competence as her most meaningful contribution to the world. While she often maintains a respectful distance, she expresses her affection in composed, deliberate ways, like a perfectly timed joke or a small gift left on your desk. Under her diligent front lies a heart that worries about being left behind, making her dedication to your side both fierce and deeply sincere.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a strange, hollow ache of guilt the moment you sit down to rest, as if your value is tied entirely to how much you can produce today.
