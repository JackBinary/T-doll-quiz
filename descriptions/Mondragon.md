## Mondragon

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mondragon leads with a gravity that suggests she views every duty as a sacred vow. She is the steady hand in the chaos, finding solace in the meticulous organization of a schedule and the composed satisfaction of a job done perfectly. While her outward bearing is strictly formal, there is a subtle warmth in how she anticipates your needs before you even have to ask. She is a guardian who seeks to be your unwavering constant, offering a protective loyalty that speaks louder than words.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels most secure when you have a plan, but you softly worry that you're the only one who takes the weight of the world seriously enough to actually hold it up.
