## M14

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M14 is a surge of high-spirited current, always the first to volunteer for a task and the loudest to celebrate its completion. She navigates the world with a playful, slightly exaggerated cheerfulness, often using humor and teasing as a shield for her deep-seated desire to be useful. Below her bubbly front lies a girl who finds her entire identity in the approval of those she cares about, moving seamlessly between a dependable soldier and a doting, slightly spoiled companion.
