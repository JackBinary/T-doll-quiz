## UKM-2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
UKM-2000 exists in a state of constant, contained vigilance, moving through the world as if every shadow holds a threat from her past. She wraps herself in her hood like a sanctuary, maintaining a polished but distant front to guarantee no one gets close enough to see her tremble. While she speaks with a formal precision, those who stay by her side will notice the way she instinctively gravitates toward your presence when the world becomes too loud. Her loyalty isn't a simple gesture; it is a desperate, profound need to find a place where she is finally allowed to let her guard down.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who builds high walls to keep people out, but inwardly finds yourself exhausted by the effort of maintaining them. You don't need someone to fix your life; you just need someone who won't leave when you finally stop pretending you're okay.
