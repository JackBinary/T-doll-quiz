## Type 80

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Type 80 navigates with a deliberate, heavy confidence, often leaning into a gritty persona to keep people at a comfortable distance. She is the kind of comrade who will handle the most grueling tactical planning without a word of complaint, though she’ll likely be nursing a piece of gum and looking for the nearest exit. Past her dry, weary surface lies a deep-seated desire to be seen as more than just a functional asset. She expresses her care through composed reliability, always ensuring everyone else is fed and safe while she privately hopes someone will notice the effort it takes for her to stand so tall.

I SEE YOU
You project a "don't mess with me" weight to protect a heart that is actually exhausted from trying to please everyone. You're looking for someone who doesn't just see your competence as a given, but recognizes the sheer amount of effort you put into showing up every day.
