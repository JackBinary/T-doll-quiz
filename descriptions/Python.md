## Python

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Python walks with the fluid, coiled pull of something that has never known the fear of being the prey. She views the world as a vast playground of trophies, treating every objective with a theatrical flair that borders on obsession. To her, affection isn't a gentle thing; it is the thrill of the chase and the satisfaction of finally cornering something she deems worthy. She navigates with a permanent, mischievous glint in her eyes, finding her greatest joy in the playful friction of testing someone's limits.

I SEE YOU
You have a secret streak of ruthlessness that you only let out when you find someone who isn't intimidated by it. You're looking for a partner who can match your intensity without asking you to dial it back.
