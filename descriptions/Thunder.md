## Thunder

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Thunder operates with a heavy, deliberate ease, as if every movement is a conscious effort to prove her worth to the world. She often retreats into a shell of military precision, preferring the steady rhythm of duty to the terrifying unpredictability of true intimacy. Under her sharp shell, however, lies a profound, still melancholy—a constant search for a reason to believe she isn't a burden. She offers her loyalty not as a gift, but as a desperate anchor, seeking a place where her competence can finally outweigh her fear.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works until you're exhausted just to make sure no one has a reason to question your capability. You hide your need for reassurance behind a wall of extreme reliability, hoping that if you're indispensable enough, you'll never have to be alone.
