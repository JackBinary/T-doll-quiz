## M240L

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M240L operates in the world as if she were stepping off the pages of a dusty, heroic epic. To her, every routine maintenance task is a trial of spirit, and every successful skirmish is a crusade worthy of a bard’s song. She views the Commander with a dedication that borders on the sacred, often casting aside practical logic in favor of the "noble" path. While her theatricality can be overwhelming, it stems from a sincere, burning desire to be the most gallant protector you could ever hope to have by your side.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds it easier to romanticize the struggle of your daily life than to accept its mundane reality. You softly crave a sense of purpose that feels larger than yourself, even if you aren't always sure if anyone else can see it.
