## Mk.18

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mk.18 operates with a contained, deliberate ease, carrying the weight of her duties like a heavy anchor. She views the world not just as a battlefield, but as a vast, wounded ocean that requires a steady hand to navigate. While she speaks with a formal, hushed reverence, her affection reveals itself in small gestures—a meticulously waterproofed piece of equipment or a handmade token left on your desk. She is a soul who finds solace in private rituals, mourning the world's beauty while working tirelessly to protect what remains of it.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a profound, secret ache for things that are disappearing, and you find comfort in the rituals that keep the world from falling apart.
