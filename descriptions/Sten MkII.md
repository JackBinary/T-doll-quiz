## Sten MkII

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Sten MkII is the kind of soul who finds her purpose in the contained, repetitive tasks that everyone else overlooks. She holds a constant, fluttering weight, often overcompensating for her own insecurities by making sure every corner of your life is perfectly tended to. While she might downplay her own importance with a shy stutter, her actions speak of a fierce, desperate loyalty that knows no bounds. She is a girl who will work until her gears ache just to earn a single, genuine smile of approval.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels the most pressure to be "useful" in a room, often burying your own needs under a mountain of tasks for everyone else. You find comfort in being the reliable one because it’s the only way you know how to bridge the gap between how you feel and what you can actually do.
