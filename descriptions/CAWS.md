## CAWS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CAWS holds herself with the weary poise of someone who has seen too much and decided that professionalism is the only safe harbor. She hides her deeper anxieties behind a wall of dry sarcasm and a "get-it-done" attitude, preferring to focus on the task at hand rather than the weight of it. Yet, behind her mercenary outside lies a contained, persistent yearning for stability and small comforts. She doesn't ask for your dedication out loud, but she will always find a way to stay just one step behind you, making sure you aren't facing the horizon alone.

I SEE YOU
You’re the type of person who puts on a competent face to keep from having to explain how tired you actually are. You'd rather be useful than vulnerable, but you're privately looking for someone who can handle your silence without needing to fill it.
