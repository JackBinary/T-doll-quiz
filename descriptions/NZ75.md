## NZ75

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
NZ75 operates with a boisterous, drill-sergeant current that demands immediate attention the moment she enters a room. She takes immense pride in her capabilities, often leaning into a sharp, authoritative persona to keep everyone—especially her superiors—on their toes. However, behind that loud bravado lies a doll who genuinely craves validation and small tokens of affection. When the professional mask slips, she is easily flustered, revealing a hidden warmth that she tries desperately to cover with another stern command.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overcompensating with competence because you’re afraid of being seen as ordinary. You want someone who will hold you to a higher standard, but only if they're willing to be the one to give you a gold star when the day is done.
