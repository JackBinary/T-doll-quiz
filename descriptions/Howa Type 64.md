## Howa Type 64

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Howa Type 64 walks with a gentle, earnest force that feels like a constant effort to be exactly what others need her to be. She is the type to spend hours perfecting a small gesture, only to find her voice trembling when it finally comes time to share it. While she often retreats to the edges of a room to observe, her heart is always leaning toward the center, seeking a steady hand to hold onto in a world that feels too loud. She doesn't just want to be useful; she wants to be enough.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who over-prepares for every social interaction as a defense mechanism against the possibility of being misunderstood. You find safety in being productive because it feels easier to be "useful" than it does to be known.
