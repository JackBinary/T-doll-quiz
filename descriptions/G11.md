## G11

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
G11 exists in the world with a heavy, deliberate sort of lethargy, as if every step is a conscious negotiation with her own exhaustion. She isn't interested in glory or high-octane heroics, preferring the restrained sanctuary of a soft chair and the simple comfort of a shared silence. Under her blunt "don't-bother-me" attitude lies a profound, weary loyalty to those who provide her with a sense of safety. To her, a peaceful afternoon isn't just a luxury; it is the hard-won prize she protects with a restrained, steady loyalty.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who uses "doing nothing" as a deliberate shield against a world that demands too much of your presence. You don't actually want to be lazy; you just want to be allowed to exist without having to perform for anyone.
