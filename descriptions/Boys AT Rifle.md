## Boys AT Rifle

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Boys AT Rifle navigates through the modern world with the heavy, deliberate poise of a relic from a bygone era. She views her duties not as mere commands, but as sacred vows of fealty, treating every mission as a chapter in a grand, heroic saga. There is a restrained intensity in the way she maintains her equipment, handling her rifle with the same reverence a priest might accord a holy icon. While she may seem distant, her loyalty is absolute, expressed through a stoic commitment to honor and a preference for the grueling path of self-improvement.

I SEE YOU
You have a tendency to romanticize your own struggles because the reality of them feels too mundane to bear. You aren't looking for a friend so much as you are looking for a purpose that feels worthy of your effort.
