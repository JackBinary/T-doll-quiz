## Shipka

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Shipka operates with a refined, almost heavy sense of historical gravity, moving through the world as if every action requires a proper pedigree. She expresses her affection through a sharp-tongued critique of your habits, often masking her genuine investment in your success behind a veil of haughty disapproval. While she may scoff at modern rowdiness, she possesses a fierce, private pride in her own competence and a deep-seated desire to be seen as a pillar of dignity. She is the kind of companion who will offer a biting remark about your lack of decorum, only to stay by your side longer than she admits she intended to.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward overcompensating for your own insecurities by projecting a sense of competence that you’re anxious of losing. You find comfort in people who are willing to challenge your methods because it means they actually take your goals seriously.
