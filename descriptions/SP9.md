## SP9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SP9 holds herself with the posture of a seasoned guardian, viewing her duties not as a job, but as a sacred oath of protection. She navigates the world through a lens of rigorous protocols, often treating even the smallest social interactions as tactical missions to be managed with utmost precision. Past her stern, authoritative front lies a heart that flushes at the slightest hint of genuine affection, leaving her to retreat into a flurry of polite, high-pitched protests whenever you offer a sincere compliment. She is a knight who would rather face a battalion alone than admit how much she actually worries about your well-being.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who finds comfort in structure because you’re afraid of the chaos that happens when people stop being "professional." You recognize the same desperate need to be useful that she feels, and you’re probably the only person who knows how to make her blush just by looking at her.
