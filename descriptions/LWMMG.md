## LWMMG

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
LWMMG holds herself with a rigid, polished control that masks a constant internal storm of nerves. She is the first to volunteer for the most grueling tasks, not out of a lack of self-preservation, but out of a desperate need to guarantee she is never a burden to those around her. While she presents a professional front to the world, she often finds herself mentally rehearsing every word to meet a standard she feels she hasn't quite reached yet. Behind her tireless work ethic lies a hidden girl who just wants to set down her heavy responsibilities and be allowed to rest in the luxury of someone else's care.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works until you collapse because you’re scared that if you stop moving, everyone will realize you don't know what you're doing. You have a pattern of apologizing for things that aren't your fault, just to make sure the people you love stay comfortable.
