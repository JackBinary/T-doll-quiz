## M9

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M9 exists in the world as if every room she enters is a stage waiting for her grand entrance. She is a flash of glitter and high-pitched laughter, endlessly curating an image of effortless perfection for everyone to admire. While she thrives on the roar of the crowd, there is a hidden side to her that seeks a more private sanctuary, where she can let the mask slip just enough for someone she trusts to see the girl behind the performance.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who works tirelessly to be the "life of the party" because the thought of being ignored is far more terrifying than the exhaustion of being noticed. You have a secret, almost painful pride in being the person everyone relies on to keep things from getting too still.
