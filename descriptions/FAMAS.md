## FAMAS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FAMAS operates with the restrained poise of someone who has mastered the art of being indispensable. She is the steady heartbeat of the squad, finding comfort in the rhythm of a well-executed plan and the satisfaction of a job completed without a single flaw. While she speaks with a polished, professional care, there is a soft, lingering shadow in her eyes that speaks of a history she prefers not to revisit. She doesn't just want to be a soldier; she wants to be the foundation upon which everyone else can stand safely.

I SEE YOU
You have a tendency to over-prepare for every situation because you’re scared of being the reason something goes wrong. You find it much easier to be useful than to be vulnerable, often hiding your own exhaustion behind a perfectly organized to-do list.
