## FN-49

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
FN-49 operates with a delicate, almost fragile poise, always hovering on the edge of an apology. She is the kind of person who stays late to make sure every detail is perfect, not out of a desire for glory, but because she is uneasy of falling short of the expectations placed upon her. While she may tremble when the spotlight finds her, her dedication is absolute, and she finds her greatest peace in the composed, domestic victories of a well-made meal or a finished task. She doesn't just want to be helpful; she wants to prove that she belongs in the room.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels like a fraud the moment you succeed, convinced that everyone is just being nice to you. You work twice as hard as everyone else because you’re scared that if you stop moving, they’ll realize you don’t know what you’re doing.
