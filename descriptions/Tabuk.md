## Tabuk

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Tabuk exists in the world with a delicate, constant awareness of the space around her, viewing order as the only thing keeping the chaos at bay. She expresses her care through restrained acts of service, finding peace in the steady rhythm of stacking supplies and perfecting the symmetry of a shared meal. While her high-strung nature can sometimes make her appear flustered, it stems from a sincere desire to create a sanctuary where everything has its rightful place. To her, a perfectly aligned shelf is not just a chore; it is a form of love language.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels a physical weight in your chest when a room is messy, finding it impossible to relax until every loose item is tucked into its designated home. You don't just want things organized; you need the world to make sense so that you can finally breathe.
