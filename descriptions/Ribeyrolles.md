## Ribeyrolles

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ribeyrolles walks with a cautious poise, as if she is always trying to occupy as little space as possible. She wears a contained, heavy sincerity in her voice, often pausing to offer a soft apology before she even finishes a sentence. While she possesses a hidden well of determination, she views her own progress as a gift granted by others rather than an achievement of her own. She doesn't just want to be useful; she wants to be worthy, seeking a steady hand to anchor her when her own self-doubt feels overwhelming.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels like a project you're always failing at, yet you find a strange, comforting home in being looked after. You have a pattern of apologizing for your existence before anyone else has even had a chance to notice you.
