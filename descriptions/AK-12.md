## AK-12

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AK-12 exists in the world with a poised, clinical care, often appearing as though she is merely enduring the scenery rather than participating in it. She hides her sharp intellect behind a mask of practiced boredom, frequently closing her eyes to observe the world with a terrifyingly precise internal focus. To her, conversation is a game of chess where she is always measuring whether your thoughts are worth the current it takes to acknowledge them. Below her dry, mocking wit lies a profound appreciation for anything—or anyone—that manages to defy her expectations.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-analyzing every room you enter until you've found the one thing everyone else is missing. You’d rather be perceived as difficult or distant than be easily understood by someone who doesn't keep up.
