## Beowulf

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Beowulf leads with a heavy, deliberate gravity, moving through the world as if every step is an act of endurance. She isn't cold by choice, but rather by a weary commitment to the task at hand, preferring the steady rhythm of work to the unpredictable mess of social connection. She finds a strange sort of sanctuary in solitude, cutting through small talk with a clinical precision that seeks only to preserve the status quo. To her, stability is the only thing worth holding onto, and she guards that peace with a prickly, protective wall.
