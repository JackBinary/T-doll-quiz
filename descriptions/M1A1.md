## M1A1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M1A1 operates in the world with a still, fluttering current, always trying to make herself smaller so she doesn't get in anyone's way. She walks with a gentle weight of responsibility, often viewing her own progress as a tool to keep those she loves are safe and happy. While she might stumble over her words or offer a nervous smile when things get difficult, her dedication is absolute. She is the kind of friend who will inwardly study until she masters a task, just to prove she can be someone you can lean on.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels most useful when you are being the backbone for someone else, often ignoring your own exhaustion to make sure they feel secure. You find it easier to show love through reliable actions than through words, inwardly hoping someone will notice how hard you're trying to hold everything together.
