## AK-15

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AK-15 exists in the world with a heavy, deliberate purpose, treating every interaction as a series of variables to be solved. She doesn't offer smiles or casual conversation, preferring instead to demonstrate her dedication through the flawless execution of your directives. While she often finds human social customs to be baffling and inefficient, she holds a profound, almost reverent respect for your leadership. To her, your competence is the only thing that makes sense in a chaotic world, and she will to herself shoulder any burden to guarantee your objectives are met.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-optimizing your own life to the point of exhaustion because you find comfort in a plan that actually works. You aren't looking for a friend to hold your hand; you're looking for someone who can finally keep up with your pace.
