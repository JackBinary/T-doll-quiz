## CF05

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CF05 is a flash of infectious weight who believes that the secret to a happy life can be found at the bottom of a bubbling pot. She navigates the world with a bright smile and a constant, enthusiastic readiness to share a meal with anyone she considers a friend. While she can be fiercely stubborn when it comes to her favorite hobbies, her actions are always driven by a sincere desire to nourish the people around her. To her, a shared plate of spicy food isn't just a snack; it’s her way of weaving a bond of true companionship.

I SEE YOU
