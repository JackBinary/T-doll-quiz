## 9A-91

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
9A-91 exists with a polished, high-presence poise that masks a deep-seated need to be noticed. She doesn't just want to complete a mission; she wants to make sure that every success is a reason for you to look at her with pride. While she can be a formidable soldier, her true heart lies in the restrained moments where her professional poise softens into a desperate, endearing earnestness. She views your approval as her ultimate purpose, consistently seeking to prove that she is indispensable to your side.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works twice as hard when you feel invisible, always over-delivering on expectations just to make sure someone finally says "well done."
