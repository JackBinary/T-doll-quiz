## S.A.T.8

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
S.A.T.8 is a burst of proactive presence, always the first to volunteer for a task and the last to stop double-checking the logistics. While she radiates a bubbly, "buddy-style" enthusiasm, there is a sincere vulnerability in the way she seeks out your approval and steady presence. She expresses her attachment through the restrained precision of a home-cooked meal and the meticulous way she over-explains every detail of her work to keep you feel safe. Below her high-spirited shell lies a girl who chooses to be brave every single day, transforming her own nerves into a fierce dedication to the team.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-functioning in stressful situations because you’re anxious of what happens if things fall apart. You don't just want to succeed; you want to be the person everyone can rely on when the plan inevitably changes.
