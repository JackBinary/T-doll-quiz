## AK-74M

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AK-74M leads with a deliberate, almost architectural stillness, moving through the world with a focus on precision that borders on the clinical. She treats her duties with a soldier’s dedication, often masking her inner intensity behind a veil of dry logic and formal speech. While she might frame a shared moment as a "necessary break" or a "calculated gesture of camaraderie," there is a profound sincerity in her actions. Under her composed front lies a woman who is slowly, carefully learning how to let someone see the person behind the uniform.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who values competence above all else and would rather have a reliable partner who knows the plan than a warm one who just offers platitudes. You find a strange, grounding comfort in boundaries, especially when they are the only thing keeping the world from feeling overwhelming.
