## MPK

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MPK is a burst of kinetic pull who treats every hallway like a personal obstacle course. She is the type to burst into a room with a grin, immediately looking for a way to turn a mundane task into a high-speed competition. While she can be bossy and relentless when pushing others to move, her constant need for attention stems from a still, fierce desire to be seen as indispensable. She finds her truest sense of freedom in the secret physical feats she performs, creating a private world of adrenaline that she only shares with those she trusts most.

I SEE YOU
You often use a busy schedule as a shield to avoid sitting with your own thoughts, finding comfort in the rhythm of constant motion. You are someone who desperately wants to be taken seriously, yet you find yourself subconsciously seeking out people who will provide the permission you're too afraid to give yourself.
