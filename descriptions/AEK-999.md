## AEK-999

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
AEK-999 lives her life as if there is a spotlight following her every move, turning the grim reality of the battlefield into a choreographed masterpiece. She doesn't just execute orders; she performs them, endlessly looking for the right "beat" to keep the momentum flowing. Below her infectious, high-force front lies a doll who deeply treasures the aesthetic of the moment, finding beauty in the small, imperfect details that others might overlook. For her, every victory is an encore, and every mission is a chance to guarantee the audience is captivated.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward turning your responsibilities into a hobby because you find it much easier to endure the grind when you can frame it as a personal project. You’re the person who cares more about the "vibe" of the room than the actual goal, yet you’re softly scared of being boring.
