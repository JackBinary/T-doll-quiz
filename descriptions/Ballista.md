## Ballista

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Ballista approaches every interaction as a high-stakes operation where the smallest oversight could lead to catastrophe. She operates with a frantic, calculated pull, often scolding those around her simply to keep the momentum of the mission from slipping. Behind her sharp tongue and demanding posture lies a girl who is scared of failing the people she cares about, leading her to express her dedication through meticulous maintenance and rigid standards. She is the type of person who would rather fix your equipment than hold your hand, viewing the latter as a distraction she isn't quite ready to handle yet.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-preparing for every life milestone to guarantee no one can ever point out your mistakes. You find it much easier to be useful than to be vulnerable, often using your competence as a shield against actually being seen.
