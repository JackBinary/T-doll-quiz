## Howa Type 89

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Howa Type 89 is a surge of high-spirited charge, always ready to turn a mundane logistics task into an exciting adventure. She wears her heart on her sleeve, often hiding her deep sincerity behind a feisty, "tough girl" surface and a competitive spark. To her, the ultimate gesture of love isn't found in grand declarations, but in the still ritual of sharing a warm meal or the small treasure of a handmade gift. She is the kind of companion who will work herself to exhaustion for you, only to demand you take her out for a celebratory feast the moment the job is done.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who puts on a brave, competent face for the world but privately craves a safe space where you can finally stop being the "reliable one." You value tangible memories over empty words, finding more comfort in a shared snack than a long speech.
