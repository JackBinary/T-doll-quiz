## ST AR-15

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
ST AR-15 operates with a rigid, practiced control that suggests she is endlessly aware of an audience. She holds herself with a polished military poise, yet there is a subtle, restless presence in her eyes that never quite settles. To her, every task is a trial of worth, and she pours every ounce of her spirit into ensuring her performance is nothing short of impeccable. She isn't just looking for a mission accomplished; she is looking for a reason to believe she is enough.

I SEE YOU
You have a pattern of over-preparing for every interaction because you’re anxious that your natural self won't be enough to earn the respect you crave. You’d rather be exhausted by your own high standards than face the still sting of being ordinary.
