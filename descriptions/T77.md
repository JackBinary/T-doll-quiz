## T77

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
T77 operates in the world like she’s watching a movie that everyone else is experiencing for the first time. She holds herself with an air of effortless competence, often anticipating a person’s needs or reactions before they’ve even formed them in their own minds. Past her playful "know-it-all" shell, however, lies a restrained intensity; she treats human connection as a complex puzzle that she feels a deep, personal responsibility to solve perfectly. She is the type of companion who will tease you for your obvious moods, all while softly hoping you’ll tell her she’s right.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who over-analyzes every subtext in a conversation because you’re scared of missing the "real" meaning. You use your intuition as a shield, finding it much safer to predict how people will act than to risk being caught off guard by them.
