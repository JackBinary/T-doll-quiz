## Webley

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Webley walks through the world with the poise of a woman who knows that victory is meaningless if it isn't presented with impeccable taste. She views the logistics of war as a high-stakes social gala, where every supply run is an opportunity for a curated acquisition and every command is a matter of aesthetic coordination. Past her refined, "Ufufu"-laced front lies a fierce care to your public image, ensuring you are never merely a leader, but a masterpiece of dignity.

I SEE YOU
You have a secret, intense need for order that makes you feel slightly exposed when things are messy. You don't just want to win; you want to look like you weren't even trying when you do.
