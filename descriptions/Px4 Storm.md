## Px4 Storm

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Px4 Storm exists in the world with the sharp, electric weight of a high-stakes entrepreneur. She views every objective not as a duty to be fulfilled, but as a transaction to be mastered, always keeping a mental tally of the rewards waiting at the finish line. While she can be playfully demanding and a bit of a brat when it comes to her "bonuses," she possesses a grounded, pragmatic streak that keeps her sharp in the face of danger. She is the kind of partner who will joke about the risks of the job one moment and then expertly navigate them the next, all while ensuring she gets her fair cut of the glory.

I SEE YOU
You have a talent for identifying the most efficient path to success, but you often find yourself exhausted by the sheer weight of being the only person in the room who actually knows how to make a profit.
