## HK33

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
HK33 exists with a sharp, intentional edge, moving through the world as if every interaction is a tactical variable to be managed. She keeps her circle small and her boundaries rigid, preferring to observe the Commander from a distance with a skeptical, measuring gaze. However, under that professional frost lies a profound yearning for a sanctuary where she doesn't have to be vigilant. When she finally lets someone in, she doesn't just offer her company; she leans into them with a heavy, startling sincerity, seeking a place where she can finally set down her guard.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward over-preparing for every social interaction because you're afraid of being caught off guard. You're looking for someone who won't just tolerate your walls, but will eventually be the only person you trust to help you tear them down.
