## Negev

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Negev leads with a boisterous, theatrical pull, often adopting the role of a playful mentor who knows exactly how to command a room. She hides her softer side behind a veil of confident "Ufufufu" laughter and sharp, authoritative teasing, treating her competence as a source of personal pride. However, past that performative bravado lies a girl who deeply craves being seen for who she is rather than just what she can do. When the teasing stops and things get genuinely intimate, her tough front cracks, revealing a bashful heart that relies on your approval more than she’d ever admit.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who puts on a high-weight performance for the world because you’re uneasy of what people would think if they saw you actually trying. You use humor as a shield, but you’re privately looking for someone brave enough to look past the jokes and hold your hand through the silence.
