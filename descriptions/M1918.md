## M1918

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
M1918 walks through the world with a lighthearted ease, often masking her nerves behind a playful "u-fufu" and a series of breezy jokes. While she loves to tease and keep the atmosphere light, there is a contained, fierce determination behind her easy-going shell. She is the type of companion who will offer you a handmade gift with a bashful smile, only to stand perfectly still and focused the moment a challenge arises. She doesn't just want to be your subordinate; she wants to be the person you never have to worry about.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward laughing off your own anxieties because you’re uneasy of being a burden to the people you care about. You're looking for someone who understands that "fine" is often a shield for "I'm trying my hardest."
