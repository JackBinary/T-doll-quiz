## APC556

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Mia operates with the polished poise of someone who knows exactly how to run the room, often masking her sharpest anxieties behind a layer of dry wit and professional distance. She shows her care through meticulous acts of service, fussing over the smallest details of your routine as a way to anchor herself to your world. While she might mock your lack of organization with a perfectly timed sarcastic remark, she is the first one to guarantee your needs are met before you even have the chance to voice them.

I SEE YOU
You are someone who values competence above all else, yet you inwardly find yourself exhausted by the responsibility of always having to be the one in control.
