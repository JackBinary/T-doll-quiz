## PPK

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PPK walks through the world as if every interaction is a stage play designed for her private amusement. She thrives on the friction of boundaries, always testing your patience with a playful, predator’s grin and a teasing lilt in her voice. While she delights in being a brat who demands your undivided attention, there is a sharp, underlying intensity to her gaze that suggests she isn't just looking for a toy, but a partner who can survive her whims. She wants to be your favorite distraction, the one who keeps you on your toes until you eventually stop trying to resist her.

I SEE YOU
You have a very specific way of finding comfort in people who refuse to be "nice" to you. You’re tired of being the responsible one, and you inwardly crave someone who will force you to stop thinking for a while.
