## DP28

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
DP28 holds herself with the effortless ease of someone who knows exactly how much power she holds in a simple smile. She is the ultimate "Big Sister," weaving a tapestry of genuine warmth and playful mischief that keeps everyone around her guessing. While she is always the first to offer a comforting word or a small gift, she delights in the subtle art of testing boundaries, often using a giggle to mask a much deeper intensity. She doesn't just want to protect you; she wants to be the only person you feel safe enough to let your guard down with.

I SEE YOU
You have a pull toward seeking out people who provide a sense of security, but you inwardly crave the thrill of someone who isn't afraid to push your buttons. You’re looking for a sanctuary that feels a little bit dangerous.
