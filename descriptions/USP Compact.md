## USP Compact

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
USP Compact holds herself with a soft, fluttering pull, always moving with a sense of purpose that seems fueled by a measured inner restlessness. She is the kind of doll who will work twice as hard as anyone else just to keep she hasn't made a single mistake, often hiding her face behind a flurry of apologies. Despite her frequent stammers and the way she shrinks back from praise, her loyalty is absolute and her heart is entirely on her sleeve. She doesn't just want to be a soldier; she wants to be someone you can truly rely on, even if she's currently tripping over her own feet trying to prove it.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who stays late to double-check every detail because you’re scared that someone else’s mistake will somehow become your responsibility. You find it much easier to provide for everyone else than to let anyone see how much you actually need their approval.
