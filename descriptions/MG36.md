## MG36

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
MG36 holds herself with a polished, professional distance that feels like a fortress, but it’s one built to protect something fragile rather than something cold. She is the first to step into the line of fire, masking her own exhaustion with a dry wit and a martyr’s sense of duty. Yet, in the still moments between shifts, she finds solace in the "old-fashioned", the hum of a classical record or the glow of a sci-fi film. She doesn't know how to ask for help, so she shows her affection through small, practical acts of service, hoping you'll notice the way she always ensures you're taken care of first.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who keeps your guard up until you find someone who is patient enough to wait for it to drop. You value competence over constant chatter, but you without a word crave a sanctuary where you don't have to be the strongest person in the room.
