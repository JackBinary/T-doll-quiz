## Suomi

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Suomi leads with a polished, almost rigid care, treating every duty as a testament to her homeland's honor. While she presents a stern and disciplined face to the world, there is a profound softness below her uniform that only reveals itself in still moments. She expresses her deepest feelings through the meticulous perfection of her work and the private melodies she hums when no one is watching. To her, true strength isn't just about endurance; it’s about the courage to remain vulnerable with the few people she truly trusts.

I SEE YOU
You take your responsibilities so seriously that you often forget how to let yourself rest, seeking out people who will hold the line for you so you can finally breathe.
