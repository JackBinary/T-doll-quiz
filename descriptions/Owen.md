## Owen

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Owen leads with a bundle of nervous pull, always looking for the perfect angle to capture a moment before it fades. While she tries her best to maintain a professional "pro" way, her genuine enthusiasm often spills over into bubbly exclamations and frantic documenting of the smallest details. She is the heart of every celebration, the one who stays awake until dawn to make sure no memory is left behind. Deep down, she just wants to prove that her passion for the story matters more than the quality of the lens she uses to tell it.

I SEE YOU
You are the person who feels a strange, heavy responsibility to be the keeper of everyone’s memories because you’re scared of how quickly things change. You’d rather be the one behind the scenes making sure the "vibe" is preserved than the one standing in the spotlight.
