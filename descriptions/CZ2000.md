## CZ2000

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
CZ2000 is the kind of presence that fills a room before she even speaks, radiating a fierce, protective pull that demands to be noticed. She leads with the bravado of a seasoned leader, always ready to jump into the fray or fuss over your well-being with a theatrical flourish. Yet, below her loud declarations and "tough girl" front lies a heart that is incredibly easy to rattle, often melting into a flustered, endearing mess the moment someone looks at her too closely. She doesn't just want to be your protector; she wants to be your most loyal, albeit slightly chaotic, companion.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of overcompensating for your own insecurities by being the loudest person in the room, but you without a word crave a space where you don't have to perform for anyone.
