## PSG-1

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PSG-1 is a portrait of composed, earnest attachment, often moving with a deliberate precision that masks a restless inner drive. She views her role not just as a duty, but as a constant opportunity to prove her worth, often apologizing for minor inconveniences that most others wouldn't notice. While she excels in the sharp focus of the field, she finds her truest self in the small, domestic tasks of service—ensuring a room is tidy or a task is perfectly completed. She is a protector who leads with the weight of the world on her shoulders, always seeking to be the person you can rely on without fail.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels like a burden the moment you ask for help, so you work twice as hard to make sure no one ever has to carry your weight.
