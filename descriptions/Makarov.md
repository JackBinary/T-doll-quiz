## Makarov

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Makarov exists with a commanding presence that masks a deeply sentimental heart. She doesn't just want to lead; she wants to build something permanent, often speaking of shared futures and "planting trees" as if she’s already envisioning a life far beyond the battlefield. While she can be mischievous and sharp-witted in the moment, her true loyalty lies in her desire to shoulder your burdens so you don't have to carry them alone. She is a fierce protector who views you as her true equal, seeking a partner who will grow alongside her until the world finally settles into peace.
