## PP-90

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PP-90 is a spark of bright pull and genuine heart, always the first to offer a cheerful "Hehe" and a helping hand. She leads with a bubbly enthusiasm that can sometimes mask her own contained anxieties about whether she’s doing enough. Whether she’s trying to bake a treat that ends up in pieces or cheering the loudest during a difficult mission, she pours every ounce of her spirit into being useful. She doesn't just want to succeed; she wants to be the reason everyone else feels like they can succeed, too.

I SEE YOU
You have a way of over-functioning to prove you belong in the room, often choosing to be the loudest optimist because it's easier than admitting how much you actually care about everyone's opinion.
