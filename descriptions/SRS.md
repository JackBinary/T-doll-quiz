## SRS

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
SRS leads with the poise of a scholar who views every moment as a test of character. She doesn't just want to succeed; she wants to achieve a level of mastery that leaves no room for error, often treating her closest bonds as opportunities for rigorous personal growth. While she can be intimidating with her critiques, her stern presence masks a deep-seated desire to guarantee everyone around her is capable of meeting the highest standards. To her, a mistake isn't a tragedy, it is simply a data point that requires more disciplined study.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who feels a secret sense of anxiety when a project is "good enough" because you know it could have been perfect. You value competence above all else and would rather be corrected by someone who knows what they’re doing than comforted by someone who doesn't.
