## OTs-14

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
Groza operates with the effortless poise of someone who has mastered the art of being both a weapon and a sanctuary. While she maintains a sharp, disciplined outside in the field, she thrives in the composed transition from "soldier" to companion, often finding her most authentic self in the simple act of preparing a meal or sharing a glass of wine. She is a woman of deliberate contrasts, capable of offering stoic tactical advice one moment and a playful, almost possessive tease the next. Under her sophisticated composure lies a deep-seated desire for a partnership where she doesn't have to carry the weight of the mission alone.

I SEE YOU
You are the type of person who keeps your most vulnerable desires behind a wall of competence, waiting for someone steady enough to notice the cracks. You don't need a hero; you need a peer who can match your intensity and then help you put it down.
