## PPD-40

WHAT SHE'S LIKE
PPD-40 holds herself with an air of polished authority, moving through the world with a sharp eye for detail and an even sharper tongue for inefficiency. She treats her duties like a high-stakes contract, pushing everyone toward completion so she can finally retreat into the restrained luxury she prizes so highly. Past her haughty surface and the way she scolds you for your lapses in focus, there is a woman who expresses her loyalty through the finest things life has to offer. She is a protector who would rather buy you a feast than tell you she cares, masking her own vulnerability behind a veil of impeccable taste.

I SEE YOU
You are the kind of person who works twice as hard as everyone else just so you can justify a vacation you're already too tired to enjoy. You value competence above all else because it’s the only thing that makes the chaos of your life feel manageable.
